## §8 Risks / counter-arguments

[src: People-NS research §9 + Game Theory research §10]:

### R-A — State counter-attack
Immigration policy / non-compete legislation / criminalization of coordination / national contracts as counter. High severity, medium likelihood. **Mitigation:** geographic distribution, no political claims, professional positioning.

### R-B — Cringe collapse
Top professionals reject if game feels forced / corporate-gamification cringe. 1 bad cohort kills brand. **Mitigation:** L1 quality > quantity, taste-curated, NO mass-market launch first.

### R-C — Ruslan-SPOF (founder bus-factor)
Cult-of-personality risk. **Mitigation:** L1 succession planning start early, Charter contains amendment process, R13 candidate «no-cult-of-personality» Tier 2 rule.

### R-D — Constitutional capture
Anti-extraction watered down под external/internal pressure. **Mitigation:** R12 elevation early (per Q2 ack); fork-and-leave right preserves checks.

### R-E — Cooperation collapse cascade
One major defection в L1 → discount rate spikes → ALL-D becomes rational locally → cascade. **Mitigation:** L1 robust screening; Charter binding signatures; forgiveness mechanism (Axelrod TFT) для noise tolerance.

### R-F — Reputation gaming / Sybil attacks
Reputation ledger недоstating without identity verification. **Mitigation:** mutual vouching mechanism; reputation slowly earned, fast lost; identity tied к real professional track-record.

### R-G — Anti-extraction tension (Hooked Model)
Mы используем engagement mechanics НЕ extractively (Indistractable principle). Тонкая грань. **Mitigation:** explicit «time well spent» metrics; self-limiting mechanisms; transparent design choices.
