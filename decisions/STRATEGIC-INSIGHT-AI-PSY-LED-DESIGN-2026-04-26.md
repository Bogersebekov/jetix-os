# Strategic Insight — AI Psy-Led Design Principle

## §0 TL;DR

**Insight (audio_531, voice manifesto-quality):** AI-системы должны проектироваться психологами / специалистами по человеческому мышлению (не только инженерами) — потому что AI взаимодействует с пользователем как поведенческий протокол, не нейтральный инструмент. Engineering-only optimisation (under engagement-economy default) ведёт к user degradation (passive, dependent, "тупее").

**Resolution:** Jetix invests Phase-2+ в research collaboration с психологами + iterative testing real benefit + user feedback loops. Применяется к product design, UX, Brain layer, voice-pipeline, Life OS подсистемам.

**Phase-1 implication:** **NONE.** D14 (Research revenue-instrumental Phase 1) keeps Phase-1 focus на consulting + producer center revenue. Psychology-research direction = Phase 2+ (post €200K validation, alongside D24 activation).

**This is NOT new Lock yet** — это новая principle на existing 28-Locks framework. **Может promote к D30 lock в Phase 2+** когда operational mechanism + budget materialise.

- **Background:** PhD / clinical psychology / cognitive science / HCI с focus на digital interaction
- **Experience:** 5+ лет research или practice involving technology-mediated cognition / behavioural design
- **AI familiarity:** comfortable работать с LLM-based tools, не AI-skeptic
- **Practitioner orientation:** хочет real-world application + iterative testing, не purely academic
- **Language:** EN required; DE plus; RU possible

### Compensation structure (Phase 2+, preliminary):

- Phase-2 (€200K-€1M): Fractional consultant first (€200-400/hr, 5-10 hrs/wk), validating fit
- Phase-2+ → Phase-3: Full-time hire (€80-120k/year + equity 0.5-1.5%) when AI-BIOS productization scales
- Long-term: Senior advisor / methodology partner role

- ❌ AI-skeptic / luddite (incompatible с D2 + D24 + D7 + D20)

- **Channels:** Academic networks (Stanford HCI / TU Berlin Cognitive Sciences / European-AI-and-Psychology consortia); fractional advisory platforms; LinkedIn Sales Navigator post-€200K.
