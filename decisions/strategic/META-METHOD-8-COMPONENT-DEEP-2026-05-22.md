## §1 C1 — «Радикальная проработка»

**Voice:** «зафиксировал вот как раз **самую ебейшую проработку**» [audio_719 claim 1]

**Definition:** Sustained, non-shortcutting engagement at depth exceeding default-effort. No «that'll do» exit.

**Examples:**
1. Watchmaker assembling Patek Philippe — every gear adjusted to 0.001mm
2. Marathon-runner taper-week preparation — nutrition + sleep tracking + pace-zone work
3. Mother preparing daughter's wedding — every detail considered, multiple iterations
4. Surgeon studying complex tumor case — 5-angle imaging review, 3 specialist consults
5. Carpenter handcrafting dining table — choosing wood, planning grain, oil-finish over months
6. Pianist preparing Rachmaninoff 3rd concerto — separate hands, slow practice, contextualisation
7. Cook preparing Thanksgiving turkey — brine 24h, dry overnight, baste every 30min

**Cross-tradition:** Ericsson deliberate practice (≈ stretch beyond comfort + focused attention); Polya phase 3 carrying-out-the-plan; Unix Simplicity Rule 5; Aristotle techne.

**Jetix:** Method V2 §J Phase 3 «глубина»; Foundation Part 5 Compound Learning.

**Failure mode:** Perfectionism trap (A1) — infinite-deferral. Mitigation: paired w/ C13 speed.

**Integration:** C1 + C4 + C13 — radical elaboration on important things, on time.

## §2 C2 — «Глубина работы»

**Voice:** «самая сука глубокая [проработка]» [claim 1]

**Definition:** Engagement that goes beyond surface; «why behind why»; mechanisms not symptoms. C2 = penetration (vs. C1 = intensity).

**Examples:**
1. Doctor diagnosing chronic fatigue — full thyroid + autoimmune + iron + sleep study panel
2. Software debug — minimal repro, full stack trace, root cause not patch
3. Therapist exploring relationship pattern — family-of-origin + attachment style
4. Investigative journalist — financial filings + insider sources + cross-referencing
5. Reader engaging with Aristotle NE — multiple translations + commentary + applying examples
6. Parent investigating school struggle — teacher meeting + classroom observation + learning-difference screening
7. Engineer designing bridge — geological + thermal + 100-year wind data

**Cross-tradition:** Polanyi tacit dimension; Beer VSM S4 Intelligence; DDD bounded contexts ubiquitous language.

**Jetix:** AUDIO-721-INSIGHTS-REPORT §3 unified framework; Foundation Part 3 KB.

**Failure mode:** Rabbit-hole (A2) — depth without portfolio-balance. Mitigation: C7 leverage portfolio-thinking.

**Integration:** C2 + C9 — «pursuing why behind why» pair.

## §3 C3 — «Качество работы»

**Voice:** Implicit cluster from C1+C2.

**Definition:** No half-finished work shipped; consistent fitness-for-purpose standard. C3 = the *consistent standard* across outputs.

**Examples:**
1. Chef's mise-en-place — every dish to same plating standard
2. Photographer's portfolio curation — only top 5% included; rest deleted
3. Software shipping — no commented-out TODOs in production
4. Lawyer's brief writing — proofread 3 times; cite-checking every reference
5. Sommelier's tasting notes — every wine logged same depth
6. Translator's published output — proofread by native + back-translated
7. Restaurant maintaining Michelin standard — every plate same standard

**Cross-tradition:** Unix Simplicity + Robustness; SAFe principle 5 objective evaluation; CMMI Level 3 Defined standards.

**Jetix:** Foundation Part 6a Provenance Officer F-G-R grading; Cycle Wave A→B→C→D quality gates.

**Failure mode:** Quality theatre (A3) — sign-off ceremony without actual quality. Mitigation: C6 honesty + measurement substrate.

**Integration:** C3 + C13 — «high-quality fast» Toyota-Production-System discipline.

## §4 C4 — «Фокус на важном»

**Voice:** «настройку **только на важных делах**» [claim 1]

**Definition:** Explicit attention budget directed to high-leverage / high-value activities; conscious refusal to engage low-priority. C4 = positive choice; C5 = negative refusal; they pair.

**Examples:**
1. Surgeon during operation — total attention to patient; no phone
2. Mother with young children — when reading with daughter, daughter is universe
3. Olympic athlete during Olympic year — training/recovery/nutrition; nothing else
4. Buffett reading 5 hours/day — clears calendar; reading time inviolable
5. Writer's morning hours — locked door, no internet, single project
6. Programmer in deep-work block — 4 hours uninterrupted; one problem
7. Therapist with client — 50-min window of total attention

**Cross-tradition:** Polya phase 2 devising-a-plan; Csikszentmihalyi flow #5 concentration; VSM S2 Coordination; Cynefin sense → respond.

**Jetix:** Foundation Part 4 IP-1 STRICT; Pillar A North Star; Method V2 §J Phase 2.

**Failure mode:** Focus on wrong thing — focus necessary but not sufficient. Mitigation: C7 leverage + C9 mechanism literacy.

**Integration:** C4 + C5 + C7 — attention triage trio.

## §5 C5 — «Отсечение неважного»

**Voice:** «**отсечение всего неважного**» [claim 1]

**Definition:** Active removal of low-value engagement, не passive non-engagement. Prophylactic + structural; build life so non-essential cannot intrude.

**Examples:**
1. Steve Jobs Apple 1997 — from 350 products to 10; everything else cancelled
2. Buffett's «no list» — 99/100 investment opportunities declined
3. Marie Kondo «does it spark joy» — items discarded systematically
4. McKeown «Essentialism» — 90% rule (if not «hell yes», it's no)
5. Tim Ferriss elimination phase — removing meetings, batch-processing email, fire bad clients
6. Doctor's vacation triage — no work email, no work calls; structurally enforced
7. Athlete's diet-cut — no alcohol, no sugar; no exceptions

**Cross-tradition:** Munger «invert always invert»; Ashby variety attenuation; Meadows leverage point 5 «rules of system»; Unix Parsimony; Lean 7 wastes.

**Jetix:** Pillar C 12 hard rules + Default-Deny table; AP-6 dissent atoms (preserve, не extinct); /project-archive ritual.

**Failure mode:** Cutting too much — over-pruning eliminates antifragility. Mitigation: C9 mechanism literacy + portfolio-thinking.

**Integration:** C5 + C4 — paired select + remove; the «not-to-do list» discipline (per Buffett).

## §6 C6 — «Полная честность с собой» ⭐ FOUNDATIONAL

**Voice:** «**полная честность с собой**» [claim 1]

**Definition:** Absence-of-self-deception across observation + interpretation + decision. Honest about results (not flattering narrative), motivations (mixed motives), capabilities (calibrated confidence), errors (no rationalisation).

**The foundational component.** Polanyi's «personal coefficient» canonical Western articulation; Aristotle's virtue of truthfulness; foundational substrate-without-which other components fail.

**Examples:**
1. Doctor's medical chart — accurate symptom note even when suggests poor compliance
2. Weight-loss tracker — recording the cake
3. Financial accounting — accurate P&L; не adjusted-EBITDA hiding losses
4. Therapist's self-supervision — admitting countertransference
5. Athlete's training log — honest mileage, honest paces, honest pain levels
6. Programmer's bug post-mortem — «I shipped without testing» admitted
7. Relationship reflection — «I was angry because X pinged my insecurity Y»
8. Project status report — «we're behind because we miscalculated effort»

**Cross-tradition (most-mapped):** Polanyi personal coefficient + tacit dimension; Aristotle virtue of truthfulness; Stoic ataraxia (Epictetus/Marcus Aurelius); Polya look-back; Ericsson feedback discipline; Wiener negative feedback; VSM S5 ethos; Schedrovitsky reflexive position; Latour responsibility for emergent.

**Jetix:** R6 inline provenance; AP-6 dissent atoms preserved; F-G-R grading; IP-1 STRICT; Foundation Part 6a Provenance Officer institutional honesty.

**Failure mode:** Honest about wrong things — surface-truth misses mechanism. Cross-coupling: C6 + C9 required together.

**Integration:** C6 + every-other-component — degrades without it. Specifically: C6 + C1 (honest effort); C6 + C7 (honest leverage); C6 + C11 (honest hunger level).

## §7 C7 — «Максимальное использование рычагов»

**Voice:** «**максимальное вообще использование рычагов** этот развитие системы и так далее» [claim 1]

**Definition:** Systematically choosing actions whose effect magnifies через multiplier (code reuse / media reach / capital compounding / people leverage / system effects). Effort with multiplier (vs. C1 = raw effort).

**Examples:**
1. Programmer writing reusable library — once-written, used 1000x
2. Teacher creating textbook chapter — students 10,000+ vs one-on-one
3. Doctor establishing clinical protocol — all clinic staff vs ad-hoc
4. Author writing book vs interview — 100,000+ vs 10,000
5. Investment in S&P 500 — 8% annual compounding vs 0.5% cash
6. Hiring great manager — multiplies output via 10 people
7. Building cohort substrate — exponential cohort growth
8. Writing strategic LOCK Foundation entry — defines institutional substrate for years

**Cross-tradition:** Meadows 12 leverage points (canonical); VSM S4 Intelligence outside-future scanning; Naval Ravikant 4-quadrant leverage (code/media/capital/people); Munger latticework + circle-of-competence; Unix Economy Rule; Karpathy LLM stack multiplier; OMG Essence patterns reuse.

**Jetix:** Foundation Part 4 IP-1 + ROY swarm; Part 10 External Touchpoints; Pillar A North Star; KM MVP /project-* skills.

**Failure mode:** Leverage on the wrong thing — amplifies wrong outcome. Cross-coupling: C7 + C6 + C9 required.

**Integration:** C7 + C8 — compound-leverage; the deepest game.

**Definition:** Treating life / business / Jetix as a system that evolves; explicit investment in system's capabilities + structure + practices; recursion-aware (meta-method itself = a system that develops).

**Examples:**

3. Engineer refactoring before adding feature — reduces technical debt first
4. Business investing in onboarding documentation — every future hire benefits
5. Author writing practice between books — keeps craft alive
6. Parent reading parenting books / therapy — system-of-self-as-parent develops
7. Software shop building shared component library — every future project leverages
8. Cohort facilitator building curriculum atlas — reusable, refinable

**Cross-tradition:** Beer VSM recursive viability; Levenchuk «инженерия личности» (direct lineage); Senge personal mastery; Taleb antifragility; Hofstadter strange-loop self-reference; Karpathy data-flywheel; CMMI Level 5 Optimising.

6. Programmer reading the codebase before refactoring — context + tests
7. Cook understanding cuisine's flavor logic — acid/fat/salt/heat (Nosrat)

**Cross-tradition:** Polya phase 1 understanding-the-problem; Polanyi tacit clues; Newell+Simon production rules; Goldratt TOC bottleneck; Senge mental models; VSM S4 Intelligence; DDD ubiquitous language; Schedrovitsky schemas.

**Jetix:** Foundation Part 3 KB; Part 8 Health Monitoring; /lint + /consolidate skills; Method V2 §6 reverse engineering pass.

**Integration:** C9 + C10 — Goldratt TOC discipline at personal scale.

## §10 C10 — «Выявление узких горлышек»

**Voice:** «**какие там у нее есть части узкие горлышки**» [claim 1]

**Definition:** Finding the system's actual constraint — place where leverage gives maximum return. Per Goldratt TOC, every system has ONE primary constraint at any time; optimising elsewhere is waste.

**Examples:**
1. Restaurant identifying dishwasher as bottleneck — not more cooks
2. Software identifying database query as bottleneck — not optimising the loop above
3. Sales pipeline — lead-generation bottleneck — not closing-skills training
4. Athlete identifying recovery as bottleneck — not training harder
5. Family identifying morning chaos — redesign mornings, не add evenings
6. Writing identifying planning as bottleneck — not more drafts
7. Health identifying sleep as bottleneck — not adding exercise
8. Business identifying key hire as bottleneck — not adding marketing

**Cross-tradition:** Goldratt TOC (canonical); Meadows leverage point 10 stocks; VSM S3 Control; DMAIC Analyse phase.

**Jetix:** Foundation Part 8 Health Monitoring SLI; /project-review skill; /company-status snapshot.

**Failure mode:** Misidentifying bottleneck — fixing wrong constraint multiplies waste. Mitigation: C8 ongoing development + C6 honesty.

**Integration:** C10 + C7 — «apply leverage where it actually multiplies».
