# Strategic Layer — Jetix-Fit Filtering

## §0 What this document is

For each of the 15 Phase-1 types, this document:
1. Applies 8 Jetix-fit criteria → P/F per cell
2. Net verdict: **PROPOSE** / **SKIP** / **DEFER Phase B+**
3. 2-3 sentence rationale

**Outcome:** 7 PROPOSE + 8 SKIP/DEFER. The +1 over the brief's "5-7 SKIP/DEFER"
target is declared honestly in §3 and traceable to the corpus signal that 4
candidate types are already covered by the Foundation Layer (Types 01, 02, 11, 12)
or already exist with adequate canonical form (Type 02). Compressing the SKIP/DEFER
list below 8 would either misrepresent existing-coverage facts or push templates that
the corpus does not yet warrant.

research output, not a final decision; net verdict integrates across cells +
considers downstream coverage.

## §3 Per-type rationale (PROPOSE list)

### PROPOSE-1 — Type 03 Locks Ledger Entry

**Cells.** All P. Locks are inherently founder-only (C1, C8 sole-strategist), AI drafts
candidates (C2), they accumulate in Phase 1 (C3 — D25/D27/D28/D29 all minted in last 30
days), critical in Phase B+ as Foundation evolves (C4), explicitly contains D29
Korp-Startup positioning (C5), they ARE part of §6 anti-scope discipline (C6),
locks span the hybrid (C7 — D11 investment-fund, D20 USB-C, D21 Matchmaker apply
across consulting/agency/platform), and `feedback_ai_does_not_strategize` is *enforced*
by Lock semantics (C8).

**Rationale.** Locks already exist as ad-hoc files (`LOCKS-D25-D28-ADDENDUM-*.md`,
`LOCKS-D29-ADDENDUM-*.md`) and inline in `JETIX-VISION.md` / `JETIX-PHILOSOPHY.md`.
The ad-hoc form has worked but lacks structural consistency: D-numbering scheme,
reversal-trigger predicate, downstream-dependency manifest, F-G-R tagging, addendum
versioning. A clean template canonicalizes the format and unblocks `/lint
--check-stage-gates` style automated coherence audits per FPF B.3 trust calculus.
