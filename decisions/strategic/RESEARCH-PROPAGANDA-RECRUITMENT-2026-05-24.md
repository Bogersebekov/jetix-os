## §2 The structural finding (1 paragraph)

Across 21 books spanning 1895 (Le Bon) to 2022 (Srinivasan), propaganda
and recruitment techniques cluster into two structural families: those
that **bypass voluntary opt-in / extract beyond agreed share / prevent
fork-and-leave** (R12-incompatible) and those that **serve latent demand
through transparent intermediation with reversible commitments**
(R12-compatible). Most modern marketing + cult + political-mobilization
techniques sit in the first family. The R12-compatible family is
smaller, slower, harder to execute well — but is the **only family
structurally compatible** with Jetix-Clan substrate without producing
cult-shape from below.

## §4 The 9-clause R12 test (operational core)

Every Jetix recruitment / Welcome-frame / cohort design move must pass:

1. **Pull, not push** — opt-in only, no broadcast pressure
2. **Disclose, not conceal** — origination + funding + intent transparent
3. **Substrate-real, never manufactured** — claims verifiable
4. **Reversible, never sunk-cost-bound** — exit preserves member identity
5. **Member-portable, never Clan-totalizing** — artifacts + reputation +
   network leave with the member
6. **Glossaried, never loaded-language** — terms have explanations,
   newcomers ask freely
7. **Falsifiable, never sacred-science** — substrate can be wrong; F-G-R
   graded
8. **Impartial, never partial-loyalty** — R12 applied to founder + members
   equally
9. **Fork-and-leave preserved** — exit possible without penalty; alumni
   respected

**Failure on any clause = revise or skip.**
