## ⭐⭐ Tier-2 IMPORTANT (additional 10-15)

### Curriculum design

- **Curriculum Development: Theory and Practice** — Hilda Taba (1962) — Taba model
- **Basic Principles of Curriculum and Instruction** — Ralph Tyler (1949) — Tyler rationale
- **The Process of Education** — Jerome Bruner (1960) — spiral curriculum

### Cognitive + memory

- **Make It Stick** (overlap Tier-1)
- **A Mind for Numbers** — Barbara Oakley (2014) — learning how to learn
- **Mindware** — Richard Nisbett (2015) — reasoning tools transferable

### Online + modern course design

- **Teaching Online: A Practical Guide** — Susan Ko + Steve Rossen
- **Hidden Curriculum of Online Learning** — Maria Andersen
- **MOOCs** research literature (Sebastian Thrun / Udacity / Coursera papers)

### Critical perspectives

- **The Case Against Education** — Bryan Caplan (2018) — critical view economics-of-education
- **Deschooling Society** — Ivan Illich (1971) — critique of institutional education

### Adult / self-directed learning

- **Self-Directed Learning** — Malcolm Knowles (1975) — andragogy
- **The Adult Learner** — Knowles + Holton + Swanson (8th ed 2014)
- **How Learning Works** — Ambrose et al (2010) — 7 research-based principles

### Russian methodology tradition (если accessible)

- **Левенчук — Образование для образованных** (likely already in `raw/external/levenchuk-corpus-2026-05-17/`)
- **Виктор Загвязинский — Теория обучения** (Russian classical)
- **Леонид Фридман — Психопедагогика**
- **Юрий Куликов — Дидактика**
