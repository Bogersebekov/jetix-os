## §3 Pattern — Mike Tyson как case study

### §3.1 Что характеризует Tyson's path (canonical narrative)

- **Young heavyweight world champion** — youngest heavyweight champion ever (20 лет, 1986)
- **Cus D'Amato as mentor** — взял Tyson под крыло в 13 лет; total dedication mentor-mentee relationship
- **Compound training** — 100% focus + best-in-class technique training + psychology
- **Speed-of-development** — годы интенсивного training с best mentor → world title at 20

### §3.2 Pattern abstraction

```
Young ambition + extreme dedication + access к world-class mentor + compound learning over years
        ↓
World-class outcome at relatively young age
```

**Key components:**
1. Clear definition of «heavyweight category» (что = «top of the field»)
2. Best-in-class mentor (not just any teacher — top expert)
3. Total dedication / no distraction (life arranged around training)
4. Compound years of work (no shortcut, but no waste either)
5. Outcome ambition explicit (world champion, не «good fighter»)

## §6 Risks / counter-arguments

**RT.1 — Boxing ≠ business literal mapping.** Boxing = solo physical performance with clear win/loss; business = team, multi-domain, no single outcome. Pattern может collapse при pushed too literally.

**RT.2 — «Young heavyweight» framing может create unhealthy obsession.** Tyson's eventual mental health collapse (post-D'Amato death) shows risks of total-dedication-narrow-focus approach. Need balance.

**RT.3 — Найти Cus D'Amato hard.** Best mentors in business не «available для adoption». Need approach Ruslan can offer them (что он даёт mentor) — implies Foundation Model + Partnership Model insights apply (Ruslan ↔ Mentor = symbiotic, не one-sided).

**RT.4 — Compound years требуют patience.** Tyson trained 7+ years с Cus before world title. Founder Phase 1 ($100K к концу лета) — much shorter horizon. Pattern timeline misalignment.
