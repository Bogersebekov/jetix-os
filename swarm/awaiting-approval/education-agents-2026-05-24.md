Even technical ID can be extraction-misused. Education agents must respect:
- Hardship-case access mandate (mass paradigm O-184)
- Anti-fork-prevention (any learner / cohort can take + leave)
- Wage-ratio cap (facilitator/owner / participant compensation governed by Charter)
- Non-consensual-distribution prohibition (learner-produced content stays learner's)

→ Cited as boundary 5 in §3.2.1.
