# Фасилитатор — поиск

## §1 Что такое фасилитатор (быстрая дисциплина)

**Фасилитатор ≠ консультант. Фасилитатор ≠ коуч. Фасилитатор ≠ trainer.**

**Фасилитатор:**
- **Process expert**, не content expert
- Helps groups (or individuals) **make decisions faster + better** через structured process
- Designs & facilitates: workshops, design sprints, strategic offsites, retrospectives, decision-making sessions, Liberating Structures, World Cafe, etc.
- Neutral / agenda-free относительно WHAT decided — focuses on HOW process happens
- **Output:** clarity, commitment, decisions emerging from group itself; not facilitator's recommendations

### 3 типа практики (наиболее релевантные Jetix)

1. **Design Sprint facilitator** (Google Ventures Sprint methodology или похожие short-format intensives) — сейчас популярно
2. **Strategic offsite facilitator** (multi-day strategy retreats / Liberating Structures / SAFe / OKR sessions) — для team-of-N
3. **Solo founder facilitator** (rare niche) — 1:1 process facilitation для founder strategic clarity. Hybrid с coaching но process-focused.

### Не фасилитатор (чтобы не путать)
- ❌ Therapist (mental health)
- ❌ Coach (long-term personal growth)
- ❌ Trainer (knowledge transfer)
- ❌ Consultant (recommendations / strategy advice)
- ❌ Project manager (execution)

## §2 Что нам конкретно нужно (Jetix-specific)

### Why
**Phase 1 (now):** Ruslan = sole strategist + sole operator. Decisions accumulate. Без structured process = decision quality erodes под cognitive load.

**Phase 2+ (€200K validation):** First 1-2 hires + Mittelstand AI Alliance founding + first paying clients. Multiple stakeholders → group decisions → process matters.

**Phase 3+ ($1M+):** Team 5-50 + roy-replication + holding governance. Process discipline becomes infrastructure-level (D2 §14 Foundation as main asset).

Фасилитатор = **process scaffolding** который позволяет Jetix не invent process под each new situation, а apply known good patterns.

### What sessions look like (3 use cases)

#### Use Case 1: Solo founder strategic clarity (immediate Phase 1)
- **Trigger:** stuck moments / pre-major-decision / weekly strategic priorities setting
- **Format:** 60-90 мин 1:1 facilitated session
- **Output:** Ruslan's decision crystallized + alternative paths visible + commitment formalized
- **Cadence:** weekly или bi-weekly
- **Frameworks:** sprint-style problem definition, decision matrices, OST (Open Space Technology) lite

#### Use Case 2: Future team onboarding facilitation (Phase 2+, €200K)
- **Trigger:** first 2-3 hires onboarding
- **Format:** 1-2 day intensive — values alignment + role clarity + decision-rights + first 90 days
- **Output:** team operating agreement + clear handoff protocols + decision-rights matrix
- **Frameworks:** Liberating Structures, Holacracy lite, Aaron Dignan's Brave New Work protocols

#### Use Case 3: Mittelstand AI Alliance founding workshop (Phase 2+ when triggered)
- **Trigger:** post-€200K validation + Foundation Option C Hybrid formalization (per L6 acked)
- **Format:** 2-3 day multi-stakeholder offsite (Jetix + 3-5 founding alliance members + DACH lawyer)
- **Output:** Foundation governance structure + membership criteria + first-year operating plan
- **Frameworks:** SAFe-style PI planning, World Cafe для multi-stakeholder, Roman voting для decisions

### Specific Jetix questions facilitator поможет навигировать (immediate Phase 1)

- **Phase-1 hypothesis selection** (Producer Services 5 hypotheses + AI Consulting DACH 5 hypotheses) — facilitated decision session
- **Weekly priority setting** under €50K Q2 2026 pressure

- **Mittelstand AI Alliance founding** workshop facilitation Phase 2+

## §3 Профиль кандидата

### Core experience
- **3+ years professional facilitation** (not «I've run a few workshops»)
- **Trained in 1+ recognized methodology:** Design Sprint (GV / AJ&Smart), Liberating Structures, Holacracy, SAFe, IDEO Design Thinking, etc.
- **Mix of corporate + startup experience** preferred — pure corporate-only too rigid; pure startup-only too undisciplined
- **AI-curious** — comfortable facilitating sessions about AI-augmented operations (но не AI specialist requirement)

### Operating style
- **Quiet authority** — controls room without dominating; D22 «adequate» (anti-bullshit)
- **Process-focused** — doesn't sneak own opinions into facilitated decisions
- **Comfortable с founder mode** — Ruslan's голод + intensity + предприниматель азарт без пугаться
- **Speed-comfortable** — Phase-1 needs fast decisions, не 6-week academic facilitation

### Anti-profile

- ❌ Pure HR / OD (organizational development) consultant — too slow + corporate-flavored
- ❌ Therapist transitioning to facilitation — incompatible roles
- ❌ Liberating Structures purist evangelist (cargo-cult one method) — adaptability matters
- ❌ Workshop trainers (teach how to facilitate) — нам нужен doer, не teacher
- ❌ Conference moderators / hosts (talking head, не process design)

### Demographics

- **Geography:** preferred DACH / EU + remote (most facilitation now hybrid). US OK для evening sessions.
- **Language:** EN required; DE plus для DACH client work later; RU plus но not required
- **Age range:** 30-55 typical (enough practice без being stuck в old methodology)

## §5 Pricing / compensation hypothesis

### Phase-1 (immediate)
- **Per-session:** €300-€800 для 60-90 мин session (mid-market facilitator rate; top names $1500+ but Phase-1 budget)
- **Per-day workshop:** €1500-€3000 (1-day intensive; quote per-day all-in)
- **Monthly retainer:** 4-8 hrs/mo = €600-€2000/mo (если weekly cadence)
- **Trial:** first session «paid trial» на full rate

### Phase 2+ (€200K validation)
- **Workshop pricing:** €3000-€8000 per multi-day intensive (Mittelstand AI Alliance founding workshop = paid project)
- **Possibly retainer model:** €1500-€4000/mo для ongoing process partner
- **Equity?** — typically not (facilitators rarely take equity — different relationship)

### Anti-pattern
- ❌ NO «free workshop» (quality signal compromised)
- ❌ NO «can you facilitate just one session unpaid?» — disrespect facilitator's craft
- ❌ NO 6-month engagement contracts upfront (advisory = month-to-month)
