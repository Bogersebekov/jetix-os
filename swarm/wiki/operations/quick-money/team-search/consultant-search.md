# Стратегический консультант — поиск

## §1 Что такое стратегический консультант (быстрая дисциплина)

**Не путать с:**
- Management consultant (McKinsey/BCG/Bain — deep analysis + reports + recommendations)
- Coach (work на personal level, не business strategy)
- Advisor (общий совет, без structured frameworks)
- Mentor (long-term wisdom-sharing, без active engagement)

**Стратегический консультант (наш search target):**
- **Practitioner**, не academic / не pure analyst
- Имеет **own scaling experience** (от 0 до $10M+ revenue или похожий journey)
- Working session-based (не проект на 6 месяцев + report) — short structured sessions с founder
- **Sounding board** for major strategic decisions
- **Frameworks-driven** но не Frameworks-trapped (умеет адаптировать, не cargo-cult)
- Может включать: fractional COO/CTO/CSO patterns, board advisor patterns, founder coach patterns

## §2 Что нам конкретно нужно (Jetix-specific)

### Why
Per **D2 §13 + Левенчук hard rule**: AI agents не стратегируют (no identity / skin-in-the-game / long-term memory). Founder = sole strategist. **НО:** sole strategist work без external sounding board = blind spots + group-of-one decision risk.

Стратегический консультант = **trusted external voice** который:
1. **Challenges Ruslan's strategic thinking** под different angles (philosophy / business / market / engineering / human)
2. **Surfaces blind spots** в Phase 1 → 2 → 3 trajectory decisions
3. **Brings practitioner pattern recognition** из своего scaling journey
4. **NOT decides** for Ruslan — Ruslan = sole strategist still — но качественно informs decisions

### What sessions look like (hypothesis)

- **Cadence:** weekly или bi-weekly, 60-90 минут
- **Format:** structured Socratic Q+A — Ruslan presents current strategic question + alternatives, consultant probes assumptions + surfaces missing angles
- **Output:** не report; не recommendations; **clarified question + identified blind spots + structured options** в Ruslan's hands. Decision = Ruslan's.
- **Tools:** Maybe whiteboard / Miro / structured frameworks (но не cargo-cult)

### Specific Jetix questions consultant поможет навигировать (immediate)

- Phase-1 hypothesis selection (Producer Services 5 hypotheses + AI Consulting DACH 5 hypotheses из cycles 8+9)
- €50K Q2 2026 path validation (cash flow §7 показывает TIGHT — €80 above kill trigger)
- Path A/B/C empirical strategy (L7 §2.3 default Path B — нужна проверка external)
- ICP filter calibration после first 5-10 outreach responses
- Pricing tier validation (L7 §2 — Phase-1 client willingness-to-pay)
- Phase-1→2 transition timing decisions
- M&A direction Phase-1 pull-forward trigger (deferred Phase-2+ but co-founder might appear)

## §3 Профиль кандидата (специфика)

### Core experience
- **Personal scaling journey** — построил/масштабировал own business до $5-50M revenue (или работал C-level в такой компании на early stage)
- **B2B services or B2B SaaS** background (не pure consumer)
- **AI-native or AI-curious** — понимает что Jetix делает (методология AI-leverage), не нужно объяснять с нуля
- **DACH context** plus (Mittelstand market understanding) — не mandatory но big plus

### Operating style
- **Practitioner**, не tower-speak academic
- Comfortable с long-horizon thinking ($1T trajectory not laughed at, not cargo-cult-believed либо)
- **Honest** — calls bullshit на own and Ruslan's blind spots; D22 5 criteria fit (startupper / azart / stable / adequate / upward)
- **Available** — actively taking advisory engagements (не just retired millionaire bored)

### Anti-profile (что НЕ ищем)

- ❌ Pure McKinsey consultant без operational experience
- ❌ AI-skeptic / luddite («AI hype») — incompatible с D2 + D7 + D20 ideology
- ❌ Sole-founder-cult («solo all the way») — incompatible с D26 team 50-100
- ❌ MLM / coaching / motivational speakers — incompatible с D22 anti-attention-economy
- ❌ Pure tech background без business scaling — нужен business strategist, не CTO
- ❌ «Big vision» speakers без execution chops — engineering-faith framing demands evidence

### Demographics / age / geography

- **Geography:** preferred DACH / EU + remote-friendly. US OK для late-night calls (Berlin time).
- **Language:** EN required; DE plus; RU possible но not required (most candidates EN-first).
- **Age range:** 35-55 typical (enough scaling experience + still active)
- **Stage:** could be still-running founder OR exited recently OR fractional advisor portfolio

### Anti-channels (НЕ ищем здесь)
- ❌ Upwork / Fiverr (wrong tier — these are project workers, не advisors)
- ❌ Toptal (consulting projects, не advisor relationships)
- ❌ McKinsey / Big4 alumni networks (wrong type — analysis-driven not practitioner-driven)
- ❌ Academic departments (wrong — researcher not practitioner)
- ❌ MLM / coaching networks

## §5 Pricing / compensation hypothesis

### Phase-1 (immediate, Ruslan self-funded)
- **Hourly:** €150-€300/hr (mid-market advisor rate; top names $400-$600/hr but Phase-1 budget constraint)
- **Per-session:** 60-90 мин session = €200-€500
- **Monthly retainer:** 2-4 sessions/mo = €500-€1500/mo
- **Trial:** first 1-2 sessions «paid trial» (full rate, no discount expected — quality signal)

### Phase-2+ (after €200K validation)
- **Possibly advisory equity:** 0.25-1.0% over 2-year vest cliff + reduced cash retainer
- **OR full-rate cash:** €1500-€5000/mo retainer
- **Decision Phase-2** based on: traction validation + advisor's value contribution post-Phase-1

### Anti-pattern
- ❌ NO «free advice» (skin-in-game per D22 — paid relationships only)
- ❌ NO equity-only Phase-1 (premature; advisor должен заработать ground first)
- ❌ NO 6-month engagement contracts (advisory = month-to-month; quality should justify continuation)

## §6 Outreach approach (per L6 §7 adapted)

### Discovery call agenda (15-20 мин)
1. Ruslan brief (3 мин): кто я, что Jetix, где сейчас (use current-situation-brief.md as TLDR)
2. Candidate brief (5 мин): их experience, recent advisory engagements, areas focus
3. Mutual fit Q+A (5-7 мин): can they bring Jetix-specific value? Does Jetix interest them as engagement?
4. Next step (2-3 мин): trial session scheduling если mutual fit

### Outreach message template (LinkedIn DM example, adapt EN/DE)

```
Hi [Name],

Pre-researched note (not generic): [specific reference to their recent work / podcast appearance / written piece — 1 sentence demonstrating real interest].

I'm building Jetix — methodology + operating system for AI-leverage в business and personal life. Phase 1 (consulting + producer center for English infobiz) targeting €50K Q2 2026; Phase 3 horizon $1T market cap. Foundation locked (28 D-locks, 14-layer system overview, 9 swarm cycles compounded). Stuck в strategist-of-one trap — AI agents support but don't strategize (Левенчук's hard rule).

Looking for fractional strategic advisor — 1-2 hr/mo session-based, sounding board for Phase 1→3 trajectory decisions. €150-300/hr Phase-1, advisory equity Phase-2+ optional.

Would you be open to 20-min discovery call next week to see if there's mutual fit?

Best,
Ruslan
```

### Anti-pattern
- ❌ Generic «Hi, want to advise me?»
- ❌ Long pitches (decision should fit on one screen)
- ❌ Asking for free advice in first message
- ❌ Pretending to know advisor's work без actual research (caught in 30 seconds)
