## §3 Alternatives (≥2 + status quo)

### Alternative A — 3 discrete options (Popper-framed, no implicit ascent)

Present the cooperation plan as 3 **discrete, independent options** Ruslan can choose any of at any time — not a sequential ladder. Each option has its own falsifier, its own R12 check, its own epistemic grounding. Framing: "Option 1 / Option 2 / Option 3 — Ruslan picks, Ruslan executes, no implied progression."

Kill-condition: this alternative fails if Ruslan's actual strategic intent IS a progressive engagement (start small, deepen over time) — in which case a ladder is the correct representation, but it must be explicitly marked as Ruslan's strategic choice (not AI-authored sequence).

Present only Tier 1 (observe, study FPF/IWE deeply via C4 benchmark execution) as the correct current action. Tier 2 and Tier 3 are deferred until C4 benchmark produces falsifiable evidence. Framing: "One action now — do the work. No cooperation asks until the epistemic basis exists."

Do not produce a cooperation plan. Continue Phase B FPF study internally; respond to Levenchuk's TG only after C4 benchmark produces results. No outreach framing, no cooperation ladder.

Kill-condition: status quo fails if Tseren warm-signal cools (no response to video proposal since 04.05.2026; pipeline state: warm but unacknowledged). Status quo has a time cost.

## §5 R1 Fit Verdict

**Test: is the 3-tier proposal "options surfaced for Ruslan to choose" (R1-compliant) or "recommended path" (R1-violation)?**

`F: F4 | G: r1-fit-audit | R: refuted_if_plan-actually-written-as-options-list`

The framing as received ("3-tier ladder") is **R1-AMBIGUOUS**. The word "ladder" is load-bearing:

- A ladder = sequential structure with an implied bottom-to-top direction. The metaphor carries a recommendation: start at Tier 1, progress to Tier 2, advance to Tier 3.
- If the plan is written "you should move up the ladder as cooperation deepens", that is a strategic recommendation authored by AI = R1 violation.
- If the plan is written "here are 3 discrete engagement modes; which one and when is Ruslan's decision", that is option-surfacing = R1-compliant.

**Required fix:** Rename "3-tier ladder" to "3 cooperation options" in the plan's framing, OR add an explicit marker: "The progression from Option 1 to Option 3 is a strategic choice Ruslan makes; the plan does not recommend progression."

**Epistemic flag (AP-PHIL-3 candidate):** The framing conflates the epistemic claim ("these are three coherent modes of engagement") with the instrumental claim ("here is the sequence to follow"). Per FPF L1003-1006, these must be separated. Epistemic claim = mine to surface; instrumental claim = Ruslan's to make.
