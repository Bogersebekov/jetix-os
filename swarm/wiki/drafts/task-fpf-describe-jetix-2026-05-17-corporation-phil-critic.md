## §5 Founder Asymmetry vs «Mutual» Framework Tension

This is the focus area that corresponds to the brief's item 5 — the same tension flagged in doc 03 review.

### §5.1 The tension

§3 describes Partners (top tier) as «со-строители» with «financial participation». §4.2 (U.RoleAssignment) states:
```
Tier 1 — Partner: ... financial participant + co-builder
```
The framing is reciprocal. However:

- Ruslan = sole strategist (R1 constitutional; CLAUDE.md §4.1 rule 1)
- Part 9 = Owner Interaction Scaffold with Ruslan as sole HITL gate
- Doc 1B §7 «5 critical filters» for client/partner selection are Ruslan-authored and Ruslan-applied

The partnership structure as described gives Partners «5 participation formats (equity / revenue share / consulting / advisory / resource exchange)» but does not declare where Partner authority ends and Ruslan's sole-strategist authority begins. A Partner with equity participation who cannot influence strategic direction is not a «co-builder» in any governance sense — they are an investor in Ruslan's solo-directed enterprise.

### §5.2 Epistemic issue — not a strategic verdict

This critic does NOT decide whether the asymmetry is correct or incorrect — that is instrumental Рациональность (investor-expert / Ruslan). The epistemic issue is:

The claim «Partners = со-строители» carries an implied reciprocity that is not falsifiable as written. Under what conditions is a Partner NOT a «со-строитель»? If Ruslan retains all strategic authority per R1, then partners are capital contributors + service recipients with curated access — which is a legitimate structure, but «со-строитель» overstates the mutual construction claim.

**FLAG ASYM-1 (AP-PHIL-1 — no falsifier for «mutual» claim).**

The partnership framing lacks a falsifier: under what observable conditions would a Partner's contribution NOT count as «со-строительство»? And inversely: what Partner rights exist to constrain Ruslan's strategic calls that affect partner stakes?

**FLAG ASYM-2 (AP-stoic-dichotomy-skipped).**

For the meta-decision «Partners are co-builders», the dichotomy of control is not declared:
- In Ruslan's control: strategic direction, ICP selection, methodology design, hiring decisions
- Not in Ruslan's control: partner willingness to join, market acceptance, regulatory environment
- In Partner's control: capital deployment decision, own network, referrals
- Not in Partner's control: Jetix's strategic trajectory (per R1)

This decomposition is absent from §3 and §4.2. Without it, the «mutual» framing is a meta-claim without a dichotomy-of-control bound — AP-stoic-dichotomy-skipped confirmed.

**Required surface (not resolution — R1):** Add to §7 a new OQ:

> **OQ-CORP-7 (founder asymmetry surface).** Partners are described as «со-строители» throughout §3 and §4.2. R1 constitutional (CLAUDE.md §4.1 rule 1) declares Ruslan = sole strategist. At what governance boundary does Partner co-building authority begin and Ruslan's sole-strategist authority end? What Partner rights (veto, voice, exit mechanism) exist under each of the 5 participation formats? This is not a defect in the design — it may be intentional and correct — but the current document does not declare it, and the gap creates an epistemic false impression of symmetry for L1 audience. Ruslan surface and resolve (or explicitly disclose the asymmetry) before L1 delivery.
