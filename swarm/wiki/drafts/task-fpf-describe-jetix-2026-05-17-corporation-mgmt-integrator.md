## §2 Operational Delivery Viability — 3 Engagement Tiers at Phase A

**Question:** Can the three tiers (Partners / Clients / Employees) be cleanly served
at Phase A with 1 person + ROY swarm?

**Mgmt verdict: Tier 2 (Clients) is the only operationally viable tier at Phase A.**
Tier 1 (Partners) and Tier 3 (Employees) are Phase 2+ constructs presented in Phase A
materials without operational backing. Detail:

### Tier 1 — Partners (Phase A operational viability: LOW)

Partners are described as «people with existing capital (financial, reputational,
knowledge), ready to enter financial participation». Five participation formats: equity /
revenue share / consulting / advisory / resource exchange.
[src: decisions/JETIX-CORPORATION-2026-05-05.md §2.4]

**Mgmt analysis:**

- Phase A = 0 registered legal entity. Equity and revenue-share formats require a legal
  entity (GmbH/UG/Ltd). Without it, two of the five Partner participation formats are
  legally unexecutable.

  between Ruslan (physical person, Berlin) and the Partner, not a legal entity.
- Resource exchange CAN be offered Phase A — value-in-kind, not cash.
- **Operational gap:** The doc describes all 5 formats as live offerings. For L1
  audience (Tseren is the named «first concrete Phase 2 partnership target»), presenting
  equity and revenue-share without clarifying the «legal entity not yet registered»
  precondition is EP-2 violation pattern (aspirational described without disclosure).

  relationship requires Part 10 External Touchpoints (Foundation) + Part 9 HITL cadence
  for key decisions. Phase A swarm is not calibrated for multi-human coordination;
  Part 4 Role Taxonomy and Part 8 Health Monitoring extend Phase A → Phase 2 per
  decisions/JETIX-CORPORATION-2026-05-05.md §2.2.

## §7 Founder Asymmetry — Mgmt Perspective

**Question:** Ruslan = sole strategist + decision-maker IN the Corporation context —
same tension as Charter §6 surfaced in Doc 03. Is this operationally sustainable as
scale increases?

### Phase A assessment

At Phase A (1 person + ROY swarm), founder asymmetry is not a defect — it is the
correct configuration. Solo-founder + AI swarm where the founder = sole HITL is FPF-
correct: Part 9 (Owner Interaction Scaffold) explicitly designs for this; Pillar C Tier
2 rule 1 «AI does NOT make strategic decisions» requires Ruslan as sole strategist.
There is no operational contradiction at Phase A.

### Phase 2 strain point (first emergence)

When Tseren joins (named Phase 2 target), the asymmetry creates the first operational
strain:

- Tseren has his own methodology (ШСМ engineering management); Jetix foundation reflects
  Ruslan's RUSLAN-LAYER exclusively.
- Tseren as Partner = advisory or resource-exchange format (Phase A viable per §2).
  But if Tseren is described as «co-building» (§1.2 in source doc), he has implicit
  expectation of influence on strategic direction.
- Ruslan-as-sole-strategist means Tseren's contributions feed the system but do not
  change its strategic direction without Ruslan's explicit HITL ack.
- This is structurally fine IF disclosed upfront. It is a trust breach if Tseren
  discovers the asymmetry post-engagement.

**Mgmt recommendation (R1 surface):** L1 materials and the Partner offer language
should explicitly name the governance model: «Ruslan = sole strategic decision-maker
in Phase 1-2; Foundation architecture and RUSLAN-LAYER are Ruslan's own; Partners
contribute within that frame.» This is honest and filters Partners who need co-founder
authority (which Jetix is not offering at Phase A-Phase 2).

### Phase 3 strain point (scalability)

At Phase 3 (community of workshops), the solo-strategist model breaks. Phase 3 trigger
requires «10+ curated makers with track record» and «tasks > single workshop». A
community with 10+ independent masters cannot be governed by a single decision-maker
without governance infrastructure (voting mechanisms, dispute resolution, role delineation).
Phase 3 requires:

- Governance evolution (Doc 04 has zero governance design for Phase 3; §2.3 describes
  Phase 3 mechanics but not governance).
- R12 anti-extraction enforcement mechanism (currently F2 — text only, no enforcement).
- Cross-workshop contract protocol (2.4 «Cross-Workshop Communication Protocol» listed
  as Phase 3+ станок but not designed).

  artefact not yet created.
