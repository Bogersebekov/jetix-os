# L5 — Product & Services: What Jetix Sells

## §1 Mission of this Layer

L5 declares the **concrete product and service portfolio** of Jetix: what is sold, to whom, in which phase, at what level of productization. It is the product layer — distinct from L6 (business trajectory gates, revenue targets, unit-economics) and distinct from L8 (Alliance-specific methodology delivery to partners). L5 answers one question: what does Jetix offer the market right now and what will it offer as gates are crossed?

The flagship internal label across the entire portfolio is **Smart AI** — Ruslan's phrase for the category Jetix operates in [src:LOCKS-D25-D28-ADDENDUM-2026-04-24.md §Smart AI]. This is an internal marker and descriptive category, not an external product name and not a locked decision. Jetix = Jetix externally; Smart AI = how the work is described in internal and early sales discussions. The formal product name question (whether to make Smart AI a public-facing brand or keep it as internal positioning) is an open question pending D29 or explicit Ruslan ack.

The portfolio spans a single arc: from solo consulting at €0 today to a standards-layer infrastructure at USB-C-level market position at Phase 3+. Ruslan's voice from audio_508 captures this arc verbatim: *«Jetix займёт позицию стандарта в мире AI-агентов как USB-C в электронике — будет присутствовать везде»* [src:LOCKS-D25-D28-ADDENDUM-2026-04-24.md §USB-C]. Every product line is a step along that arc.

2. **Which Path A/B/C for Phase-1 USB-C services?** STRATEGIC-INSIGHT §5 presents three delivery tiers for client-private AI: Path A (Jetix-hosted), Path B (client-hosted, Jetix ships configs), Path C (hybrid — client data + Jetix agents via tunnel). 3. **Matchmaker: manual → platform at G2 (€200K) or G3 (€1M)?** JETIX-VISION §5 says «post €200K validation». JETIX-PLAN says «Phase 2 (€200K → €1M) — matchmaker features live». But audio_514 and the chat-voice suggest Ruslan is already operating matchmaker manually today. The trigger question: does matchmaking become a formalized product with its own landing page, contract template, and fee structure at G2, or does it stay informal until G3 when the platform is built? The answer affects whether it belongs in the G1→G2 product activation or G2→G3.
