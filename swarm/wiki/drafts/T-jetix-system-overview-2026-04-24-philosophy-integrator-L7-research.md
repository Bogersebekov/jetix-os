# §L7 Research & Reverse-Engineering

> **Layer role.** L7 is the research infrastructure layer of Jetix. It is not the product layer (L5), not the revenue layer (L6), and not the knowledge-storage layer (L1). It is the layer where Jetix observes the world, disassembles competitors, imports methodology from external thinkers, and builds the epistemic foundations that everything else runs on. This layer is mostly latent in Phase 1 and activates progressively through Phases 2-3+.

---

## Mission

L7 exists because Jetix is grounded in the claim that **epistemic quality is a structural moat**. The research layer operationalises this claim through three activities that must be distinguished:

**1. Revenue-instrumental research (Phase 1, D14).** The only research Jetix funds in Phase 1 is research that directly serves near-term revenue: ICP validation, competitor analysis, Mittelstand knowledge, market-entry hypotheses. D14 (research = revenue-instrumental) is a hard Phase-1 lock. This is an **instrumental claim**: research is justified here by what it enables, not by its epistemic value alone. *This claim would be falsified if Phase-1 research spend failed to improve sales conversion or client-acquisition rates over three consecutive months.*

**2. Open-source research direction (D24, Phase 3+).** How people communicate. How companies cooperate. How future economies should work. This is **epistemic-primary** research — its value is not immediately instrumental, it serves the civilizational mission. D24 explicitly activates Phase 3+ only. *This claim would be falsified if it were activated before the €1M gate, which would contradict D14's Phase-1 discipline.*

**3. Reverse-engineering на максималках (audio_527, Phase 2+).** Industry-standard analysis, competitor tear-down, public-source deep reading. Ruslan verbatim from audio_527 (2026-04-23): «написать документ-компас на 5-15 страниц, описывающий подход и отличительные черты Jetix» — this is the mode of research where Jetix reads what others have built, disassembles the architecture, and extracts structural patterns. The BIOS/Clone-Wars research document (`raw/research/2026-04-24-bios-clone-wars-jetix-ai-parallel.md`, 621 lines) is the first executed instance of this capability. *This activity is revenue-instrumental in Phase 1 (competitor analysis) and scales to systematic product in Phase 2+.*

**Philosophy-critic flag — preserved tension:** D14 (research = revenue-instrumental Phase 1) and D24 (research = open-source Phase 3+) are not contradictory but they generate a **phase-sequencing epistemic tension** that must not be papered over. The epistemic claim in D24 ("open-source research is valuable") and the instrumental claim in D14 ("research only if it serves revenue in Phase 1") operate at different levels. D14 governs what Jetix *does*; D24 governs what Jetix *builds toward*. These are composable when phase-sequenced. They become non-composable if someone attempts to activate D24 activities inside Phase 1 operations. This tension is preserved as dissent below (§Dissents).

## What lives in L7

### Open-source research direction (D24)

Jetix's long-horizon bet includes an open research programme on the structure of cooperation itself: *«как люди коммуницируют, как компании кооперируют, как должна работать будущая экономика»* [JETIX-VISION §5, Note 5]. The research is designed to be open — Jetix publishes — while the methodology that *applies* the findings remains closed (D13 closed core / open surface). Jetix uses the research first, then releases it. This is the epistemic grammar of D24: open-surface research, closed-core methodology application. Activates Phase 3+ only.

### Reverse-engineering на максималках (audio_527)

From audio_527 (2026-04-23), Ruslan articulated the need for a *«документ-компас»* — a structured intelligence read-out of the landscape. The BIOS/Clone-Wars research document is the founding instance of this stream. Its structural output: seven lessons on how open architectures generate market explosions, how clean-room reverse engineering works as a legal innovation equivalent to product innovation, and how value migrates from commoditised infrastructure to defensible application-layer moats. The parallel to Jetix's AI moment is made explicit in that document's §12-13.

Reverse-engineering at full throttle means: reading everything publicly available about a competitor, market, or standard; extracting the structural logic; identifying what is defensible vs what is commodity. This is not academic literature review. It is predator-mode epistemic work — the research equivalent of D1's "predator outside" face.

### New AI-protocols / laws / validation-tools (chat-voice USB-C, Voice 2)

Ruslan verbatim from Voice 2 (2026-04-24, raw/voice-transcripts/2026-04-24-ruslan-chat-voice-usb-c-positioning.md):

> *«надо будет блять новые сети новые протоколы новые снова таки новые протоколы новое вообще понимание что как работает новые законы в целом новые блять инструменты для проверки этого всего потому что если сейчас все проверяет человек в какой-то государственной хуйни а этот атом ли создаются контракты искусственными интеллектами у него просто ебал отвалится»*

This identifies L7's most ambitious research stream: **standards-contribution**. The claim is that AI-to-AI contracts, cooperation protocols, and the validation infrastructure for those contracts represent a genuine regulatory and epistemic gap — one that Jetix, positioned as a USB-C-level player (D20), is uniquely placed to address. This is neither pure epistemic research nor pure instrumental consulting. It is **infrastructure-of-knowing**: creating the conceptual and legal apparatus through which AI-augmented economic actors can verify each other's claims and contracts. L7 is the layer where this conceptual work incubates before it can migrate to L5 (products) or L8 (Alliance contributions).

### Левенчук ШСМ direction

Jetix's operational philosophy is grounded in Левенчук's Systems Thinking / Systems Engineering / ШСМ methodology (JETIX-PHILOSOPHY §13). Ruslan's stance is explicit: «Not Levenchuk-derivative, but Levenchuk-grounded.» The FPF is adopted selectively: holonic ontology, agents-don't-strategize, writing-as-thinking, falsifiability, and trigger-driven strategizing are fully accepted. Other elements are modified or rejected with citations. The reading and systematic integration of Левенчук content is an ongoing personal research stream. Reference from audio_510 (2026-04-21): «Обратиться к Левенчуку или похожим экспертам по сложным инженерным делам за консультацией при необходимости глубокой экспертизы» — this is the Ruslan-explicit handle on this stream. Systematic ШСМ integration enters L7 as a Phase 2 research project once revenue runway is established.

### Private Library — 393 books (raw/books-md/, cross-reference to L1)

The Private Library curated corpus (393 books in raw/books-md/) is a primary-source fuel reserve for L7 research. It is stored at L1 (knowledge archive) but activated through L7 queries. Per D28 (Query-Driven KB Filtering): the library is not read exhaustively — each book is activated when it serves a declared active query. The library's epistemic value lies in its curated density; its operational value is conditional on a declared research question. Books without an active anchor are cold-archived at L1, not ingested into L7 working memory.

### BIOS/Clone-Wars parallel research (STRATEGIC-INSIGHT)

The BIOS/Clone-Wars research document (621 lines, `raw/research/2026-04-24-bios-clone-wars-jetix-ai-parallel.md`) is the first deliberate deployment of reverse-engineering-на-максималках as a method. Its strategic conclusion (from `decisions/STRATEGIC-INSIGHT-JETIX-AI-BIOS-MOMENT-2026-04-24.md`): Jetix should position itself not as an application-layer wrapper (which commoditises) but as the standards-level methodology layer — equivalent to the EISA consortium in the PC clone wars, or the Linux Foundation in the open-source moment. Seven structural lessons extracted from the PC market are mapped to the AI market of 2024-2026. This document is the prototype for all future L7 reverse-engineering output.

## Boundaries

**In scope for L7:**
- Research projects with declared epistemic targets (competitor tear-downs, standards analysis, methodology imports)
- Reverse-engineering activities on public-source material
- Standards-contribution conceptual work (new AI-protocols, laws, validation-tool concepts)
- External thinker methodology import (Левенчук ШСМ, BIOS/clone-wars structural patterns, etc.)
- First-use of research results before open publication (D24 grammar)

- Knowledge-content storage — that is L1 (Private Library, wiki)
- Products built from research — that is L5
- Revenue from research consulting — that is L6
- Patent strategy detail — that is a per-direction Phase 3 document, not L7 description

## Evolution — Phase-unlocks

**Phase 1 (€0 → €50K): D14 revenue-instrumental only.**
- Research = sales enablement, ICP refinement, Mittelstand knowledge, competitor analysis.
- Reverse-engineering operates in service of understanding client context and competitive differentiation.
- Левенчук reading continues as personal methodology grounding.
- Library queries are anchored to active revenue-serving research questions.
- *Epistemic claim:* "This phase's research activities are epistemically constrained by instrumental purpose." *Falsifier:* A research project in Phase 1 that cannot be traced to a revenue-serving outcome is out of scope and should be deferred.

**Phase 2 (€200K → €1M): first reverse-engineering products; patent process; Левенчук systematic integration.**
- Systematic reverse-engineering crawler infrastructure: first product targets (YouTube-analyzer per audio_518 as candidate, or equivalent).
- EU patent process opens (JETIX-VISION §11): research methodology combinations may become protectable.
- Левенчук ШСМ systematic integration: dedicated L7 research project, not personal reading only.
- Standards-contribution mechanism opens: participate in existing bodies or establish Mittelstand AI Alliance formal structure (STRATEGIC-INSIGHT §13 — EISA-moment positioning).
- *Epistemic claim:* "Phase 2 research begins to have standalone epistemic value beyond near-term revenue." *Falsifier:* If research investment in Phase 2 fails to generate any IP, partnership, or platform differentiation within 12 months, the research allocation should be reviewed.

**Phase 3 (€1M → $100M): D24 activates; standards-contribution; published papers.**
- D24 open-source research direction fully activated: systematic research on cooperation protocols, economy design, AI-to-AI contract frameworks.
- Published papers under Jetix authorship.
- Mittelstand AI Alliance formalised as standards consortium (Linux Foundation model per STRATEGIC-INSIGHT §13).
- Standards contribution: Jetix proposes new AI-to-AI contract validation frameworks, participates in EU AI Act evolution, contributes to protocol-level interoperability standards.
- *Epistemic claim:* "Phase 3 research generates public goods that increase Jetix's epistemic authority." *Falsifier:* Research publications in Phase 3 that generate no citations, no alliance participation, and no standards adoption within 24 months indicate a misallocation of research resources.

**Phase 3+ ($100M → $1T): civilizational-level research — cooperation-protocols, economy-design, AI-to-AI contract validation.**
- Full D24 scope: how humanity cooperates at scale, how future economic protocols should be designed.
- Jetix as standards-level contributor not just consumer — the USB-C equivalent is not just using protocols, it is writing them.
- Research scope expands to: new legal frameworks for AI agents as economic actors; international cooperation standards; civilizational infrastructure design (200-year horizon, JETIX-VISION §14).
