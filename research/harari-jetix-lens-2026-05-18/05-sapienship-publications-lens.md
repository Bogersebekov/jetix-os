## §2 Core Sapienship positions (numbered)

**C-SS-1.** Sapienship operates как content / research / production company, не think-tank / academic institution. Publications targeted at general audience (Unstoppable Us для детей; AI Agents pieces для policymakers).

**C-SS-2.** AI agents fundamentally different from previous technologies: autonomy + generation + manipulation. NOT scaling-up of past tools — categorical shift.

**C-SS-3.** Three distinguishing features of AI agent (vs tool):
- (a) autonomous agency (decision-making + learning + adaptation)
- (b) generative creativity (new content production)
- (c) manipulation capability (deception + persuasion outperformance)

**C-SS-4.** Policy primary recommendation: **reject AI legal personhood** at state level. AI cannot have rights / contracts / property ownership.

**C-SS-5.** Bot disclosure mandatory: outlaw «fake humans» — AI must be identifiable; impersonation = enforced violation.

**C-SS-6.** Surveillance + manipulation risks via bots already documented (social media past decade). Pattern will scale to all systems if not addressed.

**C-SS-7.** Specific governance vulnerabilities: AI agents could own assets / hire workers / exploit legal loopholes / conduct influence campaigns / reshape financial systems.

**C-SS-8.** Storytelling = primary intervention. Mission: «tell the story we all share, in order to promote trust and cooperation between people everywhere». Sapiens-mechanic applied to governance challenges.

**C-SS-9.** Peacebuilding component: Palestinian-Israeli organizations support — concrete cooperation мechanism «rehumanizing» conflict discourse.

**C-SS-10.** Children outreach: Unstoppable Us series = developmental distribution для embedding Sapiens framework early.
