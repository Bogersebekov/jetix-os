## §2 Path 1 — Recovery discipline (C17 candidate)

### §2.1 The option

Add explicit **C17 «восстановление» (recovery)** as 17th component of meta-method. OR alternatively, integrate recovery as paired-complement к C11 «1000% голодный» («hunger sustained via recovery»). OR alternatively, locate recovery in Foundation Part 9 Owner Interaction Scaffold (recovery as owner-self-discipline, not method-discipline).

### §2.2 Why this might fit

Phase 9 §8 identified recovery as the most-significant single gap. Sports science + sleep research + meditation traditions + Csikszentmihalyi flow data all converge: sustained high-performance requires structured recovery. C11 «1000% голодный» without recovery → A4 Burnout failure mode (Phase 9 §2.4).

Ruslan's audio_719 voice does NOT name recovery explicitly. This could be:
- Implicit (assumed but not articulated)
- Genuine gap (compensation discipline needed)
- Misunderstood (recovery = lazy in voice-internal framing; needs reframe)

Phase 9 §8 surfaced as candidate; Phase 10 articulates.

### §2.3 Trade-offs

| Pro | Con |
|---|---|
| Closes #1 high-severity gap | Diverges from voice-original 16 (adds external content) |
| Aligns w/ sustained high-performance evidence | May feel «soft» in current Ruslan framing |
| Integrates cleanly w/ Foundation Part 9 | Requires explicit reframe of «hunger» framing |
| Removes burnout risk (high-frequency for entrepreneurs) | C17 dilutes the «8+ component» one-pager hook |

### §2.4 Implementation surface

**Option A — Full C17 addition**:
- Add «C17 восстановление / recovery» к Method V2 §J 16-component list

- Update cycle pattern to include explicit recovery markers

**Option B — Paired complement to C11**:
- Reframe C11 as «1000% голодный, sustained через recovery»
- Single-line addition к Method V2 §J under C11
- No separate component
- Less ceremonious

**Option C — Foundation Part 9 locus**:
- Recovery as owner-self-discipline (per Pillar C Tier 1 manager principles)
- Surfaced via Part 9 monthly reflection cadence
- NOT in 16-component meta-method
- Preserves voice-original

### §2.5 Foundation impact

- Option A: Method V2 §J §APPEND patch (Ruslan-ack required)
- Option B: Method V2 §J inline-edit
- Option C: Foundation Part 9 §APPEND (Ruslan-ack required per IP-1)

## §3 Path 2 — 8-cluster-lead canonical form for cohort transfer

### §3.1 The option

Adopt **8 cluster-leads (C1, C4, C6, C7, C8, C9, C11, C15)** as canonical form для cohort / pitch / institutional contexts; preserve 16-component form for personal / internal use.

### §3.2 Why this might fit

Phase 7 §11 empirical evidence: 5-10 component compositions are the institutional success range. ISO 9001's 7 principles + ITIL 4's 6 activities + TOGAF's 9 phases + DMAIC 5 phases — all sit в this range. Ruslan's 8 cluster-leads matches.

Phase 9 §4.1 surfaced C1 component-overlap pathology. The 8-cluster reduction de-overlaps cleanly.

Phase 9 §5.2 surfaced D2 cohort transfer failure (Conway's Law). A more-compact form (8 vs 16) transfers more reliably to cohort members lacking Ruslan's full substrate.

### §3.3 Trade-offs

| Pro | Con |
|---|---|
| Empirically validated count (5-10 range) | Loses richness of 16 (state + motivation explicit) |
| Easier cohort transfer | Two-form maintenance complexity |
| Cleaner one-pager hook | Cohort may demand the «full» 16 anyway |
| Matches institutional comparables | Risk of «one-true» framing creep |

### §3.4 Implementation surface

- Define «canonical 8» in Method V2 §J explicit section
- Add cross-reference: «expanded 16 form для internal / phenomenological use; canonical 8 для cohort / pitch / institutional»
- Update wiki/concepts/meta-method-8-component-composition.md to clarify dual-form (already present per O-121 promotion commit `dafa5bc`)

### §3.5 Foundation impact
