## §2 C1 — «Радикальная проработка» (Maximum-effort elaboration)

### §2.1 Voice
> «зафиксировал вот как раз **самую ебейшую проработку**» [audio_719 claim 1]

### §2.2 Operational definition
Radical elaboration = sustained, non-shortcutting engagement w/ a task at depth that exceeds default-effort. Ruslan's intensifier «ебейшая» = absolute-maximum signal; no «that'll do» exit.

### §2.3 Everyday examples (≥5)
1. **Watchmaker assembling a Patek Philippe movement** — each gear adjusted to 0.001mm; not skipping the chamfering step on the bridges; checking each pivot under loupe
2. **Marathon-runner taper-week preparation** — careful nutrition adjustment + sleep tracking + pace-zone work; not «just running»
3. **Mother preparing daughter's wedding** — every detail considered, multiple iterations on seating chart, food-tasting, dress fittings; not «pretty good is fine»
4. **Surgeon studying a complex tumor case** — reviewing imaging from 5 angles, consulting 3 specialists, briefing the team, рассмотрение alternative approaches; not «standard procedure»
5. **Carpenter handcrafting a dining table** — choosing the wood, planning the grain, hand-fitting joints, multiple sanding passes, oil-finish over months; not «good enough»
6. **Pianist preparing Rachmaninoff 3rd concerto** — separate hands practice, slow practice, partial-tempo, contextualisation listening; not «play through»
7. **Cook preparing Thanksgiving turkey** — brine 24h, dry overnight, butter under skin, baste every 30min, rest 45min; not «put in oven»

### §2.4 Cross-tradition
- **Ericsson deliberate practice** (Phase 2 §5) — C1 ≈ Ericsson's «stretch beyond comfort + focused attention» [src: A8]
- **Polya phase 3** (Phase 2 §2.1) — «carrying out the plan» = execution discipline
- **Unix Simplicity rule** (Phase 4 §2.1 Rule 5) — добавлять complexity только where necessary
- **Aristotle techne** (Phase 2 §8) — craft-discipline foundation

### §2.5 Jetix substrate
- Method V2 §J Phase 3 «глубина» direct cross-ref [src: H1]
- Foundation Part 5 Compound Learning — repeated radical elaboration → cycle compound

### §2.6 Failure mode preview (Phase 9)
Risk: «perfectionism trap» — radical elaboration becomes infinite-deferral. Mitigation: paired with C13 «ускоренный» + Method V2 timing-discipline.

### §2.7 Integration hook (Phase 10)
Recommended pairing: C1 + C4 (focus) + C13 (speed) — radical elaboration on important things, on time. Without C4 / C13 pair: time wasted on radical elaboration of unimportant things.

## §3 C2 — «Глубина работы» (Depth)

### §3.1 Voice
> «самая сука глубокая [проработка]» [audio_719 claim 1]

### §3.2 Operational definition
Depth = engagement that goes beyond surface, asks «why behind why», pursues mechanisms not symptoms. Distinct from C1 (intensity) — C2 = penetration. C1 is *how much*; C2 is *how deep*.

### §3.3 Everyday examples (≥5)
1. **Doctor diagnosing chronic fatigue** — not just CBC; full thyroid panel + autoimmune + sleep study + iron + B12; pursues root cause
2. **Software debug session** — not just «add print statements»; reproducing minimally, instrumenting actually-affected code, reading the stack trace fully
3. **Therapist exploring relationship pattern** — not just current dispute; family-of-origin patterns, attachment style, past triggers; depth-oriented
4. **Investigative journalist on a corporate scandal** — not just press releases; financial filings + insider sources + cross-referencing dates; multiple-source-confirmation
5. **Reader engaging with Aristotle Nicomachean Ethics** — not just skim Book 6; multiple translations + commentary + applying examples; depth-as-grasp
6. **Parent investigating child's school struggle** — not just «do your homework»; meeting teacher, observing classroom, asking about social, screening for learning differences
7. **Engineer designing a bridge** — not just structural calc; geological survey + traffic load + thermal cycling + maintenance access + 100-year wind; depth-as-completeness

### §3.4 Cross-tradition
- **Polanyi tacit dimension** (Phase 2 §3) — depth requires tacit indwelling [src: A4]
- **Beer VSM S4 Intelligence** (Phase 3 §3) — depth-of-environmental-scan
- **DDD bounded contexts ubiquitous language** (Phase 4 §4) — depth-of-domain-vocabulary

### §3.5 Jetix substrate
- AUDIO-721-INSIGHTS-REPORT §3 unified-framework depth-discipline [src: H2]
- Foundation Part 3 Knowledge Base — depth-organised по 9 entity types

### §3.6 Failure mode preview
Risk: «rabbit-hole» — depth without breadth-check; over-investment in one area while neglecting Ashby requisite-variety across portfolio. Mitigation: C7 leverage + portfolio-balance.

### §3.7 Integration hook
C2 (depth) + C9 (mechanism literacy) = the «pursuing why behind why» pair. Strong synergy; one without the other = incomplete.

## §4 C3 — «Качество работы» (Output-quality discipline)

### §4.1 Voice (implicit cluster from C1+C2)

### §4.2 Operational definition
Output-quality discipline = no half-finished work shipped, no «that'll do» exits, the artefact's fitness-for-purpose meets a high bar consistently. Distinct from C1 (effort) and C2 (depth) — C3 = the *consistent standard* across outputs.

### §4.3 Everyday examples (≥5)
1. **Chef's mise-en-place** — every dish prepared to same plating standard; not «easy night» version
2. **Photographer's portfolio curation** — only top 5% of shoot included; rest deleted; consistent standard
3. **Software shipping discipline** — no «commented-out TODOs» в production; either complete the feature or rip it out
4. **Lawyer's brief writing** — every brief proofread 3 times; cite-checking every reference; consistent professional standard
5. **Sommelier's tasting notes** — every wine logged with same depth (nose / palate / finish / pairing); not «I liked this one»
6. **Translator's published output** — every page proofread by native speaker + back-translated for accuracy; consistent quality across volume
7. **Restaurant maintaining Michelin standard** — every plate that leaves kitchen to same standard, even at the 11th hour of service

### §4.4 Cross-tradition
- **Unix Simplicity + Robustness** (Phase 4 §2.1 Rules 5, 8) — consistent quality emerges from simple + transparent design
- **SAFe principle 5: «objective evaluation of working systems»** (Phase 7 §4) — quality-discipline measured against external standard
- **CMMI Level 3 «Defined» standards** (Phase 7 §7) — consistent process → consistent quality

### §4.5 Jetix substrate
- Foundation Part 6a Provenance Officer — F-G-R grading discipline = quality calibration
- Cycle Wave A→B→C→D structure — quality gates at each wave

### §4.6 Failure mode preview
Risk: «quality theatre» — sign-off ceremony without actual quality; or «perfectionism» that prevents shipping. Mitigation: C7 leverage + C13 speed + explicit fitness criteria.

### §4.7 Integration hook
C3 + C13 (speed) = «high-quality fast» — the elusive but achievable Toyota-Production-System discipline. C3 without C13 = slow-perfect; C13 without C3 = fast-crap.

6. **Programmer in deep-work block** — 4 hours uninterrupted; no Slack, no meetings; one problem

## §6 C5 — «Отсечение неважного» (Cut-non-essential)

### §6.1 Voice
> «**отсечение всего неважного**» [audio_719 claim 1]

### §6.2 Operational definition
Cut-non-essential = active removal of low-value engagement, не passive non-engagement. The «отсечение» (cutting-off) is stronger than «избегание» (avoiding) — it's prophylactic + structural; building one's life so the non-essential cannot intrude.

### §6.3 Everyday examples (≥5)
1. **Steve Jobs's Apple product lineup cut in 1997** — from 350 products to 10; everything else cancelled
2. **Buffett's «no list»** — 99/100 investment opportunities declined; the «no» discipline
3. **Marie Kondo's «does it spark joy»** — items discarded systematically; clothes / books / papers / komono / sentimental
4. **Greg McKeown «Essentialism» — disciplined pursuit of less** — 90% rule (if not «hell yes», it's no)
5. **Tim Ferriss 4-Hour Workweek elimination phase** — removing meetings, batch-processing email, fire bad clients
6. **Doctor's vacation triage** — no work email, no work calls; structurally enforced absence
7. **Athlete's diet-cut for competition** — alcohol, sugar, processed; no exceptions, не «occasional»

### §6.4 Cross-tradition
- **Munger «invert always invert»** (Phase 2 §7) — focus by elimination
- **Ashby variety attenuation** (Phase 3 §4) — reducing disturbance variety
- **Meadows leverage point 5 «rules of the system»** (Phase 3 §5) — structural rule-of-no
- **Unix Parsimony rule** (Phase 4 §2.1 Rule 6) — write only what's needed
- **Lean 7 wastes elimination** (Phase 7 §9) — anti-waste discipline

### §6.5 Jetix substrate
- Pillar C 12 hard rules + Default-Deny table — explicit «cut non-essential» discipline
- AP-6 dissent atoms — preserve dissent (not extinct it via «cut»)
- /project-archive — explicit closure ritual

### §6.6 Failure mode preview
Risk: «cutting too much» — over-pruning eliminates antifragility (Taleb adjacency); cutting wrong things destroys optionality. Mitigation: C9 mechanism literacy + portfolio-thinking.

### §6.7 Integration hook
C5 + C4 = paired «select + remove» — the classic «to-do list» + «not-to-do list» discipline (per Buffett). The «not-to-do list» is structurally weaker in most lives — Ruslan's C5 articulation IS the not-to-do-list discipline.

## §7 C6 — «Полная честность с собой» (Complete self-honesty)

### §7.1 Voice
> «**полная честность с собой**» [audio_719 claim 1]

### §7.2 Operational definition
Complete self-honesty = absence-of-self-deception across observation + interpretation + decision domains. Includes: honest about results (not flattering self-narrative), honest about motivations (recognising mixed motives), honest about capabilities (calibrated confidence), honest about errors (no rationalisation).

This is the **foundational component** — Phase 1 §2.3 + Phase 5 §9 audit all show C6 as the substrate-without-which other components fail. Polanyi's «personal coefficient» (Phase 2 §3) = the technical articulation.

### §7.3 Everyday examples (≥5)
1. **Doctor's medical chart** — accurate symptom note even when symptoms suggest poor patient compliance; not flattering version
2. **Weight-loss tracker** — recording the cake; not «well I forgot to log lunch»
3. **Financial accounting** — accurate P&L; not «adjusted EBITDA» that hides losses
4. **Therapist's self-supervision** — admitting countertransference; not «I was just professional»
5. **Athlete's training log** — honest mileage, honest paces, honest pain levels; not «pretty good week»
6. **Programmer's bug post-mortem** — «I shipped without testing» admitted; not «we had a process failure»
7. **Relationship reflection** — «I was angry because X actually pinged my insecurity Y»; not «she was being unreasonable»
8. **Project status report** — «we're behind because we miscalculated effort»; not «scope expanded»

### §7.4 Cross-tradition

- F-G-R grading — honest formality calibration

### §7.6 Failure mode preview

## §11 C10 — «Выявление узких горлышек» (Bottleneck identification)

### §11.3 Everyday examples (≥5)

2. **Software identifying that database query is bottleneck** — not optimising the loop above; fixing the query
3. **Sales pipeline identifying that lead-generation is bottleneck** — not closing-skills training; investing в lead-gen
4. **Athlete identifying that recovery is bottleneck** — not training harder; investing в sleep + nutrition
5. **Family identifying that morning chaos is bottleneck** — not adding evening activities; redesigning morning
6. **Writing identifying that planning is bottleneck** — not writing more drafts; planning more carefully first
7. **Health identifying that sleep is bottleneck** — not adding exercise; fixing sleep first
8. **Business identifying that key hire is bottleneck** — not adding marketing; making the key hire

### §11.4 Cross-tradition
- **Goldratt Theory of Constraints** — *the* canonical bottleneck framework (adjacency, not formally cited)
- **Meadows leverage point 10 «stocks»** (Phase 3 §5) — bottleneck = stock dynamics
- **Beer VSM S3 Control** (Phase 3 §3) — resource-allocation = bottleneck-resolution

### §11.5 Jetix substrate

### §11.6 Failure mode preview

### §11.7 Integration hook
C10 + C7 leverage = the «apply leverage where it actually multiplies» pair. Most leverage failures are bottleneck-identification failures.
