## §1 Definition + 4 enabling capabilities (text_014 §2.30)

### §1.1 What is an exokortex?

**Working definition (F2):** *Exokortex* = extended cognitive substrate operating beyond the biological brain, materialised as a structured ensemble of (a) language, (b) tools, (c) repository, (d) protocols, (e) methodology — together enabling cognition that the unaugmented brain could not perform alone, at scale, or at speed.

The term «экзокортекс» (English «exocortex») entered popular usage via Charles Stross (sf novelist, 2005+) and Ben Goertzel (2005+ AGI essays). The *concept* is older: Engelbart 1962 H-LAM/T framework is the founding articulation of the same idea under different terminology («augmentation of human intellect»). [src: Stross 2005 «Accelerando»; Goertzel 2005 essay «Exocortex»; Engelbart 1962 SRI-2049]

## §2 Primary precedents (4 thinkers, ≤500w each)

### §2.1 Clark + Chalmers — «The Extended Mind» (Analysis, 1998)

**Canonical claim:** *«Cognitive processes ain't (all) in the head.»* [src: Clark & Chalmers 1998 *Analysis* 58(1):7-19]

The «Otto's notebook» thought experiment: Otto has Alzheimer's; he writes addresses in a notebook he always carries. Inga (no Alzheimer's) recalls addresses from biological memory. Clark + Chalmers argue: **if Otto's notebook plays the same functional role as Inga's memory, it is functionally constitutive of his cognition** — not merely a tool, but a *cognitive part*.

**Parity Principle:** *«If, as we confront some task, a part of the world functions as a process which, were it done in the head, we would have no hesitation in recognizing as part of the cognitive process, then that part of the world is (so we claim) part of the cognitive process.»*

**Conditions for extension** (Clark 2008 refinement):
1. Reliable availability (always at hand)
2. Easy access (low friction retrieval)
3. Trust (epistemic confidence in retrieval)
4. Past endorsement (history of use)

**Jetix mapping:**
- Wiki (Phase 0 inventory + 5 K-research outputs + 31 K-6 components) = persistent notebook
- Brigadier dispatch = cognitive-process router (functionally part of cognition during cycle)
- Per-agent `strategies.md` = compounding individual-agent memory
- FPF same-language layer = enables Otto-style retrieval across substrate
- All 4 Clark conditions ≈ met by Jetix substrate when Workshop / Cycle is active

[src: Clark & Chalmers 1998; Clark 2008 «Supersizing the Mind»]

### §2.2 Andy Clark — «Natural-Born Cyborgs» (2003) + «Supersizing the Mind» (2008)

**Central thesis (Clark 2003):** *«We are not, and never were, the Cartesian theatre. We are constitutionally permeable, embodied-extended cognitive agents — natural-born cyborgs.»* [src: Clark 2003 OUP]

Clark elaborates Extended Mind into a research programme: cognition is *constitutively* (not merely causally) extended into tools, language, social practice, environment-structuring. Cross-link к Hutchins 1995 «Cognition in the Wild» (distributed cognition).

**4 levels of cognitive extension** (Clark 2008 ch. 4):
1. Bio-internal (neural)
2. Body-internal (proprioception + embodiment)
3. Environment-coupled (tools, notebook, calculator)
4. Socially-distributed (team, organization, culture)

**Jetix instantiation:**
- Level 1: Ruslan + Jetix users biological brains
- Level 2: voice-pipeline (speech as embodied I/O channel)
- Level 3: Wiki + FPF substrate + 5 K-research outputs (environment-coupled)
- Level 4: Workshop / Education Layer / ROY swarm (socially-distributed)

**Workshop = full-stack exokortex** = all 4 levels actively engaged.

[src: Clark 2003 + Clark 2008 OUP]

### §2.3 Doug Engelbart — «Augmenting Human Intellect: A Conceptual Framework» (1962)

**Canonical statement:** *«By "augmenting human intellect" we mean increasing the capability of a man to approach a complex problem situation, to gain comprehension to suit his particular needs, and to derive solutions to problems. Increased capability in this respect is taken to mean a mixture of the following: more-rapid comprehension, better comprehension, the possibility of gaining a useful degree of comprehension in a situation that previously was too complex, speedier solutions, better solutions, and the possibility of finding solutions to problems that before seemed insoluble.»* [src: Engelbart 1962 SRI-2049, summary]

**H-LAM/T framework:** *Human using Language, Artefacts, Methodology, with Training*. Co-evolution of these 4 components produces capability strictly greater than the human-alone baseline.

## §8 Falsifiers / test designs (4 tests)

### §8.1 10x speedup hypothesis test

**Claim (text_014 §2.30 capability 1):** exokortex enables 10x speedup vs unaugmented self.

**Test design:** measure 5 task-types (research synthesis / decision making / artefact authoring / coordination / reflection) with-Workshop vs without-Workshop. Threshold: ≥3 of 5 task-types show ≥3x speedup.

**Refutation:** if <3 task-types show speedup ≥3x, capability 1 claim refuted (or operationally not realised yet).

### §8.2 Same-language interaction enables previously-impossible collaboration

**Claim (text_014 §2.30 capability 2):** FPF + same-language enables collaboration impossible without.

**Test design:** identify 5 collaboration tasks attempted-and-failed pre-Workshop; re-attempt with Workshop substrate; success rate target ≥3/5.

**Refutation:** if Workshop-substrate collaboration doesn't improve previously-failed cases, capability 2 claim degraded.

### §8.3 Quality improvement measurable

**Claim (text_014 §2.30 capability 4):** quality / speed / efficiency improvement.

**Test design:** ≥3 cycle outputs externally-reviewed (peer Workshop / external expert); quality scores cycle-N vs cycle-1. Trend target: monotonic improvement.

**Refutation:** if cycle-N quality ≤ cycle-1 average, capability 4 claim degraded.

### §8.4 Sovereignty / R12 alignment audit

**Claim (Phase 5.5 §7.3):** Workshop instances maintain R12 anti-extraction discipline.

**Test design:** audit cycle outputs for extraction beyond agreed share; fork-and-leave provision documented per cohort; wage-ratio cap respected per Ethereum substrate.

**Refutation:** any extraction-violation = capability 4 substrate degraded; R12 enforcement reform required.
