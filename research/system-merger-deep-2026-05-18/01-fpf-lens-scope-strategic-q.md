### §1.1 Scope candidate 1 — Two-system merger as system (A.1 holonic)

- **Application:** Merged entity = supersystem; LHS host + RHS incoming = subsystems pre-merger.
- **A.1 holonic** [src: FPF-Spec.md A.1:2026-05-17 retrieved] — system as part-whole holon enables:

- **Implication:** Merger ≠ destruction of identity; it = nesting with new shared envelope.
- **Falsifier:** If incoming system cannot preserve sub-holon identity → merger collapses to absorption (loss of variety; cf. §1.5 Ashby).
