# Cluster 6 — Virtual tribes / new community forms

> **R1 surface-only.** Surfaces tribal patterns: ideology-tribes (e/acc, d/acc), game-tribes (EVE Online), open-source-tribes (NixOS, Debian), intentional-community-tribes (kibbutz). Anchors text_004 «virtual tribe» framing.

## §0 TL;DR (≤200 слов)

«Virtual tribe» паттерн делится на 5 типов:

**(1) Ideology-tribes (Twitter-Substack-loose):** e/acc (Marc Andreessen «Techno-Optimist Manifesto» Oct 2023, Beff Jezos = Guillaume Verdon disclosed Dec 2023 ex-Google quantum) — pro-AI-acceleration. d/acc (Vitalik Buterin Nov 2023 «My techno-optimism» + Jan 2025 «d/acc: one year later» blog post) — defensive + decentralized + democratic differentiated acceleration. Substrate: Substack manifestos + Twitter Spaces.

**(2) Game-tribes (EVE Online 20+ years):** Corporations + Alliances — multi-corp Alliance for sovereignty; Executor Corp requires 50%+ CEO support; thousands of capsuleers per organization. Real friendship + empires from sustained 20-year sandbox.

**(3) Open-source-tribes:** NixOS — steering committee 2-year terms, annual elections, Stichting NixOS Foundation, «community goals supersede individual interests». Debian — Debian Constitution, DPL elections. Plan9 mythos. Active member governance.

**(4) Intentional communities physical:** Kibbutz Israel (Kvutzat Degania 1909, ~270 kibbutzim peak) — direct participatory democracy, common income pool, 2005 legal split into Shitufi (cooperative) vs MitChadesh (mixed) vs MetuChavim (privatized). 100+ years lessons.

**(5) Recent crypto-tribes:** Friend.tech 2023-2024 (collapse covered Cluster 3); Praxis covered Cluster 3.

**Adjacency к vision/04 first Clan 10 people + text_004:** Kibbutz + EVE Online Corp + NixOS steering committee = **proven small-tribe substrate patterns**. e/acc vs d/acc framing = active live tribal differentiation на Substack — Jetix Cantonment может be «m/acc» (methodology/acc) tribal niche.

## §1 Key works / movements / people

### §1.1 Ideology-tribes (Twitter + Substack + Spaces)

**e/acc (Effective Accelerationism, 2022+)** — Wikipedia confirms 21st-century ideological movement; pro-tech / pro-AI / unrestricted-progress. Key figures:
- **Marc Andreessen** — «Techno-Optimist Manifesto» Oct 2023 essay. a16z founder. Added «e/acc» to public profiles.
- **Beff Jezos** = **Guillaume Verdon** (disclosed Forbes Dec 2023). Canadian ex-Google quantum computing engineer + theoretical physicist. Lex Fridman Podcast Dec 29, 2023 interview as «creator».
- **Marc Andreessen reaction to Sam Altman firing Nov 2023: «e/acc!»**.

Substrate: **Twitter Spaces + Substack manifestos.** Loose-knit, informal, open to contributions. [src: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Effective_accelerationism; https://www.bloomberg.com/news/newsletters/2023-12-06/effective-accelerationism-and-beff-jezos-form-new-tech-tribe, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**d/acc (Decentralized / Defensive Accelerationism, Nov 2023+)** — Vitalik Buterin response. Core: «decentralized, democratic, and differentiated defensive acceleration». **Originated Buterin's «My techno-optimism» blog post Nov 2023.** **«d/acc: one year later»** blog post Jan 5, 2025. Focus: tech that **shifts offense/defense balance toward defense + doesn't hand power to centralized authorities**. Applications: public health, AI safety, data security, zero-knowledge proofs. **Edge City Lanna** event hosted Vitalik talks (Network State-adjacent). [src: https://vitalik.eth.limo/general/2025/01/05/dacc2.html; https://thedefiant.io/news/people/vitalik-buterin-proposes-d-acc-philosophy-in-post-on-techno-optimism, retrieved 2026-05-17]

> Verbatim (Buterin core d/acc thesis): «Decentralized, democratic, and differentiated defensive acceleration … technologies that improve our ability to defend, rather than our ability to cause harm, and … technologies that distribute power rather than concentrating it in the hands of a singular elite.»

**CabalCity** — adjacent vibe-tribe of crypto-aligned community-organizers; informal substrate.

**Edge Esmeralda / Edge City Lanna / Vitalia / Zuzalu** — series of pop-up «temporary city» events linked to d/acc + Network State community. Real-world cluster.

### §1.2 Game-tribes (EVE Online lineage)

**EVE Online (CCP Games, 2003+)** — sandbox MMO. 20+ years sustained tribal structure. **Corporations** (in-game player organizations, can hold sovereignty) + **Alliances** (multi-corp federations). **Executor Corporation** runs alliance — requires 50%+ member CEO support. **Hundreds of capsuleers per organization** standard. Real-world economy: stories of large alliances, betrayal arcs, virtual war diplomacy. [src: https://wiki.eveuniversity.org/Alliances; https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/stronger-organizations, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**WoW guilds** — 20-year lineage of online tribes. Vanilla WoW raid guilds = pre-Discord coordination culture.

**Discord clans / Roblox tribes** — current generation gaming tribes; gen Z native.

### §1.3 Open-source tribes

**NixOS / Nix community** — governance: elected steering committee (2-year terms, annual elections for ≥half seats) + Stichting NixOS Foundation board (legal/financial/partnership). **«Community goals supersede individual interests and agendas.»** Currently transforming to community-led governance. **Diverse contributions valued** — not just engineers, also designers + organizers + event planners. [src: https://nixos.org/governance/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Debian** — Debian Constitution (one of first formal open-source governance docs, 1998). Debian Project Leader (DPL) elected annually. Technical Committee adjudicates technical disputes. Social Contract = ideological anchor. 30-year sustained.

**Apache Software Foundation** — meritocracy + «Apache Way» culture. Committer status earned through contribution.

**Plan9 / Inferno** — Bell Labs research OS lineage. Cult-tribal community. Tiny but devoted.

**OmegaWiki / Karpathy LLM Wiki Discord/forum** — emerging tribe (Cluster 2 cross-ref).

**Pieter Hintjens RFC 36/22 culture** — ZeroMQ governance model. «Make people part of the project, not just users.»

### §1.4 Intentional communities (physical+ideological)

**Kibbutz movement (Israel, 1909+)** — Kvutzat Degania first kibbutz (Lake Kinneret, 1909, religious Jews, Ottoman Palestine). **British Mandate period 1910-1948 = explosive growth**, dozens of kibbutzim across Palestine, affiliated with Labor Zionist political parties. **Direct participatory democracy + common income pool**. **2005 legal classification:** Kibbutz Shitufi (still cooperative) / MitChadesh (some cooperative systems) / MetuChavim (privatized). **115-year sustained lineage**, though substantially evolved. [src: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kibbutz; https://www.jewishagency.org/what-exactly-is-a-kibbutz/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Quaker meetings** — 370+ year lineage. Consensus decision-making. Plain-speech tradition. Small but durable.

**Benedictine monasteries** — Rule of St. Benedict 6th century. Methodology + discipline + community for ~1500 years. Distribution model = monastic chapter founding new houses.

**Mondragón** — covered Cluster 5.

**Acumen Fund + similar value-aligned investing communities** — modern adjacency.

### §1.5 Religious vs secular structures comparative

Religious communities (Quaker / Benedictine / kibbutz) frequently outperform secular intentional communities on longevity. **Hypothesis (Putnam, Rosenberg, others):** shared transcendent narrative + costly ritual signaling enables longer cohesion. Lesson for Jetix Workshop: «secular methodology» communities (TRIZ, ШСМ) may need substitute mechanisms for cohesion.

## §4 Failure cases / lessons learned

**Kibbutz decline.** 270+ kibbutzim peak → many became Kibbutz MetuChavim (privatized) by 2010s. **Cause hypotheses:** generational replacement loss + economic pressures + Israeli society shift to individualism. **Lesson for Jetix:** 100-year cooperative communities still fragile; **generational continuity = constitutional design problem**, not just charisma.

**EVE Online realism leak.** Some EVE players invested years; alliances betrayed = real psychological harm. **Lesson:** sustained tribal investment is real cost; community design must respect this — exit mechanisms (R12 «fork-and-leave without penalty» literally).

**Debian fork attempts.** Devuan (init system fork 2014) — even constitutionally-governed open-source can split. **Lesson:** governance ≠ immunity to schism. Pillar C R12 anti-extraction + corrigibility addresses this.

**NixOS recent governance tensions.** «Nix deserves great governance» discussion (NixOS Discourse 2024+) shows governance growth pains. Even healthy community evolves через governance crisis. **Lesson:** governance is **continuous practice**, not one-time design.

**e/acc tribe issues — fragmentation.** No central authority; loose-knit means: hard to coordinate, easy to dilute brand. «Beff Jezos» persona disclosed as Verdon = identity-management tension. **Lesson:** anonymous-persona tribes vulnerable to disclosure cascade.

**d/acc adoption velocity.** Despite Buterin pedigree, d/acc tribe smaller than e/acc — defensive framing less viral than acceleration framing. **Lesson for Jetix positioning:** «methodology» framing may be less viral than «acceleration». Naming matters.

**Quaker / Benedictine slow growth.** Multi-century but small. Lesson: optimal-tribe-size unclear; small + durable = legitimate strategy.

**Friend.tech, FTX «communities»**: financialized tribes collapse fast. **Lesson:** don't financialize identity/membership.

**Praxis $525M (Cluster 3 cross-ref).** Capital ≠ tribe. Money attracts participants но does not convert into shared transcendent narrative.
