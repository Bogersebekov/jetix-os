# Cluster 4 — Engineering / methodologist communities

> **R1 surface-only.** Surfaces existing methodologist communities — primary baselines (ШСМ + МИМ) уже known; этот док добавляет global counterparts.

## §0 TL;DR (≤200 слов)

Поле methodologist communities имеет 4 типа: **(1) Standards bodies** (INCOSE 21K+ members 70+ chapters, OMG SysML v2 adopted June 2025); **(2) Pioneering methodology + descendant organizations** (SEMAT Essence Kernel by Ivar Jacobson — Alphas (Opportunity/Stakeholder/Requirement/Software-System/Work/Team/Way-of-Working); Cynefin (Snowden, 1999, 5 domains) — The Cynefin Company 2005+; TRIZ — Altshuller Institute serves community; TOC (Goldratt) — TOCICO certification body, 3M+ books sold, 23 languages); **(3) Manifesto + viral** (Agile 2001, 25 years anniversary 2026 + «Reimagining Agile» movement); **(4) Russian methodology lineage** (Левенчук ШСМ → EEM Institute — Anatoly Levenchuk 30+ years strategy consulting, «Systems Thinking 2020» 715-page textbook in Russian + English; «Aisystant» — extension/community).

**Adjacency к Jetix:** SEMAT Essence Kernel = **closest direct precedent** для FPF — same ambition («common ground for software engineering» = «universal language for engineering»), same structure (Alphas with state transitions ≈ FPF objects with quality gates), same target audience (professional methodologists). Cynefin 5-domain framework = potential frame для FPF problem-type classification. INCOSE = direct membership recruitment surface при naviga partner forks.

**Lesson:** SEMAT struggled with adoption despite Jacobson's pedigree; cause hypothesized = abstract без specific tooling. Cynefin sustained 25-year community via consulting practice. TRIZ has best Russian-rooted distribution mechanism. Agile/Lean became commodity certifications с loss-of-soul; «Reimagining Agile» movement = potential ally pool.

## §1 Key works / movements / people

### §1.1 Standards bodies

**INCOSE (International Council on Systems Engineering, 1990+)** — not-for-profit; **21,000+ members, 70+ chapters, 52 working groups, 70+ countries.** 34th Annual International Symposium July 2024 (Dublin, Ireland), hybrid event. Healthcare WG specialty conference. **Direct membership surface for Jetix engineering thinkers.** [src: https://www.incose.org/; https://www.incose.org/home/2024/01/27/international-workshop/incose-iw-2024---torrance-ca-usa, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**OMG (Object Management Group, 1989+)** — UML, SysML, BPMN standards. **SysML v2.0 final adoption: June 2025.** Based on KerML metamodel with formal semantics + **textual syntax (not just graphical)**; enables AI/automation integration. SysML API + Services spec v1.0. Adjacent: AI-ready model-based systems engineering. [src: https://www.omg.org/sysml/sysmlv2/; https://www.omg.org/news/releases/pr2025/07-21-25.htm, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**IEEE / ACM** — broader professional engineering networks. IEEE Computer Society ≈ 60K members; ACM ≈ 100K members. Less methodology-focused, more academic/professional credentialing.

### §1.2 Pioneering methodology + community organization

**SEMAT (Software Engineering Methods and Theory) — Essence Kernel by Ivar Jacobson + colleagues (2009+)** — international initiative «to identify a common ground, or universal standard, for software engineering». **Alphas:** Opportunity, Stakeholder, Requirement, Software System, Work, Team, Way of Working. Each Alpha has state transitions (e.g., Software System: Architecture Selected → Demonstrable → Usable → Ready → Operational → Retired). OMG-managed Essence specification. **Direct ambition parallel to FPF.** [src: https://queue.acm.org/detail.cfm?id=2389616; https://www.ivarjacobson.com/publications/articles/essence-software-engineering-semat-kernel-published-acmqueue, retrieved 2026-05-17]

> Verbatim (Jacobson et al., ACM Queue 2012): "The kernel is a simple thinking framework to help teams understand the progress and health of their endeavor."

**Cynefin framework (Dave Snowden, 1999+ at IBM Global Services)** — 5 decision-making domains: clear / complicated / complex / chaotic / confusion (or disorder). **The Cynefin Centre** (independent from IBM 2004). **The Cynefin Company** (2005+, Snowden). Sense-making device. 2024 Snowden public acknowledgment of «overconfidence» в clarity of 'chaos' usage = ongoing refinement. [src: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cynefin_framework; https://thecynefin.co/cynefin-st-davids-2024-1-of-n/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**TRIZ (Genrich Altshuller, 1946+, USSR)** — Theory of Inventive Problem Solving. **Patent database analysis** of 40,000+ abstracts → 40 Principles of Invention + Contradiction Matrix. **Altshuller Institute** (US-based community hub) + multiple Russian/European communities. **Russian-rooted methodology distribution** = direct adjacency for Jetix Russian-English bilingual approach. TRIZ books + workshops + certifications. [src: https://www.aitriz.org/; https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TRIZ, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**TOC (Theory of Constraints — Eli Goldratt, 1984+)** — «The Goal» novel-based methodology. **3 million books sold, 23 languages translated, hundreds of universities.** **TOCICO (TOC International Certification Organization)** = certification body. **Dr. Eliyahu Goldratt Foundation** post-2011 (Goldratt's death). Annual conference + webinars + case studies. **Distribution model = novel + certification + consulting practice.** [src: https://www.tocico.org/about-us; https://www.tocinstitute.org/theory-of-constraints.html, retrieved 2026-05-17]

### §1.3 Manifesto + viral methodologies

**Agile Manifesto (2001) + Scrum (Sutherland/Schwaber 1990s+)** — 25 years anniversary 2026. **Adoption surge continues 2024** across tech / finance / healthcare / manufacturing. BUT: **«superficial implementation and conceptual misalignments»** per original signatories. **«Reimagining Agile» initiative** (active 2024-2026) — community of original signatories + practitioners reclaiming the philosophy. [src: https://agilemanifesto.org/; https://rememo.io/blog/agile-2023-an-examination-of-emerging-trends, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Lean Tech Manifesto** — 2024 effort bridging Lean Thinking + Agile + tech innovation at scale (Fabrice Bernhard, Theodo). Lean Summit 2024 keynote. Toyota Way as deep ancestor.

**Disciplined Agile (DA)** — PMI methodology framework integrating Agile/Lean. Less viral but corporate.

## §4 Failure cases / lessons learned

**SEMAT — slow adoption despite Jacobson's pedigree.** Despite OMG sponsorship + Ivar Jacobson (Unified Process pioneer) + comprehensive Essence Kernel publication (2012), SEMAT remains niche compared to Agile/Scrum. **Hypothesis:** abstract framework без specific viral incentive + competing с already-entrenched Scrum certification economy. **Direct lesson for FPF:** abstract «universal language» insufficient — need viral tooling (Karpathy LLM Wiki distribution model?) + specific payoff.

**Cynefin — 25-year sustained niche.** Cynefin built durable community through **consulting practice + speaking + framework simplicity**. Has not become commodity certification (à la Scrum) — preserved «soul» but smaller. **Lesson:** trade-off scale vs depth. Jetix possibly chooses depth (mastery) over commodification.

**Agile/Scrum — commoditization loss-of-soul.** «Reimagining Agile» movement signals practitioner frustration with certification economy + superficial adoption. **Direct lesson for FPF:** certification + scale comes with soul-loss risk. Pre-design «de-commodification» safeguards (FPF B.3 F-G-R = anti-grading-gaming?).

**TRIZ — Russian-roots + global divergence.** TRIZ split into Russian-school (more philosophical) + Western-applied (more contradiction-matrix-tooling). Lesson: bilingual methodology может divergence. **Direct lesson for Jetix:** Russian-content + English-primitives split (per CLAUDE.md §4.2 RUSLAN-LAYER) anticipates this — but watch for divergence between Russian L1 community + future English partner forks.

**TOC — book-as-distribution worked.** Goldratt's «The Goal» novel format = unusual distribution. **3M copies, 23 languages.** Lesson: **narrative + concrete case > abstract theory** for methodology spread. **Direct lesson for Jetix:** documentation alone insufficient — need narrative artifacts (vision/* set is start, but possibly need «The Goal»-style Jetix novel).

**ШСМ challenge — sole-author dependency.** Levenchuk is irreplaceable; if he stops, ШСМ uncertain. **Direct lesson for Jetix:** Ruslan-dependency is single point of failure; Foundation Architecture mitigates but doesn't eliminate. Corrigibility R12 design choices matter.

**INCOSE size + bureaucratic drag.** 21K members + 70 chapters = slow movement. Standards bodies struggle с innovation pace. Lesson: large bodies = stable but slow. Jetix Phase 0-3 = small + fast — converges later with INCOSE-like? Strategic question.
