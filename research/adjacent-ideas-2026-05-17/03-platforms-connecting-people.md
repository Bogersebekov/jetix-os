## §0 TL;DR (≤200 слов)

Поле «platforms connecting people» делится на 4 сегмента: **(1) Network-State experiments** (Balaji Srinivasan + Praxis ($525M raised, 151K «citizens») + Próspera (ZEDE legal vehicle, $11B legal battle)) — physical+digital sovereignty; **(2) Tools-for-thought networks** (Roam → Logseq/Obsidian migration, Are.na = «Internet memory palace», Karpathy LLM Wiki pattern); **(3) Creator economy** (Patreon $10B lifetime, $2B annual; Substack 5M paid subs, $450M run-rate; total creator economy $203.6B 2024 → $1.18T 2032 forecast); **(4) Decentralized protocols** (Mastodon 10M users by late 2024, ActivityPub federation; DAOs 13,000+ entities, $24.4B treasury, 3.9M token holders; Gitcoin DAO $45M grants distributed; Coordinape contributor tracking).

**Lessons:** Praxis raised $525M without delivering city — funding ≠ legitimacy. Mastodon grew 20× (500K→10M users 2022-2024) **на triggered exits** от incumbents (Twitter/X). Are.na — sustained ad-free мission community success. DAOs мать failure rate высокий (governance crisis pattern).

### §1.1 Network State experiments

Govt engagement: Singapore JTC, Dubai Expo City, ADGM, El Salvador, Palau, UK ex-PM Liz Truss. Balaji founded **Network School** retreat in Malaysia. [src: https://thenetworkstate.com/; https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balaji_Srinivasan, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Praxis (Dryden Brown + Charlie Callinan, 2019+)** — «Internet-native nation» + 10K population Mediterranean city plan. Pivoted to Greenland exploration 2024. **$525M raised (October 2024)** for crypto-friendly city. 151,068 «citizens» from 80 countries (Jan 2026 self-report). Member companies aggregate valuation $1.117B (self-reported, unverified independently). Praxis World Congress Nov 2024 in Dominican Republic. Howard Hughes Corp's David Weinreb = Vice Chair. **First Network State** per their positioning. [src: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Praxis_(proposed_city); https://www.coinspeaker.com/praxis-secures-525m-funding/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Próspera (Erick Brimen, 2017+)** — Charter city on Roatán, Honduras. **ZEDE (Zone for Employment and Economic Development) legal status** — autonomous fiscal/legal/regulatory framework. **$11B legal battle** Honduras Próspera Inc. (HPI Delaware) vs Honduran government since 2022 (status revocation). Tribunal allowed case to proceed Feb 2025. **Coinbase CEO Brian Armstrong** committed VC investment Jan 2025. Crypto Cities Summit Feb 2025. [src: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pr%C3%B3spera; https://moderndiplomacy.eu/2025/05/26/can-prospera-survive-lessons-from-the-gulf-and-kazakhstans-state-building-playbooks/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Dynamicland (Bret Victor, 2017+, Berkeley)** — physical-computing community. Not a "Network State" but adjacent: small geographically-rooted intelligence community. Realtalk = unique spatial computing system. Mission-driven not commerce-driven. [src: see Cluster 2]

### §1.2 Tools-for-thought networks

**Roam Research → Obsidian/Logseq migration** — Roam pioneered block-level bidirectional linking 2020. By 2024-2026: «The community that once drove Roam's growth has largely moved to Obsidian, Logseq, and other alternatives.» Pricing tension: Roam $15/mo vs Obsidian free ($4/mo sync) vs Logseq fully free + open-source. [src: https://www.atlasworkspace.ai/blog/roam-research-alternative; https://get-alfred.ai/blog/best-roam-research-alternatives, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Obsidian** — local-first, plugin-rich; document-first. Polish + customization. Used in Jetix CLAUDE.md "Wiki Architecture v2".
**Logseq** — outliner-first; block-level. Free, open-source, local-first.
**Roam Research** — original viral PKM; slowed development; mobile limited.

**Are.na (Charles Broskoski et al., 2014+)** — «playlists, but for ideas» / «Internet memory palace». Block-based; channels = mood boards / nascent concepts. **Member-supported community, ad-free, no likes/favorites/shares.** Used by Cory Arcangel, Margaret Lee, Mindy Seu, Martine Syms. Built as successor to Xanadu vision. [src: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Are.na; https://www.are.na/about, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Karpathy LLM Wiki (April 2026)** — covered in Cluster 2. Adjacent platform pattern.

### §1.3 Creator economy substrate

**Patreon (Jack Conte, 2013+)** — subscription patronage for creators. **$10B+ paid out lifetime, $2B+ annual processed, 25M+ paid memberships.** Predictable income alternative to ad revenue. [src: https://research.contrary.com/company/patreon; https://www.podcastvideos.com/articles/direct-monetization-patreon-substack-creator-income/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Substack (Chris Best + Hamish McKenzie + Jairaj Sethi, 2017+)** — paid newsletters + community. **5M paid subscriptions (March 2025), ~$450M run-rate gross writer revenue. $1.1B valuation Series C July 2025.** Growing into newsletter + video + podcast platform. [src: https://www.axios.com/media-trends-membership/2026/04/18/creator-tools-substack-patreon-beehiiv, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Beehiiv (Tyler Denk, 2021+)** — newsletter-first platform. Substack competitor.

**Total creator economy:** $203.6B (2024) → $1.18T forecast (2032), 24.6% CAGR. Direct fan-payment economy >$10B (Oct 2025).

**Mighty Networks (Gina Bianchini, 2017+)** — community-as-platform; courses + memberships + events.

**Indie Hackers (Courtland Allen, 2017+, acquired by Stripe 2019)** — community for bootstrapped/indie founders. Discord + forum + revenue transparency culture.

### §1.4 Decentralized protocols + DAOs

**Mastodon + ActivityPub (Eugen Rochko, 2016+)** — federated micro-blogging. **Growth 500K (early 2022) → 10M+ (late 2024) = 20×.** Driven by Twitter/X migration waves. Fediverse total active ≈1M MAU (Dec 2024) across platforms (Threads, Tumblr, Flipboard joining ActivityPub). [src: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mastodon_(social_network); https://www.webpronews.com/mastodon-surges-as-decentralized-alternative-to-x-doubles-users-by-2026/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**ActivityPub W3C standard (2018)** — federation protocol. Adopted by Mastodon, Pleroma, PeerTube, Pixelfed, BookWyrm. Threads (Meta) crosspost 2024 для non-EEA users. Tumblr + Flipboard committed.

**DAOs (general)** — 13,000+ entities, $24.4B combined treasury, 3.9M token holders (DeepDAO 2024). [src: see search]

**Gitcoin (Kevin Owocki, 2017+)** — quadratic funding for open-source projects. **$45M+ distributed in grants.** Quadratic funding = smaller contributions get amplified — addresses 1-token-1-vote critique. Owocki «still believes in DAOs» despite ecosystem fatigue. [src: https://www.dlnews.com/articles/defi/why-gitcoin-guru-kevin-owocki-still-believes-in-daos/, retrieved 2026-05-17]

**Coordinape** — DAO contributor tracking + peer-allocated rewards. Address: «who actually did the work?» problem in token-voting DAOs.

**SourceCred** — contribution graph for open-source / DAOs. Similar attestation problem.

**Friend.tech (2023)** — SocialFi experiment; trade «keys» to personal channels. **Major collapse 2024** — token crashed; lessons in financialized social. [Not detailed search but well-documented.]

**Scuttlebutt / Patchwork** — gossip-protocol social; offline-first; small but devoted community. Different from federation: peer-to-peer.

### §1.5 Discord/Slack tribes (informal substrate)

Discord (Jason Citron, 2015+) — gaming-rooted; now spans communities. >150M MAU (2024 estimate). Slack — enterprise-focused. Both informal substrate hosting Jetix-like communities (e.g., Network State Discords, mathlib Zulip).

## §4 Failure cases / lessons learned

**Praxis — $525M funding but no city.** Lesson: capital ≠ legitimacy. Network State theory легко raises money; hard part = sustained community + actual land + legal substrate. **Direct lesson:** Jetix Phase 3 Workshop MUST show real outcomes (curriculum delivered + revenue collected) before chasing Network State framing.

**Próspera $11B legal battle.** Lesson: charter city legal foundations fragile against political change (Honduras revoked ZEDE status). **Lesson for Jetix:** physical-sovereignty path requires multi-jurisdiction redundancy; legal substrate = single-point-of-failure risk.

**Roam Research → exodus.** Lesson: novel tools attract early adopters but lose them to better economics + faster iteration + open source. Roam $15/mo vs Logseq free + open-source = adverse selection of users. **Lesson:** Jetix tools must be free or value-anchored, not premium-locked.

**Mastodon growth pattern.** Lesson: federated alternatives grow on **triggered exits** from incumbents, not steady-state competition. Twitter/X migration waves (2022 Musk acquisition + 2024 algorithm controversies) drove Mastodon 20× growth. **Lesson:** Jetix может расти on triggered exits от existing methodology communities при upset events (ШСМ leadership change, INCOSE bureaucracy frustration).

**Friend.tech collapse 2024.** Lesson: financialized social = bubble + crash. Pure-monetization community substrate doesn't sustain. **Lesson:** Jetix не should attach token economics to community membership directly.

**DAO governance crises (Gitcoin, Maker, etc.).** Lesson: token voting + tooling underdeveloped = governance paralysis. Owocki's continued belief = rare. **Lesson:** Jetix Constitutional Default-Deny + Corrigibility ahead of comparable DAOs.

**Are.na sustained success pattern.** Lesson: ad-free + member-supported + sincere mission + slow growth = 10-year durable. Lesson for Jetix: «звезда» community might be small + persistent vs giant + churning.

**Substack/Patreon scale demonstrate market readiness.** Lesson: $200B+ creator economy = sufficient TAM. Even 0.001% market share = meaningful Jetix revenue substrate.
