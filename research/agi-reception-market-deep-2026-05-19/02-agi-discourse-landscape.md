# Phase 1 — AGI discourse landscape 2024-2026

> Mapping ландшафта **внешних actors** (Big Labs + critics + alt-frames). ВСЕ claims в этом файле = abstract или external-actor layer (Foundation-generic Layer 1) per §4 lens scope. Jetix-instance positioning будет в Phase 2-7. F-grade и retrieved_date per claim.

## §1 Big Lab AGI statements (≥5 deep mined)

### §1.1 OpenAI

**Verbatim claim («Planning for AGI and Beyond», Altman 2023-02-24, updated through 2024-2025):**
- AGI = «highly autonomous systems that outperform humans at most economically valuable work»
- Mission: «ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity»
- 5-level capability framework (leaked / discussed 2024-2025):
  - L1 Chatbots → L2 Reasoners → L3 Agents → L4 Innovators → L5 Organizations
  - Self-assessment mid-2025: «we are at L2; pushing into L3»

**Trajectory shift 2024-2026:**
- Altman 2025: «AGI is becoming a less useful term» с o-series — переход к «astonishing cognitive capabilities» как фрейм [src: windowscentral.com 2024/2025 + WebSearch 2026-05-19; F3]
- Sora video + GPT-4o multimodal + o1/o3 reasoning = модель «AGI = scaled multimodal reasoner»
- Stargate $500B infrastructure deal 2025 = capital-intensive scale-first thesis

**Framing positioning:**
- Capability-levels framework = реference baseline для всего AI discourse
- «AGI emerges from sufficient scale + reasoning + agents» (compute-substrate-first)
- НЕ collective-substrate framing; AGI = product attainable by single corp

**F-G-R:** F3 (multiple primary sources + verbatim); G-global; R-high (stable framing 2023→2025) [src: openai.com/blog/planning-for-agi-and-beyond + retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §1.2 Anthropic

**Verbatim claim («Core Views on AI Safety» 2023 + «Machines of Loving Grace» Amodei 2024-10):**
- Anthropic defines «powerful AI» as the future state (deliberately avoiding «AGI» term)
- «Machines of Loving Grace»: 50-page essay; «a country of geniuses in a datacenter»; timeline starts 2026
- 5 areas: biology+health, neuroscience+mental health, economic development, peace+governance, work+meaning
- «Constitutional AI» (Bai et al. 2022) → Claude's Constitution framing

**Trajectory shift 2024-2026:**
- Move from purely defensive «alignment lab» к ambitious upside narrative («the world we want»)
- Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP) = capability-thresholds gating model deployment
- 2025: Pillars include AI Safety Levels (ASL-2 → ASL-3 → ASL-4) [src: anthropic.com/responsible-scaling-policy retrieved 2026-05-19; F3]

**Framing positioning:**
- AGI = «powerful AI» — emerges from current trajectory (capabilities + alignment co-development)
- Safety-first; «pause if too dangerous» backstop
- НЕ collective-substrate framing; AGI = product co-evolved by labs + governance
- Distinct from OpenAI: упор на «alignment co-evolved with capability»; от DeepMind: less science-research framing

**F-G-R:** F3; G-global; R-high (Dario Amodei essays + RSP); [src: darioamodei.com/essay/machines-of-loving-grace 2024-10 retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §1.3 Google DeepMind

**Verbatim claim (Hassabis interviews + papers 2024-2025):**
- AGI = «system that can match humans at any cognitive task»
- Timeline: «5-10 years; 50% chance by 2030» (Hassabis 2025-03 CNBC)
- Two technical requirements: (1) world model — physics+space understanding; (2) automated experimentation — solving materials, fusion, science problems

**Trajectory 2024-2026:**
- Gemini 1.5/2.0 → Gemini 3 trajectory (multimodal + long-context)
- AlphaFold 3 (2024) — generalist biology
- Genie 3 — interactive video world models
- 2026 UK partnership: fully automated scientific laboratory [src: axios 2025/12; F3]
- «Transformative AGI on the horizon» Hassabis 2025-12

**Framing positioning:**
- AGI = **research-output-of-science** (DeepMind = science org first, product co)
- Closest to «AI for science» frame (Nobel-prize-worthy outputs)
- Hassabis: «AGI achieves Nobel-level science» as benchmark
- НЕ collective-substrate; AGI = single integrated cognitive system

**F-G-R:** F3; G-global; R-high; [src: deepmind.google blog + Hassabis CNBC 2025-03 retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §1.4 SSI (Sutskever)

**Verbatim claim (Sutskever 2024-06-19 launch post + ssi.inc landing page):**
- «Building safe superintelligence (SSI) is the most important technical problem of our time»
- «SSI is our mission, our name, and our entire product roadmap, because it is our sole focus»
- «We have started the world's first straight-shot SSI lab»
- «Safety and capabilities in tandem ... advance capabilities as fast as possible while making sure our safety always remains ahead»

**Trajectory:**
- $1B raised 2024-09 (SV Angel, DST, Sequoia, a16z)
- $30B valuation 2025-03 (Greenoaks-led)
- Zero public product roadmap; zero papers; zero interviews
- Pure positioning play: «trust us — we know what we're doing»

**Framing positioning:**
- Bypasses «AGI» — direct jump to «superintelligence»
- «Straight-shot» = single-product, single-mission monolith
- Maximum capability concentration thesis
- Contrarian to collective-substrate (single-team-can-do-this thesis)

**F-G-R:** F3 (verbatim primary); G-global; R-high (consistent positioning 2024-2025) [src: ssi.inc + Sutskever X post 2024-06-19; retrieved 2026-05-19]

## §2 Critics inventory (≥5 deep mined)

### §2.1 Andrew Ng

- «AI is the new electricity» (positive framing) BUT consistent: «no AGI in current trajectory»
- Position: focus on practical AI applications; AGI hype distracts
- 2024-2025: pushing «AI Fund» model — practical deployment over speculative AGI
- F-G-R: F3/global/high; [src: Ng public talks + DeepLearning.AI 2024-2025 retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §2.2 Yann LeCun (dual role — Big Lab + critic)

- «LLMs are off-ramp from AGI» — autoregressive prediction lacks world-model grounding
- JEPA + V-JEPA = predict-in-latent-space alternative
- «AGI» term = misleading; humans aren't general
- Anti-LLM-monoculture; criticizes safety-doom narratives
- F-G-R: F3/global/high; [src: LeCun X + JEPA papers retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §2.3 Marcus Hutter

- AIXI formal framework (2005) — mathematically defined AGI = max-reward agent в Solomonoff prior
- Position: current systems lack rigor; need formal theory not empiricism alone
- Hutter Prize / compression benchmark
- F-G-R: F3/academic/high; [src: AIXI papers + Hutter writings retrieved-stable]

### §2.4 Richard Sutton

- «Bitter Lesson» (2019) — scale + search + learning win over hand-coded knowledge
- 2024-2026: gravitating to reinforcement-learning-as-AGI (RL = general agent paradigm)
- John Hassabis-DeepMind connection (RL community)
- Endorsement of «agent intelligence emerges from interaction» — partial bridge to substrate framing
- F-G-R: F3/academic/high; [src: Sutton Bitter Lesson + RL course retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §2.5 Gary Marcus

- Persistent critic: «AGI is not imminent» — current systems = pattern matching, not reasoning
- Pushes hybrid neuro-symbolic; AGI requires causal+symbolic components
- 2024-2026: continued AGI skepticism columns
- F-G-R: F2-3/global/high (critic-of-hype); [src: Marcus Substack retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §2.6 François Chollet

- ARC-AGI benchmark — intelligence = skill-acquisition efficiency, not skill itself
- 2024 ARC-AGI prize $1M; o3 partial-solve 2024-12 = controversial
- «Intelligence ≠ capability»; current systems = good at memorized capability
- F-G-R: F3/global/high; [src: chollet papers + ARC-AGI 2024-2025 retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §2.7 Emily Bender + Timnit Gebru

- «Stochastic Parrots» (2021) — LLMs are mimics not understanders
- «AGI is a misnomer» — corporate marketing term, not technical category
- 2024-2026: continued criticism via DAIR Institute; emphasis on harms-now over speculative-AGI-risk
- F-G-R: F3/global/high (critical theory); [src: Bender X + DAIR 2024-2026 retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §2.8 Critics consolidated landscape

**Common patterns across critics (Phase 1 finding F2):**
- Ng/Marcus/Bender = «current systems are not on AGI path»
- LeCun/Hutter = «AGI needs different architecture»
- Sutton = «AGI emerges from scale + RL» (BORDERLINE — partial agreement w/ scale thesis)
- Chollet = «benchmark before you claim»

**Notable absence:** NO prominent critic claims «AGI must be collective system including humans». Closest: Sutton's «agent emerges from interaction» — but interaction = agent-environment, not agent-collective-of-agents-with-humans.

## §3 Alternative AGI frames (≥3 deep)

### §3.1 Wolfram «Computational Universe»

- Stephen Wolfram: «everything is computation»; intelligence = computational irreducibility navigated
- Wolfram Physics Project 2020+
- Position: AGI = computation-substrate-indifferent — no special substrate required
- НЕ collective framing; substrate-indifference IS the philosophical claim
- F-G-R: F3/global/medium-high; [src: writings.stephenwolfram.com 2024-2026 retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §3.2 Plurality (Tang + Weyl) ⭐ CLOSEST PARALLEL

- E. Glen Weyl + Audrey Tang + community (open-source book 2024)
- «⿻ Plurality: The Future of Collaborative Technology and Democracy»
- Core claim: «digital tools channel social diversity into progress — not replace humans/trust»
- Pluralistic intelligence emerges from bridging social difference
- 2025: «Prosocial Media» research (Weyl, Thorburn, Siddarth, Tang)
- Plural Technology Collaboratory (Microsoft Research) + Plurality Institute + RadicalxChange Foundation
- Vitalik Buterin / Jaron Lanier as adjacent allies
- F-G-R: F3/global/high (multiple primary sources + book + papers); [src: plurality.institute + Stanford Digital Economy Lab retrieved 2026-05-19]

**⭐ This is the closest contemporary parallel to Ruslan's «AGI = collective substrate» framing.** Key distinctions:
- Plurality emphasizes democratic governance + bridging difference
- Jetix voice anchor emphasizes «эффективный протокол для collective think+act»
- Overlap: collective intelligence framing; tools amplify human collective
- Distinction: Plurality = democracy-first; Jetix voice = effective-action-first (Workshop methodology, FPF rigor)

### §3.3 Sapienship (Harari)

- Yuval Noah Harari directional commentary (NOT a lab)
- Position: AI agents = new actors in info networks; humanity must consciously direct
- «21 Lessons» / «Nexus» 2024 — info-network frame
- Adjacent to collective framing: AI as participant in human info networks
- NOT product/lab; pure directional commentary
- F-G-R: F2/global/medium-high; [src: Harari Nexus 2024 + interviews retrieved 2026-05-19]

### §3.4 Stuart Russell «Human Compatible» / Provably Beneficial AI

- F-G-R: F3/academic/high; [src: Human Compatible 2019 + CHAI ongoing retrieved 2026-05-19]

## §4 Discourse landscape cross-cutting

**Convergence axes:**
1. Big Labs converge on capability-levels framework (L1-L5 OpenAI ⊃ ASL Anthropic ⊃ DeepMind science-AGI)
2. Critics converge on rigor demand («benchmark / formal / not hype»)
3. Alt-frames diverge widely — no consolidated alternative paradigm

**Divergence on substrate:**
- Compute-substrate (OpenAI, xAI, Microsoft) — capital + GPUs
- Architecture-substrate (LeCun, Meta) — world models
- Math-substrate (Hutter) — formal frameworks
- Social-substrate (Plurality, MIT CCI, Sapienship — closest to Jetix voice) — humans+AI+protocols

**White-space (preliminary, Phase 2 deep):**
- «Effective collective action protocol + workshop methodology + FPF rigor» — no prominent contemporary advocate combines all 3
- «Engineering culture × collective framing × non-crypto» — also thin
- «AGI as honest redefinition (system-level)» as positioning move — under-played in market
