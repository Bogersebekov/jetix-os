## §3 Counter-cases (R13 NOT-apply conditions)

R13 candidate text MUST include conditional applicability (per H-SD-22).

### §3.1 Counter-case 1 — Crisis innovation

- Manhattan Project (WWII; 1942-1945)
- Mulberry harbours (D-Day 1944)
- **Condition:** Existential time-pressure + cost-of-not-developing > cost-of-safety-failure

### §3.2 Counter-case 2 — Greenfield exploration

- Early antibiotic discovery (Fleming 1928)
- Early CRISPR research (2012-2018)
- **Condition:** Safety knowledge bootstrap problem — bounds cannot be specified ex ante

### §3.3 Counter-case 3 — Embedded competitive race

- AGI lab race (2022-2026)
- **Condition:** Multi-agent Nash equilibrium where individual safety-first loses race; coordinated regulation required

### §3.4 Conditional applicability for R13 rule text

R13 applies when:
- (a) Time available to bound safety
- (b) Safety knowledge sufficient ex ante
- (c) Single-agent decision context OR coordinated multi-agent context

R13 does NOT apply when conditions (a)-(c) jointly violated. In such cases: institutional escalation (Foundation Part 6b stage_gate) → Ruslan ack for develop-first deviation, with explicit safety-debt tracking + post-crisis remediation plan.
