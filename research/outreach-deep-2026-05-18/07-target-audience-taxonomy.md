### §2.1 Per-tier dispatch logic

**T1 Critical:**
- ALL responses → Ruslan personal action.
- Bottleneck = Ruslan attention budget (per Pillar C Tier 2 RUSLAN-LAYER «20 active tasks» rule).
- Mitigation: 10-team prepares decision-ready briefing (Ruslan reviews ≤30min per L1 target).

**T2 High:**
- 10-team handles dispatch + first-tier response triage.
- Positive responses escalate к Ruslan + 10-team mentor.
- Bottleneck = research + warm-link discovery capacity.

**T3 Medium:**
- 100-trained operators handle dispatch + first-tier response.
- Positive responses route к 10-team mentor + Tier 4 alumnus.
- Bottleneck = throughput (3000 personalised/month aggregate).

- L1 inner ring (Karpathy etc.): **5%+ response rate** within 12 months (per H-OUT-26).
