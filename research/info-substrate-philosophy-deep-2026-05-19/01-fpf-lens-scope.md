## §3 Scale-matching: substrate philosophy ↔ Jetix

**Substrate philosophy claim level:** Wheeler «It from Bit» = physics-of-reality (sub-quantum). Wolfram Computational Equivalence = computational metaphysics. Floridi PoI = ontology-of-information (general). Bateson Mind-as-system = biological-cybernetic ecology.

**Jetix substrate framing claim level:** social-information-system (people + ML/AI + tools + protocols at collective-action scale).

**Scale-match warning:** the 4 thinkers operate at **abstract / cosmological / ontological levels**; Jetix substrate operates at **social-organizational level**. Cross-application requires **explicit abstraction transfer** — NOT direct citation as if same level.

**Translation primitive:** all 4 thinkers' claims can be re-stated as `U.Episteme abstract pattern` (substrate-irrelevant invariant). Jetix application = RUSLAN-LAYER instance binding of that pattern at social-organizational scale.

**IP-1 enforcement:** any phase that cites Wheeler/Floridi/etc. as «proving Jetix substrate framing» violates IP-1 (conflates abstract claim with concrete instance). Correct framing: «Wheeler's claim ESTABLISHES that "information-as-fundamental" is philosophically defensible at physics level; Jetix application = analogous claim at social-organizational level (separate F-G-R per scale).»
