## §1 Twelve cross-cutting patterns

### Pattern 1 — Stacked epistemic architecture (Shannon floor → vF ceiling)

The 4 primary thinkers + 3 adjacents form a **stacked epistemic architecture**:

```
LAYER 4 (philosophy)    : Floridi PoI / ISR / hyperhistory
LAYER 3 (metaphysics)   : Wheeler info-primacy / Wolfram computational equiv
LAYER 2 (ecology)       : Bateson «difference makes difference» + 6 criteria
LAYER 1 (cybernetics)   : Wiener feedback + ethics + vF observer-recursion
LAYER 0 (quantification): Shannon entropy + channel capacity
```

Each layer presupposes lower layers. Jetix substrate framing can cite full stack OR cite selective layers per audience [src: Phases 1-5; especially Phase 5 §4.1].

### Pattern 2 — Wheeler «It from Bit» as physics-level anchor

Wheeler establishes info-primacy at **physics-of-reality level**; this is highest-prestige (quantum info physics community) but **most scale-mismatched** with Jetix (social-organizational level). IP-1 STRICT: cite as «existence proof» of info-primacy defensibility, NOT as direct evidence for Jetix-specific claims [src: Phase 1 §4].

### Pattern 3 — Information lineage has 70+ years of development

From Shannon 1948 → Wiener 1948 → Macy Conferences 1946-1953 → Bateson 1972 → Wheeler 1989 → Wolfram 2002 → Floridi 2011 → HLEG-AI 2019 → EU AI Act 2024. **«Information as fundamental» framing is NOT new fringe claim** — it is a 70+ year intellectual tradition with regulatory regime alignment. This addresses key investor risk («Jetix substrate philosophy is not just speculative pet theory») [src: Phases 1-6; especially Phase 5 §4.1 + Phase 6 §1.2].

### Pattern 4 — Strongest opposition = phenomenological embodiment

Phenomenology (Heidegger Gestell / Dreyfus embodied skill / Stiegler tertiary retention) provides **R-HIGH durable critique** that info-substrate framing reduces being to standing-reserve, eliminates embodied lived experience. **Cannot be fully dissolved**; Jetix positioning must explicitly preserve participant lived experience at RUSLAN-LAYER (substrate-description ≠ life-elimination). Mitigation language required в any external positioning [src: Phase 6 §3.5].

### Pattern 5 — Functionalism = unexpected ally with R12 tension
