> **IP-1 STRICT.** Each cycle = pattern instance bound к executor authority. Plan-mode surfaces; Ruslan acks; brigadier dispatches; cells execute within Phase 6 boundary catalog.

## §0 TL;DR (≤200w)

5 cycle types over 1 week (7 days) с Hansei rollup Day 7. Falsifiability predicate: refuted if **5 cycles fail to complete within 1 week** OR **IP-1 violation detected** OR **Hansei + Kaizen ritual unsustained**.

**Cycle types:**
1. **«5 H ack»** — Ruslan picks 5 hypotheses ML-bank for testing (Day 1)
2. **«3 packets ack»** — H6 / Pillar A Hackathon-Phase / H9 (Day 2)
3. **«First hackathon event spec»** (Day 3-4)
4. **«Sponsor outreach pilot»** — 1 sponsor approached (Day 5)
5. **«Hansei retrospective Week-1»** (Day 7)

**Per cycle:** Duration / Plan-mode output / Ruslan ack format / Execute-mode output / Reflection format / Falsifiability predicate.

**Total estimated cost:** <€3 (well within €5 halt threshold)
**Total estimated time:** 100-150 min brigadier orchestration + Ruslan ack time (varies)
