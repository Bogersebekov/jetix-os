## §21 Saturation discipline (operational rules)

### §21.1 Saturation rate per tier

- **T1 high-leverage 1-on-1** (Templates 13, 15, 17, 19): 1-2 simultaneous patient cultivations
- **T2 medium-batch personalised** (Templates 8-11, 14, 16, 18, 20): 5-10 outreaches / week
- **T3 template-batch with touches** (Templates 1-7, 12): 20-50 outreaches / week (per Ruslan «каждому запихаю»)

### §21.2 Cadence rules

- T1: initial outreach + 6-month wait + substrate-progress update + multi-touch over 12-24 months
- T2: initial outreach + 2-4 week response window + follow-up + 2-month decision point
- T3: initial outreach + 1-2 week response window + brief follow-up + move-on

### §21.3 Engagement-quality metric (100h floor)

- Per Master Picture refinement; Ruslan-acked
- 100 hours of accumulated engagement = floor for «meaningful collaboration» claim
- Per partner: multi-touchpoint depth (text → call → meeting → collaboration)
- F-G-R verifiable claims tracked (no inflated claims)
- Mutual-investment evidence (both parties commit substrate)

### §21.4 Anti-burnout discipline (own + cohort)

- Outreach load capped: Ruslan ≤20 active outreaches at any time; cohort substrate amplifies
- Quality screening at intake (avoid extractive partners)
- Cohort substrate distributes outreach load (multi-Connector substrate)
- 100h floor prevents claim-inflation
