## §1 Super-class A — Tangible

### R1 — Money / Capital

**Definition.** Financial capital — investment, grant, donation, sponsorship, revenue.

**Sub-types:**
- Pre-seed / seed / Series A+ equity
- Convertible / SAFE notes
- Revenue (sponsorship + fees + services)
- Grants (governmental / foundational)
- Donations (individual / community)
- Crypto-treasury (tokens, stablecoins)
- Sponsorship-in-kind (cloud credits)
- Patreon / membership ongoing

**Typical providers** (Phase 1 cross-ref): Cat 8 (Investors), Cat 16 (Corporate), Cat 17 (Governments), Cat 19 (Foundations), Cat 15 (Audience donations), Cat 11 (Communicators sponsorship).

**Jetix use-cases.** Operating capital; hackathon prize pools; Workshop infrastructure; Foundation endowment seeding; mentor honorariums; documentary production budget.

**Acquisition mechanism.** Fundraising (formal deck + roadshow); grant applications; sponsorship outreach; revenue (Phase 7); crypto fundraising (Phase 7 token-based variant).

**Value metric.** € amount; runway extension (months); reach multiplication (€ × Phase 1 category × Tier).

**R12 alignment check.** Acceptance per Mondragón ratio cap + fork-and-leave preservation + no value capture beyond agreed share. Extractive terms (cap table > 80% / control / R12-prohibited covenants) = DECLINE.

**Tier:** T1 (operating need immediate).
