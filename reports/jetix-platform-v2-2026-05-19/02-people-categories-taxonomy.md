## §1 Super-cluster I — Builders & Specialists

### Category 1 — Domain Experts / Specialists

**Definition.** Individuals with deep technical / domain expertise — engineers, researchers, scientists, designers, architects, biologists, physicists, economists, neuroscientists. Defined by domain mastery (FPF U.SkillSet over A.15 Work).

**Sub-roles / examples:**
- ML/AI engineers (LLM ops, alignment, agents, distributed training) [src: `research/ml-ai-engineers-2026-05-18/` 45-H bank]
- Software engineers (backend / frontend / infra / mobile)
- Hardware engineers (chip / robotics / device design)
- Research scientists (academic + industrial labs)
- UX/UI designers (interaction + visual)
- Architects (system / software / org)
- Data scientists / statisticians
- Domain scientists (biology, physics, chemistry, neuroscience)

**Typical contribution.** Direct project execution; substantive prototypes; technical de-risking; peer credibility.

**Recruiting mechanism.** Hackathon Tier 1-3 entry → mentor pipeline; cohort referral (engineer → engineer); ML/AI Workshop substrate [src: 5 deep research outputs 18-19.05].

**Activation pattern.** Hackathon participant → Workshop graduate → cohort retainer / repeat contributor.

**Q3 2026 priority:** **T1** — ML/AI subset = immediate Workshop substrate.
