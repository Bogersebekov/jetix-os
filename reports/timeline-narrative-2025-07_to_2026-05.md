2. **«Обезьяна» metaphor.** Recurring concept (Oct-Nov 2025 dominant). Дрессировка impulses = consistent challenge.
3. **Reading drives shifts.** Sapiens (Dec 2025) → values discovery. Homo Deus (Jan 2026) → ИИ discovery (Feb 2026). Books = catalysts.
4. **Effectiveness scoring** — typically 1-3 для weeks of struggle, 3-4 для productive. Few 5s. Honest self-rating.
