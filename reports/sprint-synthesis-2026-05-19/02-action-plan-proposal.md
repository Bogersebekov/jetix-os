## §9 Risks + mitigations (top 7)

- **Mitigation.** Design quality metric (e.g. «active contributions per 100h» or «cohort retention 3-month»); embed in pitch deck financials slide

### Risk 2 — L1→L2 conversion 10% assumption fails
- **Risk.** Cascade 150→15 assumes 10%; reality may be 2-5% (cold outreach baseline)
- **Mitigation.** Fallback plan: scale base outreach to 500-1000 to maintain 15+ amplifiers absolute; track conversion empirically; iterate pitch
- **Owner.** Step 5+6 operational; weekly conversion track

### Risk 3 — Engineer cohort chained dependency cascade failure
- **Risk.** BL-1 unblock fails → Q3 2026 hackathon delayed → Master Workshop delayed → all Q3-Q4 activations slip
- **Mitigation.** Parallelize: engineer cohort search (Step 6) parallel с monetization (Step 1) + pitch (Step 2); 3-track cohort sourcing (academic / open-source / Karpathy lineage / RU L2 community)
- **Owner.** Step 6 ownership с brigadier-assisted сurriculum

### Risk 4 — «All-in» (audio_686) vs R12 voluntary clauses tension
- **Risk.** «All-in» framing perceived as coercive / locks members in → R12 violation; voluntary clauses required at Charter level
- **Mitigation.** P1-9 §APPEND-2026-05-19 к 5 concept docs adding voluntary + fork-and-leave clauses BEFORE outreach scaling
- **Owner.** Brigadier authoring; Ruslan acks

### Risk 5 — «1000-year» humility reframe для external
- **Risk.** «Самый масштабный за 1000 лет» (audio_686) external perception = hubris / cult red flag
- **Mitigation.** Vision narrative C.4 frame как Responsibility-Era humanity-period (NOT personal achievement); pitch deck slide explicit «we are vehicle, not protagonist»
- **Owner.** Ruslan narrative + brigadier philosophy expert assist

### Risk 6 — Owner attention budget overflow
- **Risk.** Current 12 active items; cap 20 (Pillar C §4.2); если P1-1..P1-12 + P2-1..P2-10 all started = 22+
- **Mitigation.** Strict P1-only this week (steps 0-6 critical path); P2 next week; defer non-critical
