## §2 Per-touch time budget per tier

| Tier | Avg time per touch | Touch type composition |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier-1 partner** | 20-30 min per touch | Pre-touch research + custom-pitch drafting + send + post-touch logging |
| **L1 engineer** | 10-15 min per touch | Lighter research + template instance + send |
| **L2 amplifier** | 5-8 min per touch | Template + light personalization + send |

**Time math (daily 12-touch median):**
- 2 Tier-1 × 25 min = 50 min
- 3 L1 × 12 min = 36 min
- 7 L2 × 6 min = 42 min
- **Total = ~2h 8min outreach time**

Plus daily overhead:
- Email / response triage 30 min
- CRM logging / status updates 20 min
- Reflection / pivot decisions 10 min

**Daily outreach budget ≈ 3 hours.** Remainder of day = substrate work / Workshop / strategic prose / research.

## §4 Pillar C max 20 attention budget compliance

Per Pillar C Tier 2 RUSLAN-LAYER: Manager attention budget max 20 active tasks.

**Outreach pipeline accounting:**
- Active outreach contacts (status: contacted / warm / discovery_call) — count each as 1 active task
- Cap: ≤10 outreach active tasks at any time (50% of total budget)
- Remainder of 20-budget = research / drafting / Workshop / Foundation / partner conversations active

**Enforcement:** weekly `/lint --check-attention-budget` (assumed extension) OR manual review during Friday reflection.

**Overflow protocol:**
- If outreach active > 10 → pause new outreach initiations until pipeline movement reduces count
- Priority maintained: existing relationships > new cold outreach

## §5 Anti-burnout discipline

Per audio_681 «срочность колоссальна» voice anchor BUT acknowledged burnout risk (Risk R-2 Phase 8).

### §5.1 Burnout signals (monitor weekly)

- Touches/day declining: <10 average over 3+ days consecutive
- Response rate stagnant: no responses for 5+ days
- Quality slipping: shortened personalization / templated feel increasing
- Sleep quality / energy declining (Ruslan self-assessment)
- Skip days unplanned: ≥2 days outreach skipped в week

### §5.2 Burnout mitigation triggers

If 2+ signals present за week:
1. Halt new outreach initiations (existing follow-ups continue)
2. Reduce cadence target to 5-8 touches/day для recovery week
3. Reflect: cause = volume / quality demand / external pressure / substrate fatigue?
4. Adjust: redistribute to lighter L2 batch OR pause to substrate work
5. Resume regular cadence after 1-2 recovery weeks

### §5.3 Cohort accountability (Q3 2026+)

When Workshop founding cohort 5-15 established:
- Co-cohort accountability (peer check-in)
- Distribute outreach load across cohort (each amplifier contributes)
- Ruslan attention budget освобождается для higher-leverage activities
