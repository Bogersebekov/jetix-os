| # | Level | EQF | Verbatim defining |

## §3 Stage 0 — Личное развитие (R0)

### §3.1 What

**Course:** Рациональная работа (also Собранность); Aisystant: Личное развитие
**Duration:** 6 weeks (group format) OR self-paced ~21 sessions (per ailev/1777145 Autumn 2025 announcement: Oct 4-Dec 13)
**Format:** Group sessions Tue 18:00 + Sat 11:30 (Autumn 2025 cohort exact schedule)

### §3.2 Prerequisites

- ✅ Secondary education complete
- ✅ Active work project (any role)
- ❌ NO prior technical / systems background required

### §3.3 Cost

- **Self-study subscription:** 1,000 руб./мес. × 1.5 мес. = **1,500 руб. (≈ €15)**
- **Group format:** Pricing для R0 explicitly not surfaced на 2026-05-23 fetch; inferred ≈60,000 руб. per group residency pattern OR 30,000 руб. для shorter R0 (TBD via МИМ inquiry)
- **Books needed:** Левенчуковский materials free + Визуальное мышление 2018 (≈500 руб.)

### §3.4 5+ concrete sub-steps

1. **Регистрация Aisystant** — aisystant.system-school.ru sign-up (free baseline)
2. **Прохождение online guide** «Рациональная работа» — text + exercises (4-6 weeks self-paced)
3. **Subscription paid tier** (1000 руб./мес.) — unlocks homework + tracking
4. **(если group)** Регистрация cohort via Telegram bot @SystemsSchool_bot — кsipi.next_cohort_date
5. **Weekly group sessions** 2 раз/неделю (Tue + Sat); mentor-led разборы
6. **Working real project** в parallel — НЕ homework; reality-grounded application
7. **Closure:** complete всех exercises + Aisystant test/checklist; **NO formal certification at L1 level** (qualification = L1 Ученик через ongoing practice, не gated test)

### §3.5 Output

- **Skill outcome:** Daily attention management; rational work habits; ~10h/week self-development practice habit installed
- **Qualification eligibility:** L1 Ученик (per qualification page «daily self-development practice, 10+ часов weekly, maintains exocortex»)
- **Artefact:** Aisystant completion record + first real project documented с rational-work patterns applied

### §3.6 Decision point

**Continue к R1 Рабочее развитие? OR stay в Личное развитие (v4 — separate track)?**
- IF goal = Master qualification → continue R1
- IF goal = personal-only mastery → stay в Личное развитие track + Интеллект-стек Research
- Path bifurcation key

### §4.5 Output

## §14 Multi-angle robustness (per §11.0 mandate)

### §14.1 Student perspective

- **Pros:** Accessible (open R1 admission), affordable (€6K total for Masters cert vs MBA €50K+), real-world grounded, cohort network, AI-augmented (IWE)
- **Cons:** Russian-language primary, time commitment heavy (60+ weeks), Moscow-centric (despite remote option), faces 3-year expiration

- **Pros:** Sustainable methodology council (Methsovet) keeps curriculum fresh, Master mentors well-compensated (60K rentier per cohort × multiple cohorts/year), respectful institutional culture
- **Cons:** Continuous guide rewriting load heavy, individual mentor capacity bottleneck
