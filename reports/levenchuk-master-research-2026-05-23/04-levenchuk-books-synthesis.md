## §2 BOOK 1 + 2 — Системное мышление 2024 (Том 1 + Том 2)

### §2.1 Bibliographic

- **Том 1:** 412 pp, ISBN 978-5-0064-2853-9
- **Том 2:** 488 pp, ISBN 978-5-0064-2855-3
- **Total:** 900 pp двухтомник

- **Publisher:** Ridero
- **Aisystant course:** Системное мышление
- **R# Residency:** R5 (Системное мышление) + R6 (Системное моделирование) — split across 2 residencies

### §2.2 Thesis (Том 1 — Foundations)

**Centre thesis:** Системное мышление = transdiscipline для attention management в complex collective projects. **NOT recipe, а scaffolding для thinking.** Shared ontological vocabulary для describing systems across all reality levels (physical / biological / social / engineering) с same primitives.

**Three generations decomposition:**

| Generation | Period | Authority figures | Key contribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1st gen | 1950s-1970s | Bertalanffy GST, Wiener cybernetics, Koestler holons | Emergent properties, system-in-environment |
| 2nd gen | 1980s-2010s | Checkland SSM, ISO 15288/42010, SEMAT Essence (Jacobson) | Lifecycle, soft systems, situational method engineering |
| 3rd gen | 2010s-present | Friston Active Inference, Wolfram physics, open-ended evolution | Physics-based ontology, mereotopology, continuous everything |

**Foundation Системного мышления 2024 = 3rd generation.** Supersedes Bertalanffy + Checkland sufficiency claims.

### §2.3 Том 1 structure (foundations)

- **Часть 1.** Системный подход — что это + три поколения decomposition
- **Часть 2.** Holons + part-whole nesting (Koestler refresh)
- **Часть 3.** Emergent properties — formalisation
- **Часть 4.** System-in-environment — ontology
- **Часть 5.** Three generations critique + 3rd-gen physics grounding
- **Часть 6.** Граф создания (Creation Graph) — directed graph of creating-systems / target-systems / enabling-systems
- **Часть 7.** Mereotopology grounding (part of в category theory framing)

### §2.4 Том 2 structure (applied)

- **Часть 1.** Constructor systems — рекурсивная application
- **Часть 2.** Техно-эволюция — open-ended evolution + NQD (novelty-quality-diversity)
- **Часть 3.** Continuous everything (CD/CI scaled to system development)
- **Часть 4.** Mereotopological ontology + 4D extensionalism (ISO 15926 alignment)
- **Часть 5.** Active Inference + Free Energy Principle (Friston grounding)
- **Часть 6.** Practical applications в инженерных проектах
- **Часть 7.** Synthesis + bridges к Methodology + Engineering volumes

### §2.5 Unique contribution

1. **3rd generation formalisation** — single coherent statement of post-Bertalanffy / post-Checkland systems thinking
2. **Граф создания** vs hierarchical decomposition — recognises ecosystem of enabling systems вместо tree
3. **Past-participle convention** — machine-readable state descriptions for AI-agent consumption
4. **Physics-based ontology** — отказ от purely conceptual modelling
5. **Continuous everything paradigm** — design-time / run-time distinction enforcement (per FPF B.1.5-B.1.6)

### §2.6 Cross-cite Master Quals
