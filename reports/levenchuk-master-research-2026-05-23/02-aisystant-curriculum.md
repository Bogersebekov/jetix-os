### §9.2 Time + cost calculation для full Master path

**Self-study path (cheapest, no mentor):**
- Aisystant subscription: 1000 руб./мес. × 36 мес. (3 года) = 36,000 руб.
- Books: 9 × 1000 руб. = 9,000 руб.
- **Total: ~45,000 руб. ≈ €450**
- **Time: 36 months × 60h/month = 2,160 hours**
- **Quality: Aspirantship NOT certified без mentor + cohort presence**

**Group format path (recommended, certified):**
- 10 residencies × 60,000 руб. = **600,000 руб. ≈ €6,000-7,000**
- Books: 9 × 1000 руб. = 9,000 руб.
- **Total: ~609,000 руб. ≈ €6,000-7,000**
- **Time: 60 weeks × 10-15h/wk = 600-900 hours over ~14 months minimum**
- **Quality: Cohort + mentor + assignment feedback + certification eligible**

**Hybrid project-based:**
- 1 individual mentor project: до 200,000 руб. = **€2,000**
- Plus subscription + books = ~245,000 руб. ≈ **€2,500**
- Time: variable, project-driven

**Corporate (legal entity):** +15% markup → e.g., 690,000 руб. для group full path.

### §9.3 Critical observations для Phase 6

1. **R1-R10 = NOT just 10 sequential courses** — это **structured residency curriculum** с peer-cohort dynamics + mentor expertise + real-work integration
2. **Group format vs self-study huge quality delta** — certification requires cohort/mentor presence
3. **3-year qualification expiration** = unusual feature; normalises continual learning vs academic «one-and-done» degree
4. **Open admission R1** — anyone with secondary education + active work project; **non-elitist** vs academic gatekeeping
5. **Pricing accessible** — 60K руб. ≈ €600/residency = ниже MBA program courses (~€1500-3000/course typical)
6. **Total Master path cost €6-7K, time 14+ months** = comparable к specialized professional certification (PMP, Scrum, etc.) но deeper
