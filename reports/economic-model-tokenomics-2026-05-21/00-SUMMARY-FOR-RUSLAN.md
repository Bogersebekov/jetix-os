**Твой voice 21.05 night распакован полностью:**

- L1 каждый partner 25% → Jetix institutional
- L2 25% от total system → Jetix управленцы (subset of L1)
- L3 25% от L2 → Ruslan лично (recursive re-entry as partner)

**Triple-role partner:** каждый partner = worker (75% revenue) + investor (25% institutional stake) + promoter (10% × R_p,referred bonus 12 months) **в одном лице**. Beyond-Mondragón innovation (Mondragón 2-role; Jetix 3-role).

**Рекомендованный token form: V10 Hybrid**

Состав V10:
- ERC-1155 triple-role NFT bundle (worker + investor + promoter)
- ERC-5114 soulbound governance SBT (1-identity = 1-vote)
- Moloch DAO RageQuit (fork-and-leave)
- Mondragón 5:1 ratio cap on-chain
- Mondragón 60/40 internal routing
- Gitcoin QF matching pool quarterly
- ERC-20 utility token
- ERC-4337 account abstraction (UX)

**R12 conformance:** ✅✅✅ Strongest (triple-enforced — ratio cap + RageQuit + soulbound + QF). Каждое из 4 R12 action classes покрыто programmatic enforcement + Charter explicit.

**Audit budget:** $75-150K Phase 1 (multi-audit OpenZeppelin + Trail of Bits). Phased deployment Y1/Y2/Y3.

**Bridge funding Y1-4:** ~€245K (per DR-26 Scenario B 22.5% take rate).

**Self-sustaining timeline:** Break-even Y5 Scenario B; critical mass ~100 paying partners; K ≥ 1.0 exponential Y4.

**Fallbacks:** V5 Mondragón-style (если V10 audit budget слишком high); V6 Triple-role NFT (если V10 сложность overwhelming).
