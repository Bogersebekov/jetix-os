- For anyone seeking to regain ability to develop complex skills, gain attention control, organize life

## §4 Format mechanics

### §4.1 «Разборы» = mentor-led project review

**Per LJ 1777145 verbatim characterization:**
> «mentor-led "разборы" (review sessions) of participants' actual work projects rather than traditional lectures. Session duration adjusts based on cohort size "to allow sufficient discussion time for each participant's projects"»

**Critical principle.** NOT lectures. Participants bring their actual work projects; mentor + cohort review them through ШСМ/FPF lens. Application of frameworks к real personal/professional projects.

### §4.2 Online guides + AI guide (IWE)

**Per R-A §5.1:**
> «Онлайн-руководства (online guides): All textbook content freely accessible post-registration at aisystant.system-school.ru»
>
> «IWE (Intelligent Workflow Engine): AI personal guide that tracks learner progress, answers questions, provides route recommendations — the practical implementation of the exocortex concept within the school»

**Compositional setup:**
1. Read online guide / book chapter (asynchronous)
2. IWE clarifies on demand (asynchronous)
3. Weekly «разбор» with mentor + cohort (synchronous)
4. Apply к own project between sessions
5. Bring updated project к next разбор
