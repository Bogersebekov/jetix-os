### §1.3 Why these are «трансдисциплины» (transdisciplines)

Per R-A §4.1:
> «**Transdiscipline** — discipline applicable across all domains (e.g., logic, ontology, systems thinking).»

Per Левенчуковский framing (translated paraphrase from system-school.ru/stack):
> «Transdisciplines trace connections between scientific-technical and humanities knowledge.»

**Critical claim** (R-A §4.1 L237):
> «**Intellect Stack = explicit hierarchical layering.** Replaces STEM as the standard for 21st-century professional capability.»

### §1.4 Why 16 transdisciplines vs FPF's 5 cognitive layers

**Stack-size mismatch is NOT a contradiction** `[A-fpf-spec-5-primitives.md §3.3 last paragraph]`:
- **ШСМ 16 = discipline-names** (taxonomy of skills humans/agents develop)
- **FPF 5 = cognitive layers** (architectural levels of cognitive operations)

Different granularity, different purpose. Coexistent.

## §3 Per-discipline brief (top 6 — those most-cited в R-A + corpus)

### §3.1 Системное мышление (Systems Thinking)

**Definition (R-A §4.1 L227):**
> «A transdiscipline for attention management in complex collective projects. Provides a shared ontological vocabulary for describing systems across all levels of reality (physical, biological, social, engineering) using the same primitives. "Not a recipe but a scaffolding for thinking."»

**Note.** «Системное мышление» is FOUNDATION cross-layer practice; it's not a single layer item, but the «glue» between L1-L4.

### §3.2 Методология (Methodology)

**Per LJ 2024 post 1725757 (verbatim):**
> «Methodology connects to:
> - Semantics: theory of representations (local + distributed)
> - Ontology: abstraction levels + important objects
> - Rationality: knowledge in decision-making theories for selecting method alternatives
> - Ethics + Rhetoric: constraining choices + persuading other agents»

`[02-livejournal/key-posts-captured-2026-05-17.md §Post 5]`

**Three approaches Левенчук distinguishes (verbatim same post):**
1. **Applied Methodology** (domain knowledge) — «in concrete situation with concrete domain objects—take theories/knowledge/explanations/algorithms of domain, and act»
2. **Fundamental Methodology** (system thinking) — when domain unknown: «we select important objects from general cultural, fundamental knowledge…determine what systems exist, what methods they use, what roles they play»
3. **Distributed Representations** (AI/Neural Networks) — «neuronal networks can perform pattern detection both of method subjects and patterns of effective actions with them»

### §3.3 Онтология (Ontology)

Per R-A §3.1: Левенчук's ontological engineering background (.15926 Editor, ArchiMate Russian terminology). Per psybertron review:
> «Definition: as a Coffin for a Dead Think» — anti-essentialism. «Meanings of terms are determined statistically, not precisely» through «diagrams, texts, and sets of expanded statements containing the term of interest»

`[02-livejournal/key-posts-captured-2026-05-17.md §Post 6]`

**FPF encoding:** Notational Independence E.5.2 (no OWL/JSON-LD/category-theory dogma) + multiple notations per pattern.

### §3.4 Рациональность (Rationality)

**Per LJ 2024:** part of «decision-making theories for selecting method alternatives». Per CLAUDE.md, Левенчуковский «Рациональность» splits в:
- Epistemic Рациональность → philosophy-expert lens (in our system)
- Instrumental Рациональность → investor-expert (capital allocation as instrumental rationality)

### §3.5 Собранность (Collectedness)

**Per LJ 2025-09 Autumn residency:** R0 «Рациональная работа / Собранность» — entry-level discipline. **Practice-oriented** — attention management, self-organization, lean working practices, anti-procrastination.

**Per 10th MIM conf 2026 Section 4:** «Pyotr Leontiev: System fitness work in 2025-2026» — «system fitness» = practice (NOT a book) under Personal Development track integrating Собранность.

### §3.6 Инженерия (Engineering — applied transdiscipline)

Per R-A §4.1: Engineering = cross-layer applied. ШСМ defines «инженер-менеджер» (engineer-manager) as their target practitioner identity: «someone who does both systems engineering and systems management» `[R-A glossary]`.
