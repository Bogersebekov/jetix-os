### §1.2 Structure — Episteme Slot Graph

**Spec L581 (verbatim):**
> «In FPF, an episteme is not a static node. It is a structured **Episteme Slot Graph** (`U.EpistemeSlotGraph`, C.2.1). It has explicit slots for what it describes (`DescribedEntity`), where it is grounded (`GroundingHolon`), and through which lens it is seen (`Viewpoint`). This moves us beyond the naive "map vs territory" debate into a disciplined treatment of epistemic morphisms.»

**Four slots:**
1. **DescribedEntity** — object-of-talk (what is being described)
2. **GroundingHolon** — physical/conceptual anchor (where claims grounded)
3. **ClaimGraph** — actual claims (the content)
4. **Viewpoint** — lens / audience / purpose

### §1.3 Why epistemes matter

**Spec L588 — disciplined audit-trail (verbatim):**
> «A "Safety Case" and a "System Architecture" are not competing documents; they are two valid views of the same holon, rendered under different viewpoints and onto different surfaces. When a manager looks at a red/green dashboard, they are looking at a `U.View` (an `U.EpistemeView`), mathematically derived from underlying Work and EvidenceGraph lanes via a declared `U.Viewpoint` and `PublicationScope`. As long as that correspondence is maintained, the report cannot drift away from the reality it summarises without tearing the audit trail.»

### §1.4 Episteme morphisms (A.6.2 / A.6.3 / A.6.4)

**A.6.2 EffectFreeEpistemicMorphing** — `U.Episteme → U.Episteme` без adding claims (e.g. reformat, retextualize)

**A.6.3 EpistemicViewing** — describedEntity-preserving morphism (e.g. ISO 42010 viewpoint application)

**A.6.4 EpistemicRetargeting** — describedEntity-shift (e.g. Fourier transform — different DescribedEntity but related)

### §1.5 Why это intelligence-amplification primitive

Без `U.Episteme` discipline: «one document = one truth», но реальность multi-view → drift. С `U.Episteme`: каждый view = explicit projection через viewpoint slot; rendered surface (Plain / Tech / Interop / Assurance per E.17 MVPK) preserves underlying ClaimGraph integrity.

## §3 U.Episteme ↔ IWE связь

### §3.1 IWE как U.Episteme-aware system

IWE — это AI assistant operating над corpus of FPF-Spec + ШСМ textbooks + learner project artefacts. Каждое его recommendation = U.EpistemeView (rendered surface) над underlying ClaimGraph (FPF normative patterns) с Viewpoint = learner role + current method.

**Critical link.** Per Левенчуковский TG C5: IWE «опирается на тот же FPF» = IWE's reasoning is constrained by FPF's bounded contexts, role/method-work distinction, F-G-R assurance, abductive loop, multi-view publication. Это и есть «за счёт чего его IWE умнее конкурентов» — потому что vanilla AI assistants drift across context, conflate roles with executors, output unstructured opinion без assurance trail.

### §3.2 vs vanilla AI assistants (без FPF)

**Vanilla AI:**
- No bounded context discipline → answers mix product/safety/operations vocabulary
- No role-method-work separation → recommendations не attached к specific role's method
- No F-G-R → all outputs look equally confident
- No abductive loop → converges on first plausible answer
- No multi-view → one document per request

**FPF-loaded AI (IWE):**
- Bounded contexts declared explicitly
- Role-method-signature per recommendation
- F-G-R trust tags
- Abductive loop generates alternatives
- Multi-view publication (Plain / Tech / Assurance faces)

**This is the C4 benchmark from Левенчук TG:**
> «FPF проверяется очень просто: грузите FPF AI-агенту и спрашиваете про ваш проект, или спрашиваете про ваш проект прямо у AI-агента без FPF. Разница видна за пять минут сравнений.»
