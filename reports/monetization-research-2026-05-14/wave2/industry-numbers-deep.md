## §1 Patreon (Creator subscription)

- **MAU:** ~250K active creators (2024 estimate; «active» = receiving payments); ~8M patrons globally
- **ARPU:** Median creator earns $50-100/mo; mean ~$300/mo (skewed by top 1%); top 1% creator earns $5K-$50K/mo
- **LTV (creator):** Variable; top creators 3-5 years; long-tail 6-18 months
- **CAC:** Patreon spends ~$20-50 per new creator acquisition (estimate; not publicly disclosed)
- **Take rate:** 5% (Lite tier) / 8% (Pro tier) / 12% (Premium tier) + payment processing 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction; effective ~10-15%
- **Churn:** Top creators ~5%/y; mid-tier ~15-25%/y; bottom-tier 60%+/y
- **Cooperation indicators:** Low — mostly transactional creator-patron; some creator collaboration off-platform
- **R12 posture:** Pass with caveat — take rate clear; data export available; fork-and-leave preserves audience email (Substack-style)
- **GMV:** $300M+ paid to creators 2023 (Patreon press)

**Source:** Patreon 2023 annual creator earnings report; SaaStr industry estimates 2024.

## §2 Substack (Creator newsletter)

- **MAU:** 2M+ paid subscribers globally (2024 estimate); 17M+ free subscribers
- **ARPU:** Average subscription $5-10/mo; top tier $50+/mo
- **LTV:** Subscriber LTV ~12-24 months; longer for top creators
- **CAC:** Substack platform: low CAC ($5-15 per paid subscriber via creator audience funnels)
- **Take rate:** 10% of paid subscriptions + Stripe processing
- **Churn:** Paid subscriber ~5-10%/mo; reduced with annual plans
- **Cooperation indicators:** Medium — Notes feature 2023+ + cross-Substack discovery; creator collaboration emerging
- **R12 posture:** Pass with caveat — clear take rate; data export available; fork-and-leave preserves email list; some lock-in via Stripe data
- **GMV:** $1B+ run rate 2024 estimate

**Source:** Substack annual reports; Axios + Stratechery analysis.

## §3 Twitch (Streamer platform)

- **MAU:** 140M monthly viewers 2023; ~10M streamers (~80% inactive)
- **ARPU streamer:** Top 1% $50K-$500K+/mo; mid-tier $500-$5K/mo; bottom-tier $0-50/mo
- **LTV streamer:** Top streamers 5-10+ years; long-tail 6-18 months
- **CAC streamer:** Twitch network effect drives organic; promotional partnerships
- **Take rate:** 50/50 sub split standard (controversial; previously 70/30 favorable to creators; «Plus Program» 70/30 for high-tier 2022-2024)
- **Churn:** Viewer subscriber 30-40%/mo (high); streamer churn 60-80%/y for bottom 90%
- **Cooperation indicators:** Medium — raid culture + co-streams; community-building strong
- **R12 posture:** Concern — Amazon takes 50% sub split (extraction-end); platform algorithm controls visibility; switching cost high (audience locked to Twitch); 2022 controversy of payout reductions hostile to top streamers
- **GMV:** $2B+ revenue 2023 (Amazon segment)

**Source:** Twitch creator tools; Bloomberg + Variety industry analysis.

## §4 OnlyFans (Creator subscription)

- **MAU:** 250M+ registered users (2024); 4M creators
- **ARPU creator:** Top 1% $100K-$500K+/y; mean ~$200/y (skewed); median ~$50/y
- **LTV creator:** Top tier 2-5y; long-tail months
- **Take rate:** 20% (above industry; controversial)
- **Churn:** High — subscription model with 30-40% monthly churn typical
- **Cooperation indicators:** Low — minimal cross-creator collaboration
- **R12 posture:** Concern — 20% take rate aggressive; payment processor lock-in (legal grey areas constrain alternatives); platform reputation creates switching cost
- **GMV:** $5B+ paid to creators cumulative through 2024

**Source:** OnlyFans annual reports (public from 2021); Bloomberg coverage.

## §5 Discord Boost (Server features)

- **MAU:** 200M+ users 2024; 19M+ active servers
- **ARPU Boost:** $5-15/mo per Boost; servers may have 10-50+ Boosts
- **LTV:** 12-24 months typical
- **CAC:** Low — server-driven viral growth
- **Take rate:** N/A direct; Discord Nitro $10/mo + Server Boost contributions
- **Churn:** Nitro ~10%/mo; Server Boost stable on growing servers
- **Cooperation indicators:** **High** — Discord is cooperation-native; community emergence + maintenance
- **R12 posture:** Pass — clear pricing; data export available; Discord doesn't extract from server economy
- **Revenue:** $600M+ Nitro + Boost 2023 estimate

**Source:** Discord usage stats + revenue estimates Stratechery 2023.

## §6 Roblox (UGC platform)

- **MAU:** 80M+ DAU 2024; 200M+ MAU
- **ARPU developer:** Top 1% $1M-$10M+/y; long-tail ~$100/y
- **LTV developer:** Top tier 5-10y; mid 2-3y
- **Take rate:** ~70% platform / 30% creator (high; controversial; LeBron-style platforms challenge)
- **Cooperation indicators:** Medium — collab projects within Roblox Studio
- **R12 posture:** Concern — 70% platform take rate extractive; data lock-in; creator earnings denominated в Robux (forced conversion)
- **GMV:** $3.5B revenue 2023; $740M paid to creators 2023

**Source:** Roblox SEC 10-K 2023.

## §19 Mondragón Corp (Worker cooperative)

- **Cooperatives:** 96
- **Revenue:** €11B+ 2023
- **Pay ratio cap:** Originally 6:1; relaxed to 9:1 some sectors; 12:1 max
- **Cooperation indicators:** **Highest in sample** — 60+ year sustained cooperation
