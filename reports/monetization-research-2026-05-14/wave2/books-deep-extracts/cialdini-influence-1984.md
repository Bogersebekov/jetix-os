# Cialdini — *Influence* (1984) — Deep Extract

## §1 Author + context

Robert Cialdini — social psychologist, Arizona State University. *Influence* (1984; updated 2007, 2021) synthesizes decades of compliance research into 6 principles (later 7).

## §2 Six (now 7) principles relevant to Jetix

### Principle 1 — Reciprocity
People feel obligated to return favors. Free samples, Hare Krishna flower donations, IRS soliciting voluntary compliance.

**Jetix mapping:** H-O-001 (free trial → paid) operationalizes reciprocity — free KB access creates obligation to convert. H-A-010 (mentor cascade) embeds reciprocity formally (apprentice pays % to mentor).

### Principle 2 — Commitment + Consistency
Once people commit (especially publicly), they act consistently with that commitment. Cognitive dissonance theory.

**Jetix mapping:** **H-C-007 (Charter signing as identity affirmation)** is direct Cialdini application. Public ritual → ongoing behavioral consistency. Strongest Cialdini-Jetix link.

### Principle 3 — Social Proof
People look to others' behavior to decide their own. «Best-seller», «4 out of 5 dentists recommend», testimonials.

**Jetix mapping:** Realm rank visibility (H-C-001) is social proof at scale — high-rank members signal what Realm rewards. Manifest pattern (H-C-008) leverages social proof («9 L1 partners signed»). Onboarding funnels (H-O-005 influencer manifest) use influencer authority as social proof.

### Principle 4 — Authority
People defer to authority figures. White coat experiment; expert testimony.

**Jetix mapping:** L1 partner = methodology authority (Цэрэн / Левенчук); Jetix borrows authority signal. Cert issuance (H-M-008) confers institutional authority on members.

### Principle 5 — Liking
People comply with those they like. Tupperware parties; cross-selling friends.

**Jetix mapping:** Real-life friendship layer (H-C-017) operates через Liking. Clan composition 3-10 (Dunbar) enables friendship formation. Friendships → cooperation persistence (L4 layer).

### Principle 6 — Scarcity
Limited availability increases perceived value. «Only 3 left»; deadlines; exclusive memberships.

**Jetix mapping:** Charter L1 has 9 confirmed + 1 organic slot — scarcity by design. Mastership ranks (L0-L6) are scarce (assessment gates). Quest pool за Season may be capped.

### Principle 7 — Unity (added 2016)
People comply more when they share identity («we are alike»). Family, tribe, in-group.

**Jetix mapping:** 6 archetypes (H-C-009) create in-group identity. Clan = small in-group. «Jetix Hunter» self-image (H-C-015) operationalizes Unity.
