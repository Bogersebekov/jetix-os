# van Dreunen — *One Up* (2024) — Deep Extract

## §1 Author + context

Joost van Dreunen — NYU professor; co-founder SuperData Research (acquired by Nielsen); 20+ years gaming industry economist. *One Up* (2024, MIT Press) consolidates modern creator economy analysis — Roblox, Fortnite, Twitch, OnlyFans monetization taxonomy.

## §2 Five key concepts relevant to Jetix

### Concept 1 — Monetization Taxonomy (4 categories)

van Dreunen categorizes modern digital monetization:
1. **Direct sale** — one-time purchase (Steam game, Coursera Specialization)
2. **Subscription** — recurring (Patreon, Substack, OnlyFans, Netflix)
3. **Freemium + in-app** — free baseline + microtransactions (Roblox, Fortnite, Genshin)
4. **Creator marketplace** — UGC + transaction fees (Roblox, Steam Workshop, Fiverr)

Modern winners use ≥2 simultaneously (Roblox: subscription Premium + in-app Robux + creator marketplace).

**Jetix mapping:** H-M-003 (consulting fee) = direct sale; H-M-006 (subscription) = subscription; H-M-013 (marketplace fees) = creator marketplace; Jetix appears mixed-mode like Roblox.

### Concept 2 — Creator Marketplace Economics

Roblox developer economy: $740M paid to creators 2023; 27% of platform revenue. Top creators (1% of devs) earn $1M+; long tail ~$0-100/y. Power-law distribution dominates.

**Jetix mapping:** H-A-013 (Realm goods marketplace) will follow similar power-law unless intervention. Realm rank (H-C-001) + cooperation bonuses (H-C-010/011) can **soften** power-law via collaboration (joint Quests bundle long-tail members into joint payouts). Castronova-Mondragón counter-pattern.

### Concept 3 — Audience Capture Risk

van Dreunen analyzes Twitch / OnlyFans audience capture: creators become dependent on single-platform algorithm; platform extracts increasing share over time (Twitch 30/70 → 50/50 sub split contentious 2022; OnlyFans 20% take rate sustained but creator dependency total). **Audience capture → platform extraction** is van Dreunen's anti-pattern.

**Jetix mapping:** R12 anti-extraction posture is direct counter; Charter clause locks take rate (HQ-RC-011 currency choice affects this); fork-and-leave preserves audience portability. **van Dreunen validates Jetix R12 framing.**

### Concept 4 — Cross-Platform Creator Strategies

Modern creators don't depend on single platform: YouTube + Substack + Patreon + Twitch portfolio. Diversification = anti-extraction insurance.

**Jetix mapping:** H-A-015 (audience-rental — member with own audience rents reach to Clan) directly aligns. Members keep external audience independent of Realm; Realm = one revenue stream among portfolio. R12 fork-and-leave operationalizes this.

### Concept 5 — Esports + Community as Brand

Riot LoL Worlds = $3B+ business; not just game revenue — brand + community ecosystem. Community participation = brand asset.

**Jetix mapping:** Realm Seasons (Charter §3) + Realm-wide events (H-F-004) replicate Worlds-style community-as-brand structure. Path to Virtual State Phase 4+ recognition criteria includes brand-community asset legitimacy.
