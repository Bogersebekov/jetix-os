# Henrich — *The WEIRDest People in the World* (2020) — Deep Extract

## §1 Context

Joseph Henrich — Harvard evolutionary anthropologist. *WEIRDest* argues Western-Educated-Industrialised-Rich-Democratic populations are psychologically + culturally distinct; cooperation patterns differ across cultures.

## §2 Three key concepts relevant to Jetix

### Concept 1 — WEIRD vs non-WEIRD Cooperation Patterns
WEIRD: individualistic; trust-strangers; impersonal markets; rule-of-law cooperation.
Non-WEIRD: kin-based; trust-relatives; in-group cooperation; honor-based.

**Jetix mapping:** Realm Phase 1-3 operates predominantly WEIRD-style (impersonal Realm rank + algorithmic trust + cross-Clan cooperation). Phase 4+ при scaling 10M members may encounter cultural variance. **Realm design assumes WEIRD-style cooperation; needs adaptation for non-WEIRD member subsets.**

### Concept 2 — Marriage + Family Programs (MFP) and the Western Church
Henrich's thesis: Catholic Church's marriage rules (no cousin marriage, monogamy) dissolved kin networks → fostered impersonal trust → enabled markets, universities, science.

**Jetix mapping (indirect):** Jetix substrate fosters impersonal trust (Realm rank, R12 anchor, manifest values-based matching) — similar in function to Henrich's MFP. **Jetix substrate engineering analog of medieval church's kin-dissolution — but voluntary + portable.**

### Concept 3 — Cultural Evolution + Niche Construction
Cultures evolve через learning + transmission; niches shape psychology + vice versa. Successful cultures = those that promote group cooperation + individual achievement balance.

**Jetix mapping:** Realm niche shapes member psychology (archetype self-ID; long-cycle commitment; R12 trust). Members shaped by Realm → shape Realm (member-vote, contributions, KB authoring). Niche-construction feedback loop.

## §3 Jetix-relevant cooperation findings

- **Non-WEIRD members may struggle с impersonal Realm rank** — kin-style trust expected; rank seen as alien.
- **WEIRD members thrive on Realm rank** — already operate in impersonal-trust mode.
- **Phase 4+ multi-cultural scaling** requires hybrid mechanisms (rank + local kin-style sub-Clans).

## §4 Failure mode warning

If Jetix scales beyond WEIRD population без adaptation, cooperation mechanisms may break. Need cultural-bridge Clan templates for non-WEIRD member onboarding (Phase 3+).
