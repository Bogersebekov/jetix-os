# Schelling — *The Strategy of Conflict* (1960) — Deep Extract

## §1 Author + context

Thomas Schelling — Nobel laureate economist (2005); pioneered game theory's coordination + bargaining + tacit communication branches. *Strategy of Conflict* (1960) introduced focal points, commitment devices, credible threats / promises.

## §2 Five key concepts relevant to Jetix

### Concept 1 — Focal Points (Schelling Points)

Coordination without communication requires shared salient point. Classic experiment: «meet me in NYC tomorrow without communicating» — most pick Grand Central Station at noon. Focal points emerge from shared knowledge / culture / salience.

**Jetix mapping:**
- **Charter signing event** = focal point (everyone knows date / place / meaning)
- **6 archetypes** = focal categories (member self-identifies without external coordination)
- **Seasons 3-month boundary** = focal time-grid (Q1 ends week 13 — common knowledge)
- **Realm rank visibility** = focal information layer (everyone sees rank — common knowledge of position)

Schelling validates these as cooperation-enabling infrastructure.

### Concept 2 — Commitment Devices

Strategic value of restricting one's own future choices. «Burning bridges» creates credible commitment that changes other party's calculation. Examples: Cortés burning ships; nuclear doomsday devices; legal contracts.

**Jetix mapping:**
- **Charter LOCKED 10-15y horizon** = commitment device (public signing limits founder's option to defect later)
- **R12 fork-and-leave clause** = commitment device («members can leave» constrains Jetix from extraction)
- **Mutual no-poach 12mo (H-C-006)** = bilateral commitment device

### Concept 3 — Credible Threats vs Promises

Threats (if you do X, I'll do Y bad) and promises (if you do X, I'll do Y good) work only if credible. Credibility depends on: ability to execute; willingness; reputational consistency.

**Jetix mapping:**
- **Rank decay (H-C-013)** = credible threat (algorithmic; automatic; no human override needed)
- **Realm exclusion (H-C-014)** = credible threat at extreme (Council vote process binding)
- **Quest reward payout** = credible promise (smart-contract style transparency)

### Concept 4 — Tacit Bargaining

Bargaining без explicit communication; parties signal via actions; outcomes emerge from convergent expectations.

**Jetix mapping:**
- **Cross-Clan synergy bonus (H-C-011)** = tacit signal that cross-Clan cooperation is rewarded
- **Realm-pool bonus structure** = signal of Realm-wide cooperation preference
- **Manifest pattern (H-C-008)** = signal of values for partner self-selection

### Concept 5 — Strategic Moves (Pre-Commitment)

Schelling's analysis: strategic move = commit before opponent acts. Changes opponent's best response. Examples: build factory in market A (signals commitment); declare territorial claim (limits future flexibility).

**Jetix mapping:**
- **Charter signing as public commitment** = strategic move that pre-commits Jetix
- **Foundation v1.0 LOCKED 2026-04-28** = strategic move (constitutional anchor that prevents future drift)
- **Charter L0-L6 timeline 10-15y** = strategic pre-commitment (Charter signatories cannot easily renegotiate without breaking commitment)
