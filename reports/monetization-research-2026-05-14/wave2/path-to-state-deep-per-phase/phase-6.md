# Phase 6 — Sovereign-equivalent Deep Dive

## §1 Headline

- **Members:** 10M+ (L6)
- **Revenue:** €20B-€200B GMV
- **Governance:** Constitutional democracy + AI-substrate Tribunal
- **Legal:** Recognized network state OR diplomatic enclave (Próspera-evolved / Estonia-partnered)
- **R12:** Permanent constitutional + member-veto power

## §2 Membership threshold

### Comparable orgs at this scale
- **Mormons (LDS Church)** — 17M members; closest precedent
- **Bitcoin user base** — 200M+ wallets; closer to «cultural sovereign» than network state
- **Religious orders aggregate** — many at this scale; informal network state status

### Phase 6 = aspiration / not historical precedent
- No voluntary cooperative substrate has reached 10M sovereign-equivalent status
- Closest: Mormons (recognized religiously but not sovereign), Vatican (sovereign but territorial), Switzerland Confederation 1291 (gradual to recognized sovereignty)
- **Phase 6 is genuinely novel; precedents are partial / adjacent**

## §3 Revenue Phase 6

- GMV €20B-€200B (top 200 country GDP equivalent)
- Realm GDP measurable (Castronova framing operationalized)
- Revenue split across all H-M variants
- Possibly Realm Credits / internal currency operational (HQ-RC-011-B/C; HQ-RC-011-E rejected per Tier 2 §4.2)

## §4 Governance Phase 6

- Constitutional democracy + AI-substrate Tribunal
- Member-veto on substrate-affecting changes (Tier 2 R7)
- Distributed Council Federation (multi-Council с rotating senior body)
- Annual Federation Constitutional Review

### AI-substrate Tribunal
- AI advisory layer для precedent + factual analysis
- Human Tribunal seats (rotating + elected)
- External appeal preserved (sovereign court of partner state)

## §5 Legal jurisdiction Phase 6

### Two paths
- **Path A: Recognized Network State** — diplomatic recognition from ≥50% G20; treaty-based co-sovereignty
- **Path B: Diplomatic Enclave** — Próspera-evolved (if Honduras politics resolves) or Estonia-partnered (network state under sovereign protection)

### Likely Phase 6 outcome (per Balaji NS framework)
- Hybrid: Path B (enclave/partnership) more achievable than Path A (full recognition)
- Network state under partnership с 1-3 friendly sovereign states

## §6 Cultural Phase 6

- Realm = significant culture force
- «Jetix Hunter» identity globally recognized + market value substantial
- Multi-generational members (children of Phase 1-3 members)
- Cultural continuity mechanisms operational

## §7 Risks per phase 6

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Hostile state action | Distributed sovereignty; member protection via partner states |
| Constitutional decay | Annual review + Tribunal independence |
| Founder succession permanent | Successor protocol matured Phase 4-5 |

## §8 Precedents — Phase 6 analogs (all partial)

- **Mormons + LDS** — recognized в most states; non-territorial; closest analog
- **Sovereign Order of Malta** — sovereign entity, no territory; treaty recognition
- **Vatican City** — sovereign, territorial, but 800 residents not 10M
- **Switzerland Confederation 1291 → 1648 Westphalia** — gradual sovereignty over 357 years
- **Hong Kong → Special Administrative Region** — sovereignty under partnership с China; relevant но fragile precedent
- **Roma diaspora** — 10M+ members; non-territorial but largely unrecognized state-like status

## §9 External recognition criteria

- Diplomatic recognition from ≥10 sovereign states (formal); ≥50 informal cooperation
- Treaty with at least 1 major state
- Network state-equivalent legal status (Order of Malta parallel)
- Realm GDP measurable + auditable
- Constitutional R12 with member-veto operational
- Public annual transparency report
- AI-substrate Tribunal с external appeal

## §10 What Phase 6 «sovereign-equivalent» means in practice

**NOT** territorial state (no land claim).
**IS** recognized substrate с:
- Legal personality (Order of Malta parallel)
- Member protection (passport-like credentials)
- Internal governance autonomous
- Cross-border cooperation treaties
- Measurable economic + cultural footprint

**Open question:** Is «sovereign-equivalent» a meaningful end state, or is Phase 6 the start of continuing infinite game (per Carse)? **Carse framing: Phase 6 is continuation milestone, not end.**
