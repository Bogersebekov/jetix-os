## §4 Governance structure deep

### Phase 2 governance topology
- **Founder:** Ruslan (still BDFL; delegation begun per HQ-FB-009 chosen variant)
- **Council:** 5-9 rotating seats; archetype-balanced; 1-year terms
- **Member vote:** Required on substrate-affecting decisions (Charter changes; pay ratio adjustments; R12 boundary cases)
- **Tribunal:** First Tribunal seated Year 2-3 (per HQ-DR-013-A elected committee variant)

### Phase 2 governance failure modes
- **Election theater** — low Council voter turnout
- **Member vote fatigue** — too many votes called
- **Council capture** — homogeneous Council if archetype balance not enforced
- **Founder retains too much** — delegation incomplete

### Mondragón precedent
- General Assembly annual; one-member-one-vote
- Workers' Council + Social Council separation
- Pay ratio 6:1 enforced
- 60 years stable governance
