## H-C-002 — Season retrospection — forced reflection on past behavior

**Hypothesis statement:** End of Season 3-month → 1-week retrospection. Clan reviews work / contributions; Axelrod forgiveness window.
**Why test:** Forced retro creates Schelling focal point for accountability + fresh start; reduces defection spirals.
**Minimum viable test:** 1 Clan completes Season 1 retro (1 week); document outcomes + Q2 cooperation level.
**Success metric:** Retro completion ≥80%; Q2 cooperation level uplift; member NPS on retro positive.
**Failure metric:** Retro skipped; spiral retaliation from Q1; retro becomes blame session.
**Confounders:** Facilitator quality; Clan composition pre-existing dynamics.
**Precedent test:** Agile sprint retrospectives (1995+ XP / Scrum; now industry-standard); military AAR (After-Action Reviews, decades); Burning Man theme camp post-event debriefs; Mondragón annual review.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (built into Charter §3).
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 retest:** Verify member can opt-out of retro (record absent vs forced attendance); retro outcomes voluntary disclosure.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-001 (rank update at boundary); combines with H-C-003 (long-cycle commitment); amplifies M-A layer.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Agile retros proven.
