## H-C-005 — Faction-level standings — Clan affects Realm-wide

**Hypothesis statement:** Multiple Clans → Faction; Faction-level rankings visible Realm-wide; amplifies cooperation within + competition between.
**Why test:** Faction-level cooperation incentive scales beyond Clan boundary; intergroup competition increases internal cohesion.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2+ when 5+ Clans exist; form 2 Factions; observe inter-Faction dynamics for 1 Season.
**Success metric:** Faction loyalty observable; cross-Faction collaboration rate measurable.
**Failure metric:** Faction wars; talent hoarding within Faction; cross-Faction cooperation chilled.
**Confounders:** Phase 2 substrate immature; Faction composition pre-built.
**Precedent test:** Pokémon GO teams (Mystic/Valor/Instinct, sustained 8+ years, 200M+ players); EVE alliances; sports leagues (NFL conferences); university Greek system; Harry Potter Houses (cultural ref).
**Phase fit:** Phase 2-3.
**Required L1 fit:** Multiple L1 with Clans.
**R12 retest:** Verify member can change Faction (per H-F-002 portability); Faction-level decisions don't override member R12 rights.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-004 (Clan aggregation); combines with H-F-005 (Realm-wide infrastructure); amplifies H-C-011 (cross-Faction collab).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — Pokémon GO precedent strong; corporate Faction substrate untested.
