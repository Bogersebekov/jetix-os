## H-C-017 — Real-life friendship layer — orthogonal incentive

**Hypothesis statement:** Beyond formal mechanics, real friendships form within Clan (Dunbar 10-100 layer); friendship loyalty orthogonal to material incentives.
**Why test:** L4 intrinsic incentive resilient to material disruption.
**Minimum viable test:** Track friendship formation indicators in Clan (off-Realm interactions / personal events shared); Year 1.
**Success metric:** ≥30% of Clan pairs report friendship; off-Realm interactions occur; resilience to material disruption observed.
**Failure metric:** No friendships form; Clan stays transactional; member exits cite "no real connection".
**Confounders:** Pre-existing friendships; member geography / language.
**Precedent test:** PayPal Mafia friendships → recurring co-founding (30+ years); military buddy bonds (combat unit cohesion); college fraternity / sorority lifelong networks; Burning Man theme camp friendships.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (small Clan size 3-10 supports Dunbar).
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 retest:** Verify off-Realm communication channels permitted (Realm not surveillance state); friendships persist на fork-and-leave.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-018 (life improvement); combines with H-A-018 (network equity premium); amplifies L4.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — PayPal Mafia / military / fraternity precedent strong.
