## H-C-012 — Community contribution rewards (KB / mentor / moderation)

**Hypothesis statement:** Members earn Realm reputation for non-revenue contributions (KB docs / mentor hours / community moderation / event organization). Rep credits → rank acceleration / TRM resource credits / Quest priority.
**Why test:** Rewards orthogonal contributions; prevents free-riding on community goods (Ostrom commons).
**Minimum viable test:** Tracking system for KB commits / mentor hours / etc.; rep credits awarded; track 1 Season.
**Success metric:** ≥50% of active members contribute non-revenue work; KB grows; mentor hour distribution non-degenerate.
**Failure metric:** Contribution drops после credits introduced (crowding-out effect — Deci/Ryan SDT); inflation of credit (gaming); free-rider problem persists.
**Confounders:** Selection bias; pre-existing motivation; credit-conversion ratio.
**Precedent test:** Wikipedia editor reputation (sustained 20+ years, millions of editors); Stack Overflow rep (sustained 15+ years); Reddit karma; Discord level bots; Gitcoin retroactive funding (~$50M RPGF).
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 retest:** Verify credits non-extractive (member-portable on fork); contribution voluntary; credit-inflation rules public.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-001 (rank); combines with H-A-013 (Realm goods); amplifies L4 identity.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Wikipedia / Stack Overflow proven; SDT crowding-out caveat known.
