## H-C-008 — Manifest pattern — cooperative thesis vs legacy substrate

**Hypothesis statement:** Per H2 Partnership Model — Jetix recruits via manifesto thesis (NOT legacy consulting sell). Self-selection: those who resonate sign; filters for cooperation orientation.
**Why test:** Manifest filter increases signatory cooperation orientation; reduces post-signing dropout.
**Minimum viable test:** 10 candidate L1 receive manifest doc; track who signs + post-signing behavior 6 months.
**Success metric:** ≥30% conversion (manifest seen → signed); signatories cite manifest fit; post-signing cooperation high.
**Failure metric:** <10% conversion (manifest too radical); signatories sign without believing (post-signing dropout).
**Confounders:** Manifest wording quality; partner-network pre-existing relationships dominate.
**Precedent test:** Burning Man 10 Principles (entry filter; sustained 30+ years, 70K participants); CrossFit affiliate brand commitment; OpenAI Charter (mission-driven hires); B Corp certification (~7K orgs).
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (foundational filter).
**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 willing to publicly endorse manifest.
**R12 retest:** Verify manifest text doesn't include extractive clauses; signatory can disagree publicly без penalty; manifest revision process voluntary.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-007 (charter signing); combines with H-O-005 (manifest funnel); amplifies brand cohesion.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Burning Man / CrossFit / OpenAI proven.
