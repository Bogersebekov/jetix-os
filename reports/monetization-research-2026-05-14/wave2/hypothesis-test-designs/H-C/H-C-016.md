## H-C-016 — Rituals — Season opening / Charter signing / Quest celebration

**Hypothesis statement:** Recurring ritual events reinforce identity + community bonds. Durkheim collective effervescence.
**Why test:** Predictable rituals = Schelling focal points + emotional anchoring; sustained engagement.
**Minimum viable test:** Quarterly Season events + annual Charter renewal + Quest-completion celebrations Year 1.
**Success metric:** ≥80% participation; member NPS on rituals ≥+7; renewal commitment Year 2.
**Failure metric:** Low participation; rituals seen as performative; renewal declines.
**Confounders:** Ritual quality variance; member geography (in-person vs video).
**Precedent test:** Burning Man 10 Principles + Effigy burn (sustained 30+ years, 70K participants); Mondragón annual Cooperative Day; CrossFit Open Friday workouts (millions globally); military formation rituals; Apple WWDC keynote (annual since 1983).
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 retest:** Verify ritual participation voluntary; no penalty for non-participation; off-Realm religion / culture respected.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-002 (Season retro is ritual); combines with H-C-007/008 (signing rituals); amplifies L4 + brand cohesion.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Burning Man / Mondragón / Apple proven.
