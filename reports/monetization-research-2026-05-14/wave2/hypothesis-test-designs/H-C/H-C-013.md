## H-C-013 — Rank decay on idleness or extraction behavior

**Hypothesis statement:** Inactive / extractive members → rank decay (1 tier per Season idleness; 2 tier on extraction). Use-it-or-lose-it.
**Why test:** Prevents prestige accumulation gaming; ongoing engagement incentive.
**Minimum viable test:** Deploy decay algorithm; track active vs sabbatical members 1 Season.
**Success metric:** Decay applied fairly; sabbatical exception works; appeal rate <10%; engagement uplift among at-risk members.
**Failure metric:** Decay seen as punishment; mass appeals; sabbatical exception abused.
**Confounders:** Decay rate calibration; member personal circumstances.
**Precedent test:** LoL rank decay (sustained; 100M+ players); CFA recertification (every 3y); AWS cert expiration (3y); Estonia e-residency renewal.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 retest:** Verify decay rules transparent + appealable; sabbatical declaration mechanism; decay doesn't penalize life events (illness / parental leave); member-data on decay portable.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-001 (rank); combines with H-C-002 (Season boundary); combines with H-C-014 (exclusion at extreme); amplifies M-A layer.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — LoL / cert decay proven; calibration risk.
