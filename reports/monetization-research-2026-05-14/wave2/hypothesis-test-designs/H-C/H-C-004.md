## H-C-004 — Clan reputation — member action affects Clan rank

**Hypothesis statement:** Each member action → own E1 + Clan E3 rank. Free-riding → Clan rank drop → peer pressure.
**Why test:** Group-level reputation creates peer enforcement; shifts from substrate-imposed to peer-imposed cooperation.
**Minimum viable test:** Deploy Clan rank aggregation; track 2 Clans across Q1; observe peer pressure dynamics.
**Success metric:** Free-riding events trigger peer correction within 2 weeks; Clan rank distribution non-degenerate.
**Failure metric:** Clan rank devolves to mean (everyone hides); excessive group punishment; member exits citing peer pressure.
**Confounders:** Clan composition; pre-existing dynamics.
**Precedent test:** Sports team rankings (collective performance); guild reputation in EVE / WoW; military unit honor; academic department rankings (REF aggregate).
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (small Clan pilots).
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 retest:** Verify peer pressure stays voluntary (no formal expulsion without due process); fork-and-leave preserves member after Clan exit.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-001 (member rank); combines with H-C-005 (Faction); amplifies M-B layer.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — team / guild ranking proven; member-level integration novel.
