## H-C-015 — "I'm a Jetix Hunter" — self-image cooperation incentive

**Hypothesis statement:** Member adopts identity ("I am a Jetix [archetype]") which embeds cooperative norms by self-image. Cognitive dissonance theory.
**Why test:** Identity-driven behavior more resilient than rule-following; L4 orthogonal layer.
**Minimum viable test:** Members add archetype identity to bio + social profiles; 6-month sentiment tracking.
**Success metric:** ≥50% of members adopt identity publicly; behavior aligned 6mo later; identity persists post-fork.
**Failure metric:** Members refuse identity; behavior unaffected; identity seen as cringe / cult-like.
**Confounders:** Pre-existing identity strength; archetype name resonance varies.
**Precedent test:** Marines "Once a Marine, always a Marine"; CrossFit "Crossfitter" identity; Apple cult; Burning Man "Burner"; Harvard alum.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 retest:** Verify identity adoption voluntary; identity persists on R12 fork-and-leave (member keeps "former Realm Hunter" badge if earned); no forced identity.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-007 (signing); combines with H-C-009 (archetype); combines with H-C-016 (rituals); amplifies L4.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Marine / CrossFit / Apple identity decades-proven.
