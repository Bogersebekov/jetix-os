## H-C-011 — Cross-Clan synergy bonuses

**Hypothesis statement:** Cross-Clan Joint Quests trigger Realm-pool synergy bonus (5-10%). Incentivizes federation.
**Why test:** Material reward for cross-Clan cooperation; reduces inter-Clan rivalry.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2+; tag cross-Clan Quests; pay bonus; track participation rate.
**Success metric:** Cross-Clan Quest rate ≥20% of total Quests; bonus paid; Clan leaderships endorse.
**Failure metric:** Cross-Clan rate <5%; bonus disputed; intra-Clan favoritism dominates.
**Confounders:** Phase 2+ substrate maturity; Clan composition variance.
**Precedent test:** EVE Online alliance ops (sustained $300-500M annual market); WoW guild alliances on PvP server raids; cross-faction esports collaborations.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Multiple L1 with Clans.
**R12 retest:** Verify bonus pool funded from operating budget (not member fees); bonus calculation transparent.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-F-001 (Joint Quests); combines with H-C-010 (revenue split); amplifies federation.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — EVE alliance ops proven.
