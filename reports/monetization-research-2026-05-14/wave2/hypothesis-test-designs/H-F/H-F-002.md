## H-F-002 — Realm rank portability across Clans

**Hypothesis statement:** Member changing Clans carries earned rank + reputation; 10-20% transition penalty prevents gaming.
**Why test:** Reduces switching cost between Clans → fluid talent allocation; prevents Clan lock-in extraction.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2; 2-3 members request Clan transition; protocol execution.
**Success metric:** Transitions executed; both Clan leaders ack; member NPS positive; rank history preserved.
**Failure metric:** Transitions blocked by Clan leaderships; rank gaming via repeat transitions; protocol fails.
**Confounders:** Phase 2 substrate; small sample.
**Precedent test:** Sports league trade systems (decades-proven); EVE Online corp changes (millions of instances); academic department transfers.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Multi-L1 with Clans.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ member-initiated; alternative fork-and-leave preserved.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-014 (exclusion alternative); blocks with H-C-006 (no-poach if cross-signatory); preserves R12 mobility.
