## H-F-001 — Joint Quests across Clans

**Hypothesis statement:** Quest design tag «cross-Clan»; involves ≥2 Clans; revenue split + cross-Clan synergy bonus (per H-C-011).
**Why test:** Material reward for cross-Clan cooperation; reduces inter-Clan rivalry; enables larger scope.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2; tag 5 Quests as cross-Clan; track participation.
**Success metric:** ≥3 cross-Clan Quests filled; revenue split smooth; inter-Clan NPS positive.
**Failure metric:** Quests unfilled; Clan loyalty blocks cooperation.
**Confounders:** Phase 2 substrate; Clan dynamics.
**Precedent test:** EVE Online alliance ops (sustained); WoW guild alliances; Mondragón inter-cooperative committees.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Multi-L1 with Clans.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ voluntary cross-Clan; bonus pool transparent.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-011 (synergy bonus); combines with H-A-001 (Quest); amplifies H-F-003.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — EVE / WoW alliance ops proven.
