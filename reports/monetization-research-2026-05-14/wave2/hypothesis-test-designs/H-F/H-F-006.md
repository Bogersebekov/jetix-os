## H-F-006 — Cross-Clan mentorship

**Hypothesis statement:** Members can mentor / be mentored across Clans; cross-Clan mentorship cascade per H-A-010-012.
**Why test:** Maximizes mentor-apprentice match quality; cross-Clan info flow.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2; mentor registry Realm-wide; 5 cross-Clan pairings.
**Success metric:** Cross-Clan mentorship rate ≥30% of total mentorship; member NPS positive; Council reviews flow (no raiding).
**Failure metric:** Intra-Clan favoritism dominates; cross-Clan mentorship used as poaching cover (per H-C-006 violation).
**Confounders:** Phase 2 substrate; cross-Clan trust.
**Precedent test:** Academia cross-department advisors (centuries); YC alumni cross-batch mentoring; Reforge expert cross-cohort.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Multi-L1 with Clans.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ member-initiated; mentor cascade (H-A-010) terms preserved cross-Clan.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-010-012 (mentorship cascade); combines with H-F-002 (rank portability); amplifies cross-Clan info flow.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — academic cross-advisor centuries-proven.
