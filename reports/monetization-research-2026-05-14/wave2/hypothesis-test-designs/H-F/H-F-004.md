## H-F-004 — Realm-wide events / seasons (3-month synchronized)

**Hypothesis statement:** Realm-wide Season cycle (synchronized 3-month per Charter); seasonal narrative + cross-Clan leaderboards + Realm-wide Quest pool.
**Why test:** Schelling focal time creates Realm-wide identity beyond Clan; cross-Clan rivalry + cooperation.
**Minimum viable test:** Q1 2026 Season; 1-2 Clans run synchronized; track engagement.
**Success metric:** ≥80% members participate Season; cross-Clan engagement uplift; Season narrative resonates.
**Failure metric:** Season ignored; participation drops; Clans operate on own calendars.
**Confounders:** Season narrative quality; member geography / time zones.
**Precedent test:** Fortnite seasonal narrative (sustained 7+ years, $20B+ revenue); LoL Worlds annual (millions watch); Apple WWDC June; Mondragón annual Cooperative Day.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ participation voluntary; Season narrative not extractive.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-002 (Season retro); combines with H-C-016 (rituals); amplifies H-C-005 (Faction).
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Fortnite / LoL / WWDC proven seasonal narrative.
