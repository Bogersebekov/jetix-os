## H-F-009 — Inter-Clan trade routes — TRM resource exchange

**Hypothesis statement:** Clans trade TRM resources (Clan A Capital ↔ Clan B Audience); standardized trade protocols; Jetix fee per H-M-014 (2-5%).
**Why test:** Resource specialization → comparative advantage → cross-Clan value creation.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2; 2 Clans with differentiated TRM; 3-5 trades executed.
**Success metric:** ≥3 trades; fee collected; trade volume growing.
**Failure metric:** Zero trades; arbitrage opportunism; trade disputes.
**Confounders:** Phase 2 multi-Clan substrate; TRM differentiation requires Clan composition variance.
**Precedent test:** International trade specialization (Ricardo 1817 → centuries-proven); EVE Online inter-corp markets ($300-500M/y); Visa / Mastercard interchange (2-3% on $9T cards GDV).
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Multi-L1 with Clans + TRM differentiation.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ fee transparent; intra-Clan free.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-014 (TRM-trade fee); combines with H-A-003 (TRM operator); amplifies H-F-001.
