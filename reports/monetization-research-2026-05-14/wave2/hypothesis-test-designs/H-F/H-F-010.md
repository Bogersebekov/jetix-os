## H-F-010 — Realm-wide leaderboard — global rank visibility

**Hypothesis statement:** Global Realm-wide leaderboard (top 100 / top 1000 lists); periodic refresh; L2 (M-B) scaled Realm-wide.
**Why test:** Aspirational target for new members; visible "stars" of system.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2; deploy leaderboard; configurable privacy (opt-out); track engagement vs no-leaderboard control.
**Success metric:** ≥80% opt-in; engagement uplift attributable to leaderboard; rank gaming complaints <5%.
**Failure metric:** Opt-in <30% (privacy-prefer); leaderboard gaming dominates; toxic competition.
**Confounders:** Algorithm choice dominates; opt-in selection bias.
**Precedent test:** LoL global ranking (100M+ players); Dota 2 ladder; LeetCode global rank; Strava segment leaderboards (90M users).
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ opt-out preserved; member can anonymize.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-C-001 (rank); combines with H-A-016 (external rate uplift); amplifies M-B.
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — LoL / Strava proven.
