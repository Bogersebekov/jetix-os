## H-F-007 — Standardized dispute resolution — Realm Tribunal

**Hypothesis statement:** Realm Tribunal (rotating multi-Clan members) mediates inter-Clan disputes; standard protocol; appeal process. Funded by H-M-018 fees.
**Why test:** Independent third-party avoids Clan-vs-Clan escalation; standardized protocol reduces variance.
**Minimum viable test:** Tribunal protocol drafted; mock dispute mediated; legal review.
**Success metric:** Protocol acked by Council; mock dispute resolves cleanly; legal counsel approves Phase 3+ deployment.
**Failure metric:** Protocol contested; mock dispute escalates; legal jurisdiction unclear.
**Confounders:** Phase 3+ scale required for real disputes; legal jurisdiction (German court vs Realm Tribunal).
**Precedent test:** AAA / JAMS commercial arbitration ($300-$1500 filing); ICC International Court; Mondragón Cooperative Court (60-year); Estonia e-court.
**Phase fit:** Phase 3+ (real disputes emerge at scale).
**Required L1 fit:** Politicians archetype Phase 4+ for governance experience.
**R12 alignment:** ⚠ concern — Tribunal independence critical; rotating seats + external appeal mandatory.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-018 (fees); combines with H-C-014 (exclusion appeals); blocks with H-M-017 (Standards Body — double monopoly risk).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — AAA / JAMS proven; Realm-Tribunal legitimacy untested.
