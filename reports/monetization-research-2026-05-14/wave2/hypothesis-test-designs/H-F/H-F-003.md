## H-F-003 — Cross-Clan partnership formation protocol

**Hypothesis statement:** 2 Clans formally partner for scope / Season / venture; share resources + reward split pre-agreed. Partnership Charter v0 template (subset of First Clan Charter).
**Why test:** Enables larger-scope ventures than single Clan; explicit protocol reduces conflict.
**Minimum viable test:** Phase 2; 1 cross-Clan partnership formed; protocol tested.
**Success metric:** Partnership delivers; revenue split smooth; both Clan leaderships endorse.
**Failure metric:** Partnership disputes; Charter v0 ambiguous; legal complications.
**Confounders:** Phase 2 substrate; partnership scope ambiguity.
**Precedent test:** Corporate joint ventures (decades-proven); EVE "blue-list" alliance mechanic; Star Trek Federation member states (fiction reference).
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Multi-L1 with Clans.
