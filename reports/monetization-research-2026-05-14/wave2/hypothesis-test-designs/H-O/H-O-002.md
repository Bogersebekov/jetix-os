**Confounders:** Sales skill of Jetix team; partner network warmth.
**Precedent test:** Standard agency / fractional COO sales (6-9mo cycle); McKinsey project model.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 willing to engage.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ pilot exit-able; retainer tier downgrade allowed.
