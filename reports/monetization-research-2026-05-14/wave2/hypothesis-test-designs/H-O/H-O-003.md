## H-O-003 — Workshop intro → first Quest → Clan join

**Hypothesis statement:** Public workshop (1-day in-person or video) → first Quest invitation (week 1) → 1-Season trial in Clan → permanent. Conversion 5-15% workshop→Quest; 50-70% trial→permanent.
**Why test:** Workshop funnel converts well for hands-on archetypes.
**Minimum viable test:** 1 workshop (20-50 attendees); track to Clan join 1 Season.
**Success metric:** ≥10% workshop→Quest; ≥50% trial→permanent; member NPS ≥+7.
**Failure metric:** <5% conversion; workshop attendees passive; trial-to-permanent low.
**Confounders:** Workshop format quality; attendee pre-disposition.
**Precedent test:** CrossFit affiliate intro day (industry-standard); McKinsey insight workshops; Codility tech assessments → hire funnel.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority с willingness to teach.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ trial exits without penalty.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-O-008 (mastery class); combines with H-C-007 (signing post-trial).
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — CrossFit intro day decades-proven.
