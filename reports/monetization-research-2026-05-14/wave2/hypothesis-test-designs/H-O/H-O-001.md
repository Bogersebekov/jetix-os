## H-O-001 — Free trial → paid tier

**Hypothesis statement:** Free read-only KB access (1-2w) → first Quest invitation → paid subscription decision (week 4). Conversion target 1-5%.
**Why test:** Funnel conversion rate; free trial value adequate to motivate paid.
**Minimum viable test:** 200 free signups; track to paid conversion 4-week window.
**Success metric:** ≥3% conversion; member NPS positive; retention M2 ≥80%.
**Failure metric:** <1% conversion; high churn week 2.
**Confounders:** Funnel design quality; cohort source (organic vs paid acquisition).
**Precedent test:** Substack ~10% conversion top creators; Patreon 2-5%; Spotify Premium ~5%; Trello free→paid ~5%.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Influencer with audience for cohort source.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ free tier preserves value; cancel-any-time.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-006 (subscription); combines with H-A-001 (first Quest engagement).
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — freemium SaaS decades-proven.
