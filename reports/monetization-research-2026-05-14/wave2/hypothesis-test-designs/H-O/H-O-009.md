## H-O-009 — External speaking → community invite → Clan trial

**Hypothesis statement:** External speaking engagement → audience signal of interest → community invite → Clan trial Season → permanent member. Conversion 1-3% speaking→invite; 20-30% invite→trial.
**Why test:** Speaking funnel attracts pre-vetted members.
**Minimum viable test:** 3 external speakings → invite list → trial cohort.
**Success metric:** ≥30 invitees from 3 events; ≥10 trial; ≥3 permanent.
**Failure metric:** Speaking generates no qualified leads; invite-trial conversion zero.
**Confounders:** Speaking event audience fit; invite messaging.
**Precedent test:** Conference→community model (a16z All-In Summit→content network); YC alumni speaking → batch recruitment.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (any L1 with speaking gigs).
**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 with external speaking platform.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ no obligation post-invite.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-O-005 (influencer parallel); combines with H-O-007 (charter entry).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — speaking → community funnel works but slow.
