## H-O-011 — Speaker-author funnel — book/lecture → Realm membership

**Hypothesis statement:** External book / lecture / conference → reader audience → Realm signup invitation → Quest trial. Conversion 0.1-1% book audience→Realm signup; 30-50% signup→Quest.
**Why test:** Long-form content funnel converts deep readers at low rate but high quality.
**Minimum viable test:** Track 1 book / lecture cycle conversion 12mo.
**Success metric:** ≥100 book readers → ≥0.5% Realm signup → ≥30% Quest trial; member quality high.
**Failure metric:** Zero conversion; book audience non-overlap with Realm fit.
**Confounders:** Book reach (chicken-egg); author brand strength.
**Precedent test:** Stratechery author → membership (~2K paid members from book reads); Naval podcast → fellowship.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires Jetix book/lecture authored).
**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ low-pressure funnel; no obligation.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-O-009 (speaking parallel); combines with H-M-006 (subscription tier members); amplifies brand reach.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — Stratechery / Naval proven но requires authored work не yet existent.
