## H-A-005 — Specialized skill assessment → external sale of expertise

**Hypothesis statement:** Member specializes deeply via Realm Quests; assessed by peer + senior; offers expertise as external service. €100-€500/hr advisory; €5K-€50K project consulting.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) Realm specialization compounds faster than solo path; (b) peer-assessment creates external credibility; (c) member can secure external clients without Jetix brokerage.

**Minimum viable test:** 10 members specialize via Realm Quests; 5 secure external advisory engagements within 6 months at €100+/hr.

**Success metric:** ≥50% of 10 secure external engagement; average rate ≥€100/hr; NPS positive on Realm-specialization-pathway.

**Failure metric:** <20% secure; rates at commodity level (<€50/hr); members report Realm specialization didn't help.

**Confounders:** Member's pre-Realm expertise dominates; selection bias (members with specialization momentum opt-in).

**Precedent test:** Reforge experts ($200-1500/hr); Toptal consultants; GLG expert network ($200-1500/hr); Catalant (fractional expert).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (any member with specialization momentum).

**Required L1 fit:** Methodology partners для domain credibility.

**R12 alignment:** ✓ member sells own expertise; Jetix fee per H-M-010 brokerage (optional).

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-014 (paid services); combines with H-M-010 (Jetix brokerage); amplifies H-A-016 (rank → rate).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — expert network model proven.
