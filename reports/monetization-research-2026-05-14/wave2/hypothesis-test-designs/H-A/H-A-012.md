## H-A-012 — Group mentorship cohorts

**Hypothesis statement:** 5-15 apprentices form cohort; pay fee; multiple mentors share teaching load + fee. €1K-€5K per apprentice × 5-15 = €5K-€75K cohort pool.
**Why test:** Cohort model scales mentor capacity; standardizes content delivery.
**Minimum viable test:** 1 cohort 10 apprentices × €2K = €20K pool; 8-week format.
**Success metric:** Cohort fills; ≥80% completion; NPS ≥+7; mentors paid ≥€5K each (split).
**Failure metric:** Cohort under-fills; high dropout; mentors disputed split.
**Confounders:** Mentor quality variance across cohort sessions; cohort dynamics (some apprentices dominate).
**Precedent test:** Maven cohort courses ($500-2K/cohort, sustained); Reforge cohorts (semi-instructor model); YC batches (mentor-paid via equity, larger scale).
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (with first cohort organic from L1 audience).
**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority for cohort credibility.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ explicit fee-for-cohort; transparent.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-010 (mentor cascade — parallel revenue); combines with H-M-006 (subscription + cohort); amplifies H-O-008 (mastery class).
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Maven / Reforge growing market.
