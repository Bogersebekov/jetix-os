## H-A-009 — Cross-Clan strategic partnership formation

**Hypothesis statement:** Members from different Clans form strategic partnership / venture; cross-Clan synergy bonus from Realm pool (5%). Variable per partnership scope.
**Why test:** Realm enables venture formation across Clan boundaries; synergy bonus incentivizes federation.
**Minimum viable test:** 2 Clans, 1 cross-Clan venture; track synergy bonus + outcomes. Cost €5K bonus pool + admin.
**Success metric:** ≥1 cross-Clan venture launched; bonus paid; both Clan leadership NPS positive.
**Failure metric:** Zero cross-Clan ventures (Clan loyalty blocks); bonus disputed; venture fails.
**Confounders:** Phase 1 has only 1 Clan; H-A-009 requires Phase 2+.
**Precedent test:** Boston Consulting × McKinsey cross-recruit (rare but exists); Bankless × Optimism collaborations; cross-firm M&A advisory partnerships.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Multiple L1 forming Clans willing to partner cross-Clan.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ voluntary; bonus encourages cooperation.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-F-001 (Joint Quests cross-Clan); combines with H-C-011 (cross-Clan synergy bonus); amplifies H-F-003 (cross-Clan partnership protocol).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-Low** — cross-firm partnership exists but rare; requires Phase 2 substrate.
