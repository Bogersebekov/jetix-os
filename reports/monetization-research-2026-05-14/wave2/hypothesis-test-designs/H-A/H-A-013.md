## H-A-013 — Realm goods — templates / playbooks / tools

**Hypothesis statement:** Member creates Realm goods (digital products); sells via Realm marketplace. €20-€500 per item; bundles €1K-€10K.
**Why test:** UGC marketplace creates passive income for high-output members; deepens member retention.
**Minimum viable test:** 10 members publish goods; 3-month sales window.
**Success metric:** ≥€5K GMV; ≥3 repeat buyers per author; author retention ≥80%.
**Failure metric:** Zero GMV; quality concerns; Gumroad / Etsy alternatives dominate.
**Confounders:** Marketplace cold-start; existing creator economy alternatives.
**Precedent test:** Gumroad creators (median <$500/y; long-tail mass); Etsy digital downloads ($300M+ aggregate); Notion template marketplace.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any with creator audience.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ member retains IP; Jetix fee per H-M-013.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-013 (marketplace fee); combines with H-A-006 (courses are subset); amplifies H-A-014 (services).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — Gumroad large but commoditized.
