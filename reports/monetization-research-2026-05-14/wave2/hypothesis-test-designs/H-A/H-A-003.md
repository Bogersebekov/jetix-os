## H-A-003 — TRM-Lite resource operator earnings

**Hypothesis statement:** Member offers own TRM resources (audience reach / time / compute / knowledge / capital / team) to other partners' projects; earns retainer or per-engagement. €2K-€10K/mo per L1 engagement; scales L2-L5.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) cross-partner TRM operator demand exists; (b) members can independently deliver L1-level retainer quality; (c) Jetix substrate adds value vs direct member-to-partner contact.

**Minimum viable test:** 1 member acts as TRM operator for 1 L1 partner; 3-month engagement; member earns €5K/mo retainer; track quality + partner satisfaction.

**Success metric:** Partner extends to month 4+; member NPS ≥+5; quality matches Jetix-direct delivery; substrate adds value (member can't easily replicate alone).

**Failure metric:** Partner cancels; member quality below Jetix-direct; substrate role unclear (member bypasses Jetix).

**Confounders:** Single-member sample; member's domain expertise variance; partner-specific fit issues.

**Precedent test:** Fractional CFO / COO / CMO market; Reforge expert network; Continuum (fractional execs); MaestroQA fractional ops; Catalyst Software fractional CS.

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires member L2+ rank to operate at TRM-Lite level).

**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 willing to use TRM operator vs direct Jetix engagement.

**R12 alignment:** ✓ member chooses engagements; no exclusivity required.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-014 (cross-Clan TRM-trade fees apply if cross-Clan); combines with H-A-016 (rank → premium rate); amplifies H-F-009 (inter-Clan trade routes).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — fractional exec model strong; Realm-specific risk is member readiness Phase 1-2.
