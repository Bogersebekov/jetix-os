## H-A-017 — Realm reputation → speaking / advisory fees

**Hypothesis statement:** High-rank members invited to external speaking / advisory; €5K-€50K per speaking; €5K-€100K/y advisory retainer.
**Why test:** External speaking validates rank externally; advisory builds professional network.
**Minimum viable test:** Top-5 ranked members; track external speaking + advisory invitations 12 months.
**Success metric:** ≥3 invitations per top-ranked member; average fee ≥€5K; advisory retainer secured by ≥1.
**Failure metric:** Zero invitations; rank not seen as authority signal externally.
**Confounders:** Pre-existing speaker network dominates; conference + advisory markets seasonal.
**Precedent test:** Conference keynotes (TED / Disrupt $25K-$100K); board advisory (1-2% common equity); GLG expert network ($1K-$5K/hr).
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (rank legitimacy timeline).
**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority for credibility carry-over.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ external opportunities; member directly compensated.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-005 / H-A-016 (rank → premium); amplifies brand reach for Realm.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — speaker economy proven но Realm-rank attribution untested.
