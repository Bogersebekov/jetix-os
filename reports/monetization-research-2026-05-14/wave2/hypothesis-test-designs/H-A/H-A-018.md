## H-A-018 — Realm reputation → equity / investor introduction premium

**Hypothesis statement:** High-rank members get warm intros to investors / co-founders / employees; 1-5% better equity terms vs unwarmed approach.
**Why test:** Realm reputation → external network access measurable as equity uplift; PayPal Mafia precedent.
**Minimum viable test:** 10 high-rank members; track investor / co-founder intros + outcomes 12 months.
**Success metric:** ≥5 of 10 receive warm intros; ≥2 secure better terms; member NPS positive on network value.
**Failure metric:** Zero intros; Realm reputation doesn't carry to investor network; member dependence on Ruslan for intros (bottleneck risk).
**Confounders:** Pre-existing networks dominate; investor market state.
**Precedent test:** YC alumni network warm intros (~50% of YC startups raise via alumni); PayPal Mafia recurring co-founding; Pioneer demo days; OnDeck fellow founders.
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires alumni density).
**Required L1 fit:** Investor + entrepreneur archetypes.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ voluntary introduction network.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-007 (joint ventures); combines with H-M-002 / H-M-016 (Jetix equity + fund LP positions); amplifies brand network effect.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — YC alumni proven; Realm-specific requires time for alumni layer to form.
