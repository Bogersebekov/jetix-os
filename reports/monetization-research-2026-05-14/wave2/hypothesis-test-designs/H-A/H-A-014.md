## H-A-014 — Realm services — workshops / coaching / consulting

**Hypothesis statement:** Member offers paid service (workshop / 1:1 coaching / consulting) to external clients via Realm marketplace. €200-€5K per session; €5K-€50K per engagement.
**Why test:** Service revenue + relationship building; deeper engagement than digital goods.
**Minimum viable test:** 5 members offer services; 3-month booking window.
**Success metric:** ≥€10K GMV across 5; ≥10 client engagements; repeat booking rate ≥30%.
**Failure metric:** Zero bookings; clients prefer external platforms (Calendly etc); rate too low for member time.
**Confounders:** External platform competition; client trust takes time.
**Precedent test:** Maven 1:1 sessions; Calendly-based coaching; freelance consulting platforms; Topia coaches.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ direct member-to-client; Jetix fee per H-M-013 / H-M-010.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-005 (expertise sales); combines with H-M-010 (brokerage for higher-value).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — coaching market mature.
