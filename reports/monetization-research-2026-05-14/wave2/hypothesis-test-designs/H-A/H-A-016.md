## H-A-016 — Realm rank → external freelance rate uplift

**Hypothesis statement:** Member's Realm rank visible externally; freelance buyers pay 20-50% premium for high-rank members.
**Why test:** External rate uplift validates rank signal value; ratifies M-B layer cooperation incentive.
**Minimum viable test:** 10 high-rank members + 10 mid-rank members; same Toptal-style profile; track 6-month rate trajectory.
**Success metric:** High-rank cohort shows 20-50% rate premium vs mid-rank cohort.
**Failure metric:** No premium signal; external buyers ignore Realm rank; rank visibility doesn't translate to rate.
**Confounders:** Pre-Realm rate trajectory dominates; selection (high-rank may also have high pre-Realm rates).
**Precedent test:** LeetCode rank → FAANG offer; Toptal top-3% premium ($60-300/hr); AngelList platinum profiles; GitHub stars correlated с salary.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ if rank infrastructure deployed early.
**Required L1 fit:** Any.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ member's earned reputation.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-002 (freelance bidding); combines with H-A-005 (expertise sale); combines with H-M-008 (cert reinforces rank); amplifies M-B layer.
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — Toptal / LeetCode proven; Realm-specific visibility uncertain how external buyers value.
