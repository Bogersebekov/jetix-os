## H-A-008 — Collaborative consulting — multi-member client engagement

**Hypothesis statement:** 2-3 members combine specialties for larger client (что не мог бы один); split fees pre-agreed. €30K-€500K total engagement.
**Why test:** Realm enables multi-specialty teams that solo consultants can't match; pricing scales above solo rates.
**Minimum viable test:** 2-3 members form team for 1 client engagement; 8-week project. Cost €0 direct (members self-execute).
**Success metric:** Project delivered; total fee ≥€30K; member income ≥individual solo equivalent; client NPS ≥+7.
**Failure metric:** Team coordination cost >revenue uplift; client unhappy; member disputes on split.
**Confounders:** Single-team sample; pre-existing member relationships dominate Realm-coordination.
**Precedent test:** McKinsey project teams (3-7 people; $2M-$20M engagement); small-firm consulting (Bain alumni boutiques); A.Team (engineering teams of 3-7).
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 with client pipeline.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ direct collaboration; Realm matchmaking fee per H-M-010 if used.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-010 (Jetix brokerage); combines with H-A-002 (freelance bidding upgraded to team); amplifies H-F-001 (cross-Clan Joint Quests).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — McKinsey team model decades-proven; transposition to fractional consulting teams (A.Team) actively growing.
