## H-A-002 — Freelance projects through Realm bidding

**Hypothesis statement:** External clients post projects; members bid (transparent rates + Realm rank visible); win bid → execute → paid. Hourly €50-€500 / project €1K-€50K. Toptal-tier rates due to Realm vetting.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) external client pipeline pulls work onto Realm; (b) member bid quality reflects rank (high-rank → high-bid → win rate); (c) take rate (H-M-013) sustainable; (d) client satisfaction parallels traditional freelance platforms.

**Minimum viable test:** 5 client projects posted via Realm; 10-20 member bids; 3-month cycle. Cost €5K admin.

**Success metric:** ≥4 of 5 projects filled; client NPS ≥+7 (Toptal benchmark); ≥80% repeat client interest; rank-rate correlation positive.

**Failure metric:** <2 filled; underbid race-to-bottom; client complaints о member quality.

**Confounders:** Phase 1 client pipeline organic from L1 partner networks; competing platforms (Toptal / Catalant) commoditize.

**Precedent test:** Toptal (3% acceptance; $60-300/hr rates; ~5000 active freelancers); Upwork ($2B+ GMV); Catalant (b2b expert network); Gun.io (developer-focused); A.Team (engineering teams).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (subset of H-A-001 quest model with external client demand).

**Required L1 fit:** L1 partner who can route work to members (sales pipeline as TRM).

**R12 alignment:** ✓ member directly earns; Jetix fee transparent per H-M-013.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-016 (Realm rank → external rate uplift); combines with H-M-010 (Jetix brokerage for higher-value deals); blocks with H-A-014 if member's external service competes for same client.

**Bayesian prior:** **High** — Toptal / Catalant decades-proven freelance platform model.
