## H-A-015 — Audience-rental — member with own audience rents reach to Clan

**Hypothesis statement:** Member with own YouTube / Telegram / newsletter rents reach to Clan for joint Quests / launches; receives fee + Realm rank credit. €500-€10K per campaign.
**Why test:** Audience-rich members can earn additional revenue without losing audience ownership; amplifies cross-Clan launches.
**Minimum viable test:** 1 audience-holding member rents reach for 1 Realm campaign; track outcomes.
**Success metric:** Campaign delivers ≥1K audience-to-Realm signal; fee paid; member NPS ≥+5; Realm gains 10-50 new members.
**Failure metric:** Audience non-responsive; campaign damages member's audience trust; fee disputed.
**Confounders:** Audience-fit alignment; campaign quality independent of fee paid.
**Precedent test:** YouTube channel sponsorship integration (Honey / NordVPN-style, $5K-$500K per integration); cross-promotion deals; Substack network referrals.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (very useful for influencer activation per H-O-005).
**Required L1 fit:** Influencer / blogger archetype.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ voluntary; member retains audience ownership.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-O-005 (influencer manifest funnel core mechanism); combines with H-M-010 (brokerage); amplifies H-A-016 (rank visible to external buyers).
**Bayesian prior:** **High** — sponsorship integration model decades-proven.
