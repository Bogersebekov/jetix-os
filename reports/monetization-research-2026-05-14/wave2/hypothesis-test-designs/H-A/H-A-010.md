## H-A-010 — Mentorship monetary stake — mentor earns % of apprentice external earnings

**Hypothesis statement:** Mentor M sponsors apprentice A; A earns external income; A pays 5-15% to M, capped 2 years post-completion. Voluntary.
**Why test:** Mentor cascade creates renewable mentor incentive; apprentice gets sponsored growth without upfront capital.
**Minimum viable test:** 5 mentor-apprentice pairs; track external earnings + payments 12 months.
**Success metric:** ≥50% of pairs continue past 6 months; ≥3 apprentices generate external income ≥€10K; mentor share paid voluntarily.
**Failure metric:** Apprentices balk at % payments; mentor disputes attribution; structure perceived extractive.
**Confounders:** Pre-existing relationship effect; apprentice-mentor fit variance; cap structure novelty.
**Precedent test:** Lambda School ISA (17% of salary up to $30K cap; sustained until 2022 regulatory pushback); Pathrise income share; Modern Treasury Spring (apprenticeship style); CodePath sponsorship.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Methodology partners willing to mentor.
**R12 alignment:** ⚠ concern — apprentice payment risks extraction; cap + voluntary + 2y limit + fork-and-leave required.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-011 (placement commission, complementary); combines with H-A-012 (group cohort, scalable variant); blocks with H-M-007 if both fees stack (double-extraction).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — Lambda School proven but regulatory scrutiny risk.
