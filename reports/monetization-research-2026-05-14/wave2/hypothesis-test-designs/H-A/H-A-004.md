## H-A-004 — Mastership credential portable to external market

**Hypothesis statement:** Member earns Jetix mastership credential (per H-M-008); applies к external job/freelance/equity opportunities at premium (20-50% rate uplift, CFA precedent). Member keeps credential post-fork.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) external employers recognize Jetix credential; (b) rate uplift materializes 6-12mo post-cert; (c) credential value sustains long-term (no decay through brand dilution).

**Minimum viable test:** 20 members earn L1 credential; survey 6 months later for rate change attribution.

**Success metric:** ≥30% report rate increase; average uplift ≥20%; 3+ verified employer recognitions; renewal commitment ≥80%.

**Failure metric:** Zero recognized uplift; employer reaction neutral («unknown credential»); members don't renew.

**Confounders:** Member's pre-cert trajectory (already growing) confounds attribution; external market recognition is chicken-egg.

**Precedent test:** CFA charter (20-50% premium documented industry-wide); AWS Certified Solutions Architect ($50-100K salary uplift typical); LeetCode rank → FAANG offer rates; PMP cert (5-15% salary uplift); Google Cloud Certs.

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires established credential infrastructure).

**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority for credential legitimacy.

**R12 alignment:** ✓ credential portable; member owns.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-008 (cert issuance — supply side); combines with H-A-016 (rank → premium); amplifies H-O-008 (mastery class → cert).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — CFA/AWS proven но 5-10y recognition build-up timeline for Jetix.
