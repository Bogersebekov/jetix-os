## H-A-007 — Joint ventures within Clan — co-founded products

**Hypothesis statement:** 2-5 Clan members co-found product; equity split per contribution; Realm Quest infrastructure supports launch. 25-50% each co-founder.
**Why test:** Clan creates higher venture launch rate than solo; Realm substrate accelerates time-to-Pre-seed.
**Minimum viable test:** 1 Clan launches 1 co-founded venture; 6-month Pre-seed window. Cost €0 Jetix capital (founders self-fund or take R&D fund per H-M-016).
**Success metric:** Venture reaches Pre-seed milestone; cap table holds; co-founder NPS ≥+5.
**Failure metric:** Founders disputes; venture fails Pre-seed; equity blowup.
**Confounders:** Power-law in startup outcomes; pre-existing co-founder relationships dominate Realm-formed pairings.
**Precedent test:** PayPal Mafia rolling co-founders (informal evidence); YC batches co-founding (~10% of YC batches form intra-batch); Pioneer founder cohorts.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (Clan exists at L1 entry).
**Required L1 fit:** Entrepreneur archetype (Гиренко / Тарасов).
**R12 alignment:** ✓ members own venture; Jetix optional equity per H-M-002.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-002 (Jetix equity in member-founded ventures); combines with H-M-016 (R&D fund LP); combines with H-A-009 (cross-Clan partnership).
