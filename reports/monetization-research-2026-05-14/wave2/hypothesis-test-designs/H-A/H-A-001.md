## H-A-001 — Quest completion bounties

**Hypothesis statement:** Member completes Realm Quest (real biz task / project deliverable) → bounty €500-€5K (small) → €5K-€50K (medium) → €50K+ (large multi-member). Jetix takes marketplace fee (H-M-013).

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) Quest demand exists from sponsors (Jetix R&D fund / external clients / partners); (b) member quality matches Quest scope; (c) bounty pricing aligns с external freelance rates.

**Minimum viable test:** 5 Quests posted, 3 small (€500-€2K) + 2 medium (€5K-€10K); 20-30 member bid pool; 6-week cycle. Cost €5K Jetix admin + Quest fund.

**Success metric:** ≥4 of 5 Quests filled; bounty paid on time; member NPS ≥+5; sponsor NPS ≥+5; 2nd Quest cycle requested by sponsors.

**Failure metric:** Quest unfilled; member missed deadlines; sponsor disputes deliverables; <50% Quests result in repeat.

**Confounders:** Phase 1 sponsor pipeline thin; first-cohort selection bias; Quest scope mismatch с member skill.

**Precedent test:** Bug bounty programs (HackerOne / Bugcrowd $100-$50K typical, $200M+ paid out aggregate); Topcoder competitions; Kaggle prizes ($25K-$100K typical); Gitcoin grants (RPGF, $50M+ deployed).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (foundational).

**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority + entrepreneur partners (Цэрэн / Левенчук / Гиренко) — can source Quest demand.

**R12 alignment:** ✓ direct work earnings; member keeps all minus H-M-013 fee.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-013 (marketplace fee on Quest payouts); combines with H-C-010 (joint Quest split for multi-member); amplifies H-O-001 (free trial → first Quest).

**Bayesian prior:** **High** — bug bounty / Kaggle / Topcoder decades-proven; risk is supply (Quest sponsor pipeline) not demand.
