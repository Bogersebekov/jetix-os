## H-A-011 — Apprentice placement commission

**Hypothesis statement:** Mentor introduces apprentice to external job/freelance role; both earn placement bonus from external party. 5-10% of first-year compensation.
**Why test:** Mentor incentivized to place high-quality; reduces apprentice cost of getting placed.
**Minimum viable test:** 3 placements via mentor introduction; track commission flow.
**Success metric:** ≥3 placements; total commission ≥€10K; both mentor + apprentice NPS positive.
**Failure metric:** No placements; commission disputed; external recruiter market dominates (Jetix loses to faster recruiters).
**Confounders:** External hiring market timing; mentor network depth.
**Precedent test:** Standard recruiter commissions (15-25% first-year salary); Realm version simpler at 5-10%; Triplebyte / Hired.com platform recruiters.
**Phase fit:** Phase 1+.
**Required L1 fit:** Any with hiring connections.
**R12 alignment:** ✓ paid by external party.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-010 (mentor cascade complementary); combines with H-A-016 (rank → premium employer signal).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — recruiter commission decades-proven.
