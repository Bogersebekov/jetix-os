## H-A-006 — Course / micro-content creation within Realm KB

**Hypothesis statement:** Member authors course / playbook / template; sells via Realm marketplace. €50-€2K per piece; bundles €5K-€20K.
**Why test:** member-authored content compounds Realm KB value; revenue per author sustainable.
**Minimum viable test:** 5 members publish; 3-month sales window; track GMV. Cost €2K marketplace + curation.
**Success metric:** ≥€2K GMV across 5 pieces; ≥3 repeat buyers; author NPS positive.
**Failure metric:** Zero sales; content quality lacking; off-platform competition (Gumroad) dominates.
**Confounders:** Cold-start audience; content cannibalization (КВ included in subscription?); off-platform sellers undercut.
**Precedent test:** Reforge courses ($1-3K); Maven cohort courses ($500-2K); Gumroad creators (median ~$500/y; top 1% $100K+); Notion templates marketplace ($5-50/piece).
**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires audience).
**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority for first content seed (Цэрэн / Левенчук courses anchor marketplace).
**R12 alignment:** ✓ member retains IP; Jetix fee per H-M-013.
**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-013 (fee on sale); combines with H-A-013 (Realm goods broadly); amplifies H-M-006 (subscription bundles courses).
**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — Gumroad/Reforge proven; Realm-specific value uncertain без curation discipline.
