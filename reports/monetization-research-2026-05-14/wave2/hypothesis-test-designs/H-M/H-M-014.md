## H-M-014 — TRM-trade fees — resource trade between Clans

**Hypothesis statement:** Clans trade TRM resources (Capital / Audience / Compute / Knowledge / Time / Team) cross-Clan; Jetix takes 2-5% fee on cross-Clan trade. Intra-Clan free. R12 clean — supports Realm infrastructure; alternative direct-trade always available.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) Clans accumulate differentiated TRM portfolios; (b) cross-Clan trade demand exists; (c) 2-5% rate doesn't chill trade; (d) infrastructure (escrow / dispute) sustainable.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 2 Clans with differentiated TRM (e.g., Clan-1 audience-rich, Clan-2 capital-rich); enable cross-Clan trade protocol; 1-3 trades executed.
- Cohort: 2 Clans, 10-20 members total.
- Duration: 3 months.
- Cost: €3K (escrow / trade protocol infrastructure).

**Success metric:** ≥3 cross-Clan TRM trades executed; fee collected; both Clan leadership NPS ≥+5; trade volume growing month-over-month.

**Failure metric:** Zero trades (no demand); fee disputed; Clans bypass through informal off-Realm trades; escrow disputes lacking resolution.

**Confounders:** Phase 1 has only 1 Clan (Charter LOCKED); H-M-014 requires Phase 2 multi-Clan; small sample (2 Clans) ill-suited for fee-rate optimization.

**Precedent test:** EVE PLEX bridge (sustained $300-500M/year market); WoW Token ($15 → in-game gold); Visa / Mastercard interchange fee (2-3% on $9T cards GDV); cross-bank wire fees (corresponded banking, 1-3% typical).

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (multi-Clan emergent).

**Required L1 fit:** Multiple L1 partners forming distinct Clans с different TRM profiles.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) intra-Clan trade always free; (b) fee rate locked в charter (cross-Clan cap); (c) escrow neutral (no Jetix favoritism); (d) trade dispute resolution mechanism (H-F-007 Tribunal). R12 clean.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-F-009 (inter-Clan trade routes — same mechanism); combines with H-C-011 (cross-Clan synergy bonus — incentive to trade); combines with H-A-003 (TRM-Lite operator earns from cross-Clan engagement); amplifies H-F-001 (Joint Quests).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — interchange fee model proven, но requires Phase 2 multi-Clan substrate (not yet existent); execution uncertainty.
