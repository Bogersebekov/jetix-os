## H-M-011 — Data licensing — anonymized Realm operational data

**Hypothesis statement:** Anonymized Realm data (Quest completion patterns / cooperation graphs / TRM-trade flows) licensed to academic researchers (Castronova / van Dreunen-style virtual economy) + business intelligence buyers. €5K-€100K per dataset / recurring access tier. R12 concern — data extraction.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) academic demand exists; (b) opt-in rate ≥30% (member consent); (c) member-royalty share workable; (d) GDPR compliance achievable; (e) anonymization survives audit.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: Opt-in member consent flow; anonymization pipeline; 1 academic partner (Castronova group or van Dreunen NYU) requests data; revenue-share schema for opted-in members.
- Cohort: 100 members offered opt-in.
- Duration: 6 months (consent + data + paper cycle).
- Cost: €10K (legal review + anonymization pipeline + member-royalty admin).

**Success metric:** ≥30% opt-in; ≥1 academic dataset licensed €5K+; member royalty share distributed (proof of pipeline); zero GDPR complaints.

**Failure metric:** <10% opt-in; no academic buyer; GDPR complaint filed; anonymization audit fails (re-identification possible).

**Confounders:** Members opt-in рart based on novelty (early-stage); academic buyer pipeline slow (6-12mo typical); GDPR interpretation drift.

**Precedent test:** EVE MER (CCP Games quarterly economic report; data → 30+ academic papers; sustained 2007+); Roblox developer economy dashboard; Riot League patch reports; Steam Spy (now defunct due to Valve data lockdown — cautionary).

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires sufficient Realm data volume — minimum 500 members for anonymization viability).

**Required L1 fit:** Any; academics most likely external buyer (Phase 2-3).

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) opt-in default-off; (b) member royalty share schema active; (c) fork-and-leave deletes member data per GDPR Article 17; (d) anonymization audited annually; (e) revenue cap (Jetix can't suddenly 10x data licensing without member consent renewal). R12 concern persists unless all five hold strictly.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with §11.Д (academics overlay) — natural fit; blocks with H-M-006 if subscribers also expected to be data sources without separate consent; amplifies H-A-013 (member sells data as Realm good — voluntary participation).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — EVE MER proven но academic market small; commercial data buyer market (B2B intelligence) large но Jetix data volume Phase 2 insufficient.
