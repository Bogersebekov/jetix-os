## H-M-015 — Jetix-owned products built within Realm — incubation → equity → spin-out

**Hypothesis statement:** Jetix incubates own products inside Realm; takes founder-equity (51%+); members get 5-30% equity per contribution; spin-outs may IPO / acquire-out. R12 clean — explicit cap table; standard startup model.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) Realm membership generates buildable product ideas; (b) member-co-builders accept 5-30% equity (vs cash); (c) Jetix capital + substrate accelerates product to PMF faster than alternative; (d) spin-out exits realize value within 5-7y window.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 1 product idea green-lit; 3-5 Realm members co-build; cap table: Jetix 51% / founders pool 30% / Realm operators 10% / option pool 9%.
- Cohort: 3-5 co-builders.
- Duration: 12-18 months (Pre-seed → Seed).
- Cost: €100K-€300K (R&D fund out of 90% reinvest per H2).

**Success metric:** Product reaches Pre-seed milestone (€500K ARR or strategic acquirer interest); cap table holds through dilution; co-builder NPS ≥+5; spin-out option preserved.

**Failure metric:** Product fails Pre-seed; co-builders exit citing equity dispute; cap table cap'd out by overlapping equity grants; product cannibalize partner ventures (conflict of interest).

**Confounders:** Startup power-law (most fail regardless); team composition more predictive of success than capital; spin-out exit timing market-dependent.

**Precedent test:** PayPal Mafia spin-outs (Tesla / SpaceX / LinkedIn / YouTube from PayPal cohort); Y Combinator portfolio (10K+ companies, ~$300B aggregate value); Atomic / Pioneer / SuperHuman ventures; Andreessen Horowitz portfolio company building.

**Phase fit:** Phase 1-2 (early — Jetix retains capacity for own ventures); Phase 3+ may dilute focus.

**Required L1 fit:** N/A (Jetix-owned; partner ventures separate per H-M-002).

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) cap table transparent + signed; (b) members opt-in (not assigned); (c) Jetix-equity in member-founded ventures vs own ventures distinguished (Chinese walls per TRM §14); (d) spin-out preserves member equity; (e) no «sweat equity» for excluded members. R12 clean if (a)-(e) hold.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-007 (Joint ventures within Clan — opt-in to Jetix-owned vs member-founded paths); blocks with H-M-002 (Jetix can have equity in own AND partner ventures — Chinese walls required); combines with H-M-016 (R&D fund LP — own ventures could also receive LP capital).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — PayPal Mafia / YC proven но depends on Realm member-builder concentration (Phase 1 thin); requires founder energy not yet at scale.
