## H-M-005 — Performance fee on partner EBITDA growth

**Hypothesis statement:** Jetix берёт 15-25% performance fee on incremental EBITDA growth (vs pre-Jetix baseline) attributable to substrate contribution. Hedge fund 2/20 → Jetix 1/20 leaner model.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) partner accepts performance-based fee (alignment signal); (b) attribution methodology survives audit; (c) Jetix incremental EBITDA contribution provable; (d) clawback на bad years acceptable to Jetix.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: Partner agrees to 1% mgmt + 20% perf fee structure on incremental EBITDA; pre-Jetix baseline locked; quarterly attribution review.
- Cohort: 1-2 L1 partners with measurable EBITDA (existing business, not pure content creators).
- Duration: 12 months (annual EBITDA cycle).
- Cost: €5K admin (attribution methodology setup + quarterly review labor).

**Success metric:** Partner accepts terms; year-end incremental EBITDA ≥€500K attributable; partner pays performance fee without dispute; partner extends arrangement Year 2.

**Failure metric:** Attribution dispute requires arbitration; clawback triggered (bad year, Jetix returns prior fees); partner exits citing «too much success-tax»; <€100K incremental EBITDA Year 1.

**Confounders:** EBITDA volatile (market-driven, not just substrate-driven); attribution methodology inherently subjective; macro environment dominates micro contribution Year 1.

**Precedent test:** Hedge fund 2/20 standard (decades, $4T AUM globally); PE 2/20 carry; Tiger Cubs 1.5/20; Sequoia Heritage performance fee структура.

**Phase fit:** Phase 1-2 (works with established EBITDA partners; not for early-stage ventures with no baseline).

**Required L1 fit:** Investor-archetype (Брагинский / Федорев) OR established entrepreneur (Гиренко если his ventures already cash-flowing); NOT для pure content creators (no EBITDA baseline).

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) attribution methodology pre-agreed in writing; (b) annual independent audit (third-party CPA); (c) clawback symmetric (good year → partner pays; bad year → Jetix returns); (d) cap on Jetix performance fee per year (e.g., 30% of partner's total EBITDA growth); (e) fork-and-leave forgives future obligations (vested perf only paid). R12 clean only if all five hold.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-004 (mgmt+perf classical structure); blocks with H-M-001 (double-extraction risk if revenue share also charged on same revenue); amplifies H-O-006 (investor allocation funnel — perf fee resembles LP carry structure).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — hedge fund/PE precedent strong для investor partners; attribution methodology unproven at substrate level (vs traditional fund where EBITDA is whole entity).
