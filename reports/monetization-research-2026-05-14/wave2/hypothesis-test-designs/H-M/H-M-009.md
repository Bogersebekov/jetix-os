## H-M-009 — Realm rank premium — paid expedited progression (cosmetic-only)

**Hypothesis statement:** Member pays €100-€500/mo для expedited access to: tooling / KB sections / mentor cohort / Quest pool. NOT bypass skill gates — accelerate access only. R12 concern — pay-to-win impression risk.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) members value acceleration vs perceive it as inequality; (b) charter clause «no rank without assessment» holds operationally; (c) cosmetic-only stays cosmetic (no drift to functional advantage); (d) community sentiment positive.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 1 tier of acceleration (€199/mo premium); explicit charter clause «accelerator does NOT grant rank»; community Q&A session pre-launch.
- Cohort: 50 members; track community sentiment + complaints.
- Duration: 3 months.
- Cost: €3K Realm infrastructure (gated access tier).

**Success metric:** ≥20% opt in; community sentiment positive (≥80% poll); zero formal complaints о pay-to-win; rank ladder distribution unchanged (premium tier doesn't dominate L2+ ranks).

**Failure metric:** Social media backlash; member exodus citing pay-to-win drift; charter renegotiation request to remove premium; complaint rate >10% of premium members.

**Confounders:** Hard to operationalize «cosmetic» vs «functional» — accelerated access IS functional advantage in real terms (time-to-rank); selection bias (premium tier may be members с external income, separate from Realm-earned rank).

**Precedent test:** Dota 2 cosmetic-only (15y sustained, no P2W complaints); LoL champion rotation accelerator (mild drift to «pay for variety»); Battle Pass paid tier (XP boost, sustained но debated P2W).

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires sufficient Realm content/tools to justify acceleration tier).

**Required L1 fit:** Any; partner needs to publicly endorse «no pay-to-win» principle.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) charter clause exists; (b) assessment gate cannot be bypassed by payment; (c) free-tier path to highest rank exists (no premium-locked content); (d) member who pays + fails assessment loses fee non-refundable but rank doesn't increase. R12 concern remains unless (a)-(d) hold strictly and visibly.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-006 (subscription tier may include accelerator as feature); blocks with H-M-007 (progression fee + acceleration tier double-monetize same flow); blocks with H-M-013 (marketplace fee + tool access fee — too many simultaneous fees); amplifies H-O-001 (free → premium funnel).

**Bayesian prior:** **Low-Medium** — Dota proves cosmetic-only possible; but Realm progression is functional content (knowledge / tools) not cosmetic. Real cosmetic-only analog limited.
