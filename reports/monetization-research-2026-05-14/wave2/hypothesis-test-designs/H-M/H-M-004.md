## H-M-004 — TRM retainer — monthly fractional COO model

**Hypothesis statement:** Jetix becomes Total Resource Manager (6 resources: Capital/Time/Audience/Knowledge/Compute/Team); monthly retainer covers ongoing operations. €8K-€60K/mo per L1 (Doc 1B L1→L5 trajectory).

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) partner perceives multi-resource integration as valuable vs siloed services; (b) retainer pricing sticks; (c) tier escalation L1→L5 happens organically; (d) Jetix can deliver multi-resource at quality.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 1 L1 partner signs TRM-Lite L1 €3-10K/mo retainer; 6 resources audited; monthly Resource Operations Cadence calendar built.
- Cohort: 1 L1 partner, 6-month engagement.
- Duration: 6 months.
- Cost: €5K-€8K/mo Jetix labor (consulting + ops); partner pays retainer.

**Success metric:** Retainer paid on time 6/6 months; ≥3 of 6 resources show measurable improvement (Capital deployed / Knowledge codified / Team grown / etc.); partner extends to month 7+ at same или higher tier; NPS ≥+7.

**Failure metric:** Partner cancels mid-engagement; tier downgrade signals dissatisfaction; <2 resources show improvement; NPS negative.

**Confounders:** Partner has multiple competing service providers (attribution); 6-resource scope creates surface-area attack (something always failing); founder bottleneck on Jetix side limits capacity.

**Precedent test:** Fractional CFO / COO / CMO market (Tier 1 firms €8K-15K/mo, growing 2020-2025); CAA management retainer (talent management); Continuum fractional execs platform; Pareto-Talent.com fractional ops.

**Phase fit:** Phase 1-3 (entire L0-L5 ladder).

**Required L1 fit:** L1 partner с complex operations (not solo content creator); requires existing revenue ≥€500K/y to support retainer comfortably.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) tier downgrade always allowed any month; (b) no lock-in clause (>1 month notice required = OK; lock = R12 violation); (c) all artefacts portable on exit (KB / playbooks / data); (d) baseline resource state documented at start. R12 clean if (a)-(d) hold.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-001 (retainer + revenue share is hedged model — base + upside); combines with H-M-003 (lump-sum integration → retainer is industry-standard funnel); combines with H-M-005 (retainer + performance fee = mgmt+perf classical hedge fund / PE structure); amplifies H-O-002 / H-O-004 (discovery → pilot → retainer ladder).

**Bayesian prior:** **High** — fractional exec market established; Doc 1B L0-L5 ladder pre-designed; primary risk is Jetix capacity/quality at multi-L1 scale.
