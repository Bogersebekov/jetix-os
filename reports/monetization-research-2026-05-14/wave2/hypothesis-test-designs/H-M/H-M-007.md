## H-M-007 — Pay-per-progression — € per L0→L6 mastership step

**Hypothesis statement:** Member pays for explicit progression step (€500 L0→L1 / €1.5K L1→L2 / €5K L2→L3 / €15K L3→L4). Each step delivers credential + access to next tier. R12 concern — pay-to-progress risks pay-to-win; mitigation: skill assessment + peer review gate.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) members value progression credentialing; (b) assessment gate prevents pay-to-win drift; (c) price tiers align с perceived value-at-tier; (d) sustainable conversion across ladder.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: Define L0→L1 assessment (peer review + Quest portfolio); offer 30 members the step at €500; 70% must pass assessment to earn step.
- Cohort: 30 members at L0 level.
- Duration: 2 months (assessment cycle).
- Cost: €3K labor for assessment design + peer reviewer time.

**Success metric:** ≥50% of 30 members opt in; ≥70% pass assessment; ≥85% NPS post-step («felt valuable, not pay-to-win»).

**Failure metric:** <30% opt in (price too high or value unclear); >50% fail assessment (gate too strict OR pre-step training inadequate); NPS negative («felt extracted»); external complaints о pay-to-win on social media.

**Confounders:** Selection bias (members opting in are highest-engaged); assessment subjectivity (peer review variance); progression value depends on external market recognition (chicken-egg).

**Precedent test:** Coursera Specializations ($39-79/mo subscription model — different); Lambda School outcomes-based ISA (17% of salary, capped $30K — closer parallel); martial arts belt progression ($50-300 per belt test, sustained centuries); CFA $1K per level × 3 levels = $3K.

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires established assessment infrastructure + peer reviewer pool ≥10 experienced members).

**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 with audience interested in credentialing (methodology authority types — Цэрэн / Левенчук strong fit).

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) assessment is binding (payment doesn't bypass gate); (b) refund policy if fail assessment; (c) credential is portable post-fork; (d) public charter clause «no rank without assessment»; (e) appeal process for failed assessments. R12 concern remains unless (a)-(e) hold strictly.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-008 (progression fee + cert fee can be unified or split); blocks with H-M-006 (если subscription includes progression access — double-charge); blocks with H-M-009 (Realm rank premium — overlapping); amplifies H-O-008 (mastery class → cert → operator role).

**Bayesian prior:** **Low-Medium** — martial arts/CFA precedent works но requires generations to build credential value; Jetix unknown brand → premium uncertain.
