## H-M-006 — Subscription tier — member dues for Realm participation

**Hypothesis statement:** Audience member pays €15-€50/mo для Realm access (Quests / KB / Clan participation / tooling). Tiered Phase 2+. R12 concern — ongoing extraction; mitigation: clear value-per-month + free baseline tier + no lock-in.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) members willing to pay monthly vs prefer one-time; (b) churn rate sustainable (<8%/mo); (c) free → paid conversion ≥2%; (d) member value perception matches price tier.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: Free tier (read-only KB) + €19/mo basic + €49/mo premium tiered launch; payment via Stripe; cancel-any-time clause prominent.
- Cohort: 100 first audience members (from L1 partner audience activation).
- Duration: 3 months.
- Cost: €2K Stripe + admin; €5K basic Realm infrastructure already deployed.

**Success metric:** ≥3% free → paid conversion; ≥85% month-2 retention; ≥75% month-3 retention; member NPS ≥+5; MRR ≥€1K by month 3.

**Failure metric:** <1% conversion; >15% monthly churn; member NPS negative; complaints о value-for-money.

**Confounders:** Free tier value перетягивает paid signups (TOO much value free); first-cohort selection bias (early adopters); seasonal subscription fatigue (Q1 typical drop).

**Precedent test:** Patreon top tiers (avg paid creator ARPU $7/mo; conversion 2-5%); Substack premium ($5-50/mo, 10% conversion); CrossFit affiliate model (€100-200/mo, 60-80% annual retention); Twitch sub (€4-25, churn 30-40%/mo); Discord Nitro ($10/mo, sustained 5y).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (deployment requires Realm KB readiness; suitable for 50-500 members initial).

**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 archetype с audience ≥1K willing to test paid tier.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) cancel-any-time button visible; (b) prorated refund any time; (c) data export available; (d) free tier preserves member's KB contributions on cancel; (e) no «trap» price changes (60-day notice on price increase). R12 concern remains unless (a)-(e) all hold.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-007 (subscription tier + progression fees may double-charge — blocks); combines with H-M-008 (subscription + one-time cert fee, parallel revenue streams — OK); blocks with H-M-001 if same audience already accruing revenue share through partner (member subsidizes partner extraction); amplifies H-O-001 (free trial → subscription funnel).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — Patreon / Substack proven at scale; uncertain whether Jetix-specific value justifies €19-49/mo vs free Telegram alternatives.
