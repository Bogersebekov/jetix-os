## H-M-001 — Revenue share % on partner audience monetization

**Hypothesis statement:** Jetix берёт 15-25% от incremental revenue (above pre-Jetix baseline) которое L1 partner monetize'ит со своей audience через Realm infrastructure. R12-compliant если pre-Jetix baseline transparent, monthly economic report (EVE MER precedent), fork-and-leave preserves L1 audience.

**Why test:** Сигналь: (a) L1 partner accepts revenue share без принципиального сопротивления; (b) attribution methodology surviveит partner audit; (c) Jetix incremental contribution measurable; (d) monthly economic report sustainable workflow.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 1 L1 partner (Цэрэн или Левенчук) signs revenue-share addendum к pilot agreement; pre-Jetix baseline measured 1 month before pilot; Quest infrastructure deployed.
- Cohort: 1 L1 partner + 30-100 audience members (joint Quest participants); single Realm Clan.
- Duration: 3 months (1 Season).
- Cost: €5K-€15K (Jetix substrate deployment + admin time; partner pays €0 upfront).

**Success metric:** Incremental revenue >€10K в Season 1; partner agrees to 15-25% share without renegotiation; monthly report acked by partner; NPS partner ≥+5 («would recommend Jetix to other influencers»).

**Failure metric:** Incremental revenue <€2K (substrate didn't enable new value); partner disputes attribution methodology; monthly report admin burden >30h/season (unsustainable); NPS partner negative.

**Confounders:** Partner's pre-Jetix audience trajectory (organic growth confused с substrate effect); seasonal effects (Q4 vs Q1); single-L1 sample не generalize.

**Precedent test:** WME/CAA 15-25% talent commission (decades; legitimate); YouTube MCN 5-15% (mostly abandoned 2018-2020 — too low value); Substack 10% platform fee (sustained 2017+; 2M+ subscribers GMV).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1 (per Doc 1B §3 audience operator tier).

**Required L1 fit:** Audience-monetizing partner (blogger / Methodology authority) — Цэрэн / Левенчук / Дудь-class. Не fit для investors / politicians (different overlay).

**R12 retest:** During test verify: (a) baseline transparent; (b) monthly economic report shared; (c) partner can leave with full pre-Jetix audience + own brand; (d) attribution dispute resolution clause exists; (e) fee cap mechanism. If any (a)-(e) breaks → R12 violation → halt.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-004 (TRM retainer — same partner; complementary); combines with H-M-010 (sponsorship brokerage — non-overlapping revenue stream); blocks with H-M-006 if same audience also subscription-charged (double-extraction risk); amplifies H-O-005 (influencer manifest funnel).

**Bayesian prior:** **High** — WME/CAA + Substack + талент-agency model proven; main risk is execution + attribution rigor, not concept validity.
