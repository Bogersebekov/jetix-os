## H-M-010 — Sponsorship intermediation — Jetix brokers brand deals

**Hypothesis statement:** Jetix matches external sponsors (brands, B2B clients) с relevant Clans / individual members for sponsored Quests / content / events; takes 10-15% commission. R12 clean — classical brokerage; member can decline.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) brand demand for Realm-vetted members exists; (b) Jetix brokerage adds value over self-serve sponsorship; (c) member-brand match quality measurable; (d) commission rate sustainable competitive.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 2-3 brand inquiries fielded; 1 matched to Realm Clan; sponsored Quest executed; commission collected.
- Cohort: 1 brand + 1 Clan (3-5 members).
- Duration: 6-8 weeks (deal cycle + Quest execution).
- Cost: €2K admin (brand outreach + Quest design + invoicing).

**Success metric:** Brand deal value ≥€10K; Jetix commission ≥€1K; brand NPS ≥+5; member NPS ≥+5; brand willing to extend to additional deals.

**Failure metric:** Deal falls through; member refuses brand mid-engagement; brand quality complaint (brand-mismatch backlash); commission rate disputed.

**Confounders:** Single deal не generalize; Q1/Q4 seasonal brand budgets; brand reputation independent of Jetix.

**Precedent test:** YouTube MCN 5-15% commission (declined 2018-2020); WME/CAA 10-20% (sustained); GrapeStory / NoGood influencer marketing brokerages (5-25%); Toptal 3% (lower, transparent); upfront agency model alive.

**Phase fit:** Phase 1+ (works at any Realm size с ≥3 members per Clan).

**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 with audience attractive to B2B/B2C brands.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) member can decline brand match without penalty; (b) commission transparent (member sees commission rate); (c) brand-disclosure rules clear (sponsored = labeled); (d) member retains audience independent of brand deal. R12 clean.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-001 (revenue share + brand brokerage — non-overlapping); combines with H-A-015 (audience-rental member + brand brokerage); combines with H-A-002 (freelance bidding + brand brokerage — different deal types); amplifies H-O-005 (influencer manifest funnel — partner with audience).

**Bayesian prior:** **High** — talent agency model decades-proven; risk is volume (need brand demand pipeline) not concept.
