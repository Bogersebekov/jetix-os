## H-M-016 — R&D fund returns — Jetix invests LP-style

**Hypothesis statement:** Jetix R&D fund (90% reinvest per H2 Partnership Model) invests in Realm-member-founded startups; 1-2% mgmt + 20% carry. R12 clean — standard fund model; founders take majority equity.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) Realm members generate fundable startup pipeline; (b) Jetix fund team can do due diligence; (c) carry mechanics workable at small fund size; (d) member-founder accept dilution from R&D fund.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: R&D fund structure (Cayman LP or German GmbH & Co. KG); 3-5 first investments (€20K-€100K each); 7-year fund cycle.
- Cohort: 3-5 portfolio companies; LP base initially just Jetix retained earnings.
- Duration: 7 years (full fund cycle); 24-month check-in for early signals.
- Cost: €50K fund setup + €100K-€500K initial capital deployment.

**Success metric:** ≥3 investments closed Year 1; portfolio IRR ≥15% by Year 5 (modest); ≥1 exit (acquisition or follow-on round) by Year 5; member-founder NPS ≥+5.

**Failure metric:** Zero investments closed; portfolio IRR <0% Year 3 (entire portfolio failing); founder dispute over fund terms; regulatory issue (BaFin if mismanaged).

**Confounders:** Fund returns power-law; 7-year horizon — early signals weak; macro environment (rate cycle) dominates.

**Precedent test:** YC continuation fund / YC Growth; Pioneer Fund; Indie.vc; Calm Company Fund (Tyler Tringas — alternative VC for SaaS); 500 Startups / 500 Global ($1B+ AUM); Sequoia Heritage performance fee structure.

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires capital from 90% reinvest stream; Phase 1 capital deployed to direct revenue ops).

**Required L1 fit:** Investor-archetype L1 (Брагинский / Федорев) могут also become LPs Phase 3+.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) LP terms standard (not founder-extractive); (b) founders retain majority equity (50%+ post-investment); (c) carry vesting + clawback symmetric; (d) member-founder can opt-out of fund (alternative funding routes preserved). R12 clean.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-002 (Jetix can take direct equity + R&D fund equity in same venture, different cap table positions); combines with H-M-015 (Jetix-owned products may also receive R&D fund capital); amplifies H-O-006 (investor allocation funnel).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — fund-of-funds + accelerator-fund model proven; Jetix-specific risk is small fund disadvantages (low capital, no name recognition).
