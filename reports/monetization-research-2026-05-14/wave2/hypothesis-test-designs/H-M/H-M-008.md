## H-M-008 — Certification fee — mastership credential issuance

**Hypothesis statement:** One-time fee для официальной сертификации mastership level (€200 L1 / €1K L2 / €3K L3 / €10K L4 / €30K L5); Jetix issues credential, portable. R12 clean — member keeps credential after fork-and-leave.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) external market recognizes Jetix credential (rate uplift Toptal-class); (b) credential infrastructure (verification API, registry, blockchain-anchored optional) sustainable; (c) renewal cadence (every 2-3y) accepted by members; (d) external employers willing to verify.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 1 credential tier (L1 €200); 50 members eligible; verification page public; cohort survey 6 months post.
- Cohort: 50 L1-eligible members.
- Duration: 6 months (cert issuance + external recognition window).
- Cost: €5K credential platform setup + admin.

**Success metric:** ≥30% of 50 purchase cert; ≥10% report external rate uplift attributable to cert; renewal commitment ≥80% (at 2y, projected); 3+ employer/recruiter inquiries about credential.

**Failure metric:** <10% purchase (no perceived value); zero external recognition (credential is decorative); employer feedback negative («unknown credential»).

**Confounders:** External recognition is chicken-egg (need volume to be recognized; need recognition for volume); member already employed (no immediate uplift opportunity); regulatory drift (e.g., if Standards Body H-M-017 not pursued, credential validity legally ambiguous).

**Precedent test:** AWS Certified ($300/cert + recerts every 3y; >2M certs issued); CFA ($1K per level × 3 = $3K; 200K charter holders globally); PMP ($555 first; ~$3K total path); ICAO pilot license (national-level, regulatory backing).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1-2 (initial cert tier); Phase 3+ requires Standards Body governance (H8 candidate).

**Required L1 fit:** Methodology authority L1 (Цэрэн / Левенчук) for credential legitimacy; less fit для pure influencers.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) credential portable (blockchain anchor or verifiable credential standard W3C VC); (b) Jetix can't revoke valid credential without due process; (c) credential data exportable; (d) revocation criteria public + appealable. R12 clean if (a)-(d) hold.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-007 (progression fee + cert fee unified, or split — different revenue lines); combines with H-A-004 (member earns rate uplift via cert); amplifies H-O-008 (mastery class → cert → operator role); blocks with H-M-017 (Standards Body monopoly risk if Jetix is sole credentialing body).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-High** — AWS / CFA / PMP proven; risk is brand recognition timeline (5-10y typical для new credentialing body to gain external acceptance).
