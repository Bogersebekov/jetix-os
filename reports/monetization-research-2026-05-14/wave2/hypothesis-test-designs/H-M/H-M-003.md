## H-M-003 — Consulting fee — lump-sum upfront for substrate integration

**Hypothesis statement:** Partner pays one-time fee (€20K-€100K) для substrate integration: Realm setup / Charter signing / first Quest design / Clan composition / TRM baseline. Classical fee-for-service; R12 clean — no ongoing extraction.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) partner accepts lump-sum vs prefers retainer; (b) Jetix can deliver integration в scoped fee range; (c) partners use substrate after integration (vs abandon); (d) revenue predictability for Jetix Q1.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: Define standardized integration scope (deliverables enumerated); price 3 tiers (€20K / €50K / €100K).
- Cohort: 2-3 L1 partners receive integration; comparison across tiers.
- Duration: 6-8 weeks per integration; 3-6 months follow-up retention.
- Cost: €5K-€15K Jetix labor per integration (если scope tight).

**Success metric:** ≥2 of 3 partners purchase integration; 80% of deliverables completed on time; ≥66% of integrated partners proceed to retainer (H-M-004) or revenue share (H-M-001) within 3 months.

**Failure metric:** Partners reject lump-sum (prefer monthly или free); integration scope creep (>1.5x cost overrun); <33% retention to next-phase relationship.

**Confounders:** Partner risk aversion (lump-sum requires capital outlay); integration scope ambiguity; Jetix labor cost estimation early-stage uncertain.

**Precedent test:** McKinsey / BCG implementation fees ($100K-$5M); Bain implementation projects; Mondragón cooperative entry training fee (~€2-5K, lower); Reforge cohort fee model ($2K per learner, scales differently).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1 (foundational; works with first 9 L1 + scale through Phase 2).

**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 archetype с CFO/treasury capability to approve lump-sum capex.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) deliverables enumerated upfront; (b) no ongoing fee disguised в lump-sum; (c) partner keeps все integration artifacts after exit; (d) no renewal lock-in. R12 ≈ trivial; main concern incomplete delivery damaging goodwill.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-M-004 (lump-sum upfront → retainer ongoing — standard agency funnel); combines with H-M-001 (lump-sum can be credited against future revenue share, hybrid); blocks with H-M-006 (если member fees expected, lump-sum to partner double-charges); amplifies H-O-001 / H-O-002 (free trial / discovery → lump-sum gate is natural conversion).

**Bayesian prior:** **High** — McKinsey/BCG/Bain implementation pattern proven decades; risk is Jetix scope/delivery discipline, not buyer acceptance.
