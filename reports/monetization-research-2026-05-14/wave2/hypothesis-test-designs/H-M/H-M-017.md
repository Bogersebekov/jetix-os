## H-M-017 — Standards Body certification fees (H8 surface — R12 HIGH RISK)

**Hypothesis statement:** Phase 4+ Jetix evolves to Standards Body (per H8 candidate pool); certifies external practitioners / orgs / workflows. Fees for certification + recurring audit + standard authoring rights. **R12 ✗ HIGH RISK** — monopoly position = extraction by default.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) external market accepts Jetix as Standards authority; (b) governance structure prevents cartel behavior; (c) competing standards bodies can coexist; (d) Mondragón 6:1 pay-ratio + open competing bodies + member-vote governance + fork-and-leave preservation all hold.

**Minimum viable test (Phase 4+):**
- Setup: Phase 4+ ONLY. Requires AWAITING-APPROVAL packet + Ruslan ack + R12 mitigation framework operational.
- Cohort: First certification cohort 20-50 external practitioners.
- Duration: 12 months (cert issuance + market signal).
- Cost: €100K-€500K (Standards Body governance infrastructure + legal + audit).

**Success metric:** External market accepts cert; 0 cartel complaints; competing bodies emerge AND coexist (legitimacy signal); fee growth capped per charter.

**Failure metric:** Regulator capture complaint; cartel behavior emerges (price-fixing / barrier-to-entry abuse); member exodus citing R12 violation; competing bodies suppressed.

**Confounders:** Standards body legitimacy takes 10-20 years to build (ICAO / ISO / IEEE precedent); market timing matters; political environment volatile.

**Phase fit:** Phase 4+ ONLY. **Surface only — NOT recommended without H8 promotion + dedicated AWAITING-APPROVAL packet per Tier 2 R12 enforcement.**
