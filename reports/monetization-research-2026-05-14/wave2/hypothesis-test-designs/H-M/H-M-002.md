## H-M-002 — Equity stake in partner ventures during partnership

**Hypothesis statement:** L1 partner founds venture during partnership; Jetix takes 5-15% common equity for substrate enablement (Realm co-founders / Quest infrastructure / TRM management). R12 risk — equity locks value into Jetix-controlled vehicle vs partner-controlled.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) partner accepts equity terms vs cash retainer; (b) cap table mechanics survive subsequent dilution rounds; (c) Jetix added value justifies founder-tier dilution; (d) Chinese walls hold when Jetix has equity in competing ventures.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: 1 partner venture co-founded within Realm; offer 2 alternative terms (cash retainer per H-M-004 OR equity per H-M-002); partner picks.
- Cohort: 1 venture, 1-3 founders, possibly 5-10 Realm co-builders.
- Duration: 6 months (Pre-seed → product-market validation phase).
- Cost: ~€20K-€60K opportunity cost (foregone retainer revenue).

**Success metric:** Partner picks equity option (≥50% probability); venture reaches Pre-seed milestone (€500K ARR or comparable); Jetix's 5-15% stake survives dilution to ≥1% post-Series A.

**Failure metric:** All offered partners pick cash retainer (signal: equity not perceived as fair exchange); venture fails (Pre-seed not reached); equity dilutes below 0.5% post-Series A (substrate value diluted away).

**Confounders:** Venture success rate is power-law (most fail regardless); partner risk-tolerance heterogeneous; cap table evolution external to Jetix control.

**Precedent test:** YC 7% standard (decades; portfolio outperforms baseline); Techstars 6% + $20K; Pioneer 5% + leaderboard placement; PayPal Mafia rolling-founder equity (informal но retained).

**Phase fit:** Phase 1-2 (foundational partnership terms — early); harder retroactively.

**Required L1 fit:** Entrepreneur-archetype L1 (Гиренко / Тарасов). Не fit для pure influencers (no venture being founded).

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) partner offered alternative (cash retainer) and chose equity; (b) vesting cliff exists (4y standard); (c) cap table transparent; (d) fork-and-leave terminates Jetix equity earn (vesting cliff intact for vested only). If (a)-(d) breaks → R12 violation surfaced.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-007 (Joint venture within Clan — equity flows to multiple founders); combines with H-M-016 (R&D fund LP — Jetix может invest cash AND take equity in same venture, separate cap table positions); blocks with H-M-004 (mutual exclusive per partner choice); amplifies H-O-002 (discovery → pilot → retainer funnel becomes discovery → pilot → equity-or-retainer choice).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — YC + Pioneer precedent strong для accelerator model; less proven для post-accelerator partnership at L1 scale (where partner already established).
