## H-M-013 — Realm marketplace fees — transaction fee on goods/services

**Hypothesis statement:** Members trade goods/services within Realm; Jetix takes 5-10% transaction fee (lower than Patreon 8-12% / Substack 10% / OnlyFans 20%). R12 concern — marketplace fee = ongoing extraction; mitigation: low rate + transparent + zero-fee P2P alternative.

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) GMV growth; (b) take rate sustainable vs black-market formation; (c) zero-fee P2P alternative actually used (or just exists nominally); (d) member compliance with marketplace participation vs off-platform deals.

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: Launch marketplace with 5-10 seed listings (templates / playbooks / coaching); 7.5% transaction fee; zero-fee P2P opt-out explicit.
- Cohort: 30-50 members access marketplace.
- Duration: 3 months.
- Cost: €5K (marketplace infrastructure + payment integration + admin).

**Success metric:** ≥€5K GMV in 3 months; ≥10 transactions; seller NPS ≥+5; ≤20% of deals go zero-fee P2P (i.e., marketplace seen as value-add not just tax).

**Failure metric:** <€1K GMV; >50% of deals route through zero-fee P2P (marketplace adds no value); seller complaints о take rate; black-market formation (members hide deals from Realm).

**Confounders:** Cold-start problem (chicken-egg sellers/buyers); Realm size in Phase 1 insufficient for marketplace network effects; substitute platforms (Gumroad / Etsy) compete.

**Precedent test:** Roblox 30% creator split (anti-pattern Jetix avoids); Patreon 8-12% (sustained); Etsy 6.5% (mature); eBay 13% (declining); Substack 10%; Gumroad 10%; Apple App Store 30% (regulator scrutiny).

**Phase fit:** Phase 2+ (requires ≥100 members for marketplace viability).

**Required L1 fit:** Any L1 with audience interested in member-to-member transactions.

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) take rate locked в charter; can't unilaterally raise; (b) zero-fee P2P alternative functional (not just nominal); (c) fee transparent at transaction time; (d) member dispute resolution at marketplace level (refund mechanism). R12 concern persists unless (a)-(d) hold.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-A-013/014/015 (member sells via marketplace); combines with H-M-014 (cross-Clan TRM-trade fee — different transaction type); blocks with H-M-007/H-M-009 if marketplace access gated behind progression fee (double-charge); amplifies H-O-002 / H-O-004 (TRM ladder uses marketplace as ops layer).

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium** — marketplace fee model proven но Realm-specific value (cooperation premium vs commodity marketplace) untested.
