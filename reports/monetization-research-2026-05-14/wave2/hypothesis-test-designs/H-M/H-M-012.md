## H-M-012 — B2B SaaS substrate — Jetix infra licensed white-label

**Hypothesis statement:** Other organizations / professional networks license Jetix substrate (Realm software / Charter template / governance protocol / TRM tooling) as white-label. €10K-€100K/year per customer org. R12 clean (org-level not member-level).

**Why test:** Сигнал: (a) external orgs perceive Jetix substrate as competitive vs in-house build; (b) packaging Realm software as SaaS achievable; (c) customer support burden sustainable; (d) brand/IP boundaries clear (white-label vs Jetix-branded).

**Minimum viable test:**
- Setup: Build 1-page sales deck + demo Realm instance; reach out to 5 candidate orgs (consulting firms / methodology authorities / professional associations); first paying customer at €10K/year.
- Cohort: 5 sales conversations → ≥1 closed deal.
- Duration: 6-9 months (B2B sales cycle).
- Cost: €15K (sales materials + demo setup + Customer Success time).

**Success metric:** ≥1 deal closed Year 1; deal value ≥€10K; customer NPS ≥+5; renewal commitment by Year 2.

**Failure metric:** Zero closed deals; demo abandons (substrate not ready for productization); support tickets overwhelm Jetix team capacity.

**Confounders:** B2B sales cycle long (6-9mo typical); substrate productization vs custom deployment unclear scope; competitive landscape (Discord/Slack alternatives mature).

**Precedent test:** Discord Nitro for Servers (Phase: B2C → B2B drift; mixed success); Slack Enterprise Grid ($15-25/user/mo, $1B+ rev); ConvertKit Creator network platform; Substack publication tools.

**Phase fit:** Phase 2-3 (requires productized substrate, post-Realm v1 stabilization).

**Required L1 fit:** N/A (B2B customer is org-level, not Jetix L1 partner).

**R12 retest:** Verify: (a) customer org has own R12-equivalent commitment for its members; (b) Jetix doesn't extract from customer org's members directly (passthrough); (c) customer can fork substrate (Open Core or self-host option); (d) standard SaaS T&C with reasonable price-change notice. R12 clean.

**Cross-pollination:** Combines with H-F-005 (shared infrastructure — Realm KB could become SaaS product); blocks with H-M-017 (Standards Body — if Jetix becomes monopoly substrate, R12 violated at customer-org member level); amplifies wider Foundation Part 3 leverage.

**Bayesian prior:** **Medium-Low** — SaaS B2B market is mature but commoditized; Jetix differentiator (R12 + Charter + Realm gamification) unclear vs Slack/Discord; needs strong productization to compete.
