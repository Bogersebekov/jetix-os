## §0 Discipline framing

### §0.1 What narrative sequence means here

«Narrative sequence» = pedagogically-sound + R12-compatible arc через Stage 1-7. NOT a marketing funnel (which optimizes for conversion); это **learning journey** which:
- Honors Bloom progression (Phase 2 §1.2)
- Respects voluntary opt-in at every stage (R12 + Welcome-frame O-144)
- Allows fork-and-leave at any stage без penalty (R12)
- Surfaces appropriate CTA only when stage-criteria met (Phase 2 §4.1)
- Adapts to archetype voice / examples (Phase 4)

### §0.2 Anti-pattern to avoid (R12 STRICT)

| Anti-pattern | Description | R12 violation |
|---|---|---|
| Sales funnel maximization | Optimize для conversion to highest-tier CTA | extraction_beyond_share |
| Bait-and-switch | Promise educational; deliver pitch | fork_prevention_attempt |
| Manipulative narrative arcs | Hero's journey for cult formation | propaganda violation |
| Stage skipping | Surface Stage 5 trial при Stage 1 awareness | Bloom violation + extraction |
| Hidden ladder | Force learner up tier ladder без explicit consent | extraction_beyond_share |

### §0.3 Pattern to follow (R12 paired-frame applied)

Per Welcome-frame O-144 + R12 Tier 2 §11 + Phase 3 §3 default surface:
- **Voluntary at every stage**
- **CTA-NULL always available**
- **Fork-and-leave preserved**
- **Stage transition learner-initiated** (we offer ladder; learner climbs at own pace)

## §1 Master narrative arc (Stage 1 → 7)

### §1.1 High-level narrative shape

```
Stage 1 Awareness   → «у тебя есть выбор: накапливать факты vs ставить прошивку. Нам есть что предложить.» [HOOK]
Stage 2 Interest    → «вот как это применимо к твоей сфере [archetype-specific examples]» [TRANSLATION]
Stage 3 Engagement  → «вот один концептуальный + один практический шаг — попробуй неделю» [APPLICATION]
Stage 4 Discovery   → «где у тебя реальные gaps? Давай посмотрим вместе» [DIAGNOSIS]
Stage 5 Trial       → «вот substrate; попробуй 1-4 недели; измеряй» [EVALUATION]
Stage 6 Partner     → «если работает — есть форматы partnership с full disclosure» [CREATION]
Stage 7 Advocate    → «учи других, contribute substrate — на твоём pace» [TRANSMISSION]
```

### §1.2 Narrative discipline — what NOT to imply

- ❌ «Higher stages = better people» (status-shaming)
- ❌ «You'll be left behind if you don't progress» (FOMO)
- ❌ «We choose you» (cult selection frame)
- ❌ «Only the worthy advance» (gatekeeping)
- ✅ «Each stage is complete in itself; you can stop anywhere» (R12 respect for autonomy)

## §2 Per-Stage detailed content map

### §2.1 Stage 1 Awareness

**Bloom level:** Remember (L1)

**Outcome target:** Learner recalls core thesis + decides whether to engage further

**Narrative arc (3 beats):**
1. **Hook** — surprising fact / counter-intuitive observation. E.g.: «AI делает рутину в 100,000 раз дешевле электричества. Это меняет что мы должны учить.»
2. **Reframe** — alternative paradigm articulated. E.g.: «вместо накопления фактов → ставить «прошивку» (методология + системное мышление + инженерный подход).»
3. **Invitation** — Stage 2 ladder offered. E.g.: «если интересно — вот короткий 5-min explainer / 1-pager / GitHub repo.»

**Principle emphasis:** U1 + U3 (lead) + soft hint к U5

**Content artifacts:**

| Artifact | Form | Duration | Per-archetype variant |
|---|---|---|---|
| Twitter / LinkedIn / mass-platform post | text + image | 1-min read | Engineer + Researcher + Creator + Methodologist + Entrepreneur (5 variants) |
| YouTube short | 60-90 sec video | 1.5 min | 1 universal + 2-3 archetype cuts |
| Telegram channel post | text + image | 2-min read | Russian primary + English option |
| Landing page (entry) | static page | 5-15 min explore | 1 universal с archetype-self-select option |
| 5-min explainer video (Plan A V1 cut) | video | 5 min | 1 universal + 3-4 archetype intros |
| 1-pager standalone | PDF / web | 2-3 min read | 5 archetype variants |

**CTA surface (per Phase 3 §3 + Phase 4):**
- CTA-01 Free explore (repo / wikis link) — primary
- CTA-02 Subscribe (email / Telegram / RSS) — secondary
- CTA-NULL — always available

**Stage transition trigger (per Phase 2 §4.1):**
- Engaged ≥5 min OR returned within 1 week → ladder к Stage 2

**R12 paired-frame check:**
- ✅ No manipulation tactics
- ✅ Voluntary opt-in (no subscription wall)
- ✅ Fork-and-leave (close tab anytime; no tracking beyond standard logs)
- ✅ Specific contact (links given)
- ✅ NO premature commitment ask

**Failure mode (if Stage 1 done wrong):**
- Learner perceives as «yet another productivity guru» (lock-in framing failure)
- Learner cannot recall main message after 24h (no retention)
- Learner conflates с known categories (e.g. «это GTD?»)

## §3 Cross-stage transition decision tree

### §3.1 Transition decisions per stage

```
Stage 1 → 2?
├── Engaged ≥5 min ✅ → Stage 2 invitation
├── Returned within 1 week ✅ → Stage 2 invitation
└── Else → Stage 1 retention loop (or learner drops off — R12 honored)

Stage 2 → 3?
├── Explained back ≥1 principle ✅ → Stage 3 invitation
├── Read ≥2 deeper docs ✅ → Stage 3 invitation
└── Else → Stage 2 deeper content (or learner stays / drops — both OK)

Stage 3 → 4?
├── Applied ≥1 principle in own work ✅ → Stage 4 invitation
├── Asked ≥1 specific Q ✅ → Stage 4 invitation
└── Else → Stage 3 deeper substrate + self-audit kit

Stage 4 → 5?
├── Discovery call completed ✅
├── Gap inventory documented ✅
├── Learner-initiated next steps ✅
└── ALL 3 → Stage 5 invitation
   else → Stay Stage 4 (or fork-and-leave OK)

Stage 5 → 6?
├── Trial completed ✅
├── Outcomes documented ✅
├── Learner-initiated partner conversation ✅
└── ALL 3 → Stage 6 invitation
   else → Stay Stage 5 (extended trial) OR fork-and-leave (R12 honored)

Stage 6 → 7?
├── Partner ≥6 months ✅
├── ≥1 original contribution ✅
├── Mentoring readiness signal ✅
└── ALL 3 → Stage 7 invitation
   else → Stay Stage 6 (deeper partnership)

Stage 7 → ongoing
└── (sustained content cadence + mentee progress)
```

### §3.2 R12 enforcement at every transition

- ✅ Learner-initiated transition (we don't push)
- ✅ Criteria explicit (no hidden gates)
- ✅ Fork-and-leave honored at every stage
- ❌ NO «Why aren't you progressing?» nagging (recruitment-dynamics R12 violation)
- ❌ NO «Other learners are at higher tier» social proof (manipulation)
