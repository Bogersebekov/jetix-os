# Phase 2 — Learning Outcomes Specification

> **Mandate ⭐⭐⭐:** specify per-stage (1-7) what learner **can do** (Bloom Apply+),
> **knows** (knowledge outcomes), **умеет** (skill outcomes), **делает** (behavior
> change outcomes) после consumption.
>
> **Substrate:** Bloom Taxonomy 1956 (6 levels) + Anderson-Krathwohl 2001 revision

## §1 Discipline framing

### §1.1 Why Bloom progression matters

Per Bloom 1956 + Anderson-Krathwohl 2001 — learning hierarchies. NOT all outcomes are equivalent. **Apply** (Stage 3) requires more scaffolding than **Remember** (Stage 1). **Create** (Stage 6) requires established **Analyze** + **Evaluate**. Skipping levels = «cargo cult» learning.

Per UbD (Wiggins-McTighe) — **backward design**: start with desired results → evidence of achievement → learning activities. We design content Stage 1-7 starting from outcomes, not from content.

[src: Bloom 1956 + UbD 1998 + RESEARCH-EDUCATION 2026-05-24 §1 + Tomlinson-McTighe 2006]

### §1.2 Bloom levels (Anderson-Krathwohl 2001 revision)

| Bloom level | Cognitive verb | Description | Stage map (default) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 Remember** | recall / list / name / identify | recognise / recall facts | Stage 1 Awareness |
| **2 Understand** | explain / summarise / interpret / paraphrase | grasp meaning / translate | Stage 2 Interest |
| **3 Apply** | use / apply / implement / demonstrate | use in new situation | Stage 3 Engagement |
| **4 Analyze** | compare / differentiate / break-down / diagnose | break into parts; see relationships | Stage 4 Discovery |
| **5 Evaluate** | judge / critique / audit / assess | make judgments based on criteria | Stage 5 Trial |
| **6 Create** | design / construct / produce / formulate | put pieces together as new whole | Stage 6 Partner |
| **(meta) Transmit** | teach / mentor / explain to others | Feynman test (post-Bloom proper, but functional) | Stage 7 Advocate |

[src: Anderson-Krathwohl 2001 + Bloom 1956 + Feynman test (folk knowledge) + RESEARCH-EDUCATION 2026-05-24 §3.1]

### §1.3 Knowledge dimension (Anderson-Krathwohl 2001)

| Knowledge type | Description | Where in our funnel |
|---|---|---|
| Factual | facts / terminology / specifics | Stage 1 «всё = информация + методы» |
| Conceptual | classifications / theories / models | Stage 2-3 «meta-method» / «системное мышление» |
| Procedural | how-to / skill / method | Stage 3-4 «как применять» |
| Metacognitive | strategic / self-knowledge | Stage 5-7 «self-audit» / «adequate intellect chooses» |

[src: Anderson-Krathwohl 2001 4×6 matrix]

### §1.4 Critical: NO tool-specific outcomes

Per Phase 1 §6.1 anti-content discipline — outcomes specified **только в terms of universal principles + own-work application**, NOT в terms of «using Notion» / «using Claude Code» / «navigating Jetix wiki». Tool-specific outcomes deferred Stage 5+ (Trial onwards) with learner explicit ack.

### §2.2 Stage 1 Awareness (Bloom: Remember)

**Bloom level:** Remember (Anderson-Krathwohl L1)

**Knowledge type:** Factual + early Conceptual

**Learner CAN do (after consumption ~15 min):**

1. **Recall core thesis** — «всё = информация + методы переработки; качество жизни = качество методов × selection»
2. **Recall AI-stratification one-liner** — «AI = external system feedback amplifier, не замена мышления»
3. **Recall the «adequate intellect» distinction** — выбирает методы которые развивают intellect, не shortcuts
4. **Identify** что есть Jetix-style universal approach (vs tool-specific tutorials)
5. **Name** 1-2 universal principles (U1 + U3 minimum)

**Knowledge outcomes (что знает):**
- Существует **alternative learning paradigm** (anti-shortcut / question-first / прошивка-first)
- AI ≠ замена мышления
- Methods > facts (один method для выбора method > 100 facts to memorize)

**Skill outcomes (что умеет — minimal Stage 1):**
- (нет skill mandate Stage 1 — only Remember)
- ⭐ But: minimum signal of intent to apply (subscribe / bookmark / save)

**Behavior change outcomes (что делает):**
- Pause perfectionism шорткаты mindset; consider alternative
- Subscribe для дальнейшего contact (Stage 2 trigger)
- Refers 1-2 others (optional viral)

**Evidence of achievement (UbD Stage 2):**
- Can answer «what was the main message?» in own words
- Can name 1-2 principles
- Subscription / bookmark action

**Content artifact (forward-cite Phase 5):**
- 1-pager landing
- 5-min explainer video (V1 anchor 12-15 min OR 5-min cut)
- Twitter / LinkedIn post / YouTube short

**Failure mode (Bloom miss):**
- Learner forgets within 24h (no retention scaffolding)
- Learner confuses Jetix как «yet another productivity tool» (lock-in framing failure)

### §2.3 Stage 2 Interest (Bloom: Understand)

**Bloom level:** Understand (L2)

**Knowledge type:** Conceptual

**Learner CAN do (after ~1-3h):**

1. **Explain meta-method principle к own domain** — «if I'm engineer, meta-method для меня = choosing architecture pattern under constraint»; «if researcher = choosing research design»; etc.
2. **Translate** universal principle to own vocabulary
3. **Summarise** «question-first not function-first» discipline
4. **Compare** Jetix-style approach vs other learning paradigms learner has tried (университет / online courses / bootcamps / self-learning)
5. **Recognise** «прошивка» concept (системное мышление + инженерный подход + ответственность)

**Knowledge outcomes (что знает):**
- 7 universal principles (U1-U7) at conceptual level
- Distinction между «прошивка» (baseline) vs «tool layer» (instrument)
- AI stratification framework (где AI = max, где human = max)

**Skill outcomes (что умеет):**
- Can articulate «what's my baseline прошивка gap?» in own work
- Can identify 1-2 places где она applies AI shortcut vs developing method choice

**Behavior change outcomes (что делает):**
- Pauses workflow и asks «зачем я это делаю?» occasionally
- Tries 1 new framing in own work (e.g. «hypothesis-driven» instead of feature-list)
- Reads 1-2 additional pieces (deepening)

**Evidence of achievement (UbD):**
- Can explain principles в own words к colleague
- Can identify 1-2 places где principles apply в own work
- Engages с longer-form content (Stage 3 trigger)

**Content artifact:**
- Welcome-frame doc
- 1-pager tier-distinct (per archetype)
- FAQ with 10-15 common questions
- 5-min explainer video с per-archetype examples
- Method V2 distilled (high-level overview, NOT full Method V2)

**Failure mode:**
- Learner understands intellectually but cannot translate to own context (gap)
- Learner conflates с other frameworks они уже знают (e.g. «это просто agile / lean / GTD?»)

### §2.4 Stage 3 Engagement (Bloom: Apply)

**Bloom level:** Apply (L3)

**Knowledge type:** Procedural + Conceptual

**Learner CAN do (after ~5-10h):**

1. **Apply «question-first» к own workflow daily** — pause перед task, ask «зачем + какая гипотеза + что ожидаю узнать»
2. **Apply «method-of-methods» к 1 specific decision** — identify 2-3 candidate methods, pick один с rationale
3. **Apply AI-stratification audit к own AI usage** — list 3 tasks где AI = max leverage, 3 где human = max
4. **Use** «прошивка baseline» frame для own gap identification
5. **Implement** 1 concrete behavior change (e.g. weekly self-audit ritual)

**Knowledge outcomes (что знает):**
- All Phase 1 7+3 principles at procedural level (not just conceptual)
- Knows 1-2 frameworks для applying principles (e.g. weekly review checklist; pre-task pause protocol)
- Knows where Jetix specifically provides templates / examples (Stage 4+ ladder)

**Skill outcomes (что умеет):**
- Pre-task pause + question-first protocol (operationalized)
- AI-stratification audit (one-time)
- Method-of-methods selection (1 case study completed)
- Weekly self-audit ritual (started)

**Behavior change outcomes (что делает):**
- Daily: pre-task pause (at least 1x/day)
- Weekly: 15-min self-audit
- Monthly: re-audit AI-stratification
- Reads 5-10 deeper Jetix substrate articles
- Has «aha» moment about gap in own workflow

**Evidence of achievement (UbD):**
- Documented 1 specific application of principle в own work (journal entry / blog / Slack)
- Has 1-2 questions for deeper discovery (Stage 4 trigger)
- Engages with content for 5-10h cumulative

**Content artifact:**
- Navigation Guide (substrate evidence index)
- 5-7 deeper substrate articles
- Self-audit template (downloadable)
- Pre-task pause checklist (1-pager downloadable)
- CTA: book discovery call (free)

**Failure mode:**
- Knowledge-without-action gap (understands but doesn't apply)
- Applies once, doesn't sustain (habit-formation gap per Dirksen)
- Confuses self-audit с «journaling» (loses precision)

[src: prompt §3.A + Bloom L3 + Dirksen 2015 habits gap + Make-It-Stick 2014 spaced practice + Phase 1 U1-U7+NEW-3]

### §2.5 Stage 4 Discovery call (Bloom: Analyze)

**Bloom level:** Analyze (L4)

**Knowledge type:** Procedural + Metacognitive

**Learner CAN do (after 30-60 min discovery + 2-5h self-work):**

1. **Diagnose** own methodology gaps (what's missing in прошивка baseline?)
2. **Compare** own current method-of-methods skill vs «adequate» level
3. **Differentiate** между cases где AI works для них vs where it fails
4. **Break down** own workflow into component methods (decomposition)
5. **Identify** 2-3 specific Jetix substrate pieces that would address gaps
6. **Articulate** own learning question (what they specifically want to learn next)

**Knowledge outcomes (что знает):**
- Own gap inventory (post-diagnosis с brigadier / human reviewer)
- Jetix substrate map at high level (knows what's where)
- Aware of L5/L6/L7 partner tier options (но не tier-specific economic mechanics)

**Skill outcomes (что умеет):**
- Self-diagnose intellectual leverage gaps
- Decompose own workflow into methods
- Compare own approach к peer / SOTA

**Behavior change outcomes (что делает):**
- Books 30-60 min discovery call (with brigadier / Ruslan / proxy)
- Prepares 2-3 specific questions
- Self-audit becomes consistent (weekly+)
- Considers trial commitment (1-week → 1-month)

**Evidence of achievement (UbD):**
- Discovery call completed
- Gap inventory documented (joint with reviewer)
- 2-3 next-steps articulated
- Stage 5 trial commitment OR Stage 3 deeper engagement

**Content artifact:**
- Discovery call script (for reviewer)
- Pre-call questionnaire (for learner; 10 min fill)
- Gap inventory template
- Trial commitment options doc (no tier-economic detail; only commitment levels)

**Failure mode:**
- Learner gets analytical paralysis (diagnoses too many gaps; can't act)
- Discovery call surfaces extraction-style ask too early (R12 violation; learner backs off)
- Reviewer over-prescribes Jetix substrate (lock-in failure)

[src: prompt §3.A + Bloom L4 + Dirksen 2015 gap analysis 6-categories + Phase 1 NEW-3 self-audit]

### §2.6 Stage 5 Trial (Bloom: Evaluate)

**Bloom level:** Evaluate (L5)

**Knowledge type:** Metacognitive + Procedural

**Learner CAN do (after 1-4 weeks trial):**

1. **Audit own intellectual leverage** (AI usage stratification) — formal evaluation
2. **Critique** own method-of-methods selection skill (где applies / где fails)
3. **Assess** Jetix tool / template / substrate fit к own context
4. **Judge** quality of own «прошивка» baseline (системное мышление + инженерный + ответственность scoring)
5. **Evaluate** trial outcomes against pre-trial baseline (objective metrics — time freed / decisions improved / etc.)

**Knowledge outcomes (что знает):**
- Personal evaluation framework (own metrics for «adequate intellect» growth)
- Detailed knowledge of 1-2 Jetix substrate pieces that helped most
- Trade-offs известны (что не работает у learner specifically)

**Skill outcomes (что умеет):**
- Self-evaluation cadence (weekly minimum; monthly deep)
- Method comparison protocol (A/B in own work)
- Trial outcome measurement (defined metrics)

**Behavior change outcomes (что делает):**
- 1-4 week trial of Jetix tools / templates / substrate use
- Daily / weekly application с metrics tracking
- Honest evaluation at trial end (continue / pivot / leave)
- Decision: go to Partner stage OR continue Trial OR Stage 7 Advocate OR fork-and-leave

**Evidence of achievement (UbD):**
- Documented trial outcomes (own metrics)
- 1-page reflection on Jetix-fit
- Decision made (continue / pivot / leave) with rationale
- (If continue) trial-to-partner conversion conversation booked

**Content artifact:**
- Trial onboarding kit (1-week / 2-week / 4-week variants)
- Trial measurement template
- Trial-end reflection prompt
- Partner tier options doc (L5/L6/L7 introduced here с economic mechanics surfaced)
- R12 paired-frame disclosure document (Mondragón cap / fork-and-leave / 30-day opt-out)

**Failure mode:**
- Trial without measurement (gut-feel decisions = high regret)
- Sunk-cost continuation (trial failed но learner persists out of obligation)
- Premature partner conversion (learner not yet evaluated honestly)

### §2.7 Stage 6 Partner (Bloom: Create)

**Bloom level:** Create (L6)

**Knowledge type:** Metacognitive + Conceptual + Procedural

**Learner CAN do (after 3-12 months partnership):**

2. **Construct** novel content / template / method for cohort use
3. **Design** experiments to test new applications of principles
4. **Produce** original wiki content / blog posts / videos applying principles
5. **Formulate** improvements to Jetix substrate (contribute back)
6. **Build** new tier-economic patterns OR contribute к existing (per R12 paired-frame)

**Knowledge outcomes (что знает):**
- Deep Jetix substrate (Method V2 / Strategic Plan / Foundation Architecture / R12 paired-frame / IP-1 STRICT / Pillar C Tier 2 — full vocabulary)
- Tier economic mechanics (75/25 / QF / Mondragón cap / NFT-stake / Charter)
- Cohort dynamics (cohort target ontology / Welcome-frame / R12 anti-extraction)
- Novel domains application (beyond Jetix-original substrate)

**Skill outcomes (что умеет):**
- Co-create methodology applications (substantive contribution)
- Mentor Stage 1-5 learners (teaching = strengthens own understanding per Feynman test)
- Design extensions to Jetix substrate
- Apply R12 paired-frame discipline in own contributions

**Behavior change outcomes (что делает):**
- L4/L5/L6/L7 partnership entry (per Charter signed; R12 disclosures ack)
- Regular contribution cadence (weekly / monthly)
- Cohort participation (peer learning + mentoring junior members)
- Self-audit at L6 cadence (monthly minimum; quarterly deep)

**Evidence of achievement (UbD):**
- Charter signed
- 1+ original contributions to Jetix substrate (per partnership level)
- Mentoring 1+ junior member
- Quarterly review with brigadier / partner-circle

**Content artifact:**

### §2.8 Stage 7 Advocate / Transmit (post-Bloom: Teach)

**Bloom level:** Transmit (functional post-Bloom; Feynman test = teach to learn deeper)

**Knowledge type:** All 4 (Factual + Conceptual + Procedural + Metacognitive) + Pedagogical Content Knowledge (Shulman)

**Learner CAN do (ongoing):**

1. **Teach approach to others** at Stage 1-6 (mentoring + advocacy + content production)
2. **Refer / bring others** (network effect; voluntary opt-in per R12)
3. **Author content / wiki / video** original work
4. **Mentor cohort** at scale (1:few / 1:many)
5. **Refine** own teaching based on student outcomes (Feynman test loop)
6. **Defend** Jetix-style approach against critique (deep knowledge required)

**Knowledge outcomes (что знает):**
- All Stage 1-6 knowledge mastered
- Pedagogical Content Knowledge (Shulman 1986 — how to teach this specifically)
- Student misconception patterns (что typically misunderstood)
- Adaptation patterns per archetype (engineer / researcher / etc.)

**Skill outcomes (что умеет):**
- Stage-appropriate teaching (calibrates к learner Bloom level)
- Common misconception correction (anticipates + addresses)
- Pacing / scaffolding (Vygotsky ZPD applied)
- Authentic enthusiasm (not performative)
- R12 paired-frame discipline в own teaching (anti-extraction; voluntary opt-in)

**Behavior change outcomes (что делает):**
- Regular content production (weekly / monthly / quarterly per cadence agreed)
- Mentoring (1+ Stage 1-5 learner concurrent)
- Refers 5-25 others / year (voluntary network effect)
- Refines own understanding through teaching (Feynman compound)

**Evidence of achievement (UbD):**
- Original content portfolio
- Mentee progress (mentees reach Stage 4+ over time)
- Community contributions (forums / cohort / events)
- Quarterly review with senior partner circle

**Content artifact:**
- Advocate materials (badges / kits / talking points)
- Teaching templates (per-archetype variants)
- Content authoring guidelines (with R12 audit checklist)
- Mass-advocate ethics doc (anti-MLM / anti-extraction discipline)

**Failure mode:**
- Performative advocacy (signals belonging but doesn't teach effectively)
- Cult-like behavior (excessive enthusiasm without critical thinking; R12 violation)
- Pyramid-recruitment temptation (refers others for status, not learner benefit; R12 anti-extraction violation Halt-Log-Alert)

## §4 Stage transition triggers (when to surface next-stage CTA)

### §4.1 Transition criteria (Stage N → N+1)

| Transition | Trigger criteria | If criteria not met |
|---|---|---|
| 1 → 2 | Learner engaged ≥5 min OR returned within 1 week | Stay Stage 1 retention loop |
| 2 → 3 | Learner explained back ≥1 principle OR read ≥2 deeper docs | Stay Stage 2 (more variants / FAQ) |
| 3 → 4 | Learner applied ≥1 principle in own work OR asked ≥1 specific Q | Stay Stage 3 deeper substrate + self-audit kit |
| 4 → 5 | Discovery call completed AND gap-inventory documented AND learner-initiated «next steps» ask | Stay Stage 4 (more substrate / second call) |
| 5 → 6 | Trial completed AND outcomes documented AND learner-initiated partner conversation | Stay Stage 5 (extended trial) OR fork-and-leave (R12-honored) |
| 6 → 7 | Partner ≥6 months AND ≥1 original contribution AND mentoring readiness signal | Stay Stage 6 (deeper partnership) |
| 7 → 7+ | (ongoing) — content production cadence sustained | Reduce production cadence / sabbatical option |

### §4.2 What NOT to do at transition

- ❌ Push CTA before criteria met (R12 anti-extraction; manipulation)
- ❌ Skip stages (Bloom violation; not durable)
- ❌ Default to «higher tier = better» framing (lock-in pressure)
- ❌ Imply staying at Stage N = «not enough» (shame frame; R12 violation)
- ❌ Surface advanced CTAs (donate / partner / co-create) at Stage 1-2 (premature commitment ask)

## §6 Failure-mode inventory (cross-stage)

### §6.1 Bloom-level violations

- **Skip Apply (Stage 3)** → learner stays at Understand forever; never embodied. Result: «yet another framework I know but don't use.»
- **Skip Analyze (Stage 4)** → Trial without diagnosed gaps; trial fails because addresses wrong gap.
- **Skip Evaluate (Stage 5)** → Partner without honest evaluation; high churn risk + R12 violation pressure.

### §6.2 Knowledge-dimension violations

- **Procedural without Conceptual** → cargo cult («I follow checklist but don't know why»)
- **Conceptual without Procedural** → armchair philosopher («I understand but never apply»)
- **No Metacognitive Stage 4+** → cannot self-improve; cannot mentor; advocacy hollow

### §6.3 Time-investment mismatch

- **Stage 1 → 6 in 1 week** → premature commitment; high regret risk
- **Stage 3 → 3 для 2 years** → engagement plateau; learner drifts away
- **Stage 6 indefinitely without 7 progression** → comfortable partner who never contributes back; cohort dilution

- **Cannot fork-and-leave at any stage** → R12 violation; trust collapse
- **Tier obligations not surface-able pre-Stage 5** → discovery without disclosure manipulation
