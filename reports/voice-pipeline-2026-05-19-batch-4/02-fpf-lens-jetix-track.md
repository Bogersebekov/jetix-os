## §4 Critical FPF coherence checks

### §4.1 Voluntary cooperation (R12 + corrigibility)

Tension identified (cell 1.3 + 5.3): «заставляем сотрудничать» + «all-in или нихуй по-другому» rhetoric vs R12 anti-extraction + corrigibility (no lock-in).

**Resolution proposal**: 
- Operational rhetoric (private/internal): "all-in", "Jetix всё равно придёт"
- Public-facing rhetoric: explicit voluntary + fork-and-leave + R12 anti-extraction
- Concept docs §APPEND should explicit voluntary clause

### §4.2 Stage 1 elitism vs Stage 2 equal-conditions

Tension (cell 4.3 + 6.3): «умненькие vs обезьяны» (audio_685) vs «equal conditions, no exclusives» (audio_687).

**Resolution**: Two distinct phase semantics. Stage 1 = selectivity (elite engineering substrate). Stage 2 = universality (equal participation). NOT contradiction; phase-distinction.

### §4.3 Anti-Sisyphus framing

Camus-compatible reframe (cell 3.3): Jetix = meaning-construction substrate, NOT objective-meaning metaphysics. Avoid philosophical overcommitment.
