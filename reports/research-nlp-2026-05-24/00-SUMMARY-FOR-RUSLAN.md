## 🔑 5 most important findings

### 1. NLP's most-marketed claims ARE FALSIFIED

Settled empirical questions:
- **VAK как fixed cognitive type** → falsified by Sharpley 1984 + Pashler 2009 *Psychological Science in the Public Interest*

- **«Cure phobia / addiction / depression в одном сеансе»** → no replicated RCT support (Heap 1988, Sturt 2012 BMJ systematic review)
- **NLP-sales / NLP-rapport-for-influence** → U.S. National Research Council 1988 explicitly: «no evidence ... improve influence on others»

Эти claims в NLP-marketed материале до сих пор появляются. **HARD SKIP regardless of context.**
