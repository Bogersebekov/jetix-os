> **R12 STRICT pre-screen**: каждая technique surfaced ниже carries: (a) ethical-use precondition, (b) R12-compatibility check, (c) abuse-mode flag, (d) empirical-status pending Phase 6. Default-deny если ANY missing.

## §1.0 Founders' frame — что НЛП было заявлено как

Bandler (математик/программист, ~21 лет на момент Vol I) + Grinder (лингвист, transformational grammar) — UC Santa Cruz, ранние 1970s. Стартовали из попытки **smodel**: формализовать что три **звезды психотерапии** делают одинаково/различно — Fritz Perls (Gestalt), Virginia Satir (family therapy), Milton Erickson (medical hypnosis).

> «We call ourselves modelers. What we essentially do is to pay very little attention to what people say they do and a great deal of attention to what they do. ... We know that our modeling has been successful when we can systematically get the same behavioral outcome as the person we have modeled.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 p ~262-275]

**Frame founders adopted**: NLP не теория личности; НЛП = **explicit modeling discipline** для therapeutic effectiveness, без epistemological claim о том «как мозг устроен». Однако в commercial popularization (Phase 4: Robbins) этот frame потерян; cognitive-science-style claims о «как мозг работает» появились позже без empirical backing.

### §1.0.1 Foreword frame Steve Andreas + R12 pre-screen

> «A few specific examples of things you can learn to accomplish are: (1) cure phobias and other unpleasant feeling responses in less than an hour, (2) help children and adults with 'learning disabilities' (spelling and reading problems, etc.) often in less than an hour, (3) eliminate most unwanted habits — smoking, drinking, over-eating, insomnia, etc. — in a few sessions ... (4) make changes in the interactions of couples, families and organizations so that they function in ways that are more satisfying and productive, (5) cure many physical problems — not only most of those recognized as 'psychosomatic' but also some that are not — in a few sessions.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 foreword p ~53-65]

**R12-relevant abuse-mode flag**: эти claims «curing phobia in under an hour» / «curing physical problems in few sessions» = **empirically falsified or unsupported** (Phase 6 will document). При R12 paired-frame STRICT — promoting such claims without empirical caveat violates **R6 provenance** (claim cited без validation) AND indirectly **R12 extraction_beyond_share** (user pays for promised cure, gets technique без replication evidence).

> **SKIP-list candidate #1**: any claim о NLP «curing» clinical disorder in single session → SKIP unless externally replicated. Andreas foreword represents original founders' overclaim that downstream popularization amplified.

## §1.1 Meta-model (Structure of Magic Vol I 1975)

### §1.1.1 Theoretical claim

Meta-model = formal grammar-derived therapy technique. Borrowed from Chomsky's transformational grammar (Deep Structure → Surface Structure).

> «The transformational Meta-models describe these concepts and mechanisms explicitly — they are specific cases of the general modeling processes of Generalization, Distortion and Deletion. In adapting the concepts and mechanisms of the transformational model of the human representational system of language for the purposes of therapy, we developed a formal Meta-model for therapy.» [src: bandler-grinder-structure-of-magic-1975 p ~310-330]

**Premise**: client's Surface Structure (что говорит) — impoverished projection of Deep Structure (полный experiential model). Через специфические questions therapist surfaces deletions, distortions, generalizations и reconnects client к более полной carte.

### §1.1.2 12 Meta-model patterns (canonical list)

Three families × four patterns:

**A. Gathering Information (Deletions + lack of Referential Index + Unspecified Verbs + Nominalizations):**

1. **Simple Deletions** — «Я несчастен» → «По поводу чего? Несчастен с какого аспекта?»
2. **Comparative Deletions** — «лучше», «хуже» без сравнения — «Лучше чем что?»
3. **Lack of Referential Index** — «Они меня не любят» → «Кто конкретно?»
4. **Unspecified Verbs** — «Он меня обидел» → «Конкретно как обидел? Что сделал?»
5. **Nominalizations** — превращение процесса в существительное («депрессия» вместо «депрессирую»). «Что конкретно происходит, когда вы говорите 'у меня депрессия'?»

**B. Expanding Limits (Universal Quantifiers + Modal Operators):**

6. **Universal Quantifiers** — «никогда», «все», «всегда». «Никогда? Ни одного раза?»
7. **Modal Operators of Necessity** — «должен», «обязан», «нельзя». «Что случится, если этого НЕ сделать?»
8. **Modal Operators of Possibility** — «не могу». «Что вас останавливает?»

**C. Changing Meanings (Complex Equivalence + Presuppositions + Cause-Effect + Mind-Reading + Lost Performative):**

9. **Complex Equivalence** — «Он не звонит = он меня не любит». «Откуда вы знаете, что A означает B?»
10. **Presuppositions** — implicit assumptions. «Если вы знали бы, что моя жена меня любит, я бы не страдал» → presupposes «моя жена меня НЕ любит».
11. **Cause-Effect** — «Он меня раздражает». «Как именно его поведение CAUSES вашу реакцию?»
12. **Mind-Reading** — «Я знаю, что он думает обо мне плохо». «Как вы это знаете?»
13. **Lost Performative** — judgments без speaker («Это плохо»). «Согласно кому?»

[src: bandler-grinder-structure-of-magic-1975 throughout Vol I + summary Vol II p ~10767-10948]

### §1.1.3 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**R12-compatibility:** ✅ Mostly compatible — Meta-model questions = **Socratic questioning разновидность**. Practitioner делает explicit предложение surface deletions/distortions; client volunteer-engages.

**Abuse-mode flag:** ⚠️ Meta-model questions can be deployed as **attack pattern** (rapid-fire challenge без rapport) — manipulative interrogation. Anti-pattern: «interrogate prospect with meta-model until objection collapses». Sales abuse documented.

**Empirical status (Phase 6 preview):** Meta-model questions = compatible с broader Socratic / cognitive-therapy tradition (Beck, Ellis). Independent empirical support for **questioning-as-cognitive-restructuring** = strong (CBT meta-analyses). NLP-specific framing (deep-structure recovery) ≠ validated; the **functional component** (asking «what specifically?» вместо acceptance of vague statement) overlaps с CBT discipline.

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** Meta-model maps strongly к **FPF F-G-R triple discipline** — every claim should be questioned for (F)ormality / (G)roup-scope / (R)eliability. Meta-model = F-G-R operationalized в conversational interaction. POTENTIAL IMPORT.

## §1.2 Representational Systems (VAK — Visual, Auditory, Kinesthetic)

### §1.2.1 Theoretical claim

NLP claims people have **highly valued / preferred representational system** (PRS) — predominantly visual, auditory, or kinesthetic. Identified via:
- **Predicates in language** — «I see what you mean» (V), «sounds good» (A), «I feel that's right» (K)
- **Eye accessing cues** — looking up-left = visual remembered, up-right = visual constructed, etc.

> «Some people will be primarily visual, internally generating images. Some people will be auditory, generating sounds or words internally. Some people will be kinesthetic, generating feelings or sensations.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 p ~546-555]

> «When you ask visual memory questions and a person looks up to their left as they respond to questions about visual memory ... Barbara, here ... left for visual eidetic images and up and to their right for constructed visual.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 p ~804-830]

### §1.2.2 Two distinct claims to separate

1. **Communication adaptation (weak claim)**: notice predicates client uses, match them → improved rapport. Compatible с pragmatic communication literature.
2. **Preferred Representation System as fixed cognitive type (strong claim)**: person «is visual» / «is auditory» — quasi-trait. **EMPIRICALLY FALSIFIED**.
3. **Eye accessing cues as reliable indicators of internal processing modality (specific claim)**: looking direction = which sub-modality being accessed. **EMPIRICALLY FALSIFIED** (Sharpley 1984/1987 + multiple replications failed).

### §1.2.3 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**R12-compatibility (weak claim — matching predicates):** ✅ Compatible если voluntary + transparent. Comparable к active-listening discipline (Carl Rogers).

**R12-compatibility (strong/specific claims):** ⚠️ Problematic in two ways:
- (a) **Pseudoscientific authority extraction** — practitioner claims to «know» client's internal state from external cue → extracts authority беспардонно. R12-adjacent: **extraction_beyond_share** if billed as «expert reading».
- (b) **Empirical falsification** — promoting falsified claim violates R6 provenance.

**Empirical status (Phase 6 preview):**
- Sharpley C.F. (1984). «Predicate matching in NLP: A review of research on the preferred representational system.» *Journal of Counseling Psychology* 31(2): 238-248. — **no support for PRS as stable trait**.
- Sharpley C.F. (1987). «Research findings on neurolinguistic programming: nonsupportive data or untestable theory?» *Journal of Counseling Psychology* 34(1): 103-107. — eye accessing cues + PRS predicates **not validated**.
- Heap M. (1988). Heap survey — same conclusion.
- Druckman & Swets (1988) NRC report — **no scientific basis для NLP performance claims** including representational systems.

> **SKIP-list candidates #2-#4**:
> - SKIP #2: VAK as fixed cognitive type → empirically falsified (do NOT import)
> - SKIP #3: Eye accessing cues as internal-process detector → empirically falsified
> - SKIP #4: Pseudoscientific authority extraction via «I see you're an auditory person» framing

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** ONLY weak claim (predicate matching as communication discipline) candidate for import — and even then, generic active-listening literature (Bolton Phase 5) provides equivalent guidance without NLP-specific pseudoscientific baggage. **DEFAULT SKIP entire VAK framework**; import generic active-listening from Bolton instead.

## §1.3 Rapport / Pacing / Leading

### §1.3.1 Theoretical claim

**Rapport** = state of trust + responsiveness between communicator and respondent. NLP claims it can be **systematically built** via:

- **Mirroring** — match body posture, breathing rate, voice tempo
- **Pacing** — match the *content* of experience (predicate language, current emotional state)
- **Leading** — after sufficient pacing, change own behavior; if rapport established, respondent will follow

> «If you want to get good rapport, you can speak using the same kind of predicates that the other person is using. ... 'rapport' for a ghetto person, 'rapport' for a white middle-class person, and 'rapport' for someone in the top one hundred families in this country, are radically different experiences.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 p ~558, 623-624]

### §1.3.2 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**Weak claim (general empathy/communication-matching = pro-relationship):** ✅ R12-compatible if transparent. Aligns с Rogerian congruence + Bolton's reflective listening Phase 5.

**Strong claim (covert mirroring → behavioral compliance via «leading»):** ❌ **R12-incompatible.**
- **Pacing-leading sales pattern** — establish rapport, then «lead» к purchase decision = **covert behavioral influence**. Violates R12 voluntary opt-in.
- **Mirroring без disclosure** = manipulation если intent is не «communicate empathy» but «extract compliance».

**Empirical status (Phase 6 preview):**
- Mirroring + matching = real phenomenon (chameleon effect — Chartrand & Bargh 1999, empirically validated).
- Mirroring-as-systematic-influence-technique = weaker evidence. Effect sizes small; works for **liking** but not robustly for **decision change**.
- Pacing-leading as deployed in NLP-sales = **no controlled studies validating compliance gain** beyond standard rapport effects.

**Abuse-mode documented:** NLP sales seminars широко teach pacing-leading explicitly для closing technique — directly R12-violating use case. Robbins (Phase 4) popularized.

> **SKIP-list candidate #5**: Covert pacing-leading в sales contexts → R12 incompatible (extraction_beyond_share + non_consensual influence).

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** Disclosed rapport-building (active listening discipline) ≅ Bolton People Skills + Welcome-frame O-144 §2 «listen-before-pitch». IMPORT weak claim with R12 framing. SKIP covert pacing-leading.

## §1.4 Anchoring

### §1.4.1 Theoretical claim

Pavlovian conditioning + sub-modality manipulation framework. NLP claims:
- **State → anchor** mapping: client recalls vivid resourceful state; at peak, practitioner applies unique stimulus (touch on knuckle, specific tone). Stimulus = «anchor».
- **Stacking** — same anchor applied across multiple resourceful state recalls accumulates response strength.
- **Collapsing anchors** — negative state anchor + positive state anchor simultaneously triggered → state interference → negative state diminishes.

> «Now, what I'd like you to do is to remember a time when you were very ... resourceful. ... At the peak of that experience, I'll touch this knuckle ... now I'm building an anchor.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 + bandler-grinder-trance-formations-1981 throughout — anchoring chapters]

### §1.4.2 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**R12-compatibility:**
- ✅ **Self-anchoring** (client builds own resourceful-state anchor for own use, transparent purpose) — compatible.
- ⚠️ **Other-applied anchoring with informed consent + clinical context** — compatible if practitioner discloses + client agrees.
- ❌ **Covert anchoring** — practitioner anchors client's positive state to product/leader/idea WITHOUT disclosure = R12 violation (non_consensual_distribution of own affective response).

**Empirical status (Phase 6 preview):**
- Classical conditioning = robust empirical phenomenon. NLP anchoring ≅ conditioning + cued recall.
- NLP-specific claim of single-trial conditioning leading to durable state change = **weaker empirical support**; clinical applications (e.g. trauma resolution via «collapsing anchors») — no replicated RCT support.
- Generic state-recall via cued imagery = compatible с CBT imagery techniques.

**Abuse-mode documented**: Sales seminars + romantic-persuasion («pickup») literatures use covert touch-anchoring patterns explicitly. Direct R12 violation.

> **SKIP-list candidate #6**: Covert anchoring в commercial/relationship contexts (touch-anchoring during product demo, etc.) → SKIP. Self-anchoring + clinical informed-consent anchoring = OK to acknowledge but не central technique для Jetix.

## §1.5 Reframing (Reframing book 1982)

Reframing = **transformation of meaning**, separable from changing event/behavior itself.

### §1.5.1 Two types

**Content reframing:**
- **Meaning reframing** — same behavior, different meaning. «My son's stubbornness is bad» → «His stubbornness shows he can hold his ground; useful когда друзья давят на drugs.»
- **Context reframing** — same behavior is useful in different context. «My boss criticizes me — that's bad» → «In what context would receiving criticism be useful? Quality-control feedback at moment-of-execution.»

**Six-step (process) reframing:**

> «You have all learned the six-step reframing model. In that model you establish communication with a part, determine its positive intention, generate alternative behaviors, ecology check, and future-pace.» [src: bandler-grinder-reframing-1982 p ~241-251]

Canonical 6 steps:
1. **Identify the behavior/symptom** to change
2. **Communicate с «part»** generating behavior (Yes/No signal — ideomotor)
3. **Establish positive intention** of that part — what is it trying to achieve for the person?
4. **Creative part generates alternatives** — request three new behaviors satisfying same positive intention
5. **Part accepts alternatives** — agreement to try them
6. **Ecology check** — any other parts object? Future-pace.

### §1.5.2 Theoretical premises requiring scrutiny

- **«Parts» metaphor** — internal multiplicity model. Compatible с Internal Family Systems (IFS, Schwartz 1995, empirically supported), Gestalt parts work (Perls), Voice Dialogue (Stone+Stone). NLP claim of **systematic part-negotiation protocol** = specific technique within broader tradition.
- **«Positive intention behind every behavior»** — strong assumption. Therapeutic utility = reframing prevents self-attack ; truth-claim = unfalsifiable.
- **Ideomotor signals** (involuntary yes/no movements) — Erickson-derived hypnosis technique; phenomenon real but interpretation as «part communication» = metaphorical.

### §1.5.3 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**R12-compatibility:**
- ✅ **Cognitive reframing as personal-development tool** (compatible с CBT cognitive restructuring — strong empirical base).
- ✅ **Self-applied reframing** — fully voluntary.
- ⚠️ **Sleight of Mouth attack reframes for outreach** — see Phase 3 (Dilts) — adversarial reframing of objections = R12-incompatible if hidden.
- ⚠️ **Reframing without informed consent in therapeutic relationship** — meta-issue: therapy itself requires informed consent.

**Empirical status (Phase 6 preview):**
- Cognitive reframing (CBT) = strong RCT evidence.
- NLP-specific 6-step part-protocol = no replicated RCT evidence.
- IFS (Schwartz) protocols (post-NLP) = emerging RCT evidence — IFS adopted parts-work framework and built independent empirical base.

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** Reframing-as-meaning-transformation = strong candidate for IMPORT (already partial overlap with Jetix R12 paired-frame — every offer carries paired anti-extraction reframe). 6-step parts protocol = SKIP (not validated; «parts» metaphor not necessary for what Jetix needs).

## §1.6 Milton Model (Trance-formations 1981)

### §1.6.1 Theoretical claim

**Inverse of Meta-model.** Where Meta-model = de-deletes, de-distorts, surfaces specifics; Milton model = **strategic deletion / vagueness / artful ambiguity** — generates intentionally vague language that listener fills with own meaning, inducing trance/altered state.

> «If I tell you about going snorkeling on my recent honeymoon in the Yucatan and describe to you the swift movements of the brightly-colored tropical fish, the rhythmic sound of the gentle waves against the shore, and the feeling of rising and falling with the warm waves ... hopefully I will alter your state of consciousness so that you can experience some representation of what I experienced.» [src: bandler-grinder-trance-formations-1981 p ~5-7]

> «One of the simpler hypnotic patterns is the 'negative command.' If I say 'Don't think of blue,' you have to think of blue in order to understand my sentence. If a hypnotist says 'I don't want you to relax too soon' the listener often finds himself beginning to relax as a way of understanding what those words mean.» [src: bandler-grinder-trance-formations-1981 p ~7]

> «All communication is hypnosis ... I disagree, nothing is hypnosis; hypnosis doesn't exist. ... If you think of hypnosis as altering someone's state of consciousness, then any effective communication is hypnosis.» [src: bandler-grinder-trance-formations-1981 p ~6-7]

### §1.6.2 Patterns

- **Pacing current experience** — describing accurately what listener is hearing/seeing
- **Mind-reading patterns** — «You are wondering as you read this»
- **Cause-effect implications** — «As you read this, you will begin to relax»
- **Universal quantifiers + lost performatives** — «People often realize...»
- **Embedded commands** — «You can ... relax ... now» (italicized embedded)
- **Ambiguous syntax** — phonological / scope / punctuation ambiguities
- **Quotes pattern** — communicating restricted message via reported speech: «My teacher used to say to me, 'Just sit down and do it now.'»

### §1.6.3 R12 pre-screen + empirical status — **MOSTLY R12-INCOMPATIBLE**

This is the **largest single SKIP block** для Phase 7. Reasons:

**R12-compatibility:**
- ✅ **Therapeutic Ericksonian hypnosis with informed consent** (clinical setting, licensed practitioner) — compatible.
- ❌ **Covert Milton patterns in sales/influence/seduction** — direct R12 violation. The technique's defining feature = **covertness** (Erickson called it «utilization of indirect suggestion»). Disclosed Milton model = mostly just creative storytelling.
- ❌ **Embedded commands without disclosure** = non_consensual influence by definition.

**Empirical status (Phase 6 preview):**
- Ericksonian hypnosis (clinical) = mixed empirical evidence; some applications supported (pain management, anxiety reduction).
- NLP appropriation as «communication technique» = no validated RCT support for sales/influence applications beyond standard rapport.

**Abuse-mode documented:** Most widely abused NLP framework. NLP-pickup, NLP-sales, multi-level-marketing scripts heavily borrow.

> **SKIP-list candidate #7 (LARGE BLOCK)**: All Milton model patterns deployed in NON-clinical, NON-disclosed contexts → SKIP. Includes embedded commands, covert pacing-current-experience for compliance, ambiguous-syntax-for-deniability, quotes-pattern-for-restricted-message.

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** **Welcome-frame O-144 is the explicit antithesis of Milton model deployment** — disclosed framing, transparent intent, no embedded commands, no covert anchoring. Milton model goes on Phase 7 EXPLICIT skip-list as primary R12 violation cluster.

## §1.7 Strategies (TOTE) — Generic Procedures of Excellence

### §1.7.1 Theoretical claim

Adopted from Miller-Galanter-Pribram (1960) *Plans and the Structure of Behavior*: **T**est-**O**perate-**T**est-**E**xit — feedback loop model of behavior.

NLP claims that excellence in any domain = **specific sequence of representational system states** that practitioner can elicit + transfer.

> «Modeling … pay very little attention to what people say they do and a great deal of attention to what they do.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 ~262]

Pattern: identify TOTE structure of expert; teach novice to install same TOTE.

### §1.7.2 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**R12-compatibility:** ✅ Mostly compatible. Modeling = skill-extraction protocol; transparent + voluntary.

**Empirical status:** TOTE model itself = standard cybernetics (Miller-Galanter-Pribram 1960, well-established). NLP-specific claim of «complete behavioral installation in single session» = unsupported. **Disciplined modeling** = compatible with skill transfer literature (apprenticeship, deliberate practice).

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** Strategies/Modeling discipline = strong overlap with **DR-38 8-component meta-method** + Method V2 §J + methodology-engineer ROY agent. Candidate IMPORT (with caveats: skill transfer is slower than NLP claims; deliberate practice literature more rigorous).

## §1.8 Submodalities

### §1.8.1 Theoretical claim

Within each VAK system there are **sub-distinctions** (sub-modalities):
- **Visual**: brightness, distance, color/B&W, size, location, framed/panoramic, movement
- **Auditory**: volume, tone, tempo, location, internal/external
- **Kinesthetic**: pressure, temperature, location, intensity

Claim: changing submodalities of internal representation changes affective response. E.g. shrinking + dimming visualization of feared object → reduced fear response.

### §1.8.2 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**R12-compatibility:** ✅ Self-applied technique with informed practitioner support — compatible (similar к exposure therapy + imagery rescripting).

**Empirical status (Phase 6 preview):**
- Imagery rescripting (cognitive therapy) = strong RCT evidence для PTSD, phobia, depression.
- NLP submodalities specifically = no specific RCT evidence; **functional overlap with imagery rescripting** is strong but NLP framing doesn't add unique validated component.
- «Fast phobia cure» single-session claim via submodality manipulation = no validated RCT support (Phase 6 will document).

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** Submodalities not central to Jetix needs. SKIP unless self-applied imagery technique brought in from CBT literature (Bolton/Andreas Phase 5).

## §1.9 Well-formed Outcomes

### §1.9.1 Theoretical claim

Before any change work, client must specify outcome in **well-formed** way:

1. **Stated positively** — what you want, not what you don't want («I want to stay calm in meetings» not «I don't want to be anxious»)
2. **Within own control** — can be initiated and maintained by self, not requiring others' behavior change
3. **Sensory-specific evidence procedure** — how will you know when you've got it? («I'll see my hands relaxed, hear myself speaking at moderate tempo, feel breath in belly»)
4. **Context-specified** — where, when, with whom?
5. **Ecological** — secondary gains considered, no negative consequences
6. **Resources identified** — what do you have / need to access it?

### §1.9.2 R12 pre-screen + empirical status

**R12-compatibility:** ✅ Fully compatible — voluntary discipline of specifying what you want.

**Empirical status:** Aligns with goal-setting research (Locke & Latham 1990 *A Theory of Goal Setting*, robust empirical base). NLP «well-formed outcomes» adds **sensory-specific evidence procedure** as distinct twist; this maps к OKR-style measurable-outcome discipline.

**Jetix lens parallel (Phase 7 preview):** STRONG IMPORT candidate. Maps to:
- Workshop hypothesis architecture (specific testable hypothesis + evidence criterion)
- Method V2 §J component 2 («outcome specification with evidence procedure»)
- DR-38 first component
- FPF F-G-R triple (each well-formed outcome = a claim with Formality, Group-scope, Reliability)

## §1.10 Frogs into Princes — broader teaching frame

The 1979 *Frogs into Princes* = transcript of seminar — gives founders' communication style. Three things stand out for R12 assessment:

### §1.10.1 Founders' authority frame

> «There are several important ways in which what we do differs radically from what others do. ... Some people will do experiential workshops in which you will be put through experiences ... They will demonstrate by their behavior that they are quite competent in those areas. ... There's also a group of people who are theoreticians.» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 p ~193-213]

Founders explicitly positioned NLP as **NOT theoretical** and **NOT another experiential workshop**, but as **modeling discipline**. This frame ≠ what NLP became (largely experiential workshops with theoretical overclaim).

**R12-relevant**: founders' original framing was relatively careful about epistemological claims; deterioration of frame in commercial popularization (Phase 4) = compounded R12 risk.

### §1.10.2 «Outcome ≠ manipulation» framing

> «I would ask them what outcome they were working toward ... And they would say outcomes that would be something bad, called 'manipulating.'» [src: bandler-grinder-frogs-into-princes-1979 p ~255-261]

Founders preemptively defended specific-outcome orientation against «manipulation» charge. **R12 assessment**: this framing is **insufficient**. Whether outcome-orientation = manipulation depends on:
- Whether the outcome is **client's** outcome or **practitioner's** outcome for the client
- Whether the technique is **disclosed** or **covert**
- Whether **fork-and-leave** is preserved

NLP-as-marketed routinely fails all three tests. R12 paired-frame STRICT requires explicit attention to all three; NLP foundational framing addresses only first.

### §1.10.3 «Therapeutic wizardry» mystique

> «We began the process of making the magical skills of potent psychotherapists available to other practitioners in a learnable and explicit form.» [src: bandler-grinder-structure-of-magic-1975 p ~265-280]

«Magic / wizardry» frame = **commercial overclaim risk**. Even when founders carefully qualified, the *Structure of Magic* title and «magic» framing set up downstream popularization to amplify magical-thinking marketing. R12 implication: any technique promoted under «magical results in minutes» framing = candidate for empirical scrutiny + SKIP-list.
