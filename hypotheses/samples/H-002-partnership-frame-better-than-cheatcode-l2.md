# H-002 — Partnership-frame outperforms cheat-code для L2 outreach

## Гипотеза

L2 founders/operators хуже реагируют на «cheat-code shortcut» позиционирование
(triggers скепсис / over-claim allergy) и лучше — на «pre-existing partnership»
позиционирование (offers concrete artefact + invitation, not sales pitch).

Если confirmed:
- Updates outreach template library (favor partnership-frame)
- Validates underlying thesis в wiki/concepts/pre-existing-partnership-positioning.md
- Provides empirical signal для wider pitch substrate

## Метод теста

A/B test, 10 cold/warm outreach к L2:

### Frame A — «Cheat-code» (5 outreach)
```
«Hi <name>, I have an AI methodology / framework that gives builders
like you a real shortcut to <X> — would love to show you how it works.»
```

### Frame B — «Partnership» (5 outreach)
```
«Hi <name>, I've been building a methodology substrate around <X>
based on FPF / Левенчук systems thinking. I'd like to explore a
co-creation partnership — sharing an existing artefact and inviting
input. Open к 30-min call?»
```

### Channel
- LinkedIn DM OR direct email (depending on contact)
- Same 5-7 day window

### Metrics
- Reply rate per frame
- Meeting conversion per frame
- Subjective quality (1-5 scale): interest expressed?

## Условия / scope

- L2 ICP (≥1y operating; existing infrastructure)
- Catallactic region (mutual benefit framing)
- 10 total outreach (small N; signal not conclusive but directional)
- Within 30 days of test launch
