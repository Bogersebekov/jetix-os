## Метод теста

### Step 1 — Read Левенчук Интеллект-стек 2023 verbatim
Mapping какие 12 components Левенчук precisely defines; precise boundaries.

### Step 2 — Self-audit моих reasoning workflows
За 2 weeks daily notes — каждый раз когда reasoning involves:
- Generating novel scenarios / counterfactuals
- Mental simulation of consequences
- «What if X were different?» exploration

Marker: is this reducible к existing 12 components? If genuinely distinct → tally.

### Step 3 — Draft 5-7 example argument
Per example:
- Concrete reasoning task
- Why imagination distinctly required
- Why NOT reducible к {modeling / hypothesis generation / abstraction / inference / ...}

### Step 4 — Philosophy critique
Submit draft к philosophy-expert agent (ROY swarm).
Per critique: defensible or not?

### Step 5 (optional) — Левенчук cross-review
Если step 4 passes, draft message к Левенчук с 1-page argument.
This step requires confirmed status (post step 4) и Ruslan ack.
