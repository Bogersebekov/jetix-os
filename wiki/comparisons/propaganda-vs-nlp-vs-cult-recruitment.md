# Propaganda vs NLP vs Cult Recruitment — Cross-Cluster Comparison

## TL;DR

Три кластера influence techniques operate на разных уровнях (mass / individual / cohort) — кроссобласти overlap, но agents responsibility разделены по IP-1.

## Сравнительная таблица

| Dimension | Propaganda | NLP | Cult Recruitment |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Scale** | mass / media | individual interactions | cohort / group |
| **Time horizon** | short → long | minutes → years | months → lifetime |
| **Substrate canon** | Bernays, Ellul, Chomsky, Heath | Bandler-Grinder, Dilts, Erickson | Hoffer, Lifton, Hassan |
| **Primary mechanism** | symbol / narrative | language pattern | substrate immersion |
| **Reversibility** | high (re-framing possible) | high (counter-pattern) | low (BITE depth) |
| **R12 risk grade** | F2-F4 | F4 strict | F4 critical |
| **ROY agent** | propaganda-expert | nlp-expert | recruitment-dynamics-expert |

## Overlap zones

- **Symbol + language patterns:** Bernays operational protocols pair с Sleight-of-Mouth Dilts
- **Group dynamics:** Le Bon crowd psychology pair с Hoffer mass-movement
- **Defensive use:** все три share defensive audit applicability
- **R12 paired-frame mandatory** для всех трёх (per [[concepts/r12-paired-frame-template]])

## IP-1 split rationale

ROY agents разделены по primary substrate corpus + dispatch boundary (per Phase 4 §4.10). Cross-consult через brigadier, не direct agent-to-agent.
