# Popper / Kuhn / Lakatos / Feyerabend — Phil-Sci Quartet

## TL;DR

Четвёрка философов науки в дебатах 1960-70х — substrate любой SOTA-tracking discipline.

## Сравнительная таблица

| Author | Year | Core thesis | Demarcation criterion | Progress model |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Popper** | 1934-63 | Theories must be falsifiable | Falsifiability | Conjecture-refutation cycle |
| **Kuhn** | 1962 | Paradigm shifts not cumulative | None (sociological) | Normal → crisis → revolution |
| **Lakatos** | 1978 | Programmes с hard-core + protective belt | Progressive vs degenerating | Programme history |
| **Feyerabend** | 1975 | "Anything goes" / pluralism | None (epistemological anarchism) | Counter-induction |

## Дебаты

- **Popper vs Kuhn:** rationalism vs sociology of science
- **Lakatos as synthesis:** Popper baseline + Kuhn realism про paradigm-loyalty
- **Feyerabend как radical:** Kuhn pushed дальше — никаких normative criteria for science

## SOTA-tracking application

- **Popper test:** is this claim falsifiable?
- **Kuhn test:** are we в normal-science или crisis?
- **Lakatos test:** is this programme progressive (novel predictions confirmed) или degenerating (ad-hoc)?
- **Feyerabend test:** are we excluding alternatives prematurely?
