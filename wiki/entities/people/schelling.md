# Thomas Schelling

## Кто / что это
Economist (1921-2016); Strategy of Conflict (1960) — strategic interaction + focal points; Micromotives and Macrobehavior (1978) — segregation model; Nobel 2005.

## Контекст
- Роль: applied game theory для conflict, segregation, нuклеарной strategy
- Mining focus: focal points, commitment devices, brinkmanship

## Факты
- PhD Harvard 1948
- Strategy of Conflict (1960) — defining text Cold War strategy
- Micromotives and Macrobehavior (1978) — segregation model
- Nobel Economics 2005 (с Aumann)
- US government advisor через Cold War
