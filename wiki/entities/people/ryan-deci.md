# Richard Ryan & Edward Deci

## Кто / что это
University of Rochester psychologists; co-developed Self-Determination Theory (SDT) — meta-theory motivation focused on autonomy, competence, relatedness.

## Контекст
- Роль: foundational SDT meta-theory authors
- Mining focus: SDT 3 needs, OIT (Organismic Integration Theory), CET (Cognitive Evaluation Theory)

## Факты
- Deci PhD Carnegie Mellon 1970
- Ryan PhD Rochester 1981
- SDT formal articulation 1985 (Intrinsic Motivation and Self-Determination in Human Behavior)
- 2000 American Psychologist paper canonical citation
- 6 mini-theories within SDT meta-theory
- selfdeterminationtheory.org official site
- Why We Do What We Do (1995) accessible book
