# Eyjólfur Guðmundsson

## Кто / что это
Icelandic economist; **first PhD-grade economist hired в game studio** (CCP Games 2007-2014); founded EVE Monthly Economic Report — pattern для virtual economy transparency.

## Контекст
- Роль: pioneer «real economist в game studio» role
- Mining focus: MER methodology, EVE economy analysis publications, post-CCP work

## Факты
- PhD Economics (University of Tromsø, Norway)
- CCP Games Lead Economist 2007-2014
- Founded EVE MER 2007
- Current: Rector of University of Akureyri
- Authored academic papers on EVE economy
