# Yanis Varoufakis

## Кто / что это
Economist (former Greek Finance Minister 2015), public intellectual, **served as Valve's in-game economist 2012-2017** для Team Fortress 2 / Steam Marketplace; ключевой connector между «реальной» econ + virtual economies.

## Контекст
- Роль: bridge between virtual economy practitioners and progressive policy
- Mining focus: Technofeudalism (2023) — argues virtual economies foreshadow real economies

## Факты
- PhD Economics
- Greek FM 2015 (Syriza)
- Valve in-game economist 2012-2017 (Steam Marketplace, TF2 economy)
- 2023 book Technofeudalism — argues Big Tech rents extract = feudal lord model
- DiEM25 founder (pan-European movement)
