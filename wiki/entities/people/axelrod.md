# Robert Axelrod

## Кто / что это
Political scientist (Michigan); 1984 «Evolution of Cooperation» — empirical демонстрация tit-for-tat dominance в iterated PD tournaments.

## Контекст
- Роль: foundation для cooperation theory + iterated games
- Mining focus: tournament methodology, Tit-for-Tat strategy, evolution к cooperation

## Факты
- PhD Yale (Political Science)
- University of Michigan professor
- 1979-80 ran iterated PD tournaments — Tit-for-Tat won both
- Book Evolution of Cooperation (1984) — cited 50K+ times
- Subsequent: Complexity of Cooperation (1997)
