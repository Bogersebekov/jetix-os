# Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi

## Кто / что это
Hungarian-American psychologist (1934-2021); developed «flow» concept (1975+); author seminal «Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience» (1990).

## Контекст
- Роль: foundational positive psychology / flow research
- Mining focus: flow state conditions, challenge-skill balance, autotelic experience

## Факты
- PhD University of Chicago 1965
- Hungarian Holocaust survivor (extreme conditions shaped interest)
- 30+ years researching flow
- Co-founder positive psychology movement с Seligman
- Claremont Graduate University professor
