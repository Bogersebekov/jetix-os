# John Nash

## Кто / что это
Mathematician/economist (1928-2015); 1950 paper «Equilibrium Points in N-Person Games» — основа non-cooperative game theory; Nobel Economics 1994.

## Контекст
- Роль: foundational game theorist (non-cooperative theory)
- Mining focus: Nash equilibrium concept + applications

## Факты
- PhD Princeton 1950 (28 pages dissertation!)
- Nash equilibrium (1950 PNAS)
- Nobel Prize Economics 1994 (с Harsanyi и Selten)
- Abel Prize 2015 (для math)
- A Beautiful Mind biography (Nasar 1998) + film 2001
