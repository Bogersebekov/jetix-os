# Robert Cialdini

## Кто / что это
Social psychologist (Arizona State University, emeritus); 1984 «Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion» — 6 (later 7) persuasion principles widely cited в marketing + product design.

## Контекст
- Роль: foundational persuasion psychology
- Mining focus: 6 principles — reciprocity, commitment-consistency, social proof, authority, liking, scarcity (+unity 7th)

## Факты
- PhD UNC 1970
- Influence (1984) — 5M+ copies sold
- Pre-Suasion (2016) follow-up
- 6 principles: reciprocity / commitment-consistency / social proof / authority / liking / scarcity
- Unity (7th) added in Pre-Suasion
