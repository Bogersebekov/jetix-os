# B. F. Skinner

## Кто / что это
American psychologist (1904-1990); foundational behaviorism + operant conditioning; pioneer variable-ratio reinforcement (linked to gambling psychology, slot machines).

## Контекст
- Роль: foundational behaviorism / operant conditioning
- Mining focus: variable-ratio schedule, operant conditioning, reinforcement schedules

## Факты
- PhD Harvard 1931
- Harvard professor 1948-1974
- Skinner box / operant chamber invention
- The Behavior of Organisms (1938) foundational
- Science and Human Behavior (1953)
- Beyond Freedom and Dignity (1971) — controversial
