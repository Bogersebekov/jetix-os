# John Maynard Smith

## Кто / что это
British evolutionary biologist (1920-2004); introduced game theory к biology; 1982 «Evolution and the Theory of Games» — ESS (Evolutionarily Stable Strategy) concept.

## Контекст
- Роль: bridge game theory + biology + evolutionary dynamics
- Mining focus: ESS, hawk-dove model, replicator dynamics

## Факты
- PhD Aircraft engineering THEN biology
- Sussex University founding member (1965)
- ESS concept introduced 1973 with George R. Price
- 1982 book defining evolutionary game theory
- Crafoord Prize 1999
