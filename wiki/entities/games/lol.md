# League of Legends

## Кто / что это
MOBA Riot Games с 130M MAU, archetype упрощённого entry curve + глобальной esports ladder (LEC/LCK/LCS).

## Контекст
- Роль: archetype global esports + ranked ladder
- Mining focus: ranked tiers (Iron-Challenger), seasonal reset, Riot Points dual-currency

## Факты
- ~130M MAU
- Tencent acquired 100% Riot Games 2015 for $400M
- 160+ champions (rotation free + permanent unlock)
- Annual Worlds tournament (Faker/T1 legend)
- Dual currency: RP (paid) + Blue Essence (earned)
