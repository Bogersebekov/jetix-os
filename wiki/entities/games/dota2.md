# Dota 2

## Кто / что это
MOBA Valve, archetype высокого skill ceiling + The International tournament prize pool funded by community via Compendium.

## Контекст
- Роль: skill mastery archetype, esports экосистема
- Mining focus: ranked ladder MMR, hero pool design (130+ heroes), cosmetic-only monetization

## Факты
- ~11M MAU SteamCharts (declining from 13M peak 2016)
- The International 2021 prize pool $40M (community funded — battle pass 25% to prize)
- 130+ heroes (vs LoL's 160+, but больше mechanical depth)
- F2P; cosmetic-only revenue model (Arcanas, Immortals)
- Yanis Varoufakis worked as Valve's economist 2012-2017
