# PUBG Mobile / Free Fire

## Кто / что это
Battle royale mobile games (~70M MAU combined), archetype emerging-markets gaming (India, SEA, LATAM).

## Контекст
- Роль: emerging markets archetype
- Mining focus: BR mechanic, low-end device optimization, $1-5 microtransactions

## Факты
- PUBG Mobile ~50M MAU; Free Fire ~20M MAU (post-India-ban estimates)
- $1-3 average ARPPU vs $20+ для western games
- BGMI (PUBG India re-skin) — strategic regulatory adaptation
- 100-player matches in 25-minute windows
