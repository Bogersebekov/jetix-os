# Lineage Cluster 3 — Phil-Sci Canon

## TL;DR

Phil-sci canon: Popper (1934 falsifiability) → Kuhn (1962 paradigm) → Feyerabend (1975 anything goes) → Laudan (1977 problem-solving) → Lakatos (1978 programmes synthesis) → Chalmers (1976 textbook) → Hacking (1983 entity-realism) → Longino (2002 social epistemology). Plus Polanyi (1958/1966 tacit knowledge) — adjacent canon.

## Ключевые выводы

- **Popper 1934/1963** — falsifiability как demarcation
- **Kuhn 1962** — paradigm shifts (challenge к Popper)
- **Feyerabend 1975** — radical pluralism
- **Lakatos 1978** — Popper+Kuhn synthesis (research programmes)
- **Laudan 1977** — problem-solving model (alternative to truth-progress)
- **Chalmers 1976** — comparative textbook exposition
- **Hacking 1983** — entity realism + experimental practice
- **Longino 2002** — transformative criticism criteria, social epistemology
- **Polanyi 1958/1966** — tacit knowledge dimension (adjacent но critical)
