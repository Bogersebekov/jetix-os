# PSYCHOLOGY Domain Synthesis

## TL;DR
23 entries по 5 frameworks: SDT (Ryan-Deci) — meta-theory + 3 needs; Flow (Csikszentmihalyi) — optimal experience + challenge-skill; Variable rewards (Skinner→Eyal) — most powerful + ethically loaded; Social proof + commitment (Cialdini) — persuasion; Dopamine + hedonic treadmill — neuroscience. **SDT = primary anchor for Realm**: directly maps к E1-E3.

## Ключевые выводы

### SDT (Ryan-Deci) — primary anchor
- 3 universal needs: **Autonomy** (E4 quest selection), **Competence** (E1/E2 visible progression), **Relatedness** (E3 clan)
- Intrinsic vs extrinsic continuum
- CET: external rewards CAN crowd out intrinsic motivation

### Flow (Csikszentmihalyi)
- 8 conditions of optimal experience
- Challenge-skill balance critical (E4 quest difficulty calibration)
- Time distortion, effortless concentration

### Variable rewards (Skinner→Eyal)
- Most powerful reinforcement schedule
- **Ethically loaded** — gambling psychology adjacent
- Realm: use SPARINGLY (predictable rewards primary)

### Social proof + commitment (Cialdini)
- 6 principles framework
- Onboarding application (commit ladder)
- Public testimonials, leaderboards

### Neuroscience
- Dopamine prediction error (variable rewards strong)
- Hedonic treadmill (money rewards diminish)
