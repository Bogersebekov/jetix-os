# Clan mechanics amplify engagement

## Точная формулировка
Guild / Faction / Corporation structures (3-1000 members) consistently amplify member retention 3-5x over solo play across genres + decades of MMO design.

## Контекст
Empirical: WoW guilds 4-5x retention vs solo (Williams Ninja-Metrics + multiple academic papers). Torn 20-year Faction system stable. EVE corporations 95% of active player time. SDT theoretical basis (relatedness need fulfilled).

## Аргументы за
- WoW empirical (Williams + others)
- Torn 20-year operation primarily through Faction
- EVE corporation/alliance structure 95%+ activity
- SDT relatedness need theory
- Cialdini commitment-consistency reinforcement

## Аргументы против
- Solo «explorer» minority exists
- Bad guild experience can DRIVE attrition
- Requires care in guild formation

## Что опровергнуло бы это утверждение
Sustained MMO с no guild system retaining members at similar rates (no precedent).
