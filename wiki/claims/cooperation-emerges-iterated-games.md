# Cooperation emerges in iterated interactions

## Точная формулировка
В iterated Prisoner's Dilemma с long shadow of future, cooperative strategies (Tit-for-Tat) dominate defection — empirically validated Axelrod tournaments 1979-1980 + 40+ years subsequent.

## Контекст
Axelrod tournaments (1979, 1980) gathered submissions; Tit-for-Tat won both. Subsequent research confirms with modifications (Tit-for-Two-Tats, Generous Tit-for-Tat, win-stay-lose-shift). Foundational для cooperation theory.

## Аргументы за
- Axelrod 1980 tournaments empirical
- WWI live-and-let-live trench warfare
- Bacterial cooperation studies
- Business reciprocity longitudinal data
- 50K+ citations Axelrod book

## Аргументы против
- One-shot games still favor defection
- Reputation systems essential for translation
- Noise / errors complicate

## Что опровергнуло бы это утверждение
Iterated PD experiment где always-defect dominates against modern strategies (no such finding).
