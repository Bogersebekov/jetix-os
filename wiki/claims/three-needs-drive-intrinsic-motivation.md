# Three SDT needs drive intrinsic motivation

## Точная формулировка
Autonomy + Competence + Relatedness are universally-required basic psychological needs; satisfying all three is necessary AND sufficient для intrinsic motivation — empirically validated across 30+ years cross-cultural research.

## Контекст
SDT — most empirically-validated motivation meta-theory; 10K+ citations Deci-Ryan 2000 paper. Cross-cultural studies (US, Russia, China, India, Brazil, Korea) consistent findings. Educational, workplace, sport, health domains validation.

## Аргументы за
- Deci-Ryan 2000 American Psychologist paper
- 30+ years cross-cultural research (Soenens, Vansteenkiste, Sheldon, etc.)
- Meta-analyses across domains
- selfdeterminationtheory.org bibliography 1000+ papers

## Аргументы против
- Some cultures (East Asian) show different autonomy expression
- Hedonic happiness studies suggest 4th need (e.g. meaning)
- Operationalization debates

## Что опровергнуло бы это утверждение
Sustained intrinsic motivation observed без one of 3 needs being met (no precedent).
