# Visible progress bars increase completion

## Точная формулировка
Visible incremental progress bars (XP, completion %, achievement tracking) consistently increase task completion rates 30-50% across A/B tests + observational data.

## Контекст
Empirical: WoW XP bar — primary engagement driver. Civilization tech tree visibility. Duolingo streak counters. Foundation: SDT competence (visible mastery), goal-gradient effect (Kivetz et al 2006).

## Аргументы за
- WoW 20-year retention primary through XP loops
- Civ tech tree visibility crucial for engagement
- Goal-gradient effect (Kivetz 2006 paper, ranked papers)
- Duolingo streak retention spike before reset

## Аргументы против
- Endless bars create anxiety (Diablo IV launch issues)
- Pure cosmetic-progress can feel hollow

## Что опровергнуло бы это утверждение
Game / app demonstrating similar completion rates без visible progress signals.
