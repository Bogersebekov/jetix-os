# Variable rewards drive retention

## Точная формулировка
Variable-ratio reinforcement schedules generate stronger behavior persistence than fixed schedules — empirically validated across Skinner experiments + modern product analytics (Eyal Hook Model case studies).

## Контекст
Foundation: Skinner operant conditioning research 1930s+. Applied: Eyal Hooked 2014 case studies (Pinterest, Twitter, Instagram). Mechanism: dopamine prediction error neuroscience (Schultz-Dayan-Montague 1997).

## Аргументы за
- Skinner Schedules of Reinforcement (1957) — empirical
- Eyal Hooked 2014 case studies multiple successful products
- Dopamine prediction error neuroscience papers
- 5+ top games use (loot boxes, gacha, drops)

## Аргументы против
- Ethically loaded (gambling psychology adjacent)
- Eyal himself wrote Indistractable (2019) counter
- Belgian / Dutch / Chinese loot box regulations 2018-2021

## Что опровергнуло бы это утверждение
Sustained product with fixed-reward design matching variable-reward retention (no clear precedent at scale).
