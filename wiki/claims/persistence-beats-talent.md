# Persistence beats talent because mastery = accumulated information

## Точная формулировка

**Упорство бьёт талант, потому что мастерство = накопленная информация × метод; талант = генетически заложенный baseline способности обработки информации.** Persistence amplifies accumulation; talent supplies baseline. При прочих равных, sustained persistence outscales talent ceiling because mastery scales with accumulated information (per O-64 mastery formula). [src: audio_696@19-05-2026_17-05-37.ogg claim 16]

## Аргументы против

- **Talent ceiling argument** — beyond a threshold, raw cognitive capacity / pattern-recognition speed may bound mastery acquisition rate (Hambrick & Macnamara 2014 meta-analysis: deliberate practice explains 26% of variance in skill across studies; not 100%)
- **Domain dependency** — in highly creative domains (arts, theoretical math), talent / aesthetic sensitivity may dominate
- **Sampling bias in success stories** — survivor bias inflates persistence-success correlation
- **Quality matters multiplicatively** (per mastery formula) — wasted persistence ≠ accumulated learning; method-quality critical
- **Definition of «beats»** — at what timeline? Persistence accumulates over years; talent shows immediately

## Что опровергнуло бы это утверждение

- Longitudinal study showing talent-baseline correlates with mastery outcomes >50% above persistence-correlation, controlling for accumulated practice hours
- Domain finding where talent-ceiling is hit before persistence-accumulation can overcome (e.g. extreme math / theoretical physics — open question)
- Genetic study showing >50% mastery-variance attributed to genetics in fields previously assumed practice-dominant
