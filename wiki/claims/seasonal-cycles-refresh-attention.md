# Seasonal cycles refresh attention

## Точная формулировка
3-month seasonal cycles с new theme + leaderboard reset generate retention spikes at start/end vs steady-state — empirically validated Fortnite, WoW, LoL.

## Контекст
Empirical: Fortnite battle pass purchase patterns (peak weeks 1-2, low weeks 5-8, peak weeks 11-12). WoW Mythic+ seasonal participation curves. LoL ranked season participation. Foundation: Schelling focal points + variable rewards + scarcity.

## Аргументы за
- Fortnite seasonal data multiple years
- WoW Mythic+ rotation data
- LoL ranked patterns
- Mid-season live events sustain peak

## Аргументы против
- Forced reset can frustrate top tier players
- 3 months may be wrong length для some communities

## Что опровергнуло бы это утверждение
Sustained game с no seasonal structure matching seasonal-game retention rates.
