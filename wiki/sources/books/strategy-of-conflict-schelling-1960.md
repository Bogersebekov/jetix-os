# The Strategy of Conflict

## TL;DR
Schelling 1960 — defining Cold War strategic interaction text. Focal points, commitment devices, brinkmanship. T1 anchor.

## Summary
Reframed game theory от pure-conflict zero-sum к mixed-motive analysis. Even adversaries have common interest. Focal points (coordination salience). Commitment devices (binding future self). Brinkmanship (calibrated risk for leverage). Foundational для conflict studies + econ + IR.
