# Exodus to the Virtual World

## TL;DR
Castronova 2007 argument: significant share of human attention/leisure migrating to virtual worlds — implications для real-world economies + politics.

## Summary
Sequel to Synthetic Worlds. Castronova predicts mass migration. Implications: real-world tax base erosion, real-world entertainment industry disruption, political organization новых типов. Some predictions held (gaming as primary entertainment), others not (no full social-life migration).
