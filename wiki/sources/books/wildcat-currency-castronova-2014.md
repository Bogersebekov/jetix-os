# Wildcat Currency

## TL;DR
Castronova 2014: virtual / private currencies (game tokens, frequent flyer miles, crypto) emerging как modern «wildcat banking» era — pre-regulated, private issuers, fluctuating value.

## Summary
Detailed treatment of virtual currency design + regulation. History of wildcat banking 1837-1864 (US). Modern analogues: V-Bucks, Robux, frequent flyer miles, Bitcoin. Regulatory horizon — Castronova predicted increased oversight (partially happened post-2022 crypto crash).
