# Flow

## TL;DR
Csikszentmihalyi 1990 foundational positive psychology book on flow state. T1 anchor. 5M+ copies.

## Summary
30+ years research synthesis. 8 conditions of flow. Applications across domains (work, sports, creative). Challenge-skill balance central. Foundational for game design (Schell explicitly cites). Subsequent books: Finding Flow (1997), Good Business (2003).
