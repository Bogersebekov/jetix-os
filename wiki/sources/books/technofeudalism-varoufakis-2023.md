# Technofeudalism

## TL;DR
Varoufakis argues Big Tech platforms (Amazon, Apple, Meta) extract rents from «vassals» — pattern resembles feudalism more than capitalism. Realm anti-pattern reference.

## Summary
Capital has been replaced by cloud-capital в power dynamics. Platform owners (Amazon, Apple) = cloudalists, third parties (merchants, creators) = digital serfs. Examines historical parallels (manorial system 9th-14th century). Drawing on Varoufakis' time at Valve. Antidote: democratic ownership / digital commons / co-ops.
