# The Evolution of Cooperation

## TL;DR
Axelrod 1984 foundational: documented iterated PD tournaments + Tit-for-Tat dominance. T1 anchor.

## Summary
Two tournaments (1979, 1980) invited researchers to submit IPD strategies. Tit-for-Tat (4 lines, Anatol Rapoport) won both. Book analyzes WHY: nice, retaliatory, forgiving, clear properties. Applications: WWI live-and-let-live trench warfare, bacterial cooperation, business reciprocity. 50K+ citations.
