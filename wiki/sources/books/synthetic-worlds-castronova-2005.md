# Synthetic Worlds

## TL;DR
Castronova 2005 foundational book establishing economics of virtual worlds; **T1 anchor for Jetix Realm economy design**. ~330 pages.

## Summary
Castronova synthesizes 4 years of research (post-2001 EverQuest GDP paper). Chapters: «What are synthetic worlds?», «Player attitudes», «The economics of virtual worlds», «The role of design», «Public policy implications», «The synthetic future». Establishes:
- Virtual economies follow standard economic principles
- Game designers play central-banker role
- Real-money trading inevitable consequence
- Public policy implications (taxation, fraud)

## Ключевые цитаты
> «The synthetic world is not a game; it is a place.»
> «The Sims Online is no more 'just a game' than Wall Street is.»
