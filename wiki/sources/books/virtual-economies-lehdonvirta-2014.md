# Virtual Economies: Design and Analysis

## TL;DR
MIT Press textbook на virtual economy design — **defining T1 reference** for Jetix Realm economy design. EVE Online case study extensively.

## Summary
Co-authored Lehdonvirta (Oxford) + Castronova (Indiana). 12 chapters covering: foundations of virtual economy, design building blocks (currencies, items, markets), social context, regulation, case studies (EverQuest, EVE, FarmVille). Most rigorous academic textbook field has.
