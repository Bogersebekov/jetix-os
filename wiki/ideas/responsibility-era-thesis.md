# Responsibility-Era Thesis

## §1 Core claim

Two trend lines converge → adequate period для collective human responsibility-taking:
1. **Tools heavier + more dangerous** (AI / bio / nuclear / cyber convergence) — irreversibility risk ↑
2. **Humanity accumulated experience + matured** (~270K years «играла, хихикала, хакала, билась» — adolescence done)

→ This is the period when humanity must «взять на себя ответственность повзрослеть». Jetix mission: «каждому запихать» the responsibility-frame; substrate that enables collective maturation.

**F-grade:** F3 (voice anchor F5 ; H9 Octagon LOCK candidate; needs adversarial review за «infantilization» counter + tool-danger overstatement counter)
