# WoW Guild Progression → Realm Clan Leveling

## Суть
Lift WoW guild XP + perks + bank tabs pattern на Realm E3 Clan leveling layer (orthogonal to individual reputation).

## Mapping table
| WoW Guild | Realm Clan |
|---|---|
| Guild XP from members' activity | Clan XP from members' quest completion |
| Guild perks (mass-rez, XP%, repair) | Clan perks (shared AI credit pool, premium mentor access) |
| Guild bank tabs | Shared playbook + contact pool |
| Achievement showcase | Public clan profile achievements |
| Guild level 1-25 | Clan tier 1-10 |

## Применение
- Two-axis progression: individual E1 + clan E3
- Clan perks unlock с tier (premium mentor at T5, R&D budget at T8)
- Visible public showcase
- Stable membership rewarded (perks tied to membership tenure)

## Что подтверждает
- WoW 20-year retention through guilds (post-Cataclysm guild system)
- Empirical: guild members retain 4-5x longer than solo
