# Polanyi Tacit Knowledge

## Суть в одной строке

Michael Polanyi — "we know more than we can tell"; tacit/explicit dialectic + apprenticeship + fiduciary trust в research community.

## Определение

Personal Knowledge (Polanyi 1958) + The Tacit Dimension (1966):

- **Tacit knowing:** знание, которое не может быть полностью артикулировано (бицикл-езда, recognition лица, scientific intuition)
- **Subsidiary / focal awareness:** мы attend ОТ subsidiary деталей К focal объекту
- **Emergence (proximal-distal):** ourselves dwell в subsidiary, observe distal
- **Fiduciary framework:** science requires trust в community / tradition / mentor
- **Apprenticeship:** tacit transfer requires direct master-apprentice contact

## Применимость

- **SOTA-tracking epistemology substrate:** не всё знание falsifiable — tacit slot exists
- **Methodology-engineering:** Schön reflective practice substrate; Levenchuk методология direct inheritance
- **Apprenticeship discipline:** Jetix education layer должен сохранить tacit-transfer slot
