# Humans-as-Bugs Trap — failure mode 1 reference

## Суть в одной строке

**Debug metaphor implies humans = errors needing correction.** Highest reputational risk for Jetix society-as-code framing (F8 severity per К-3 Phase 5 §1). Historical precedent catastrophic.

## Historical record — catastrophic precedent

| Case | Period | Mechanism | Death toll |
|---|---|---|---|
| Soviet «engineering of human souls» (Stalin's 1932 phrase) | 1930s-1953 | Ideological reshaping; gulag system; purges | Millions |
| Khmer Rouge Year Zero (Cambodia) | 1975-1979 | Explicit «debug contaminated population» | ~1.7M |
| Chinese Cultural Revolution «struggle sessions» | 1966-1976 | Public reeducation as «debugging» | ~1-2M |
| Chinese social credit system (modern parallel) | 2014+ | Algorithmic behaviour scoring | Surveillance-state ongoing |

**Pattern:** «Humans = bugs needing patches» framing reliably leads к dehumanisation when operationalised by power-actors. Reputational / political catastrophe inevitable если Jetix metaphor unguarded.

[src: Conquest 1968 *The Great Terror*; Kiernan 1996 *The Pol Pot Regime*; Dikötter 2016 *The Cultural Revolution*; Liang et al. 2018 — К-3 Phase 5 §1.2]

## Counter-arguments (К-3 Phase 6 §2 inventory)

Five counter-arguments ready для FM-1 mitigation:

1. **«Debug code NOT humans»** — metaphor refers к **societal patterns** (institutions / norms / incentives) — NOT к individuals
2. **Education Layer empowers SELF-debugging** — opt-in only; participants debug their own life NOT others'
3. **R12 anti-extraction enforced** — нельзя do «debugging on people» beyond consent
4. **Constitutional Tier 2 rule 4** «agents do NOT impersonate human externally» = safeguard
5. **Cross-cultural language testing** — Phase 7 positioning Option B qualifies metaphor explicitly
6. **Educational discipline:** humans = co-authors NOT objects per VISION-FUNDAMENTAL Layer 1
