# Evolutionarily Stable Strategy (ESS)

## Суть в одной строке
Strategy that, once prevalent в population, cannot be invaded by mutant alternative — refined Nash equilibrium concept biology.

## Определение
Maynard Smith & Price 1973. Strategy S = ESS если: (a) E(S,S) > E(S',S) для все S' ≠ S, OR (b) E(S,S) = E(S',S) AND E(S,S') > E(S',S'). I.e. population playing S resistant к invasion. Classic example: Hawk-Dove game.

## Ключевые свойства
- Population-level stability
- Resistant к invasion
- Subset of Nash equilibria
- Mixed strategies possible
- Hawk-Dove demonstration

## Применимость к Realm
- Clan strategies stable to «mutant» strategies
- Avoid invasion by anti-pattern strategies
- Long-term population-level
