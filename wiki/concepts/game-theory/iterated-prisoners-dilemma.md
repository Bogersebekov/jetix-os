# Iterated Prisoner's Dilemma

## Суть в одной строке
PD played repeatedly between same players — cooperation emerges as dominant strategy when «shadow of future» long enough (Axelrod 1984).

## Определение
Iterated PD: known long horizon → cooperation viable. Axelrod 1979-80 ran tournaments inviting researchers submit strategies. **Tit-for-Tat won both times** (cooperate first, then copy opponent). Insight: simple, nice, retaliatory, forgiving — these properties выигрывают long-term. Foundational for cooperation theory.

## Ключевые свойства
- Long horizon (or uncertain end)
- Strategy persistence
- Reputation tracking
- Reciprocity-based cooperation
- Tit-for-Tat dominance

## Применимость к Realm
- E3 clan repeated interactions
- Reputation-based cooperation
- Long-horizon project commitment
