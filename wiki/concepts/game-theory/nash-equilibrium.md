# Nash Equilibrium

## Суть в одной строке
Strategy profile где no player benefits from unilateral deviation — stable point of strategic interaction (Nash 1950).

## Определение
Nash equilibrium = strategy combination such that, given other players' strategies, no player gains by changing own strategy unilaterally. Existence guaranteed (Nash 1950) for finite games. Multiple equilibria common. Quality varies (some bad для everyone — Prisoner's Dilemma defect-defect).

## Ключевые свойства
- Stability (no unilateral incentive to change)
- Existence guaranteed для finite games
- Multiple equilibria common (focal-point selection)
- Can be Pareto-inferior (PD)
- Mixed-strategy equilibria when pure fail

## Применимость к Realm
- Quest design (E4): ensure cooperative equilibrium (not defection)
- Clan formation (E3): coordination equilibria
- Mechanism design ensures desired equilibria
