# Strategy of Conflict

## Суть в одной строке
Schelling 1960 framework: even adversaries have common interest (avoid mutual destruction); strategic interaction = mix conflict + cooperation.

## Определение
Schelling reframed strategic interaction: pure-conflict zero-sum games rare; most situations mixed (some shared, some opposed). Commitment devices, brinkmanship, threats как strategic moves. Brinkmanship = generate risk of mutual disaster для leverage. Commitment devices = bind future self to specific action для credibility.

## Ключевые свойства
- Mixed-motive interactions
- Commitment devices
- Brinkmanship
- Credibility through irrevocable moves
- Tacit bargaining

## Применимость к Realm
- Inter-clan competition (Faction Wars equivalent)
- Commitment devices в quest contracts
- Credibility through track record
