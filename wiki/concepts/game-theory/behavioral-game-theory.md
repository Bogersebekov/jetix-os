# Behavioral Game Theory

## Суть в одной строке
Empirical experiments показывают game theory predictions часто fail; real players follow level-k thinking, fairness norms, bounded rationality.

## Определение
Camerer 2003 «Behavioral Game Theory» synthesized experimental results. Players deviate от Nash predictions: Ultimatum game (fairness > pure self-interest), Beauty contest (level-k thinking, not infinite induction), Public goods games (conditional cooperation). Cognitive Hierarchy model: level-0 random, level-1 best-response to level-0, etc.

## Ключевые свойства
- Empirical deviations from Nash
- Fairness preferences
- Bounded rationality (level-k)
- Conditional cooperation
- Other-regarding utility

## Применимость к Realm
- Design assuming bounded rationality (not perfectly rational players)
- Fairness norms reinforced (avoid pure-self-interest equilibrium)
- Conditional cooperation patterns
