# Focal Points (Schelling Points)

## Суть в одной строке
Default coordination solutions players naturally converge to absent communication — «12 noon at Grand Central» canonical example.

## Определение
Schelling 1960: in coordination problems with multiple equilibria, players gravitate к solutions с salient property (prominent, simple, culturally focal). Demonstrated experimentally («where would you meet in NYC tomorrow?» → most respondents Grand Central, 12 noon). Crucial для tacit coordination.

## Ключевые свойства
- Salience-based selection
- Culturally / contextually relative
- No communication needed
- Self-fulfilling
- Failures = miscoordination

## Применимость к Realm
- E3: default meeting times / formats (Mondays 09:00, weekly all-hands)
- E4: standard quest formats (clarity of expectation)
- Schedule conventions
