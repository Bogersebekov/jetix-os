# Matching Markets

## Суть в одной строке
Markets где price ≠ allocator (medical residency, kidney exchange, school choice); matching mechanisms align preferences (Roth, Nobel 2012).

## Определение
Alvin Roth (Stanford, Harvard) co-designed: National Resident Matching Program (US medical residency), New England kidney exchange, NYC + Boston school choice. **Deferred Acceptance algorithm** (Gale-Shapley 1962) underlies most. Applications где money не used / cannot solve allocation (kidneys cannot be sold).

## Ключевые свойства
- Two-sided preferences
- Deferred acceptance algorithm
- Stable matching (no blocking pair)
- Strategy-proofness (truthful preference revelation)
- Applications: housing, education, organ exchange

## Применимость к Realm
- Match quests к clan (preferences both sides)
- Mentor-mentee matching (preferences both sides)
- Stable matching (no member wants to swap clans за better alternative both prefer)
