# Incentive Compatibility

## Суть в одной строке
Mechanism property where truth-telling = dominant strategy — agents always benefit from revealing real preferences.

## Определение
Foundational mechanism-design property. Vickrey 2nd-price auction = incentive compatible (truthful bidding dominant). Bayesian incentive compatibility (truth-telling expected utility-maximizing). **Strong** (dominant strategy IC) vs **weak** (Bayesian IC) versions. Revelation principle: any social choice function implementable как direct IC mechanism.

## Ключевые свойства
- Truth-telling = best response
- Strong (dominant) vs weak (Bayesian) IC
- Revelation principle
- VCG auctions (canonical example)
- Avoid manipulability

## Применимость к Realm
- Quest preference declaration (truthful)
- Skill self-assessment (incentive-compatible review)
- Pricing mechanism (no strategic underbidding)
