# Whaling Monetization

## Суть в одной строке
Game economy designed для extracting outsize spend from <1% «whale» segment (often gambling-prone or addicted), generating 50%+ revenue. **ANTI-PATTERN.**

## Определение
Shokrizade documented toxic monetization: whaling identifies high-spenders, design tightens screws (energy gates, frustration injection, FOMO timers) to extract more. Top 0.1% players generate 50%+ revenue in many F2P mobile games. **Ethically problematic** — exploits behavioral biases, often gambling-adjacent.

## Ключевые свойства
- <1% players generate >50% revenue
- Frustration injection (paid skip)
- FOMO timers
- Variable rewards (gambling psychology)
- Gambling-adjacent (loot boxes regulated 2022+)

## Применимость к Realm
- **EXPLICIT AVOID** anti-pattern
- No frustration-induced purchases
- No FOMO-trapped timers
- No variable-reward loot boxes
- Healthy whales (high-spend healthy adults) OK; vulnerable population exploitation NOT OK
