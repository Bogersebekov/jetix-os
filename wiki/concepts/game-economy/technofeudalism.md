# Technofeudalism

## Суть в одной строке
Varoufakis 2023: Big Tech platforms (Amazon, Apple, Meta) extract rents from third-party «vassals» (merchants, creators) — modern feudalism, not capitalism.

## Определение
Argument: Capital ceased to be central. Cloud-rent (Amazon merchant fees, Apple App Store 30%, Twitch/YouTube cuts) = pure rent extraction from vassals using platform infrastructure. Platform owners = «cloudalists», vassals = digital serfs. Antidote: democratized digital commons, alternative ownership structures. **Realm relevance: design Realm NOT as extractive platform**.

## Ключевые свойства
- Rent extraction (vs. value creation)
- Platform monopoly («cloud capital»)
- Third-party «vassal» dependency
- Lock-in pattern (cannot easily move)
- Antidote: democratic ownership

## Применимость к Realm
- AVOID extractive Realm design
- Clans own their work output
- Reasonable platform fee (10-15%, NOT 30%+)
- Membership transferable / portable
- Knowledge token portability outside Realm
