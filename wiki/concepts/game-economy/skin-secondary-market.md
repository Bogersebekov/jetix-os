# Skin Secondary Market (CS2)

## Суть в одной строке
Cosmetic items tradeable через Steam Market + third-party — investment psychology emerges (rare skins appreciate as collectibles).

## Определение
CS2 skins (originally Counter-Strike GO 2013+) tradeable Steam Community Market. Multi-billion total market cap. Knife skins $5K-$500K, Karambits, AWP Dragon Lore. Third-party sites (CSFloat, Skinport) for Sale outside Steam ($ conversion). **Pattern**: cosmetics with secondary trading become asset class. Risk: gambling/scam adjacent.

## Ключевые свойства
- Steam Market (in-game trading)
- Third-party USD-bridge sites
- Float/wear values (skin condition)
- Rarity tiers (Consumer → Covert → Knife)
- Appreciation potential (rare drops)

## Применимость к Realm
- LIMITED: Realm shouldn't speculate
- Pattern observation only: tradeable cosmetic + appreciation
