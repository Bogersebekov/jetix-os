# One Up — Gaming Industry Macro Analysis

## Суть в одной строке
Van Dreunen 2020: gaming industry as $180B+ globally — accessible macro framework для understanding business of games.

## Определение
One Up book: «Creativity, Competition, and the Global Business of Video Games». Three eras (publishing 1980-2000, attention 2000-2015, platforms 2015+). Maps industry structure: publishers, platforms, developers, infrastructure. Accessible to non-technical readers. **Useful for Realm market-positioning analysis.**

## Ключевые свойства
- 3 eras framework
- Industry structure mapping
- Business model evolution
- Cultural + economic lens
- Forward-looking analysis

## Применимость к Realm
- Position Realm в gaming landscape (substrate, not game)
- Learn from platforms-era (2015+) mistakes
- Cross-industry analogies
