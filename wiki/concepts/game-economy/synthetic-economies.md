# Synthetic Economies

## Суть в одной строке
Game-world economies (EverQuest, EVE Online) сопоставимые в размере / complexity с small national economies; Castronova's foundational framing.

## Определение
Castronova (2001 paper, 2005 book Synthetic Worlds) coined «synthetic economy». EverQuest virtual GDP exceeded Bulgaria (2001). These economies real (real-money trading possible), follow standard econ principles (supply/demand, inflation, labor markets). **Substrate-level concept** для всех виртуальных экономик.

## Ключевые свойства
- Real economic mechanisms (not «just game»)
- Real-money trading bridge (legal or not)
- Inflation, deflation, currency speculation
- Labor markets (player-employees)
- Government-like role (game designer = central bank)

## Применимость к Realm
- Realm economy IS synthetic economy по definition
- Apply standard econ tools (sinks/faucets, transparency reports)
- Real economic effects (members can earn living)
