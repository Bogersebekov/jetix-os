# Sinks & Faucets Balance

## Суть в одной строке
Game economy balance principle: faucets (currency creation: bounties, quest rewards) vs sinks (currency destruction: NPC fees, repair, taxes) — must balance for stable inflation.

## Определение
Sinks/faucets = canonical balance framework virtual economies (Castronova, Lehdonvirta). **Faucets**: NPC payouts, drops, mining = currency creation. **Sinks**: NPC vendors selling required items, repair costs, taxes, ship destruction = currency destruction. Mismatch → hyperinflation (too much faucet) or deflation (too much sink).

## Ключевые свойства
- Faucet rate must approx = sink rate (steady-state)
- Faucet excess → inflation (Diablo III early days)
- Sink excess → deflation (rare)
- Player population growth modifies balance (more faucets)
- Visible sink (luxury items, sovereignty fees) acceptable; invisible (drain) creates frustration

## Применимость к Realm
- Knowledge tokens issued (faucets: quest completion, peer kudos)
- Knowledge tokens destroyed (sinks: AI credit purchase, mentor session fees)
- Quarterly review of balance в Realm MER
