# Real Money Trading (RMT)

## Суть в одной строке
Exchange of in-game currency / items / accounts за real money — historically illegal (TOS violation), now mostly legalized via PLEX / WoW Token bridges.

## Определение
RMT history: grey market trading EverQuest items eBay early 2000s, professional Chinese «gold farming» operations (Diablo III RMAH 2012, since closed). Modern: official bridges (EVE PLEX, WoW Token) legalized, capturing revenue and removing scam risk. Castronova framed RMT как unavoidable consequence virtual economy realness.

## Ключевые свойства
- Item / currency / account types
- Grey market vs official bridges
- Black-market risks (scams, account theft)
- Sets implicit $-to-virtual exchange rate
- Some jurisdictions tax (South Korea)

## Применимость к Realm
- Official bridge (Premium credits)
- NO grey market (gated by KYC and Knowledge token regulation)
- Knowledge token tax-treatment Question (Phase 3)
