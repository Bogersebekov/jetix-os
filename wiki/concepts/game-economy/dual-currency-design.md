# Dual Currency Design

## Суть в одной строке
Two currencies — earned (Blue Essence/RP equivalent) + premium ($-purchased) — players choose: time или money buy progression.

## Определение
LoL Riot Points (RP, paid only) + Blue Essence (BE, earned only). Same products available в obeих, different price points. Players с money fast-buy; players с time grind. **Cleaner UX than convertibility** (no exchange rate confusion).

## Ключевые свойства
- Two currencies (earned + premium)
- Same products available
- No conversion (clean separation)
- Both viable paths to progress
- Premium currency typically cheaper-per-unit (volume discount when buying $)

## Применимость к Realm
- E5: Knowledge tokens (earned via quests) + Premium credits (purchasable)
- Both unlock same things
- Time-rich earn, money-rich pay — same outcome
