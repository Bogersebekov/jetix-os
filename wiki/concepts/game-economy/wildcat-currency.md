# Wildcat Currency

## Суть в одной строке
Castronova 2014 thesis: virtual currencies (game tokens, frequent flyer miles, crypto) emerging как wildcat-banking-era pattern — pre-regulated, private issuers, fluctuating value.

## Определение
«Wildcat Banking» (US 1837-1864) era — anyone could issue paper currency. Castronova argues today's virtual currencies (V-Bucks, Robux, crypto, frequent flyer miles) resemble that era: private issuers, no central regulation, value fluctuates на trust. Predicts eventual regulation but acknowledges current freedom as innovative space.

## Ключевые свойства
- Private issuer (game studio, platform)
- No central regulation (mostly)
- Trust-based value
- Convertibility varies
- May (eventually) be regulated like fiat

## Применимость к Realm
- Realm Knowledge tokens as wildcat currency (initially)
- Plan regulation horizon (if reaches financial-product threshold)
- Trust-building primary
