# Flow State Design (Csikszentmihalyi 1990)

## Суть в одной строке

Csikszentmihalyi Flow (1990) — 8 dimensions optimal experience; engagement substrate, designed-for-user-benefit (НЕ extraction).

## Определение

Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience (Csikszentmihalyi 1990) — 8 dimensions:

1. **Challenge-skill balance** — task на edge of capabilities (не too easy / hard)
2. **Clear goals** — что нужно достичь
3. **Immediate feedback** — на каждое action
4. **Concentration** — full attention
5. **Sense of control** — agency
6. **Loss of self-consciousness**
7. **Time distortion**
8. **Autotelic experience** — activity rewarding in itself

## Применимость

- **Engagement substrate** для education / product / community design
- **Apply ethically — designed-for-user-benefit:** flow benefits user, не extraction
- **Pair с Hook model** — caution: Hook может использовать flow механики для extraction (R12 violation)
- **Cross-ref Koster Theory of Fun** (mastery dynamics layer)
