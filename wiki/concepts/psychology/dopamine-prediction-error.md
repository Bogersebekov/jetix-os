# Dopamine Prediction Error

## Суть в одной строке
Dopamine signals reward-prediction-error: actual reward minus expected reward — explains why variable rewards more addictive (high error variance).

## Определение
Schultz, Dayan, Montague 1997 paper «A Neural Substrate of Prediction and Reward» Science. Dopamine neurons fire не при reward delivery per se, но при unexpected reward (or omission of expected). Predicted rewards → no dopamine spike. Unpredictable rewards → maximum dopamine variance → addictive feel.

## Ключевые свойства
- Encodes prediction error (not reward magnitude)
- Maximum response при unexpected positive
- Negative error при missing expected reward
- Foundation of reinforcement learning
- Explains gambling neuroscience

## Применимость к Realm
- AVOID overuse of unpredictable rewards (addictive design)
- Use predictable rewards mostly (no dopamine spike → no addiction, sustainable motivation)
- Occasional surprise bonuses OK
