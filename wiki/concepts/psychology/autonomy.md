# Autonomy (SDT)

## Суть в одной строке
Need для feeling self-direction в actions — choosing «what» и «how» rather than externally compelled.

## Определение
SDT autonomy ≠ independence (alone) but volition (self-endorsed). Acting because chosen, not because pressured. Even socially-connected behavior can be autonomous if internally endorsed. Critical для intrinsic motivation. Antithesis: controlled behavior (must, should, deadlines without buy-in).

## Ключевые свойства
- Volition (not isolation)
- Self-endorsement
- Choice availability
- Internal locus of causality
- Coexists with relatedness

## Применимость к Realm
- E4: quest selection optional (player picks); never assigned
- Multiple paths within quest (autonomy in execution)
- Optional structure (newcomers benefit; advanced ignore)
