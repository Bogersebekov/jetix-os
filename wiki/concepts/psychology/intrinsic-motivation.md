# Intrinsic vs Extrinsic Motivation

## Суть в одной строке
Intrinsic = activity itself rewarding; extrinsic = doing for external reward; **CET shows external rewards CAN reduce intrinsic motivation** (crowding out).

## Определение
Deci 1971 famous puzzle experiment: paid participants для solving puzzles → after pay stopped, played less. Unpaid control continued. **Cognitive Evaluation Theory (CET)**: extrinsic rewards perceived as controlling reduce autonomy → reduce intrinsic motivation. Reward тип matters (informational rewards OK; controlling rewards bad).

## Ключевые свойства
- Intrinsic: activity itself rewarding
- Extrinsic: external reward
- Crowding-out effect (CET)
- Reward type matters (informational vs controlling)
- Continuum (not binary)

## Применимость к Realm
- Avoid controlling rewards (excessive monetary payment for activities should be intrinsically rewarding)
- Informational rewards (visible mastery, peer recognition) preserve intrinsic
- Balance careful
