# Flow State

## Суть в одной строке
Optimal experience где activity feels effortless, time distorts, self-consciousness dissolves — emerges when challenge matches skill.

## Определение
Csikszentmihalyi 1975+ identified flow state в creatives, athletes, surgeons, chess players. 8 conditions (Csikszentmihalyi 1990): (1) clear goals, (2) immediate feedback, (3) challenge ~ skill, (4) action-awareness merging, (5) concentration, (6) sense of control, (7) loss of self-consciousness, (8) time distortion. Often intensely intrinsically rewarding.

## Ключевые свойства
- 8 conditions
- Challenge-skill match (critical)
- Effortless concentration
- Time distortion
- Self-consciousness dissolves
- Intrinsically rewarding

## Применимость к Realm
- E4 quest design: clear goals + immediate feedback
- Challenge calibration (per E1 player level)
- Quest difficulty escalation matching skill growth
- Avoid boredom (challenge < skill) + anxiety (challenge > skill)
