# Relatedness (SDT)

## Суть в одной строке
Need to feel connected к others, belonging к group, caring + being cared for.

## Определение
SDT relatedness = sense of belonging, mutual care, secure connections. Distinct from autonomy/competence (third pillar). Group identification, shared goals, vulnerability allowed. **Direct mapping для E3 Clan structure.**

## Ключевые свойства
- Belonging
- Mutual care
- Secure connection
- Group identity
- Vulnerability allowed

## Применимость к Realm
- E3 clan direct mapping (small 3-10 group)
- Long-term membership encouraged
- Vulnerability mechanisms (peer support, mentorship)
- Shared identity
