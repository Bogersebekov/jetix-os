# Commitment & Consistency

## Суть в одной строке
After committing к stance / action, people act consistently with it — Cialdini's principle (foot-in-the-door, written commitments).

## Определение
Cialdini 1984: после public commitment, internal pressure to remain consistent. Foot-in-the-door technique (small ask → big ask). Written commitments stronger than verbal. Public stronger than private. **Powerful для community formation, weak adoption паттерн.**

## Ключевые свойства
- Public > private commitment
- Written > verbal
- Foot-in-the-door progression
- Internal consistency pressure
- Self-perception alignment

## Применимость к Realm
- Onboarding commit ladder (Day 1 small → Week 4 large)
- Public clan commitments
- Written goals visible to clan
- Class declaration as public commitment
