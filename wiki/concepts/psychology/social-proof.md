# Social Proof

## Суть в одной строке
Humans look к others' behavior для cues on what to do, especially in uncertain situations — Cialdini's 2nd influence principle.

## Определение
Cialdini 1984: «We view a behavior as correct в a given situation к degree that we see others performing it». Strongest когда uncertain (newcomer setting) + similar others (peer reference). Examples: laugh tracks, restaurant queues, testimonial walls. **Critical для onboarding + adoption.**

## Ключевые свойства
- Effective in uncertainty
- Stronger with similar others
- Visible signals matter (counts, names)
- Negative social proof can backfire
- Cultural variation

## Применимость к Realm
- Onboarding: «X clans completed Y quest this week»
- Leaderboards (social proof of progression)
- Peer testimonials
- Similar-to-me filtering (показывать people in same class/tier)
