# Self-Determination Theory (SDT)

## Суть в одной строке
Meta-theory motivation: humans thrive when 3 basic psychological needs met — Autonomy, Competence, Relatedness.

## Определение
SDT (Deci & Ryan 1985, formalized 2000) = meta-theory comprising 6 mini-theories (Cognitive Evaluation, Organismic Integration, Causality Orientations, Basic Psychological Needs, Goal Contents, Relationships Motivation). Core thesis: humans intrinsically motivated when 3 universal needs satisfied. External rewards can crowd out intrinsic motivation (CET).

## Ключевые свойства
- 3 universal needs (Autonomy / Competence / Relatedness)
- Intrinsic vs extrinsic motivation continuum
- Crowding-out effect (CET)
- Self-Determination Continuum
- Empirically validated across cultures

## Применимость к Realm
- E1 / E2 / E3 — directly engineer 3 SDT needs
- Autonomy: free quest selection
- Competence: visible skill progression
- Relatedness: clan structure
- Avoid extrinsic-dominant rewards (CET warning)
