# Scarcity

## Суть в одной строке
Things less available perceived more valuable — limited time / quantity / access trigger desire (Cialdini 6th).

## Определение
Cialdini 1984: scarcity heuristic ancient evolutionary. Limited time / quantity / access. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) primary modern manifestation. **Heavy use в battle pass design (season ends → cosmetics unavailable forever).** Backlash if perceived manipulative.

## Ключевые свойства
- Time-limited (FOMO)
- Quantity-limited (rare items)
- Access-limited (membership)
- Loss-aversion trigger
- Manipulative if artificial

## Применимость к Realm
- E6 seasonal exclusive cosmetics
- Limited cohort size (10 founding members)
- BE cautious с artificial scarcity (manipulation backlash)
