# Operant Conditioning

## Суть в одной строке
Behavior shaped by consequences: reinforcement (positive/negative) increases, punishment (positive/negative) decreases behavior frequency.

## Определение
Skinner 1930s+ operant conditioning framework. 4 quadrants: positive reinforcement (add good → repeat), negative reinforcement (remove bad → repeat), positive punishment (add bad → stop), negative punishment (remove good → stop). Reinforcement schedules: fixed-ratio, variable-ratio (slot machines!), fixed-interval, variable-interval. **Variable-ratio = most powerful + addictive.**

## Ключевые свойства
- 4 quadrants (reinforcement × punishment × positive × negative)
- Reinforcement schedules
- Variable-ratio = strongest
- Generalization + discrimination
- Extinction (stop reinforcement → behavior fades)

## Применимость к Realm
- Use positive reinforcement (rewards) over punishment
- Variable-ratio cautious (manipulation risk)
- Fixed-ratio for skill building (predictable)
- Variable-interval для retention (notifications)
