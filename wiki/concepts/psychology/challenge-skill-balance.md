# Challenge-Skill Balance

## Суть в одной строке
Flow occurs в narrow band where challenge ≈ skill; too easy = boredom, too hard = anxiety — both prevent flow.

## Определение
Csikszentmihalyi's flow channel: x-axis skill, y-axis challenge, diagonal band = flow. Below diagonal = boredom (skill > challenge). Above = anxiety (challenge > skill). Designer's task: match challenge tightly к skill, increment both together. Game design — escalating difficulty matched to skill growth.

## Ключевые свойства
- Narrow flow channel
- Boredom region (low challenge)
- Anxiety region (high challenge)
- Skill-challenge co-growth pattern
- Dynamic calibration

## Применимость к Realm
- E4 quest difficulty matched к E1 player level
- Auto-calibration based on past quest completion
- Quest difficulty graduation paths
