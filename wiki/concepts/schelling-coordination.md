# Schelling Coordination (1960)

## Суть в одной строке

Schelling Strategy of Conflict (1960) — focal points / commitment devices / credible threats — canonical coordination-game reference.

## Определение

The Strategy of Conflict (Schelling 1960) — game-theoretic substrate для coordination problems:

- **Focal point (Schelling point):** solution, выбираемый когда нет communication, но есть shared salience (классический: meet в Grand Central NYC в noon)
- **Commitment devices:** binding constraints, делающие threats credible
- **Credible threats / promises** — must быть rational для threater после fact
- **Tipping points** — small perturbations triggering large coordination shifts
- **Bargaining theory** — focal-point selection в bargaining games

Pair с Axelrod Evolution of Cooperation (1984) — tit-for-tat в iterated PD как coordination substrate.

## Применимость к Jetix

- **Network-State substrate** — focal-point identification для Jetix-Clan coordination
- **Virtual-economy design** — commitment devices для cohort retention (без R12 violation)
- **Tipping-point analysis** для community growth
