# Cascade 150→15 Amplification

## Суть в одной строке

**150 base outreaches** → **~15 amplifiers** (10% conversion) → **1M downstream users** (avg ~67K downstream per amplifier).

## Cascade diagram

```
Layer 0: Ruslan + small core team
        ↓
        ↓ outreach × 10-20/day × ~30 days
        ↓
Layer 1: 150-300 base contacts (table-of-hundreds)
        ↓ ~10% conversion to amplifier-tier
        ↓
Layer 2: 15 amplifiers (large bloggers / corporate / state contacts / Mr. Beast-tier)
        ↓ ~67K avg downstream per amplifier
        ↓ (1-2 mega-amplifiers + 12-13 mid-amplifiers)
        ↓
Layer 3: 1,000,000 active users (EOY 2026 target)
```

## Layer-by-layer math

| Layer | Count | Mechanism | Time investment |
|---|---|---|---|
| L0 → L1 | 150 outreaches | 15-20 min pitch × 10-20/day × 1 month | ~30 hours Ruslan time |
| L1 → L2 | 15 amplifiers (10% of L1) | Amplifier accepts story + commits resources/audience | Conversion-rate F-grade: F3 (plausible based on B2B sales benchmark) |
| L2 → L3 | 1M users | Amplifier broadcasts to audience; product-pull from masses | Viral coefficient required ~1.5+ |

## Critical assumptions

1. **L0 → L1 conversion**: 100% (all 150 contacted) — F5 (operational)
2. **L1 → L2 conversion**: 10% — F3 (B2B sales benchmark ~5-15%)
3. **L2 → L3 conversion**: amplifier-quality dependent — F3 (Mr. Beast = trivial; mid-amplifier = needs viral coefficient)

## Mega-amplifier vs mid-amplifier mix

- **Mega-amplifier** (Mr. Beast 300M+ subs / large state contact / corporate giant): 1 push = 100K-1M downstream trivially
- **Mid-amplifier** (50K-500K subs): 1 push = 1K-50K downstream
- **Cascade requires 1-3 mega + 12-13 mid** for 1M target

## Risk vectors

- L1 → L2 conversion < 10% → 1M target unreachable, need cascade rebuild
- Mega-amplifier failure (Mr. Beast не подключается) → cascade math breaks
- Mid-amplifier viral coefficient < 1.5 → flat growth
