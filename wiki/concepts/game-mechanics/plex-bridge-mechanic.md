# PLEX Bridge Mechanic (EVE)

## Суть в одной строке
Player buys PLEX за реальные деньги, sells за ISK игрокам — legal bridge real $ ↔ virtual currency, replacing illegal RMT (real money trading).

## Определение
EVE PLEX = $20 paid item which converts to 500 PLEX tokens or 30-day subscription. Sellable on in-game market for ISK. **Legalizes** real-to-virtual money flow — captures premium revenue, prevents black market, allows poor-but-skilled players to earn subscription через gameplay. WoW Token = direct copy of PLEX mechanism.

## Ключевые свойства
- Real money purchase
- In-game tradeable
- Sets ISK ↔ $ exchange rate (player-discovered)
- Captures RMT revenue legally
- F2P enabling (good play → free subscription)

## Применимость к Realm
- **Pattern**: legalize real-to-virtual conversion (Knowledge tokens, AI credits)
- Avoid black market arbitrage
- Allow earning-via-mastery model
