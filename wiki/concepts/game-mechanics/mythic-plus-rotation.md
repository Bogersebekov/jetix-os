# Mythic+ Seasonal Rotation (WoW)

## Суть в одной строке
8-dungeon pool rotating per season (3-month cycle) с scaling difficulty +seasonal affix — retains 5-player coop content forever через rotation.

## Определение
WoW Mythic+ scales dungeon difficulty (key levels 2-30+). Each season changes affix (mob behavior modifier: «Bursting», «Sanguine»), forcing strategy refresh. 8 dungeon rotation pool. Encourages weekly play (cap +1 dungeon weekly chest). Strong retention without new content.

## Ключевые свойства
- Scaling difficulty (key level)
- 8-dungeon rotation pool
- Seasonal affix (mob behavior mod)
- Weekly cap (vault chest)
- Leaderboards (cross-realm)

## Применимость к Realm
- E6 anchor: seasonal mechanic affix
- Recurring quest pool с seasonal twist
- Weekly cap + leaderboard
