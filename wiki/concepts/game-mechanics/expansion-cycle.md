# Expansion Cycle (WoW)

## Суть в одной строке
~2-year major expansion (new continent, level cap raise, new feature) — multi-year retention rhythm параллельно с seasonal patch cadence.

## Определение
WoW expansion = ~$50 paid major update, ~2 years between, brings new max level (+10-20), new continent/zones, often new class/race, new system (e.g. Garrisons в WoD, Class Halls в Legion). Between expansions — 4-6 free patches with raids. Двухуровневая retention design (macro: expansion; meso: patch; micro: weekly resets).

## Ключевые свойства
- 2-year major cadence
- Level cap raise (gear reset)
- New zone/feature/system
- 4-6 patches between expansions
- Pre-expansion события («patch 5.4», «11.0», etc.)

## Применимость к Realm
- E6 macro layer: 2-year Realm major release cycle (significant new feature, like AI agent collaboration)
- Patches = 3-month seasons
- Pre-expansion = anticipation marketing
