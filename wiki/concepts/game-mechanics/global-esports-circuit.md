# Global Esports Circuit

## Суть в одной строке
Regional leagues (LEC, LCK, LCS, LPL) feed into annual Worlds — pyramid structure с visibility for top players.

## Определение
LoL esports: 5 regional leagues spring/summer (separate seasons), Mid-Season Invitational, World Championships ($2-3M prize annually). Riot designs around это — celebrity creation, narrative arcs (Faker / T1 dynasty). **Pattern**: pyramid recognition system, top stars become brand.

## Ключевые свойства
- 5 regional leagues
- Promotion/relegation system
- Annual MSI + Worlds
- Player celebrity creation
- Sponsorship + brand association
