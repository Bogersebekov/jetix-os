# UGC Marketplace

## Суть в одной строке
User-generated content platform where creators build experiences, list them, earn revenue share — Roblox DevEx ≈ $700M/yr to creators (2023).

## Определение
Roblox UGC marketplace: 1M+ user-created games («experiences»), 60M unique creators. Creators monetize through in-experience Robux earnings, gamepass sales, etc. Roblox takes 60-70% (varies), creators receive remainder (DevEx convert Robux → USD at minimum threshold).

## Ключевые свойства
- Creator tools (Roblox Studio, Lua scripting)
- Marketplace listing
- Revenue sharing (30-40% to creator)
- DevEx threshold (100K Robux min)
- Top creators earn millions/year

## Применимость к Realm
- Realm marketplace для clan-built playbooks, templates, services
- Revenue sharing with knowledge contributors
