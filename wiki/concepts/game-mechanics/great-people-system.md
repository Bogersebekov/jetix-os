# Great People System (Civilization)

## Суть в одной строке
Named historical figures (Da Vinci, Einstein) recruited через accumulating «points» в category — provide unique strong bonus when used.

## Определение
Civilization Great People: 5 categories (Scientist / Engineer / Artist / Merchant / General). Accumulating respective points unlocks named figure. Each имеет 1-2 strong abilities. Player chooses when to use (urgent boost or wait for better). **Pattern**: legendary individuals visible в game.

## Ключевые свойства
- 5+ categories
- Named historical figures
- Earned through point accumulation
- One-time powerful effect
- Choice timing crucial

## Применимость к Realm
- E1 visible «legend» status для mastered individuals
- Mentor invitation as one-time strong boost
- Named «Realm Legends» visible to all
