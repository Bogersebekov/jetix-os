# Era Progression (Civilization)

## Суть в одной строке
Game divided in 8-9 eras (Ancient → Information → Future) с phase transitions unlock new mechanics + visible game-stage marker.

## Определение
Civ era system: после researching critical-mass tech от current era, civilization progresses next era. Visible transition (UI changes, музыка changes, units update). Creates strong phase identity — early game vs mid game vs end game. **Pattern для Realm scaling**: clan transitions от «founding era» (1-3 members, MVP work) к «growth era» (4-7 members, multiple parallel quests) к «mastery era» (8-10 members, branded reputation).

## Ключевые свойства
- 8-9 eras
- Triggered by tech threshold
- Visible transition (UI/music)
- Unlocks new mechanics (e.g. religion at Classical)
- Per-civ pace (different rate of progression)

## Применимость к Realm
- E6 secondary: macro era-progression beyond seasons
- Clan era markers (founding / growth / mastery)
- Visible identity per era
