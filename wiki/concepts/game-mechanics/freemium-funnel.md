# Freemium Funnel

## Суть в одной строке
F2P-acquisition → frustration-pinch → small purchase → habituation → whale conversion — common monetization funnel mobile games (Candy archetype).

## Определение
Standard mobile F2P funnel: install free → easy onboarding → at level 30-50 face difficult level → either retry many times OR buy boosters ($0.99). After first purchase psychologically lowered barrier → more purchases. **0.1% players become whales** ($1000+/yr). 99% players free. Whales fund the game.

## Ключевые свойства
- Free download
- Easy first 20 levels (positive psychology)
- Difficulty spike (frustration injection)
- $0.99 «just one» offer
- Subsequent purchases higher tier
- Whale segment (0.1% = 50%+ revenue)

## Применимость к Realm
- **ANTI-PATTERN** if whale-dependent
- Pattern: free entry + premium services accessible
- AVOID frustration-induced purchase pressure
