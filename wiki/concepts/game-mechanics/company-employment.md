# Company Employment Mechanic (Torn)

## Суть в одной строке
Players hire other players в свои companies (firearms factory, casino, etc.) — direct mapping для Jetix-as-platform pattern.

## Определение
Torn Company system: player-owned company (одна на player) с positions (1-10 slots), specializations. Workers нанимаются player-CEOs, выполняют 8-hour shifts, get paid wage + bonuses. Direct work model (без abstractions) — самый близкий precedent к gamified Jetix.

## Ключевые свойства
- Player-owned companies (CEO role)
- Hire/fire workers (player-to-player employment)
- Specializations (firearms, biotech, music studio, etc.)
- Shift work (time-based labor)
- Wage + performance bonus

## Применимость к Realm
- Jetix as «company» template hosting кланов
- Internal labor market (cross-clan hiring)
- Specialization match (E2 class match to company need)
