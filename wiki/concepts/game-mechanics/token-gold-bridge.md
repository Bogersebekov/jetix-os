# WoW Token Gold Bridge

## Суть в одной строке
WoW Token = $20 purchase, converts to in-game gold at player-discovered rate — direct EVE PLEX copy (2015).

## Определение
Blizzard launched WoW Token 2015 после observing EVE PLEX success. Tokens покупаются за $20 USD, sells in-game для gold at floating rate (currently ~250,000 gold per token). Players с временем но без денег earn subscription; players с деньгами но без времени skip grind. **Killed grey-market RMT** (Blizzard captures revenue).

## Ключевые свойства
- Real money purchase ($20)
- In-game tradeable (gold)
- Floating price (player-discovered)
- Game-managed exchange (no third party)
- Subscription convertible

## Применимость к Realm
- Knowledge token / AI credit conversion
- Players без free time → buy resources
- Players с free time → earn resources
