# Crossover Collaborations (Fortnite)

## Суть в одной строке
Limited-time external IP integration (Marvel, Star Wars, Dragon Ball, music artists) — drives net-new player acquisition + lapsed reactivation.

## Определение
Fortnite pioneered IP crossovers as monetization + retention engine. Marvel mid-season events (Avengers Endgame, etc.), Star Wars seasons, Dragon Ball collab, Travis Scott concert. Each crossover brings IP fanbase. **Pattern**: temporary brand collaboration as marketing channel.

## Ключевые свойства
- Time-limited (1-4 weeks)
- Major IP (recognized globally)
- Cosmetic + light-mechanic integration
- Cross-promotion (IP company benefits too)
- Limited supply (FOMO)

## Применимость к Realm
- E6 seasonal crossovers (e.g. «Pharma Season» partnering with pharma industry guest mentors)
- Limited-time guest mentor / brand partnership
- Cross-niche introduction
