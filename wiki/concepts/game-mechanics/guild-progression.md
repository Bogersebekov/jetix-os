# Guild Progression (WoW)

## Суть в одной строке
Guild-level XP, achievements, perks (mass-resurrect, increased XP, repair discounts) — collective leveling параллельно с individual.

## Определение
WoW Guild Progression introduced Cataclysm (2010). Guilds накапливают XP collectively, unlock perks. Active raid guilds = max level fast; casual social guilds — slow. **Mechanic encourages stable membership** (guild perks transferable only via stay). Direct mapping для Realm clan reputation + perks.

## Ключевые свойства
- Guild XP from member activity (quests, raids, BG wins)
- Level 1-25 (originally), with progressive unlocks
- Perks include economic (10% gold) + utility (mass-rez, fast travel)
- Guild bank tabs unlocked at levels
- Achievements add to prestige (visible reputation)

## Применимость к Realm
- E3 anchor: clan-level XP from member quest completion
- Perks: shared AI credits, joint mentor access, premium templates
- Achievements visible на public clan profile
