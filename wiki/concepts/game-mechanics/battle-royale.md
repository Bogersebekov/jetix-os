# Battle Royale

## Суть в одной строке
100 players → shrinking play zone → last one (or team) wins — accelerating tension через space compression.

## Определение
BR canonical structure: drop-in deployment (100 players), pick up gear, eliminate opponents, stay in shrinking circle, fight final concentrations. Time-pressured (matches 15-25 min). PUBG popularized 2017, Fortnite mass-adopted 2018. **Mechanic shines through tension curve**: early exploration → mid combat → late high-stakes.

## Ключевые свойства
- 100 players (or 60-150)
- Shrinking zone (zone damage outside)
- Loot-and-scoot mechanic
- Single life per match
- 15-25 min matches

## Применимость к Realm
- E4: limited-time competitive matchmaking («deal battle royale»)
- Multiple clans pitch single client, narrow down
