# Emerging Market Monetization

## Суть в одной строке
$1-3 ARPPU vs $20+ Western — bundled smaller purchases, regional pricing, payment-method localization.

## Определение
PUBG Mobile, Free Fire optimized для India, SEA, LATAM. ARPPU 5-10x lower than Western. Адаптации: payment methods (Paytm, GCash, Boleto), smaller bundles ($0.50-$1), regional events, low-end device support. **Pattern**: meet market где он есть, не force premium pricing.

## Ключевые свойства
- $1-3 ARPPU baseline
- Smaller bundle SKUs
- Regional payment methods
- Low-end device optimization
- Locally-relevant content

## Применимость к Realm
- Pricing по geography (PPP-adjusted)
- Smaller membership tiers
- Localized payment
