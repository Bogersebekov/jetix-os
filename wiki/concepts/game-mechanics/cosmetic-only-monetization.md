# Cosmetic-Only Monetization

## Суть в одной строке
Monetization purely через cosmetic items (skins, voice lines, particle effects) — no Pay-to-Win, gameplay equal for all.

## Определение
Dota 2 (Valve), CS2 (Valve), League of Legends (largely): no advantages purchased. Cosmetics provide social signaling, customization, support for favorite team. **Anti-whaling pattern** + ethical monetization model. Generates ~$300M+ annual revenue Dota despite no power purchase.

## Ключевые свойства
- No gameplay advantage purchases
- Cosmetic-only (visual/audio)
- Player-traded (Steam Marketplace)
- Battle pass for collections
- Treasures (cosmetic loot boxes — controversial)

## Применимость к Realm
- E5 anchor: ethical monetization
- Premium customization (profile flair, badges)
- NO advantage purchases (NO pay-to-skip-energy etc.)
