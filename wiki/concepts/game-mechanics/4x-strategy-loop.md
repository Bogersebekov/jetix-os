# 4X Strategy Loop

## Суть в одной строке
4X = eXplore / eXpand / eXploit / eXterminate — canonical loop strategy genre (Civ, Stellaris, EU4).

## Определение
4X genre framework: discover map (Explore) → settle/grow territory (Expand) → optimize resources (Exploit) → win conflicts (Exterminate). All 4 phases continuous, not sequential. Different civs / leaders boost different X — strategic asymmetry. **Realm parallel**: discover niche (Explore market) → grow clan (Expand recruits) → optimize ops (Exploit playbook) → win deals vs competition (Exterminate metaphor → outcompete).

## Ключевые свойства
- 4 continuous activities
- Asymmetric civ design (different bonuses)
- Resource curve from exploit
- Conflict resolution (war or diplomacy)
- Tech tree backbone

## Применимость к Realm
- Clan-level strategy loop
- Marketplace exploration
- Recruitment expansion
- Ops optimization
- Competitive deal-winning
