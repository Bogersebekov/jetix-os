# Daily Streak Retention

## Суть в одной строке
Daily login streak counter с escalating rewards — exploits loss aversion («don't break streak») для 7-30 day habit formation.

## Определение
Candy Crush, Duolingo, Snapchat use daily streak pattern. Day 1 reward, Day 7 bigger, Day 30 значительный. **Loss aversion** (Kahneman-Tversky) → players check in just to maintain. Highly effective retention for 30-60 days then plateau.

## Ключевые свойства
- Daily check-in required
- Escalating rewards (Day 1 small, Day 30 large)
- Streak break = restart from Day 1
- Visible counter
- Sometimes «streak protect» purchases (manipulative)

## Применимость к Realm
- Limited: avoid manipulation pattern
- Daily reflection / micro-quest = soft habit
- NO «streak break = lose all» (too harsh)
