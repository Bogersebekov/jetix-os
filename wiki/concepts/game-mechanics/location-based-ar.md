# Location-Based AR

## Суть в одной строке
Gameplay tied to physical location (PokeStops at real-world points-of-interest) — bridges digital game to physical reality.

## Определение
Pokemon Go (Niantic, 2016) integrated AR + real-world GPS. PokeStops at real POIs (museums, parks, landmarks). Players walk to engage. Raid bosses spawn in specific locations. **Connects digital reward к physical activity** — health benefit side-effect. Niantic also Ingress, Monster Hunter Now.

## Ключевые свойства
- GPS-based gameplay
- Physical location markers
- Real-world events (Community Days local)
- Walking required (Eggs hatch via distance)
- AR mode (camera overlay) optional

## Применимость к Realm
- E4: location-specific quests (Berlin meetup, conference attendance)
- IRL clan events (raid coop in-person)
- Physical reality tie-in
