# Full-Loot PvP (EVE)

## Суть в одной строке
Player death = full ship loss (modules drop, hull destroyed) — позволяет real economic consequence playing-as-investing.

## Определение
В EVE death PvP destroys ship + drops значительную часть cargo. Это создаёт REAL economic stakes (player может ПОТЕРЯТЬ $100s of work). Контрастирует с WoW (death = repair fee + grave run). Effect — sink mechanism, скромный rate inflation control, и **real-stakes feel** which other MMOs avoid.

## Ключевые свойства
- Death = ship + modules destroyed
- 50% drop to attacker (hull always destroyed)
- Pod (escape capsule) can also be killed (loses implants)
- Insurance partial reimbursement
- Knowledge of risk creates real combat psychology

## Применимость к Realm
- **Limited application** — Realm не игра, нельзя «терять» work product
- Pattern: skin in the game — failed quest = lost resources sunk (real risk)
- Investment proportional to expected reward
