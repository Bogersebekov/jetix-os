# Emergent Gameplay

## Суть в одной строке
Simple rules combine into complex unscripted outcomes (Minecraft redstone, EVE political wars, Dwarf Fortress narratives).

## Определение
Emergent gameplay = system-level outcomes не designed explicitly. Simple components (block + redstone + lever) combine into anything. EVE «Bloodbath of B-R5RB» (2014) — 7600 players, $300K destruction, all unscripted political war. **Pattern**: Design rules, not scenarios. Players generate content / narrative.

## Ключевые свойства
- Simple primitives
- Combinatorial complexity
- Unscripted outcomes
- Community storytelling (EVE chronicles)
- Designer-as-rule-setter (not author)

## Применимость к Realm
- E4: rules of engagement, не predetermined quest list
- Players invent quest types
- Stories emerge from system
