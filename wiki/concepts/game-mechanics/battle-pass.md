# Battle Pass

## Суть в одной строке
Premium seasonal pass ($5-$15 за 3-month season) с tier progression unlocked by XP earnings — replaces loot boxes как dominant monetization.

## Определение
Battle Pass invented в Dota 2 (2013 Compendium), popularized by Fortnite (2018). Player pays once at season start, earns XP через play, unlocks reward tiers (cosmetics, premium currency). Free track gives starter rewards; paid track gives 80-100 tiers. **Replaced loot boxes** in major Western games (regulatory pressure).

## Ключевые свойства
- One-time purchase per season
- XP-based tier progression (visible bar)
- Free + Paid tracks
- Time-limited (FOMO if not bought start of season)
- Cosmetic + functional rewards
- Skippable tiers (paid via premium currency)

## Применимость к Realm
- E6 direct anchor: 3-month season + tier progression
- Premium pass with bonus rewards (NOT money-skip — earned)
- Cosmetic + functional rewards (titles, profile flair, AI credit boost)
