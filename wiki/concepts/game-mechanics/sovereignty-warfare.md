# Sovereignty Warfare (EVE)

## Суть в одной строке
Player alliances claim, hold, contest «sov» — звёздные системы — through coordinated multi-day conflict для control resource-rich regions.

## Определение
EVE 0.0 (null-sec) space = player-controlled. Alliances claim systems, place infrastructure (citadels, jump bridges, gates), defend через scheduled timers. Wars длятся месяцы. **Tactical scope outside Realm direct application** — но pattern «territorial possession + active defense» mappable to «owning niche market position».

## Ключевые свойства
- Player claim mechanic (extractor, hub)
- Timer-based defense windows
- Coordinated multi-thousand-player battles
- Infrastructure investment + risk
- Region-specific resources

## Применимость к Realm
- Limited (Realm не territorial)
- Pattern: «owning niche» (clan owns специфическую niche — Pharma, B2B AI consulting, etc.)
- Active defense = continued client/community engagement
