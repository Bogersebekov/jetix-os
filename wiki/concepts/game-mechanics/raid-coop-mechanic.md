# Raid Coop Mechanic

## Суть в одной строке
Coordinated multi-player (10-40 в WoW, 5-20 в PoGo) high-difficulty content где роли (tank/healer/DPS) обязательны и mistake-tolerance низкая.

## Определение
Raid pattern: scheduled coordinated event, fixed roles, high-difficulty boss(es), unique rewards. Roles enforce specialization (tank takes damage, healer restores, DPS produces damage). Wipe (full team death) — common. Encourages voice-comms, leadership, team coherence over time.

## Ключевые свойства
- 10-40 participants
- Scheduled (weekly raid night)
- Role enforcement
- High difficulty curve (multiple wipes expected)
- Unique rewards not obtainable solo
- Boss mechanics require coordination (positioning, timing)

## Применимость к Realm
- E3 + E4: «raid quest» pattern для major projects (10+ people, 1-2 weeks scheduled, coordinated)
- Role enforcement = specialist match (E2 class matching)
- Unique reward (≠ solo work output) = high-value client deal
