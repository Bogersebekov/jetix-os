# Outreach System Scalable

## Суть в одной строке

Operationalized outreach pattern: **10-person video team** → **100 trained operators** → **personalized video per target**; «чтобы не я это делал» (Ruslan не scaling bottleneck).

## Определение

Per text_009 Thread 5 + 13: outreach pattern должен быть described + operationalized для scaling к 3000+ targets (миллиардеры / миллионеры / разрабы / инженеры / платформы).

**Stage 1 (60 days):** 10-person video team assembly:
- 2 video producers + 2 copywriters + 2 on-screen talent + 2 researchers + 2 CRM operators.
- Output: 50-100 baseline outreach video templates по target classes.

**Stage 2 (Q4 2026):** 100 trained operators:
- 10-team trains 100 cohort members.
- TPS mentor-pairing pattern.
- Per-trainee specialization (target class subject-matter expertise).

**Stage 3 (Q1 2027+):** Personalized scale:
- Each operator covers ~30 targets/month.
- 100 operators × 30 targets/month × 12 months = 36,000 personalized engagements/year.
