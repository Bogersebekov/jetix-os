# Pre-existing Partnership Positioning

## §1 Суть в одной строке

Outreach frame: каждый человек уже работает над «проектом человечества» (project-of-humanity) — partnership пред-существует by virtue of shared meta-project; задача — articulate it explicitly.

## §3 Определение

**Pre-existing Partnership** = positioning predicate: addressee shares meta-project «развитие человечества» с outreach-initiator by virtue of working on any sub-project that contributes (positively or negatively) to humanity-level outcome. Partnership = articulated descriptive frame, not ask или sales pitch.

**Three integrated theses:**
1. **Zoom-out frame** (audio_688 C1-C2): each person's project = sub-project of project-of-humanity
2. **Pre-existing partnership** (audio_688 C3/C7, audio_697 C8): partnership exists already; task = articulate
3. **Bidirectional resource flow** (audio_688 C8/C11): paired offer-ask is constitutive; unilateral demand violates frame

## §4 Operational template

### Canonical outreach script (audio_688 C7 verbatim → template)

```
1. Greet by name + acknowledge specific project they work on
2. Zoom-out: «над каким проектом проекта-человечества ты работаешь?»
3. Articulate: «мы уже партнёры by virtue of shared meta-project»
4. Paired offer (specific): «вот что я вижу — где могу помочь вашему проекту»
5. Paired ask (specific): «вот в чём мне нужна помощь в моём»
6. Hypothesis frame (audio_697 C26): «погнали тестировать»
```

### Mandatory paired-frame discipline (R12 protection)

**MUST preserve:** offer + ask paired in same conversation. Removing offer-side reduces to unilateral resource demand → R12 anti-extraction violation. AP-6 dissent: aggressive recruiting tone (audio_688 C10-C12) preserve as Ruslan's authentic voice, but template form must keep paired structure.
