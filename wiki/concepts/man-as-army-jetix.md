# Man-as-Army × Jetix integration

## Суть в одной строке

Тарасов «человек-армия» (один человек = разведчик + стратег + воин + дипломат) применён к Jetix: каждый Clan member = self-army; «армия таких воинов» = distributed resilience architecture.

## Определение

text_006 (18.05 morning Ruslan dictation) applies Tarasov «человек-армия» pattern к Jetix Workshop / Clan / Self-OS:

**Core claim:** «Всё то, что может сделать армия — может сделать и один человек... быть разведчиком, стратегом, воином, дипломатом... вопрос насколько хорошо.» (text_006 ¶2)

**Inverse claim:** «может один человек, но не может вся армия — полностью честным самим собой, держать культа максимальную дисциплину, своим указом полностью повиноваться.» (text_006 ¶3)

**Application к Jetix:** Each Clan member = self-army; aggregate = «армия таких воинов» с distributed resilience properties (text_006 ¶3-4).

## Ключевые свойства

### Individual self-army level

- **Multi-role holder** — same person carries 4+ роли (`U.Role` multi-instantiation per A.2)
- **Cross-role capability composition** (`U.Capability` A.2.2)
- **Self-directed discipline** — что НЕ может армия: self-obedience (`E.5 Guard-Rails` interior)
- **Self-cultivation** — `B.4 Canonical Evolution Loop` at individual scope

### Aggregate (army-of-armies) level

- **Composition emergence** (`B.2 MHT` — individual holons → supersystem holon)
- **Distributed resilience** — broken parts → other regenerate / survive / propagate (`A.5 Kernel Modularity` + `B.4 Evolution Loop`)
- **Mission alignment** — shared `U.Episteme` (A.16) + `U.WorkPlan` (A.15.2)
- **Anti-feudal positioning** — «безопасным для общества + полезным + интеллектуальной» (text_006 ¶4-5; `A.7 Strict Distinction`)

### Sequencing model (text_006 ¶7)

- 10 → 100 → 1000 → 100k progression
- «Foundation builds parallel к expansion» (text_006 ¶8) — `B.4 Evolution Loop` continuous

## Применимость

**Когда применимо:** Clan member model design; Workshop participant onboarding; Self-OS substrate spec; first-clan activation (vision/04 §3.4 progression model).

**Когда НЕ применимо:**
- Mass-scale (100k+) — high-touch delivery method («лично каждому донести» text_006 ¶5) doesn't scale; Workshop platform + Pattern Language artefact carry Stage 3+
- Specialized expert teams (single-domain) — composition premise breaks
- Pure individual contributors без mission-alignment — composition glue absent

## Falsifier conditions

- DRA-T1: First 10 Clan signatories don't demonstrate ≥3-role competence within 90 days → multi-role claim refuted
- DRA-T2: Member-attrition simulation event (1 of 10 leaves) → if other 9 cannot maintain operation 30+ days post-event, distributed resilience refuted
- DRA-T3: Mission-alignment FPF shared-vision co-authorship rate <80% → mission-alignment glue refuted
- DRA-T4: R12 fork-and-leave clause exercised → substrate must continue without retaliation
- DRA-T5: Workshop hosts simultaneous multi-self-army cooperation event (≥3 partners) without central coordinator

## Cross-domain precedents (60+ years substrate)

| Precedent | Period | Pattern match |
|---|---|---|
| Mondragón (direction 06) | 68 years | Distributed ownership + 5:1 ratio cap + voluntary engagement |
| EVE Online (direction 13) | 23 years | Alliance/Corporation/Executor governance + B-R5RB stress test survived |
| TPS Toyota (direction 14) | 70 years | Mentor-pairing tacit→explicit + Andon empowerment + Kaizen |

Triangulation: distributed resilience through mission-aligned + capability-distributed + voluntarily-engaged self-managing members **IS empirically sustainable at multi-decade scale**.
