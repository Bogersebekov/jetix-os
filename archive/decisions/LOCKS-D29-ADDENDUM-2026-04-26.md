## §4 Implications (4 уровня)

### Level 1 — Self-narrative discipline (anti-impostor)

- **Ruslan internal voice:** "I am founder of Jetix corporation на early-stage", не "I am a solo AI consultant".
- **Cognitive frame:** decisions made as if responsible за хундреды тысяч людей и миллионы долларов (даже когда operationally solo).
- **Anti-drift mechanism:** при дрейфе обратно в "freelancer" framing (через стресс / низкий ревенью / external pressure), D29 lock surfaces — used as anchor to return.
- **Связь с affirmation-ritual** (concept audio_532): "ощущение себя на уровне" reinforces D29 self-narrative.

### Level 2 — External messaging consistency

- **Outreach templates** (`personal-mentor-search/mentor-portrait.md`, `team-search/consultant-search.md`, future Mittelstand outreach) — frame Ruslan as "founder of Jetix corporation на early-stage" + Phase 1 €50K + long horizon $1T. Already aligned per текущие templates.
- **Partner conversations** (discovery calls, intro pitches): emphasise corporate trajectory + scale ambition + responsibility frame, не one-off transaction.
- **Brand voice** (website copy, video pitches, ahead-content): consistent enterprise-fast + responsibility tone, без freelancer humility / solo-hero narrative.
- **Refinement (minor):** в outreach templates можно selectively emphasise "корпорация-стартап в early-stage" framing, если резонирует с specific archetype (e.g., investors, enterprise prospects).

### Level 3 — Team-hire framing

- **psy-PM hire (audio_535):** **NOT** "помощник Ruslan'а" / "personal coach"; **IS** "первая non-architect role в Jetix corporation в early-stage" + "seed-position для Phase 2 team". Меняет budget framing: не €500/мес тестовый, а seed-position в growing corporation (long-term value, not short-term experiment).
- **Strategic consultant hire (audio_534):** framed as "Strategic Advisor для Jetix corporation в Phase 1 → 2 → 3 trajectory navigation", не "consultant project for solo founder".
- **Future hires (Phase 2+):** все позиции framed как roles in growing corporation, не contractor relationships. Compensation discussions starting от corporate-trajectory framing (advisory equity Phase-2+ optional, retainer cash Phase-1 baseline).

### Level 4 — Brand voice anchor

- **Public communications consistent:** corporation-scale ambition, founder responsibility, non-freelancer.
- **Anti-aesthetics excluded:** "indie hacker" / "solopreneur" / "one-million-company" / "personal brand" — все эти aesthetics anti-pattern (D26 conflict + D29 internalisation).
- **Substance markers:** $1T trajectory, 200-year vision, civilizational infrastructure — все consistent с D29.
