The past informs practice. Unix has a long and colorful history, much of
which is still live as folklore, assumptions, and (too often) battle
scars in the collective memory of Unix programmers. In this chapter
we'll survey the history of Unix, with an eye to explaining why, in
2003, today's Unix culture looks the way it does.
