# Keynes on the Currency

</div>

<div class="content">

> There is no subtler, surer means of overturning the existing basis of
> society than to debauch the currency. The process engages all the
> hidden forces of economic law on the side of destruction, and it does
> it in a manner which not one man in a million is able to diagnose.
>
> – John Maynard Keynes
