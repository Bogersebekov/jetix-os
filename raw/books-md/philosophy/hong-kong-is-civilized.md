# Hong Kong is Civilized

</div>

<div class="content">

Free wifi in the airport, need I say more? Ok, what is stifling about
airports and modern travel is the lack of control. Stand in line. Wait.
Sit on the plane. Fasten your seatbelt. Put away your tray table. Close
the windowshade. What is web-surfing? Here’s a blank location bar – now
go, wherever you will. As in the Matrix, when your body is confined, the
mind wishes to roam. Many a government could ease the suffering of its
citizens by offering them mental freedom when it imposes physical
confinement. The web is powerful and American in its origins in that it
exports the one classic American virtue – freedom.
