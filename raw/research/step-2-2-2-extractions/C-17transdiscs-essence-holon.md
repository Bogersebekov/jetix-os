## Part 1 — The trans-disciplines (canonical 2023 list, 16 items)

Definitions verbatim from R-C §2; layers per §3.1.

### Foundation layer (1–5)

**#1 Понятизация** (Conceptualization / figuring-out) — "Teaches how to isolate figures from background — to pick out objects of consideration and give them names. The discipline of noticing what deserves attention." Bottom of stack. [R-C §2.1]

**#2 Собранность** (Collectedness / attentional mastery) — "Teaches how to hold objects — already identified by Понятизация — in attention over time, using not 'bare brain' but external memory apparatus (exocortex)." [R-C §2.2]

**#3 Семантика** (Semantics) — "Teaches how to link physical/real objects with mathematical/abstract/mental/ideal ones, and how to work with signs that denote objects. The bridge between the physical world and the world of descriptions." [R-C §2.3]

**#4 Математика** (Mathematics) — "Teaches what kinds of 'mental' (abstract/formal) objects exist, how they behave, and how some are constructed from others. The catalogue of ideal objects that descriptions of the physical world borrow from." *Added in 2023.* [R-C §2.4]

**#5 Физика** (Physics) — "Teaches what kinds of physical objects exist in the real world, and how we use mathematical/mental objects... to represent physical objects. Information theory is treated as a branch of Physics in the 2023 version." *Added in 2023.* [R-C §2.5]

### Formal reasoning layer (6–9)

**#6 Теория понятий** (Theory of Concepts) — "Teaches how we think about concepts... The 'type machine' (машинка типов): all objects resemble each other in some way, and this is described by types." [R-C §2.6]

**#7 Онтология** (Ontology) — "Teaches how to describe/model the world: how we determine what is important and what is not (modelling), and how we use models to answer questions. Covers meta-modelling, system levels (part-whole), emergence, constructive ontology." [R-C §2.7]

**#8 Алгоритмика** (Algorithmics / Computational Thinking) — "Addresses how to compute efficiently — to conduct a given sequence of operations on memory contents that represent mathematical objects. Covers all types of universal computers: human minds, classical computers, quantum computers." [R-C §2.8]

**#9 Логика** (Logic) — "Addresses which modes of computation-as-reasoning over models yield the most error-free results: logical inference, functional evaluation, mathematical function computation, intuitive human assessments, estimation, prediction." [R-C §2.9]

### Knowledge-building layer (10–11)

**#10 Рациональность** (Rationality) — "The discipline of creating *correct explanations* — theories/models that describe causes and effects. Rationality = explanations + decision-making + actions." *Consolidated in 2023 from split 2021 slots (Исследования + Объяснения).* [R-C §2.10]

**#11 Познание / Исследования** (Cognition / Research) — "How good explanations are obtained through the research cycle: making guesses (conjectures) about causal models/theories, then criticising those guesses through logical consistency tests and experimental confirmation. Popperian critical rationalism applied as practice." [R-C §2.11]

### Coordination & communication layer (12–14)

**#12 Эстетика** (Aesthetics) — "Criteria of beauty and elegance in the results of thinking and applied labour... what response our behaviour elicits in agents — not just in humans, but in agents generally, including AI. Style is multilevel patterning." [R-C §2.12]

**#13 Этика** (Ethics) — "What goals are acceptable to pursue and what means are acceptable to use. Ethics discusses style of life and the governance of research results." [R-C §2.13]

**#14 Риторика** (Rhetoric) — "How to persuade an agent to take actions... Rhetoric is constrained by Ethics... It accounts for the fact that both humans and AI are implemented as neural networks, not logical machines." [R-C §2.14]

### Action & engineering layer (15–16)

**#15 Методология** (Methodology) — "A scale-free teaching about agents' activities aimed at changing the world and themselves. Its primary object is the *method of work* (метод работы / способ работы / практика)." Primary home of роль, альфа, граф создания, стратегирование (R-C §4 ★★★). [R-C §2.15]

**#16 Системная инженерия** (Systems Engineering) — "A normative trans-discipline — it not only studies but *prescribes* at a high level of abstraction (meta-meta-model) how activities for creating systems should be structured." Apex of 2023 stack. [R-C §2.16]

### 2021 "17" variant — items dropped or merged in 2023

Per R-C §2.A version table:
- **Теория информации** → absorbed into Физика.
- **Объяснения** → absorbed into Рациональность.
- **Экономика** → removed; praxeological content moved into Методология.
- **Системное мышление** → no longer standalone; now the *application* of the full lower stack.
- **Труд (инженерия, менеджмент, предпринимательство)** → 2021 apex combined slot; replaced by Системная инженерия alone.

[R-C §1.2, §2.A]

## Part 3 — Essence kernel (from R-D)

### What Essence is

R-D §ES verbatim: **Essence** is "an OMG-standardized meta-framework for describing software engineering methods. Proposed by SEMAT... published as OMG Essence v1.0 in November 2014... revised as v1.1 (2015), v1.2 (2018), and an alpha draft v2.0 (February 2024)."

"Its central insight is that every software engineering endeavor, regardless of the specific methodology employed, works with the same seven fundamental things — called **alphas** (Abstract-Level Progress Health Attributes) — each of which moves through a sequence of past-participle-named states verified by checklists. Alphas are not documents or processes but role-based functional objects: the things you *track* rather than the things you *produce*."

**Two layers**: Language (type system for authoring kernels/practices) + Kernel (software-specific instantiation — the seven alphas). Levenchuk preserves Language verbatim; replaces Kernel. [R-D §1.1]

### The 4 kernel element types [R-D §1.2]

**Alphas** (essential things to work with); **Activity Spaces** (15 essential things to do); **Competencies** (6 core capabilities × 5 levels); **Patterns** (named bundles of synchronized alpha states). Plus **Work Products** — physical artifacts manifesting alpha states; "one alpha is typically manifested by 10–15 different work products." R-D §1.2: "This separation — functional alpha vs. physical work product — is the philosophical heart of Essence."

### The 7 alphas, 3 areas, states [R-D §1.3 verbatim]

**Customer:**
- **Stakeholders** — "people, groups, or organizations who affect or are affected by a software system." States: Recognized → Represented → Involved → In Agreement → Satisfied for Deployment → Satisfied in Use.
- **Opportunity** — "circumstances that makes it appropriate to develop or change a software system." States: Identified → Solution Needed → Value Established → Viable → Addressed → Benefit Accrued.

**Solution:**
- **Requirements** — "what the software system must do to address the opportunity and satisfy the stakeholders." States: Conceived → Bounded → Coherent → Acceptable → Addressed → Fulfilled.
- **Software System** — "a system made up of software, hardware, and data." States: Architecture Selected → Demonstrable → Usable → Ready → Operational → Retired.

**Endeavor:**
- **Work** — "activity involving mental or physical effort done in order to achieve a result." States: Initiated → Prepared → Started → Under Control → Concluded → Closed.
- **Way of Working** — "the tailored set of practices and tools used by a team." States: Principles Established → Foundation Established → In Use → In Place → Working Well → Retired.
- **Team** — "a group of people actively engaged in the development, maintenance, delivery or support of a specific software system." States: Seeded → Formed → Collaborating → Performing → Adjourned.

### Activity Spaces & Competencies

**Activity Spaces** [§1.4]: 15 method-independent placeholders with Entry/Completion Criteria as alpha states — Customer (4), Solution (6), Endeavor (5).

**Competencies** [§1.5]: 6 core × 5 levels (Assists, Applies, Masters, Adapts, Innovates): Stakeholder Representation, Analysis, Development, Testing, Leadership, Management.

### Essence ↔ ШСМ per R-D

**Borrowed unchanged** [§2.1]: alpha construct, past-participle state naming (замыслено, воплощено, эксплуатируется), state-checklist structure (AND-logic yes/no control questions), alpha-state graph (cycles and returns valid), work products as manifestation, "things to care about" framing, alpha containment.

**Modified** [§2.2]: Solution-area replaced — "Requirements / Software System" → "**System Definition / System Embodiment**" (Определение системы / Воплощение системы), aligned with ISO 42010. Middle-period (2014–2020) substitution of **Технология** for Way of Working. 2023–2024 editions stabilize around four universal alphas (verbatim): "воплощение системы, описание системы, функция/метод работ (way of working), работы системы."

**Added by Levenchuk** [§2.3]: **граф создания** (3-level mereology: super-system / target system / enabling system — "given following the motifs of OMG Essence 2.0:2024"); **стратегирование** ("method of choosing a method"); **интеллект-стек**; **трансдисциплины**; **мышление письмом**; **AI agents as role executors**.

**Rejected / sidelined** [§2.4]: activity spaces as compositional layer; practice-composition with OCL constraints; the software-specific kernel itself.

### Does Essence's alpha differ from Levenchuk's alpha?

Language preserved; definition generalized. R-D §2.2: "Levenchuk explicitly reinterprets 'alpha' not as 'ALPHA' backronym but as 'the subject of a method' (предмет метода) — any important object whose state changes must be tracked throughout a project." Broadens scope from software tracking to universal method modeling.

Levenchuk's 2024 Language/Kernel rule verbatim [R-D §2.5]: «В нашем курсе мы рекомендуем использовать OMG Essence, но берём из него только language, а не описанный в нём пример kernel — ровно как этого и требует стандарт, ибо для каждой новой предметной области разработки надо бы разрабатывать свой kernel.»

R-D verdict [§ES]: **"Essence's Language is the ontological primitive; Levenchuk's kernel is the domain-neutral instantiation of that Language for any engineering endeavor; the original Essence kernel applies only to software.** For Jetix OS, the ШСМ generalization is more appropriate as a foundational layer, but the alpha state-machine mechanism (past-participle states + checklists) must be preserved verbatim — it is the sharpest tool in the framework."

## Part 4 — Mereology / holon hierarchy (from R-E)

### Definitions verbatim

**Mereology** [R-E §1.1] — "from Greek μερος, *meros*, 'part') is the philosophical and formal theory of part-whole relations... not about the content of things but about the structural logic of how things compose and decompose." Formal beginning: Leśniewski (1916, 1927–1931); modern formulation: Leonard & Goodman (1940). "In mereology, a whole is *nothing over and above* its parts — it is the fusion of them. The whole is concrete, not abstract."

**Holon** [R-E §2.1] — Koestler coinage (*The Ghost in the Machine*, 1967), from *holos* (whole) + *-on* (part). Koestler 1969: *"Sub-wholes on any level of the hierarchy are referred to as holons."*

**Holarchy** [R-E §2.1] — "A hierarchy of holons... in a pure hierarchy, nodes are either pure parts or pure wholes. In a holarchy, every node is simultaneously whole-and-part."

**Partonomy (партономия)** [R-E §1.3, Levenchuk's preferred term] — "An ordered tree of part-whole relations where each node is a holon. Unlike a flat mereological sum, a partonomy emphasizes the *hierarchical organization* of parts."

### Operational rules

**Janus phenomenon** [R-E §2.1, Koestler 1.4 verbatim]: "Biological holons are self-regulating open systems displaying both autonomous (whole) and dependent (part) properties... holons face simultaneously inward (to their parts) and outward (to their containing whole), like the two-faced god Janus."

**Self-assertive (S-A) vs Integrative (INT)** [Koestler 4.1 verbatim]: "Every holon has the dual tendency to preserve and assert its individuality as a quasi-autonomous whole; and to function as an integrated part of an existing or evolving larger whole."

**Dynamic equilibrium** [Koestler 9.1]: "An organism or society is in dynamic equilibrium when the S-A and INT tendencies counterbalance each other." Two failure modes:
- **9.4** — over-excited holon "monopolizes functions, to the detriment of the whole" (S-A excess).
- **9.5** — "power of the whole over its parts erodes their autonomy" (INT excess, groupthink).

**Upward / downward cohesion** (R-E §2.1 applied): Every holon simultaneously (a) **looks inward as a whole** — governs its parts, sets their context, resolves intra-holon coordination; (b) **looks outward as a part** — responds to parent holon's goals, contributes outputs upward, cannot act contrary to parent charter.

### Formal axioms of mereology [R-E §1.2]

Core M: **Reflexivity (P.1)** ∀x Pxx; **Transitivity (P.2)** (Pxy ∧ Pyz) → Pxz; **Antisymmetry (P.3)** (Pxy ∧ Pyx) → x=y; **Weak Supplementation (P.4)** PPxy → ∃z(Pzy ∧ ¬Ozx) — "a proper part cannot exhaust its whole"; **Strong Supplementation (P.5)** entails extensionality; **Unrestricted Composition (GEM, P.15)** — philosophically contested, operationally irrelevant for Jetix.

### Levenchuk's "lightweight mereology" — what is included [R-E §5.1 verbatim]

Source: «За основу мы берём мереологию холонов (эпистем, дисциплин, систем, сообществ и т.д.). Холоны отличаются конструированием их из частей и входимостью в другие целые как части.»

1. **Holons as the primitive unit** — not atoms. Partonomy is preferred structure: "ordered multi-level hierarchy, not flat fusion."
2. **Role as mask (маска роли)** — "Roles are not separate holons but *masks* that holons wear in context... the role exposes some features of the holon's behavior in a given time window."
3. **Interface and encapsulation** — "Holons expose interfaces that encapsulate internal assembly. The interface acts as an invariant at the holon's boundary."
4. **Three-level creation graph** — target system / creating system / supersystem. "Мереологически ориентирован — creating systems are themselves systems that someone creates. The recursion is explicitly managed through the metaholon transition (метахолонный переход) concept."
5. **Constructor theory vocabulary** — "Transformations... are taken from constructor theory... the task specification defines possible/impossible transformations. This is the vocabulary only, not the full physical theory."
6. **"Advanced mereology" apparatus** — multi-type parthood, hierarchical kind-representation, renormalization-group-style metaholon recursion. CT2R-LOG and Kind-CAL architheories referenced.

### What Levenchuk excludes [R-E §5.1, §5.2 verbatim]

From CEM/GEM: unrestricted composition; extensionality as identity; flat no-preferred-decomposition stance.

From philosophical apparatus: Kit Fine's qua-objects, mereological coincidence, full location pluralism, full hylomorphism; constructor theory's quantum foundations; BFO ("негодная онтология для инженерных задач" — generates "mereological monsters (a herd has a tail)"); Van Inwagen's organicism; pure extensionality.

Levenchuk's critique: «Теоретическая мереология: красивые логические формулы, никакой связи с жизнью.»

### Applied to a 6-agent swarm (R-E §4.2 PROSA mapping verbatim)

| PROSA holon | Jetix analog |
|---|---|
| Product holon | Service/product specification (e.g., AI audit methodology) |
| Resource holon | Agent capabilities (sales-lead with CRM tools, strategy-lead with research tools) |
| Order holon | Client engagement / project instance |
| Staff holon | Strategic-management role (Ruslan) as advisor to execution agents |

R-E §4.2 Anthropic pattern verbatim: "an orchestrator (lead agent, Claude Opus 4) spawns 3-5 subagents (Claude Sonnet 4) in parallel. Each subagent operates in its own context window with defined objective, output format, tool access, and task boundaries... This is the Janus-face in practice: each subagent is a whole... and a part."

**For Jetix 6-agent swarm**: brigadier = parent holon containing 5 sub-holons (expert-1..5); each expert = holon containing its sub-artefacts (strategies.md, wiki niche slice, scratchpad.md, mailbox). Each expert-holon is Janus-faced. Role manifest is the *mask*; underlying LLM is fill.

### Explicit rules from R-E that must be respected [R-E §7.1 verbatim]

**A. Koestlerian holon duality as design principle** — "Every role manifest should explicitly state: (a) S-A scope — what this holon governs autonomously, (b) INT obligation — what this holon contributes upward to the containing holon."

**B. Three-level creation graph** (target / creating / supersystem) — "the correct operational mereological tool. Ensure every L3 business direction has an explicit creation graph."

**C. Role-as-mask** — "Role manifests should be written as if they will be filled by any competent executor — human or AI... The role is not a part of the holon; it is the mask the holon wears."

**D. Constructor theory vocabulary for capability mapping** — "Add 'possible tasks' and 'out-of-scope tasks' to each role manifest."

**E. PROSA analogy** — "Staff holons (Ruslan as strategic-management) give advice; they do not command."

**F. Granularity vocabulary for grey zones** — add "granularity at which this belongs to Jetix / Life-OS / shared."

**Anthropic-derived mereological principles** [R-E §4.2]: "Teach the orchestrator how to delegate" = define holonic boundary precisely; "Separation of concerns: distinct tools, prompts, exploration trajectories" = holonic encapsulation; "Subagent output to filesystem to minimize game of telephone" = reduce inter-holon comm overhead via explicit interface contracts; "Scale effort to query complexity" = dynamic holarchy.

**Hard cost constraint** [R-E §4.2]: "Multi-agent systems use ~15x more tokens than chat. This is the INT overhead... Holonic decomposition is only justified when parallelization gain outweighs coordination cost."

**Middle-path rule** [R-E §8]: "Import exactly as much formalism as is needed to solve a problem informal language cannot solve. No more."

## Part 5 — Synthesis notes (3 bullets per source, for AI-swarm design)

### R-C (trans-disciplines)

- **Canon is 16, not 17.** Do not confuse the 2021 list (Образование для образованных) with the 2023/2024 Интеллект-стек. Dropped/merged slots: Экономика (removed), Теория информации (→ Физика), Объяснения (→ Рациональность), Системное мышление (no longer standalone), Труд (→ Системная инженерия alone).
- **Brigadier-mandatory disciplines** are Собранность, Логика, Этика, Методология, Системная инженерия, plus baseline Понятизация/Семантика. These cut across every expert — coordination prerequisites, not specialist territory. R-C §7.2 top-5 leverage shortlist (Онтология, Рациональность, Методология, Собранность, Семантика) is the pragmatic core.
- **Методология is the operational home of all 5 ШСМ primitives** (R-C §4 ★★★ rating for роль, альфа, граф создания, стратегирование). The brigadier MUST own Методология; non-negotiable for multi-agent coordination.

### R-D (Essence × ШСМ)

- **Preserve Essence's Language verbatim; author Jetix's own Kernel.** Levenchuk's rule: take only Language, develop domain-specific kernel. Alpha construct, past-participle state naming, yes/no checklist AND-logic, state graph with cycles, alpha containment — preserved unchanged. Jetix authors its own alphas + states + checklists for AI-consulting / life-ops domain.
- **Use the 2023–2024 four-alpha universal core**: воплощение системы, описание системы, функция/метод работ (way of working), работы системы. External project roles, opportunities, team are sub-alphas. Do NOT revive the middle-period Technology-for-Way-of-Working substitution.
- **AI agents are first-class role executors.** Levenchuk verbatim: «Создатели – это агенты, всё что из методов умеет делать агент – это личность агента.» Past-participle alpha states serve as **machine-readable AI agent onboarding protocol** — describing current alpha state is the most compact briefing. Design primitive, not convenience.

### R-E (mereology / holon)

- **Janus duality (S-A ↔ INT) must be explicit in every role manifest.** R-E §7.1A is prescriptive: state (a) S-A scope governed autonomously, (b) INT obligation contributed upward. Failure modes are named: 9.4 = S-A excess (monopolizes), 9.5 = INT excess (defers everything). Use in retrospectives.
- **Role is a mask, not a part.** Role manifests are executor-independent (Claude now, any LLM later, human contractor possible — holarchic structure invariant). The holon = behavior-bearing unit; the LLM is fill, not identity. Apply Weak Supplementation (P.4): no holon may have only one proper part — a cluster with a single L1 role is not a holon.
- **Holonic decomposition costs tokens (~15x vs chat); justify it.** Decompose only when parallelization gain beats INT coordination cost. Apply Levenchuk's middle-path rule: import exactly as much formalism as is needed to solve a problem informal language cannot solve. Honor his explicit exclusions: no Kit Fine hylomorphism, no constructor-theory-as-physics, no category theory at current scale, no BFO, no Van Inwagen organicism, no pure extensionality. Avoid "академический театр" — formalism as performance rather than instrument.
