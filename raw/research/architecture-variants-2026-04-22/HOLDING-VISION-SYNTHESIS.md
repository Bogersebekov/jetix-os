### 3.2 Nuances requiring Ruslan attention

**Nuance #1 — Note 6 Token economy vs D3 Closed-outside/Open-inside:**

If Jetix tokens trade публично (IPO-like), это потенциально blurит inside/outside membrane. Public investor → имеет "stake" без прохождения membrane.

**Resolution options:**
- (a) Token utility для specialists only (internal currency, non-transferable) — keeps D3 clean
- (b) Limited public issuance с redemption rules — partial blur acceptable
- (c) Public token = только economic claim, не governance / не community access — membrane protected
- (d) Delay all token considerations до Phase 3+ когда holding structure clarifies

→ **FLAG for Ruslan decision.** Proposed Decision 23 (Token Economy Exploration) keeps option open; actual design deferred.

**Nuance #2 — Note 2 "самая большая афера века" framing vs D12 anti-attention-economy:**

Is "invitation to biggest adventure" mass-attention tactic? Or quality-filter via self-selection?

**Reading:**
- Ruslan framing: not generic hook, а **filter-via-framing**. Only люди with "startupper + azart + adequate + upward-direction" self-identify as "авантюрист". Generic consumer passes by.
- Comparison: "присоединяйтесь к самой большой авантюре" ≠ "подпишитесь на мой канал". Первое — призыв к действию с прояснением character; второе — attention extraction.
- D12 anti-attention-economy targets mass-attention for sake of engagement metrics. Note 2 framing targets specific self-selecting demographic.

**Resolution:** Compatible. Framing belongs в predator-outside tier (D1) — bold, provocative, self-selecting. Not mass-attention addiction. No new decision needed — just nuance в D1/D2 sections.
