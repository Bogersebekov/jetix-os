## §5 Phase 4 — Recursive partnership flow detail (1h)

**Output:** `decisions/strategic/RECURSIVE-PARTNERSHIP-MECHANICS-2026-05-21.md` (~3000w, sub-doc)

### Content

**§A 3-layer recursion full mechanic**

For total system revenue R per period:

**Layer 1: Partner → Jetix**
- Each partner generates revenue R_p
- 25% × R_p goes к Jetix institutional treasury
- Used for: management / new product development / amplification

**Layer 2: Total system → Jetix управленцам**
- TOTAL system revenue R = Σ all R_p
- 25% × R goes к Jetix управленцам (operational team)
- Choice: «в карман» (personal compensation) OR reinvestment OR mix
- Decision: governance via Layer 1 token holders

**Layer 3: L2 pool → Ruslan personally**
- 25% × L2 = 25% × (0.25 × R) = 0.0625 × R
- Goes to Ruslan personal account
- Ruslan then re-enters as partner → contributes back 25% × 0.0625 × R = 0.0156 × R к Jetix L1
- Recursive cascade: 0.0625 → 0.0156 → 0.0039 → 0.001 → ... → geometric series sum = 0.0625 / (1 - 0.25) = 0.0833 = effective 8.33% Ruslan slice

**§B Closed-loop verification**
- Total share Ruslan extracts: 8.33% (per geometric series)
- L2 управленцы pool: 25% - 6.25% (Ruslan slice from L2) = 18.75% to other управленцы
- Worker pool: 75% direct
- Sum: 75% + 18.75% + 6.25% = 100% ✓
- Recursion via Ruslan re-entry maintains closed loop without leakage

**§C Sensitivity scenarios**

If Layer 1 take rate varies (10-25% per partnership per DR-26):
- L1 = 10%: worker pool 90% / Jetix institutional 10%
- L1 = 25%: worker pool 75% / Jetix institutional 25%
- L2 (25% of total): unchanged percentage
- L3 (25% of L2): unchanged percentage
- Ruslan effective slice: 0.25 × L2 / (1 - 0.25) = depends on L2 setup

**§D R12 audit recursive structure**
- Layer 1 = «extraction beyond agreed share» check: Charter explicit + 30-day opt-out
- Layer 2 = разделение между управленцами: governance vote + transparent on-chain
- Layer 3 = Ruslan stake: published explicit in Charter
- Recursion: voluntary opt-in to re-enter (Ruslan can fork-leave too)
- Mondragón 5:1 ratio cap: payout top (Ruslan effective 8.33%) vs bottom worker = within 5:1 if cohort ≥6 person
