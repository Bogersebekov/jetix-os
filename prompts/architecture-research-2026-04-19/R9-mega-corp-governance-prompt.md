# R9 — Mega-Corp Governance: от solo до mega-corporation

## Зачем этот research

Jetix закладывается с Day 1 под **mega-corporation** с Phase 1 (solo) → Phase 2 (team 5-50) → Phase 3 (mega-corp 100-1000+). Scale-up-first design требует что вся архитектура **справляется с 10x ростом без rebuild**.

R1 (career levels) дал baseline C-suite timing (CFO / COO / CEO / CTO). Но **deep governance mechanics** требуют dedicated research:

1. **C-suite deep evolution** — за R1 basic overview, но когда именно нанимать и кого, реalistic compensation structures, decision-rights deep
2. **Divisional architectures** — Berkshire Hathaway / Constellation Software / Danaher / 3M / J&J conglomerate patterns. Как разделить когда multiple products/markets
3. **OKR at mega-scale** — Google / Intel original + adaptations. Как 50 teams синхронизированы
4. **Board governance** — когда нужен board, structure, compensation, meetings cadence
5. **Executive rituals** — monthly all-hands, quarterly reviews, annual planning. Actual cadence и artefacts
6. **Crisis governance** — incident response на corporate level, when CEO involved, communication patterns
7. **M&A governance** — Constellation acquisitions method (70+ deals/year), due diligence processes, integration
8. **Compensation & equity** — для AI-era (меньше людей, AI leverage), stock structures
9. **Legal entity architecture** — BV / OÜ / GmbH / LTD / LP, multi-jurisdiction (DACH + EU + possible US)
10. **Founder mode at scale** — как preserve founder влияние когда 100+ employees (Paul Graham 2024)

Выход — critical input для **Phase 2/3 evolution plan** + advanced L7 Portfolio patterns + legal structure decisions.

### Часть C — OKR at mega-scale

1. **OKR origins:** Andy Grove Intel (1970s), John Doerr at Google (1999), «Measure What Matters» (2018).

3. **OKR at 50+ teams scale:**
   - Cascading patterns (company → dept → team → individual)
   - Alignment vs autonomy balance
   - Quarterly vs continuous

4. **Common failure modes:**
   - OKR becomes KPI (stretch vs sandbag)

   - Disconnected from strategy

5. **Alternatives:**
   - Amazon's culture of writing + PR/FAQ
   - Basecamp's Shape Up (no OKRs)
   - Linear method (product-focused)
   - Bullet Journal / GTD personal productivity scaled

6. **Jetix-specific:**
   - OKRs для Phase 1 (solo) — needed?
   - Phase 2 (team of 5) — yes, lightweight
   - Phase 3 (team of 100+) — full cycles

7. **AI-augmented OKRs:**
