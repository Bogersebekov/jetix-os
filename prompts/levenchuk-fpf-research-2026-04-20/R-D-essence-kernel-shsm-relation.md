## Background context

Essence Kernel is an OMG (Object Management Group) standard defining
universal "kernel" elements for software engineering endeavours: 7
alphas, 4 activity spaces, etc. SEMAT (Software Engineering Method and
Theory) initiative developed it in 2010s.

Anatoly Levenchuk has adopted, extended, and modified Essence into ШСМ
framework. His **alpha primitive** (one of 5 ШСМ primitives) directly
derives from Essence's alpha concept.

I need:
1. Authoritative understanding of original Essence Kernel
2. Specifically what Levenchuk **borrowed**, **modified**, **rejected**,
   and **added**
3. Why differences exist (intellectual / practical justifications)
4. Implications for using Levenchuk's alpha primitive correctly

This research feeds Jetix OS's FPF (Foundational Philosophy Framework)
document — needed for ontological precision when defining 8 true alphas.
