You are conducting research on **Анатолий Левенчук's "17 trans-disciplines"
(17 трансдисциплин)** — the curriculum framework that ШСМ (School of

practice.

## Background context

Levenchuk teaches that systematic thinking + practice requires mastery
of 17 trans-disciplines, organized hierarchically. These trans-
disciplines are the **building blocks** for any methodology / framework
applied to a specific domain (engineering, management, education, etc.).

I need:
1. Complete enumeration of all 17
2. Hierarchical structure (which depend on which)
3. Relations between them
4. Application examples for each
5. Mapping to specific Levenchuk courses + book chapters
6. Current 2024-2026 versions (some have evolved)

This research will inform how Jetix OS applies / references these
trans-disciplines in its FPF (Foundational Philosophy Framework) document.

- Sequence of learning recommended
