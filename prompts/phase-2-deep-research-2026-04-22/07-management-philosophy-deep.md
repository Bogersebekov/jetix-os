## Specifically required deliverables

### D1. The 3-5 Universal Principles

Across all 10 sub-domains (A-J), identify **3-5 management principles that appear consistently in every canon**. For each universal principle:

- Principle name (1-3 words)
- Core statement (1-2 sentences)
- How each canon (A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H, I, J) expresses this principle (10 expressions, one per canon)
- Why this principle is invariant (mechanism)
- AI-native adaptation (if any)
