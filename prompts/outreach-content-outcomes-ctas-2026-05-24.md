## §4 Phase 3 — CTAs design (что делать в отношении Jetix)

### §4.A CTA variants per Jetix relationship type

| CTA type | What | When applicable | R12 paired-frame |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free explore** | Read repo / wikis / docs (GitHub public) | Stage 1-2 | ✅ no extraction |
| **Subscribe updates** | Email list / Telegram channel | Stage 1-3 | ✅ voluntary opt-in |
| **Discovery call** | 30-60 min Zoom — обсудить applicability | Stage 3-4 | ✅ mutual exchange |
| **Donate** | Patreon / OpenCollective / crypto | Stage 3-6 если support без commitment | ✅ no obligation |
| **Buy course** | Workshop format (когда launched) | Stage 4-5 — paying user L7 | ✅ fork-and-leave preserved |
| **Trial cohort member** | L6 cohort member (20-25% take rate) | Stage 5-6 | ✅ 30-day opt-out |
| **L5 partner** | Cohort partner (15-20% take) | Stage 6 | ✅ Charter signed |
| **L4 Founding partner** | Founding partner (10% take) | Stage 6 — long-term commitment | ✅ Mondragón 5:1 cap |
| **Co-create content** | Author wiki / module / video | Stage 7 advocate | ✅ attribution preserved |
| **Mass advocate / refer** | Bring others (network effect) | Stage 7 | ✅ no MLM pyramid |
| **NO CTA** (educational only) | Just learn and apply alone | Stage 1-N | ✅ default-deny — никогда обязательно |

### §4.B Per-stage CTA recommendation

- Stage 1-2: **Free explore + Subscribe** only (no monetary CTA)
- Stage 3: + Discovery call option
- Stage 4: + Donate option (if learner values + wants support)
- Stage 5: + Trial cohort
- Stage 6: + L4/L5/L6 partner options
- Stage 7: + Co-create + Mass advocate

### §4.C R12 paired-frame discipline per CTA

Per CTA — 8-item checklist:
1. Offer explicit
2. Ask explicit
3. Voluntary opt-in
4. Fork-and-leave preserved
5. Cooperative share framing (NOT extraction)
6. Mondragón 5:1 cap mentioned where applicable
7. No manipulation (NO fake scarcity / social proof games)
8. Specific contact + timeline

**Halt if violated:** влияние-ethics-expert auto-fires per R12 audit.

### §4.D Anti-CTAs (что НЕ делаем)

- ❌ Manipulative urgency («только сегодня!»)
- ❌ Social proof manufactured («1000 customers!»)
- ❌ Hidden price escalation
- ❌ Lock-in without exit
- ❌ MLM / pyramid referral
- ❌ «Save the world» savior framing
- ❌ Cult-like signature commitments
